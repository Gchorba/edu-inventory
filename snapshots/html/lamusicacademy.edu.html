Welcome to the LA Music Academy

Turn your passion for music into a music career

A Letter from the President

I CAN'T TELL YOU how many people I've met who dream of a music career, but find ways to talk themselves out of it. Who end up wondering "what if" they'd pursued their dream, instead of settling for an ordinary life.

There are opportunities available for people willing to make it happen. Are you one of them?

I'm very grateful that every day I get to come to work and be around people pursuing their dreams. At LA Music Academy our students are willing to do what it takes to make their careers as professional musicians, songwriters, performers, producers and engineers. They are making things happen.

So if you are one of the special ones willing to do the work to get a career in the music business, we are here to help. We've put together a world-class faculty and modern facilities to help you become a professional in the art and business of the music industry.

We offer Associate of Arts Degrees and Diplomas in Music Performance and Music Production, including:

  • Music Producer
  • Drum Performance
  • Guitar Performance
  • Vocal Artist Development
  • Bass Performance

So explore this website. There is tons of great information, stories, free lessons from faculty and industry information. And don't forget to fill out the form on the right column to request a free information kit. It's risk-free and could just be the first step to turning your passion into a music career!

Carpe Diem!

Tom Aylesbury
President, LA Music Academy

PS: Don't forget to check out our Summer Program, LAMA Jams and Drummer Reality Camps.

Free Information Kit

Fill out the form to receive a free "Get to the Music" Info Kit including a 24-page color brochure and additional information on LA Music Academy and our programs. Take the first step to discovering your new life as a professional musician!

We respect your privacy and do not rent, sell, or share any information you provide.

Accreditation

LA Music Academy is accredited by the National Association of Schools of Music (NASM). Financial Aid is available to those who qualify.
