TSCT Named Top PA Two-Year College

Thaddeus Stevens College was recently named by the Aspen Institute as one of the top 120 two-year colleges in the country. In addition, the college was the only institution from PA to make the list.

Branch Relocation
Information

During the 2011-2012 academic year, the branch campus at Clark Street will be closed due to renovations. Mechanical Engineering Technology, Computer Aided Drafting, Masonry, Construction Electrician, Carpentry (2nd year) & Respiratory Care will be temporarily housed at 1740 Hempstead Road at what is called the Veitch Building.

For information and directions about this location, please click here.
