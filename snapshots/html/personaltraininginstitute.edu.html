Accredited Personal Trainer School & Personal Trainer Certification

The National Personal Training Institute of Colorado is an NCA CASI / AdvancED accredited personal trainer school offering personal trainer certification preparation and applicable education. Our students insist on sustaining a career as a personal trainer at an ACCREDITED personal training school that is recognized by the U.S. Department of Education and approved for Title IV Federal Student Financial Aid. The personal trainer education covered by NPTI Colorado's curriculum is critical to becoming a certified personal trainer and starting a personal training career. NPTI Colorado is an education partner and / or teaches content from the American Council on Exercise (ACE), the National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM), HeartZones, the International Kettlebell Fitness Federation (IKFF), the National Strength and Conditioning Association (NSCA), the American College of Sports Medicine (ACSM), the International Health and Racquet Sportsclub Association (IHRSA) and other industry recognized health & fitness organizations and certifying agencies. Major employers like 24-Hour Fitness, Lifetime Fitness, Fitness 19, Snap Fitness, Bally's and others actively seek out our graduates.
