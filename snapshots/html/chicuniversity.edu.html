CHIC

Chic University of Cosmetology

Back to All Brands

Graduates of Chic University of Cosmetology are now part of the Empire Beauty Schools alumni and brand. Chic University of Cosmetology joined Empire Beauty Schools during the Regis School Division merger with Empire Beauty Schools in 2007. The beauty school was initially established in 1961 with the purpose and passion to train beauty students. Today, the three beauty schools, located in the state of Michigan, continue to offer high-quality cosmetology education through Empire's dedicated Cosmetology training program and specialized Teacher of Cosmetology training program.

The schools use Empire Beauty School's exclusive and dynamic CLiC Curriculum. The schools also have implemented the same 3D teaching aids as Empire's other schools, as well as integrated highly certified Cosmetology Educators, faculty and staff at each of the schools to offer the most advanced hands-on technical training. Students learn in a salon environment and practical applications inside the classroom.

Each school is NACCAS accredited and licensed in the state of Michigan, as well as approved to offer Title IV financial assistance.

Chic University of Cosmetology, now an Empire Beauty School, operates on a continuous basis throughout the year. Classes start soon and new students may enroll at any of the three locations in:

  • Grand Rapids-East
  • Grand Rapids
  • Portage
