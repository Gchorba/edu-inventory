Welcome to Allen School of Health Sciences

Thank you for visiting the site for Allen School of Health Sciences. In just a few clicks, you can be on your way to understanding more about the Allied Health Services and how you can begin your journey towards a new and exciting career with an unparalleled education from any one of our locations. More ⇓

We even offer Online and Flex Learning programs for students looking to segue into a new career in the health care industry, but that currently have ongoing obligations that keep them from attending onsite classes full time.

Contact us today to learn why our accredited programs can offer you the best opportunity to succeed and why we have continued to hold the confidence of employers in the Allied Health Industry for half of a century. Hide ⇑

A Leader in Healthcare Training Since 1961

For the past 50 years, Allen School has been dedicated to graduating successful professionals into rewarding careers in many aspects of patient care such as nursing assisting, medical assisting and medical insurance billing and coding. More ⇓

The career outlook for each of these fields continues to grow as advancements are always being made in technology and the demand for qualified professionals in the workforce expands.

Our staff includes some of the most highly regarded experts in their respective fields and we are constantly evaluating and evolving our programs to ensure that our students have the best opportunities to obtain work once they graduate. To date, we have launched the careers of over 50,000 medical professionals since our inception in 1961. Hide ⇑
