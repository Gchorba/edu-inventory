Creativity Starts Here

At the International Academy of Design & Technology (IADT), we see the world as a creative place with a growing need for highly skilled, professionally trained individuals with the talent, imagination and commitment to roll up their sleeves and push their passion to the next level.

Come here for the opportunity to learn from experienced professionals who have already blazed trails through numerous creative industries. Come here to fuel your imagination and build a get-noticed portfolio that showcases your best work. Come here to get creative about your future!

At IADT, location doesn't limit your opportunities.

No matter what time zone you live in, there's probably an IADT campus not far away where creative and technology-minded individuals are preparing themselves for exciting opportunities in the design, fashion, and media arts and technology industries.

Even if you don't live close to one of the major metropolitan areas where our modern facilities are located, you're never more than an Internet connection away from logging in and joining our growing community through IADT Online, our amazingly interactive virtual campus.

Creative people come from all over the world and from all walks of life. The geographic and cultural diversity of our student population fuels inspiration and expands the creative thinking across our entire community. Whether you come to us in person or online, we hope to see you soon!

We'll Help You Find Ways to Pay for Your Education.

At IADT, we don't think you should have to be a financial whiz to understand all of the options that may be available to you to help pay for your education. That's why we offer the services of experienced financial aid representatives who know how to explain everything in clear, simple terms. They can help take the mystery out of identifying the various financial aid options you may qualify for, including scholarship opportunities, grants, loans, tuition reimbursement or federal work-study programs.

Think of your education as an investment in yourself and your future. Then, let us help you figure out the best ways to go about paying for it. We've done our homework, and we know how to help reduce the out-of-pocket expenses associated with earning your degree.

Visit the IADT Financial Aid Page

Join the Who's Who of IADT.

IADT's Alumni Association is a growing network of talented individuals committed to pursuing their passions throughout the dynamic design, fashion, and media arts and technology industries. Tap into this network and you can benefit from a lifetime of personal friendships and professional connections that can open the windows to opportunities.

Our community is a collaborative, supportive group. Membership, which is automatic for all alumni and free of any dues or fees, begins when you graduate and continues for as long as you choose to take advantages of the many benefits that come with it. We look forward to sharing your accomplishments and encouraging you to reach your goals.
