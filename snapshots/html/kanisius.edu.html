A Jesuit School



As a school for boys, Canisius College Senior High School blends the national curriculum with valued-centered education and development of each Canisian student to become Indonesian youth, in pursuit of fully developed personality: familiarity with God, honesty and intelligence, eagerness for continuous learning, courage to search for truth, openess to cherish diversity as a treasure of the nation and available to serve - to be men for and with others.
