A College Degree On-Campus or Online

Westwood College offers more than 35 diploma, associate, bachelor's and master's programs, ranging from Construction Management to Graphic Design to Business Administration to Information and Network Technologies. You can go to college online or at one of our campuses. Westwood offers a unique hands-on, career-focused curriculum providing degrees in high-growth fields. Over 24,000 graduates have transformed their lives by obtaining the skills, tools, experience and connections necessary to achieve meaningful careers.

Westwood Accreditation

Westwood is accredited by the national accrediting agency ACICS, which is recognized by the U.S. Department of Education. Because our accreditation is recognized by the U.S. Department of Education, students have access to federal grants and loans.
