    ProspectiveStudents

    Saint Leo University offers 41 academic programs including some of the most sought-after majors you'll find anywhere.
    Saint Leo provides working adults with flexible class schedules, affordable academic excellence and a top-tier learning environment.
    With classes offered at flexible times, you can continue to meet your professional and personal obligations while earning a graduate degree.
    With Saint Leo University's Center for Online Learning, set your own weekly study schedule and attend class anytime, anywhere with a PC.
    Saint Leo serves students on military bases in seven states and worldwide through our Center for Online Learning.
