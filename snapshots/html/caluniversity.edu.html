CalUniversity Holds Its First Texas Cluster

California InterContinental University is proud to announce the start of our Student and Alumni Cluster Meetings. These informal get-togethers amongst CalUniversity students, alumni, and faculty will be held throughout the nation, and serve to connect the CalUniversity community. In the true tradition of an online educational institution, we will also be hosting virtual clusters for those who are overseas or who are unable to attend a local cluster meeting.

CalUniversity held its first Student and Alumni Cluster Meeting in San Antonio, Texas. Our Dean of Academic Affairs, Dr. Fathiah Inserto met up with Dr. Kelly Nix (DBA alumnus), Kim Erwin (final-year DBA), and 1st-year DBA students, Gale Goodman and Karen Ezell for an intimate breakfast in San Antonio.
