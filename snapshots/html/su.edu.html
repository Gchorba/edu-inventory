SU Calendar

  • University Chapel @ Noon Sunday, May, 6, 2012

    University Chapel @ Noon in Goodson Chapel is a blend of the best of ancient traditions and emerging, artful, expressive worship. Contact Worship Arts Coordinator DeLyn Celec at 540/665-3485 or goodsonchapel@su.edu    

  • Final Alphabet Soup Concert Monday, May, 7, 2012

    Final Alphabet Soup Concert begins at 8 p.m. in Armstrong Concert Hall. The program includes Terry Riley’s ‘In C,” and the New Music Ensemble Class will play a variety of improvisations. FREE    

  • Late-Night Breakfast Monday, May, 7, 2012

    Late-Night Breakfast will be served to Shenandoah students by faculty and staff from 10 p.m. to midnight in Allen Dining Hall. FREE    

  • Measure of Academic Proficiency & Progress Exam Friday, May, 11, 2012

    Measure of Academic Proficiency and Progress (MAPP) Exam is from 10:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. in the Brandt Student Center, Ferrari Room. This assessment exam is mandatory for all undergraduate candidates for bachelor degrees. Individuals who are receiving certificates or graduate degrees on May 12 are exempt.     

  • College of Arts & Sciences Honors Ceremony Friday, May, 11, 2012

    College of Arts & Sciences Honors Ceremony begins at 12:30 p.m. in Halpin-Harrison Hall, Stimpson Auditorium. FREE     

  • Commencement Rehearsal Friday, May, 11, 2012

    Commencement Rehearsal begins at 3 p.m. in Armstrong Concert Hall and is mandatory for all graduating students who are participating in the Commencement ceremony on Saturday.    

  • Grad Bash Friday, May, 11, 2012

    Grad Bash a campus community celebration of the Class of 2012, follows Commencement rehearsal in the quad near the big SU (Mumaw Plaza). Grad Bash is conveniently located near Armstrong Concert Hall, where Baccalaureate begins at 7 p.m. Everyone – all students, family and friends – is invited to join the celebration. Enjoy dinner and beverages, including beer for individuals 21 and over, while grabbing final photos with friends. FREE    

  • Graduate Admissions Open House Friday, May, 11, 2012

    School of Education & Human Development Graduate Admissions Open House begins at 5 p.m. in the Brandt Student Center, Ferrari Room. Contact Associate Director for Graduate Admissions Donna Raczynski at draczyns@su.edu    

  • Baccalaureate Service Friday, May, 11, 2012

    Baccalaureate Service begins at 7 p.m. in Armstrong Concert Hall. Baccalaureate is non-denominational service full of beautiful music, thoughtful reflection and peace. All students, their family and friends, and university employees are invited to enjoy this calming hour.    

  • School of Business Awards & Hooding Ceremony Saturday, May, 12, 2012

    Harry F. Byrd Jr. School of Business Awards & Hooding Ceremony begins at 8:30 a.m. in Halpin-Harrison Hall, Stimpson Auditorium. FREE
