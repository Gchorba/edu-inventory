  • Welcome to New England Culinary Institute

    At NECI, you'll experience a highly intense culinary education. For over 30 years, NECI has created a niche all its own – small classes, personal attention and our teaching restaurants. Our programs provide the training and real life experience necessary to thrive in the fast-paced food and beverage industry. Employers tell us that NECI graduates can hit the ground running.

  • Time to Follow Your Passion? Looking to Boost Your Career?

    If you’ve been considering formal culinary training, NECI will help you fast-track your way to a prestigious, industry-recognized degree in culinary arts, baking and pastry arts, or hospitality and restaurant management in as little as a six-month residency.

    Read More

  • Take a Tour

    Schedule a tour and see for yourself why NECI is different from any other culinary arts school. See the facilities, meet the instructors, and talk to current students. We conduct our campus tours on Friday mornings.

    Read More

  • High School Seniors

    If you’re a high school senior with a passion for food and a commitment to excellence, make New England Culinary Institute your first choice for a culinary education. NECI has been hailed as one of the top three culinary schools in the Nation*, and for good reason. With an intense curriculum and a strong tradition for hands-on learning, we immerse our students from day one in the passionate business of food.

    Read More

  • Career Changers

    If you’ve been working in another profession but your love of food has grown to more than a hobby, take a new direction with New England Culinary Institute. Many of our students are adults who are just starting a career in the culinary arts. NECI students come from a broad range of backgrounds, from those just out of high school to seasoned food service professionals to career changers from all over the world. They all share a common bond: a passion for food.

    Read More

  • Military and Veteran Transitioners

    When it's time to move back into civilian life, you may find that New England Culinary Institute offers exactly the path of new opportunities that you are looking for. Whether you are interested in using skills you have developed in the service, or in acquiring new ones, you should consider the options available to you at NECI.

    Read More

  • Industry Professionals

    If you’re already working in the culinary industry but you’d like to expand your skills and career opportunities, New England Culinary Institute is the right school for you. With NECI’s intense curriculum and comprehensive training, you’ll prepare for a more fruitful and rewarding career in food service.

    Read More

  • International Students

    New England Culinary Institute has been the educational choice for students from more than 30 countries from around the world. Our Admissions Team is here to help make the application process as simple as possible for you.

    Read More

  • Online Education

    The curriculum in NECI's specialized E-learning offerings has been specifically designed to be delivered to students who are pursuing their degrees outside the classroom, in your own homes, offices or study space. The work is no less rigorous nor the support systems less complete than they are for those who are physically attending class. But NECI's online programs in allow you to learn in a manner that best fits your lifestyle.

    Read More

Passion – If you've got the passion, we've got the program and a genuine interest in your success. You'll experience our targeted hands-on approach and small class sizes.

Intensity – We mean it when we say "You Learn by Living It." New learning situations constantly put your education to the test. When you graduate, you will be prepared for the rigors of this demanding industry and its incredible opportunities.

Our Internships – As part of your NECI education, you'll practice the skills you've learned at a NECI-approved internship site. They include some prestigious restaurants, resorts, and corporations.

Looking for an intense Culinary College educational program focused on preparing you for future career success? Take the opportunity to speak to one of NECI's Admissions Professionals to explore whether NECI is right for you.

Culinary Arts – Combines the old world tradition of European apprenticeship with the American holistic approach to food prep and entrepreneurship.

Baking and Pastry Arts – You are as much artist as technician. You'll learn to create one-of-a-kind specialties appreciated by the most discriminating consumer.

Hospitality and Restaurant Management – Finance, human resources, technology, service, purchasing, communication, and more put you on the fast track. In the classroom and in the field, you'll develop your critical and creative thinking skills.

Online Programs – Specifically designed for pursuing your degree outside the classroom, in your own home, office or study space. The work is just as rigorous and the support systems just as complete as they are on-campus.
