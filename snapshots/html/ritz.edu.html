  • Programmes

    César Ritz Colleges Switzerland are the world leaders in International Business, Tourism and Hospitality Management education.
     
    Our school has a global reputation for quality and excellence in education that spans over 30 years.
     
    Discover the perfect combination of traditional academic learning, practical classes and paid internships to get your hospitality career off to a flying start.
     
    All programmes maximize your learning experience and give you the skills and confidence needed for your future success.
     
    Any question, ask César Ritz Colleges

  • Higher Diploma / Bachelors

    Get a career head start with our undergraduate programmes and experience the exciting world of the international hospitality and tourism industry.
     
    Designed for high-school graduates, our Swiss Higher Diploma and our Bachelor programmes combine academic learning with practical excellence.
     
    Study the two and a half-year Swiss Higher Diploma, which appeals to those wishing to build careers in hotel and restaurant management, or decide to go further with our Bachelor programme, providing the knowledge required for more executive positions in the hospitality and tourism industry.

  • Postgraduate / Masters

    If you decide to change your career track or want to take your hotel management studies further, these postgraduate courses are for you.

    Designed for university graduates and candidates with extensive work experience, these courses develop the skills and knowledge needed for employment at a top management level and are ideal for those who seek a career change or are willing to develop their existing careers.

    After completing our intensive nine-month Postgraduate Diploma programme, continue your studies with either our Master of Arts in International Business or with our Master of Science in International Hospitality Management.

  • Culinary Institute Switzerland

    The Culinary Institute Switzerland combines practical kitchen skills with modern management method classes for a successful and creative culinary career!

    Choose the length of study that suits your needs; consider the Foundation degree if you want to quickly acquire a solid foundation of culinary knowledge, for aspiring head chefs and managers the more in-depth Bachelor degree will help fast track your career options.

    Small, hands-on classes allow you to work under the personal supervision of our internationally experienced chef instructors. Gain experience and practice your skills with paid internships in Switzerland, fully integrated into our programmes.
