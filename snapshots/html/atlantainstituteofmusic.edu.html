Welcome to Atlanta Institute of Music

Atlanta Institute of Music has a uniquely effective approach to vocational training for musicians, production, and engineering students utilizing a well-rounded curriculum, classroom instruction, private instruction, audio, DVD and video labs, a computer lab and live clinics. The AIM faculty is comprised of industry professionals whose education and work experience as session players, performing artists, producers, engineers, and instructors have prepared them to teach the demanding curriculum.

Programs

Atlanta Institute of Music offers programs for Guitar, Bass, Drums, and Recording. Learn More...

Guitar Atlanta Institute of Music provides the non-beginning guitar student with the instructional environment necessary to develop their musical talents and acquire the skills to become one of today's professional guitarists.
