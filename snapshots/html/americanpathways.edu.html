Institutional Accreditation (AALE)

American Pathways University is a member of the American Academy for Liberal Education (AALE), national accreditor of outstanding liberal arts colleges and universities, with the status of full institutional accreditation. AALE accredits institutions offering quality general education programs in the liberal arts that meet the Academy’s stringent educational, administrative, and financial criteria.
