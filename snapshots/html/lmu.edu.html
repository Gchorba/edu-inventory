Students Rally to Save Cal Grant Program

The rally urged legislators to reject a proposal by Gov. Jerry Brown to slash the maximum Cal Grant award by nearly half, which would severely reduce college affordability in California and limit the ability of current and future LMU students to pay for their education.


City Shows Optimism
20 Years After Riots

A new survey from LMU shows ethnic tensions in Los Angeles are lower and animosity toward the police department has lessened in the two decades since riots burned across a large part of the city. Some signs of pessimism remain, specifically around the local economy.
