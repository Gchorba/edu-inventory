Texas Science Scholar Program

At a meeting of the UT System Board of Regents, The University of Texas of the Permian Basin received approval for the Texas Science Scholar Program. Beginning in Fall 2012, UTPB will offer a Bachelor of Science degree to qualified, full-time students majoring in Chemistry, Computer Science, Geology, Information Systems and Mathematics at a cost of $10,000 in tuition and fees for the four-year curriculum.

Apply Today!

If you are in the top half of your high school graduating class, apply to UTPB today for a $1,000 per semester scholarship. It's that easy.

Transfer student? Apply to UTPB today for a $1,500 per semester scholarship.

Finish@UT

The University of Texas System is advancing its efforts to help adults complete their college degrees by launching a new program that will also leverage the nation’s efforts to have the highest percentage of college graduates in the world.

The new program provides opportunity for students to finish their Bachelor’s Degree Online and offers up to $60,000 in scholarships.
