Welcome to Grantham University
Grantham University, one of the oldest, private, accredited degree granting distance education universities in the United States, is now in its 60th year of providing distance education to the adult learner. The University’s 100% Accredited Online Degrees are convenient, portable and designed so students can fit education into their lives instead of arranging their lives around their education.

Accredited Online Degrees

Accredited since 1961 by the Accrediting Commission of the Distance Education and Training Council, Grantham University has compiled a distinguished record as a respected and accredited distance learning university spanning six decades of service to education.

As the world becomes more technology driven, working adults who wish to keep pace require a dependable, flexible way to obtain the specialized knowledge and training needed to compete in today’s workplace.  Grantham University offers a quality online education that is both accommodating and accessible to those who want to pursue higher education and secure an accredited online degree.

Business
Grantham University’s Mark Skousen School of Business offers online degree programs that deliver professional, real world, business skills.  The five undergraduate business degree programs and seven master’s degree business programs that comprise the School of Business are delivered in an online collaborative learning environment.

Online Degrees

Business Administration Degree - The Associate of Arts in Business Administration and the Bachelor of Arts in Business Administration prepare students to use analytical skills in evaluating cultural issues. In addition, students analyze theories, principles, and concepts in each area of business.

Online MBA - The Master of Business Administration program provide students with a practical knowledge of today’s business environment.  The program emphasizes finance, financial and managerial accounting, human resource management, information management, macroeconomics and microeconomics, marketing, organizational behavior, and quantitative analysis.

Computer Science
According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, “Computer scientists and database administrators are expected to be among the fastest growing occupations through 2014.” The computer science field is growing dynamically.  Information technology focuses on our devices, our media, and how we communicate, and career professionals are needed to design, build and maintain these vehicles of the modern age.

The Computer Science online degree programs, within the College of Engineering and Computer Science, are designed to meet the needs of changing industries and workplace demands, providing each Grantham online student with the knowledge base needed to succeed in today’s competitive job market.

Online University

The Associate of Science in Computer Science (AS-CS) and the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BS-CS) prepare students for computing technology careers in a continuously changing global society. Graduates prepare to work in a range of positions, from entry-level computer scientists and systems engineers to project managers.

Criminal Justice
Grantham University’s offers associate and bachelor’s degrees in the field of Criminal Justice.  The Criminal Justice degree program prepares graduates for the detection, apprehension, detention, pretrial/posttrial release, prosecution, adjudication, correctional supervision or rehabilitation of accused persons or criminal offenders.

Online Degrees

The Associate of Arts in Criminal Justice prepares students to demonstrate a basic working knowledge of law enforcement, corrections, investigations, and juvenile justice. Students gain the core curriculum needed to be successful in the criminal justice workforce or to pursue a more advanced degree in criminal justice.

Grantham University’s Bachelor of Arts in Criminal Justice is available as a general program of online study or with a concentration in Homeland Security or Computer Science. This program engages students in the theories of criminal justice as related to computer forensics and cyber crime, emergency planning, and national security.  Students gain the ability to analyze both theories and concepts of crime and employ analytical skills to make decisions and solve problems.

Engineering
Businesses are looking for graduates who have backgrounds in engineering design and analysis. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, “Job opportunities in engineering are expected to be favorable.”

The Engineering Technology programs within Grantham University’s College of Engineering and Computer Science are designed to meet the needs of changing industries and workplace demands, providing each student with the knowledge base needed to succeed in today’s competitive job market. Accredited Online Degrees effective, convenient and 100% Online.

Online Classes

The Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering Technology emphasizes electronics lab work and includes courses in DC/AC circuit design, advanced microprocessor design and applications, and digital and analog electronics and signal processing.

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering Technology prepares students to design and develop digital systems, including the associated software. The curriculum includes electronics and computer engineering technology courses, as well as computer science courses.

Nursing
A nursing degree can open many doors in the healthcare industry. In 2008, the healthcare sector added 419,000 jobs and the outlook for this industry remains strong.  The registered nursing career field is expected to grow by almost 600,000 jobs over the next 10 years, and registered nurses who decide to further their education will have an advantage in the marketplace.

Before selecting a degree, it might be best to think about the type of medical environment that suits you, and then apply yourself in that direction. Nursing homes, doctor’s offices, hospitals, clinics, mental health facilities, education, labs, home health, emergency care, or emergency response are just a handful of career choices.

Online Classes

Grantham University offers two online nursing degree options: a RN-BSN Degree Completion Program and a Master of Science in Nursing. These programs provide excellence in undergraduate and graduate nursing education and provide a means for the Registered Professional Nurse (RN) to achieve his or her undergraduate nursing education (BSN), and to further his or her educational achievements through the graduate nursing educational programs.
