  3. Experienced Professionals

  5. Financial Aid
  6. Job Placement Assistance
  7. Small Class Sizes
  8. Day and Evening Classes
  9. Accreditation
    Med-Assist School of Hawaii is accredited by the Accrediting Bureau of Health Education Schools (ABHES). "ABHES is the only agency recognized by the U.S. Department of Education as an institutional and specialized accreditor focusing on health care education and training." Visit the ABHES website for more information.
  10. National Certification
    We offer the national Registered Medical Assistant (RMA) exam given here at the school through American Medical Technologists (AMT). Although you do not need to be certified to practice medical assisting in Hawaii, we encourage our students to take the exam so they have the option of practicing medical assisting anywhere on the mainland.
