Become a Respected Medical or Business Professional

The Hands-On Training, Practical Skills & Dedicated Support to Pursue a Successful Professional Career That Really Matters to You

If you’re interested in the stability, personal satisfaction and potential financial security that can come from a professional career, and are looking for the skills and training to enter a new field, you owe it to yourself to see what Valley College has to offer.

Our accelerated career programs in medical and professional fields are designed to efficiently meet the needs of employers. Many students are career-ready in 8-10 months!

Choose from convenient Morning and Evening schedules. You can balance your career training around work, family and other responsibilities. Choose the schedule that works best for you.

Whether you’ve been out of school for a while, are juggling work and/or family or have struggled with traditional schooling in the past, you’ll feel fully supported and able to achieve at Valley College. Valley College is dedicated to what we call the “culture of caring.” Our helpful staff and faculty will support you and work with you one-on-one to help you pursue your academic and professional goals.
