Take a look at PTS:
     
 
PTS offers Christian graduate theological education in service of an intelligent, just, & compassionate society. We welcome students to a safe space for truth-seeking conversations about the Bible, Jesus, and faithful living. MDiv, MAMC, MTS, DMin, and certificate program courses are available on campus, by distance, and online.
