For more than 80 years, Berkeley College has specialized in preparing students for professional and personal success. Proven, career-focused Bachelor's and Associate's degrees are offered in a diverse range of areas. The College is widely recognized for providing students with a competitive edge and the skills sought by employers. This innovative combination of benefits is known as The Berkeley Advantage®.
