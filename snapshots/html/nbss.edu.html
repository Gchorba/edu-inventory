  • Pianos for Sale

    A 1915 Henry F. Miller 5'6" grand piano (pictured here) and a 1929 Mason & Hamlin model T, 5'4" grand piano are available for sale from NBSS. These pianos have been completely rebuilt by advanced piano technology students. More information about these pianos can be found on the NBSS gallery web page. Learn more about the rebuilding of pianos by following the online updates on the rebuilding of a grand piano. To inquire about purchasing a piano, contact Debbie Cyr at dcyr@nbss.edu / 617-227-0155.
