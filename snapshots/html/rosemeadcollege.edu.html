A Language school and TOEFL prep

Rosemead College of English (RCE) is a language school, located in sunny Southern California. It was established in 1992 and has two campuses, one in Rosemead, just 12 miles from Downtown Los Angeles, and a South Bay campus in Torrance. Both campuses have been approved by the INS to issue the SEVIS I-20 form to foreign students who intend to apply for a student visa (F-1).


Here's why RCE is your best choice to learn English:

  • Help with student F-1 Visas
  • Fast services for students' Accommodation
  • Perfect programs in ESL, TOEFL, TOEIC, and Special Emphasis IEP (NCLEX-RN Prep.)
  • Dedicated Admissions Counselors assist students in gaining entry to a U.S. College or Graduate School
  • U.S. Official TOEFL and TOEIC testing center
