Seminary

Welcome to the exciting world of advanced theological training!

Temple Baptist Seminary offers a variety of opportunities for advanced preparation for ministry or for personal enrichment.

For over half a century  Temple Baptist Seminary has been training successful pastors, evangelists, missionaries, Christian educators and administrators, youth directors, and other dynamic leaders for the ministry. Study under some of America's best conservative scholars through our fall and spring semester courses and modular programs on campus or through our distance education programs where you serve.

Temple Baptist Seminary's programs are academically respected, biblically sound, and professionally relevant.

There are many advantages of attending Temple Baptist Seminary. You can get the best possible degree in your field of study trained by the best faculty in conservative Christian circles.
