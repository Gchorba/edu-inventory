Undergrads Recognized for Research

Research conducted by more than 60 undergraduate students was showcased at the recent Undergraduate Research Conference. As part of the event, the first recipients of the Stephen H. Mosier Undergraduate Research Award for Science and the J. Murrey Atkins Library Research Awards were honored.

Richard Hardin and Anthony Harris were first- and second-place winners of the Mosier Award in the category of physical sciences engineering for the projects “Preparation Characterization and Reactions of Argentates Using Rapid Injection NMR” and “Pipe Traversing Robot,” respectively. Ryan Baxter and Natascha Moestl were the first- and second-place winners of the library award. A complete list of conference honorees is available online.

Important End-of-Semester Dates

It’s easy to forget how quickly the semester is going! End-of-semester dates are important for all students, so don’t forget that the last day of spring semester classes is scheduled for Tuesday, May 1. Reading day will be Wednesday, May 2, and final examinations will take place from May 3 - 10.

Commencement will be held on Saturday, May 12.
