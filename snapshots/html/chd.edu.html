Important Student Disclosures

Become a Successful Cosmetology, Barber, or Esthetics Professional

Launch an Exciting Professional Salon Career in 14 months.

 

Welcome to College of Hair Design in Lincoln, Nebraska where you can realize your dream of becoming a successful salon professional. Our program is designed to inspire and to provide the skills and hands-on experience you need to launch a rewarding professional career. College of Hair Design offers these professionally accredited programs:


  • Barber Arts
  • Cosmetology
  • Esthetics
  • Barber Arts Dual License Program
    (Cosmetology Pre-Required)
  • Cosmetology Student Instructor
How to Embed Video by VideoLightBox.com v1.7m

At College of Hair Design we're dedicated to teaching you the See, Think, Create and Adapt method. Our innovative and multi-media education program is from Pivot Point International, the world's leading hair and beauty education program. If you're interested in the latest styles in fashion, personal appearance and beauty and are looking for a career that incorporates your creativity, personality and style -- becoming a highly-skilled salon professional could be an excellent career path.

 

Discover if a Professional Salon Career is Right For You

To learn more about how College of Hair Design can help you launch a rewarding professional salon career, browse our site and explore the various career options open to you.

 

The best way to discover if Salon Career Training is right for you is to come in for a tour of our on-campus student salon and spa, here in Lincoln. See for yourself just what it's like to train and work in the professional beauty business. You can speak with our instructors who are working professionals in their fields and sit down with an Admissions Rep. to go over all your options -- including scheduling and financial aid. For more information or to schedule your College Tour, contact College of Hair Design today.
