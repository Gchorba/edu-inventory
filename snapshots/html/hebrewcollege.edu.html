Hebrew College At a Glance

Graduate, Adult and Youth Education

Hebrew College offers a diverse array of high-quality educational programs that provide many points of entry to Jewish learning. The institution strives to be a modern equivalent of Abraham’s tent, with all sides open to anyone who wishes to join our passionate pursuit of Jewish learning, through our graduate, adult, and youth education programs.

Online and On-campus Study

Our Newton Centre campus serves students in the Greater Boston area and throughout New England. Reaching out to a national and global audience, the College has been a pioneer in online Jewish studies and continues to expand our range of online degree and certificate programs, as well as online courses in Hebrew language and other subject areas.

Pluralistic Community

As a pluralistic institution, we recognize and value human diversity. Within our own k'lal Yisrael community and in dialogue with people from other faith traditions, we actively engage a multiplicity of experiences and perspectives in an environment of mutual respect. The encounter with different points of view prompts us to ask honest and searching questions of ourselves and of one another, and to see this process as a source of wisdom and strength.
