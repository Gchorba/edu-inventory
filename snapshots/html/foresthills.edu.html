AHS 4x800 Relay Team Breaks School Record
The 4X800 relay team of Casey Gallagher (2:01), Kameron Powell (1:59), Sean Batt (1:59), and Nick Vogele (1:53) set a new school record on April 26, 2012, at the Anderson Invitational with a time of 7:54.4. The old school record was set in 1984 at 7:58.5. This year’s relay team also set a meet record that dated back to 2002 held by Elder.

Eight FHSD Schools Earn Energy Star Certification
Forest Hills School District has earned the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency’s (EPA) ENERGY STAR certification for eight of its nine buildings. The rating signifies that each building performs in the top 25 percent of similar facilities nationwide for energy efficiency and meets strict energy efficiency performance levels set by the EPA.
