The Alabama Supercomputer Authority (ASA) is a state-funded corporation founded in 1989 to operate the Alabama Supercomputer Center (ASC) and the Alabama Research and Education Network (AREN).

 

There is a scheduled meeting of the Alabama Supercomputer Authority Board of Directors on Thursday, May 10th, 2012, at 1:00 P.M. at the Bradley Arant Conference Room, Suite 712, Alabama Center for Commerce, 401 Adams Avenue, Montgomery, Alabama.  The Executive Board will meet at 11:30 A.M. prior to the Board of Director's meeting.
