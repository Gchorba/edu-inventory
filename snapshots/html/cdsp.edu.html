Welcome to CDSP

Church Divinity School of the Pacific, the only Episcopal seminary and graduate school on the West Coast, integrates scholarship, reflection and worship with the ministry of the Gospel of Jesus Christ. In our classrooms, in our communities, and in our partnership with the ecumenical and inter-religious Graduate Theological Union, we define and enrich our faith, and embrace the future.
