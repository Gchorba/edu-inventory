At the leading edge of creativity and change

For more than 40 years, CIIS has pioneered a unique educational vision. The university has grown to encompass 17 leading-edge graduate programs and an undergraduate program that attract passionate learners and international renown.

GRADUATE PSYCHOLOGY PROGRAMS

We offer five Master's in Counseling Psychology
programs, with concentrations in:

Community Mental Health
Drama Therapy
Expressive Arts Therapy
Integral Counseling Psychology
Somatic Psychology

We also offer a PsyD in Clinical Psychology.

ONLINE GRADUATE PROGRAMS

Join a vibrant community of like-minded online graduate degree students from diverse backgrounds and an increasingly international student body that spans four continents.

MFA PROGRAMS

Learn about our MFA in Writing and Consciousness and our MFA in Creative Inquiry, Interdisciplinary Arts.

CONSCIOUSNESS AND TRANSFORMATION

Learn about our graduate degree options in the School of Consciousness and Transformation.

UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAM

Earn a degree in interdisciplinary study through the Bachelor of Arts Completion Program in San Francisco. The program serves returning learners who have completed the equivalent of the first two years of college.
