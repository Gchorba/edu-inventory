GCC Wins National Climate Award

Planet Forward and Second Nature recognized GCC's sustainability efforts and climate innovation in Second Nature's Climate Leadership Awards in the Associate College category.

Jack Kent Cooke Scholarship Award

Congratulations Amou Ajang for receiving the Jack Kent Cooke Foundation Undergraduate Transfer Scholarship, providing up to $30,000 a year! The Foundation selected 60 Scholars from among 786 applicaionts. Amou, demonstrated outstanding records of achievement, not only academically, but also in service, leadership, and community involvement.
