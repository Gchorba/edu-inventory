Medical School of the Indian Ocean Islands

Knowledge. Dedication. Excellence.

The charming, and scenic Seychelles Islands in the Indian Ocean with enviable record of peace and harmony offer you the finest environment for your studies.

The medical education system of our college follows the USA medical curriculum and syllabus. Small class sizes allow for close interaction between students and faculty.

Diverse school faculty and student body provides an enriching educational experience. Students with 3 A' level in Sciences or with pre-medical school subjects will be eligible for our pre-clinical program.

Our school has special programs that prepare students for the future independent medical researches. We expose our students from their early years to hospital environment by making frequent trips to USAIM affiliated Victoria hospital located in Seychelles.

Flexible Clinical Rotations in worldwide network of our affiliated teaching hospitals. In the "Medical Board Review" course the student is prepared for the United States Medical Licensing Exams (USMLE).

Global affixations with hospital for clinical rotation. Our medical school has competitive tuition and fee structure.
