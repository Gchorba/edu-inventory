Welcome! 

INSTE provides a complete educational program in two levels of study:

Making Disciples
Certificate - Four 16-week discipleship courses that prepare strong, committed disciples in the local church.

Developing Leaders
Diploma and Degree - 24 accelerated 7 to 10 week courses that lead to a Diploma in Christian Ministry or Christian Leadership or a B.A. in Bible and Theology.
