Attend Asheville, NC's Longest Established and ONLY COMTA Accredited Massage School !

CLASSES START MAY 28TH—APPLY ONLINE TODAY!

Celebrating 14 successful years in the Asheville community and over 1000 graduates, our Massage School is located in Downtown Asheville's Pack Square Cultural District, in the HEART of the City!

Attend our Career Day Event, including our 4-Hour Massage Workshop for only $25 at our Downtown Asheville Campus.

With brick sidewalks and full growth trees, our massage school and massage therapy clinic is surrounded by many popular restaurants, cafe's, shops and galleries, all within walking distance of our exciting downtown campus.
