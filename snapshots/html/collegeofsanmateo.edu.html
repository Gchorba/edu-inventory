    • Listen to music, support the Child Development Center at CSM

      Do you like reggae music? Are you a fan of children’s programs? Then you might be interested in a special concert planned at the College of San Mateo on May 12. The Mary Meta Lazarus Child Development Center hosts a spring benefit concert featuring the Bay Area band “Native Elements” and the San Francisco Panhandlers Steel Drum Band. It begins at noon on CSM’s Main Quad next to the Health and Wellness Building 5.
