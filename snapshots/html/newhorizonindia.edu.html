We live in a world that is driven intensely by knowledge where literally and figuratively, Knowledge is Power. Exercising this power with diligence and dynamism is a formidable task and requires a mindset that is a combination of wisdom and professionalism. True education should aim at training the young minds in this critical area.
In the global scenario, professional education in India plays a key role since Indian professionals contribute considerably to the knowledge bank of the world. In this context, our professional training
should be characterized by a judicious blend of the values of our ancient wisdom and the rapidly changing concepts in technology and management. Knowledge management should be carefully and wisely configured to create a better world, a better environment and a better life for all living creatures on earth.
New Horizon has recognized these subtle changes in the training methodology and has adopted an innovative mode of leading the mind from Knowledge to Wisdom. We do not simply produce professionals, but create leaders who can bring about a positive change, leaders who are mentally, emotionally, socially and intellectually capable, leaders who can channel human resources of the future towards successful accomplishments.
