A large part of a student’s ESL experience takes place outside of the classroom, in a new location, far away from home. At BEI, students are allowed the opportunity to adapt to their new environment in small, personal classrooms. Bilingual Education Institute places a high priority on providing students a safe and nurturing setting in which to practice English and experience American culture. BEI’s goal is to promote international friendship and understanding, while learning English with detailed and personalized attention.

BEI distinguishes itself in providing:

  • A small and safe campus setting
  • 8 Levels of Intensive English Classes
  • Vital Personal and Cultural Development
  • Temperament and Personality types learning tools
  • Affordable tuition
  • TOEFL & GRE Preparation Available
  • Exceptional, Native Speaking Instructors
  • Fun outings and activities each cycle
  • Guaranteed Learning
  • Location includes entertainment all around the institute
