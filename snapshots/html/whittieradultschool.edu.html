Mission Statement
Whittier Adult School is committed
 to providing our diverse student population
 with a quality, standards-based
 instructional program
 guided by our fully-qualified staff
 in a safe and supportive environment.
 We provide an engaging educational
 program through which students can
acquire the 21st century learning skills
 that enable them to achieve their academic,
 vocational and personal goals.
