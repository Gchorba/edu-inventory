Arturo Delgado, Ed.D.
Los Angeles County
Superintendent of Schools
 
 

 
The Los Angeles County Office of Education (LACOE) is the nation’s largest regional education agency.
We provide a range of programs and services to promote the academic and financial stability of the county’s 80 school districts and the achievement of more than 2 million preschool and school-age children.
