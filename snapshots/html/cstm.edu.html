On May 5, 2012 the College of Saint Thomas More publicly announced that it was adding St. John Fisher as co-patron, to become The College of Saints John Fisher & Thomas More (or “Fisher More College” as it is referred to colloquially). The transition to Fisher More College began with the launching of a new website at www.fishermore.edu on the First Saturday in the Month of Our Lady (May) and the Feast of Pope Saint Pius V.

On June 22, 2012 (the Feast of Saint John Fisher and the 477th anniversary of his martyrdom), the name of the College will be legally amended in the State of Texas.

The College invites everyone to visit its new website and join us in celebrating this important new chapter in the history of the College.
