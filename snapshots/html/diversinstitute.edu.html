The Divers Institute Experience

We are the Divers Institute of Technology. Commercial diving is a competitive industry. Only the best divers perform to the highest standards, while never compromising safety. We train the best of the best.

Divers Institute of Technology offers top candidates the training that will help ensure a career, not just a job. We are one of two commercial dive schools in the U.S. to be Internationally Certified by the DCBC, allowing our graduates to work globally.
