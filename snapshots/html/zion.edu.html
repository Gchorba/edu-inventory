Preparing for God's Call

At Zion Christian University, we are dedicated to teaching the truth of God's word, which renews the mind and transforms the heart. It has been said, “Doctrine will determine your destiny.” As such, ZCU seeks to provide an excellent education, with sound teaching, that honors the Lord, prepares students for the call of God, and leads them to a destiny that glorifies His holy name.
