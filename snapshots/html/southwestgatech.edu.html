SWGTC Hosts Thomasville High School Students for Shadow Day

SWGTC hosted a Program Shadow Day for Thomasville High School on April 24, 2012. Students from the high school chose a program of interest at SWGTC and spent 2 hours of their day shadowing.

SWGTC Holds Spring Fling Student Activity Day

SWGTC hosted a Spring Fling Student Activity Day on Thursday, April 19th. There were many activities for the students to participate in, including trike races, corn toss, volleyball, and a Wii tournament.

SWGTC SKillsUSA Students Participate in Community Service

On Tuesday, April 17, 2012 student members and advisors from SWGTC's SkillsUSA club conducted their annual “Adopt a Mile” highway clean-up service project.  Donning orange safety vests, the group collected trash along the shoulders and median of US Highway 19.
