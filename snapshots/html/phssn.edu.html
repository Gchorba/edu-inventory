Message From Our Dean:

Thank you for your interest in the Patty Hanks Shelton School of Nursing (PHSSN).

PHSSN is a consortium of Abilene Christian University, Hardin-Simmons University, and McMurry University. PHSSN is the only intercollegiate program in the nation that serves three private universities and has become a model for other programs seeking to efficiently provide a high-quality professional program in nursing.
