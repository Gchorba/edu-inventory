"The best investment you can make is in yourself." Those words spoken by renowned businessman and investor Warren Buffett hold true today more than ever. The fact is that investing in education not only keeps you sharp and develops skills that you can apply to your work environment, but it will generally provide you with the highest rate of return on investment. It will pay you back over a lifetime.

Taft University delivers online college degree programs via distance learning. Students work at their own pace under the guidance of uniquely qualified and credentialed faculty. For most working adults, going back to school in the traditional sense is just not possible. Call us today and let's discuss how you can fit an accredited college degree into your busy life.

William Howard Taft University is accredited by the Accrediting Commission of the Distance Education and Training Council (DETC). The DETC is listed by the U.S. Department of Education as a nationally recognized accrediting agency, and is also recognized by the Council for Higher Education Accreditation (CHEA).

The Boyer Graduate School of Education offers Masters and Doctorate degrees designed to serve a national market of educators. Programs prepare professional educators to serve the needs of both public and private institutions from the elementary to postsecondary level in their community and beyond.

The W. Edwards Deming School of Business offers professional degree programs designed to accommodate and benefit working professionals. Our business programs have specific appeal to individuals in professional practice environments and to entrepreneurs pursuing careers in law, accounting, and small business ventures.
