Online MBA Programs and Masters in Strategic Leadership

California Miramar University offers Associate, Bachelor's, Master's and Doctorate degree programs in Business Administration. Some of our most popular graduate programs include Doctor of Business Administration, Masters of Business Administration and Masters of Science in Strategic Leadership. Popular undergraduate programs include Bachelor of Science in Business Administration and Associate of Science in Business Administration. CMU also offers English as a Second Language (ESL). Students choose to learn on campus, online, or hybrid. Our diverse student body comes from across America, around the globe, all branches of the armed forces and veterans of the US military.

California Miramar University's School of Business & Management is proud to be included in "The List" of Top 10 MBA Schools in San Diego County, published in the San Diego Business Journal. California Miramar University stands out amongst all those included in the San Diego list for having the most affordable MBA tuition. California Miramar University's MBA program is offered via MBA Hybrid or 100% online. The MBA Hybrid program meets once per week and the rest is online. The 100% online MBA has no in-class requirement and is accessible from anywhere in the world.

CMU believes that higher education should be affordable and that you shouldn't have to break the bank to finance your education. On campus, online, and hybrid programs all offer year round enrollment. The faculty members at CMU drive the University's innovative capacity as experts in their fields because of their combined academic and professional knowledge and expertise. At California Miramar University, faculty members are chosen not only based upon their academic qualifications, but for their desire to teach and share their first hand professional experience. Contact the Office of Admissions today for more information, class schedules, or to visit the campus. Call the Office of Admissions at 858-653-3000 x12 or email admissions@calmu.edu to learn more.

CMU's Miramar Campus is located just twenty minutes from San Diego's world famous venues including historic downtown Gaslamp District, world renowned San Diego Zoo, the beaches of La Jolla Shores, and the San Diego Convention Center. Furthermore, CMU is surrounded by some of the country's leading biotech, pharmaceutical, and telecommunication companies. San Diego is an ideal destination for students from around the world to pursue their education and enjoy America's Finest City. Visit the Google map to locate California Miramar University.

California Miramar University and the School of Management at Alliant International University have signed a historic partnership creating a matriculation agreement between the two business schools. California Miramar University is accredited by both ACICS and DETC and Alliant International University is accredited by WASC. This historic partnership allows students maximum educational opportunities within San Diego North County's thriving educational hub.

Our Mission

California Miramar University's mission is to provide students from around the world with programs that enable them to acquire skills, competencies, and academic excellence that will enhance their careers and professions, enrich their personal lives, and enable them to make significant contributions to the global community. We are committed to providing quality educational programs that will enable our students to acquire expertise in strategic decision making and implementation, marketing, financial management, leadership, and organizational behavior and development. Our faculty members are committed to guiding and supporting all our students to a successful completion of their degree programs. Our programs are designed for students who are seeking an educational delivery system (whether on campus, online or via hybrid) that will enable them to earn an academic degree at their own pace, time, and location using the most innovative and modern instructional and technological advances.
