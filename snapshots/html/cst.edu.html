Welcome to the
Future of Your Faith

Here is an invitation. Come learn with us. Bring your challenges, your passions, and your deepest intuitions. Bring your questions. Study and engage the deep resources of the Christian tradition, all in the context of a ground-breaking interreligious university.

Claremont School of Theology can help turn your questions into action, your faith into motion – and your tradition into life. This is the time to live into your call and engage a world that needs your heart, your mind, and your involvement - now, more than ever.

From the ancient church to the future of the faith, Claremont explores it all ... and so can you »
