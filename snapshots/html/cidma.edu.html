Our students are encouraged to achieve their educational goals by providing individualized learning experiences, personal advising, a relevant curriculum and a job placement assistance service.

Our Mission

 The Cleveland Institute of Dental-Medical Assistants, Inc. has a strong and unwavering commitment to train and place personnel in the para-medical field who are qualified to assist physicians and dentists. Specialized training enables graduates to further extend the services offered by health-care professionals to greater numbers of people with competence and efficiency. Students must excel both clinically and academically. They are encouraged and motivated to become an integral part of and a credit to the allied health professions.
