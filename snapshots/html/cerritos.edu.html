Attention Students! 2012 Commencement information available.
Enrollment Fee Increase. Beginning with enrollment for the summer, 2012 term, the state-required enrollment fee changes from the current $36 to $46 per unit. The new amount is covered by the BOG-FW for eligible students.
Cerritos College offers a full-time Intensive English Program where we help you develop your English language skills. Please visit the International Student Center homepage for additional information.
New first-time applicants – Assessment & Orientation-Counseling are required prior to enrollment. Please be sure to go to Required AOC and Cerritos ESP for requirements and details.
