And it wasn't always easy. It wasn't always "five stars" in my brain. I didn't pick up some things as fast as some of the other students. But, I had  awesome instructors like Matt Hom, Bryden Muggleston, Eric Thrane and Carerra Bailey, who told me, "Hang in there. We know it's tough, but we believe in you." And when it came time for State Board, as with most Bellus grads, not only did I pass the State Board the first try, but I passed it with flying colors. (I believe the school has something like a 90+  pass  percentage rate with its grads.)

There is a reason Bellus graduates get some of the best entry level salon and spa jobs, after earning their license. They go beyond the usual fair of "get them their hours and get them their license." They more than prepare us for State Board. They help us SHINE. They offer externships to top students, and when I found the job market to be rather hostile to a person of my age, I was able to meet with counselors, and re-assess my next steps. After a few tweaks of the resume and a few job leads, I was hired as an assistant at an incredible salon. I couldn't ASK for a better fit for me,  or more awesome co-workers and bosses. Had I needed further education? I could have attended courses they offer to professional alumni, as well. They really went out of their way to help me succeed. That is what I am most thankful for. They CARE about our success. Let it be known, that I highly recommend Bellus Academy to anyone pursuing a career in the beauty industry.  I am proud not not only be a beauty school graduate...but a BELLUS ACADEMY Graduate.

Explore Your New Career

 

Cosmetology
We strive to graduate the top industry professionals by integrating our S.T.A.R. Precision Haircutting System with our innovative styling techniques. Our customized 1600-hour cosmetology training includes professional training in all aspects of the beauty industry.

Advanced Cosmetology
This advanced 150 hour course is dedicated to educate, inspire and prepare you for a successful career in the dynamic world of Cosmetology. This program provides time for the creative and advanced technical skills required by salon owners today.

Esthetics
Our 600-hour esthetician program offers comprehensive professional training in all aspects of the esthetics industry, including skin analysis and conditions, facials & electrical facials, chemical treatments, skin treatments, hair removal & make-up application.

Master Esthetics
Medical Esthetics has continued to boom requiring estheticians to seek out specialized education. To meet the needs of this rapidly increasing segment, Bellus Academy launched California's first certified Master Esthetics program.

Make Up Artistry
We have partnered with Make-Up Designory (MUD) to offer their 150-hour “Beauty Make-Up Artistry” training course. We are one of the only academies outside of New York and Los Angeles to offer a professional make-up course at this level.

Spa Nail Technician
Our 600-hour Spa Nail Technician course is the first in the nation to go beyond the basics of manicuring and add the spa treatment training that is demanded by luxury salons and spas.

Massage Therapy & HHP
Our 1000-hour Holistic Health Practitioner (HHP) course incorporates basic massage along with advanced techniques such as Thai, Shiatsu, Hot Stone, Aromatherapy, Reflexology, Deep Tissue and Sports Massage.

BELLUS BEST IN BUSINESS
It takes more than just great technical training to be successful in the beauty industry. A majority of your success will be based on your business, personal and marketing skills. Graduate with skills that will help you to be immediately successful.

NEW Exclusive Bellus Programs

The S.T.A.R. Precision Haircutting System

Developed by Artistic Director Diego Raviglione and Master Educator Nick Berardi, this precision haircutting system is designed to give the best foundation in the art of haircutting. S.T.A.R. is the only haircutting system that evolves as the student’s technical skills and creativity become stronger, providing support to nurture the novice to the master stylist. The S.T.A.R. iPhone and iPad apps allow you to learn anytime, anywhere by watching all the videos on your mobile device or computer.

Bellus Academy is the only academy that currently offers the S.T.A.R. Precision Haircutting System and Nick Education. Nick Education is the premiere online education site, with professional members internationally. Lifetime support program is included to carry you through your entire career as a master stylist.


Financial Literacy

Financial literacy is knowledge of personal finance and understanding how to apply it to the real world. It is not enough in this competitive market to only have hands-on skills in your chosen profession. It is an absolute necessity to be financially savvy.

We are committed to creating business-savvy salon and spa professionals. We believe that much of this is grounded in a thorough understanding of financial basics. Program is taught over six modules that address everything from Budget Basics to Interview Skills.
