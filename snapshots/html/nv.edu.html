You're Never Too Old for the Honor Roll...

Do you miss being an Honors student? Well there's a new way to let your excellence shine at NVCC by applying for a coveted slot in the Honors Institute.

The Honors Institute affords students a better teacher-student ratio, smaller classes, more direct access to faculty mentoring, more independent study opportunities and early entrance into Phi Theta Kappa Honor Society.

Needless to say, success in the Honors Institute increases transfer opportunities, gains student recognition at the Honors Showcase, banquet, and commencement, and includes honor status on your transcript.

2012 Graduation Information Available

The 2012 Naugatuck Valley Community College Commencement Ceremony will take place at 2 pm on Friday, May 25 at the Palace Theater in Waterbury.

All students who will be graduating next month are required to complete and submit a graduation application by April 30 in order to receive their diploma on stage.

More information about tickets, what to wear, getting there and everything else you need to know about this year's Commencement under "Current Students".
