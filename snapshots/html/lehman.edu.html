  • Lehman Latin Jazz Ensemble to Perform May 8
    The Lehman Latin Jazz Ensemble will celebrate spring with a concert “Fiesta en Primavera” on Tuesday, May 8, at 4:30 p.m. in the Music Building’s Hearth Room, located on the third floor.
  • May 10 Conference: Latino Politics and the Rights of Migrants
    Leading scholars in the field of Latino politics and issues concerning migrants in America will convene for Lehman’s Center for Human Rights and Peace Studies’ conference, titled “Nueva Vision: Latino Politics and the Rights of Migrants in the Americas.”

Achieving Success

Lehman College is ranked among the nation's Top 100 colleges and universities for the number of minority students earning bachelor's or master's degrees in several different fields, including education, the health professions, accounting, social work, and computer science.

 

Visit http://diverseeducation.com/top100 and search for "CUNY Lehman College."
