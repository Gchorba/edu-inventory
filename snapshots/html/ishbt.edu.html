Welcome to ISHBT

The vision of International School of Health, Beauty & Tecnology (ISHBT) in Florida is to be a leader in the field of health, beauty & technology education, focused on the success and satisfaction of students throughout their careers.

The instructors at our school are professionally trained and they dedicate their efforts to each and every student. Helpful guidance on career orientation and job prospects are given to our students at every step. We strive hard for your success.

ISHBT offers educational programs in Cosmetology, Skincare, Nails, and Health programs such as Medical Assistant, Medical Administrative Assistant (online), Dental Assistant, Massage Therapy and Technology programs such Secure Wireless & Internet, Business Enterpreurship at our brand new Lauderhill Campus (see the tour). We also have English as Second Language for local and foreign students (I-20 student visas available upon request & approval). All of our programs provide formal hands-on training in building a solid educational foundation, obtaining a lucrative employment in field of your choice, and managing your own successful career.
