Save-the-Date

October 22 - 26, 2012, Monday - Friday

Cambridge Center Marriott
Cambridge, Massachusetts

Course schedule to be posted June 2012
 

 
You will be thoroughly engaged. Your learning enhanced. The issues and opportunities you face each day addressed. This and much more, at the 31st offering of the Primary Care Internal Medicine course from October 17-21, 2011 in Boston, MA.

You'll learn from the best and brightest minds at Harvard Medical School. They are clinicians on the front-lines of patient care each day just like you. All the topics are evidence-based and applicable to your practice. You'll have plenty of time to get your questions addressed, so you'll go home with practical, straight-forward information to enhance your patient care.

As one participant from last year's course stated, "This is the BEST medicine course I have taken".

That's our goal in offering this course to you. We hope you'll join us!
