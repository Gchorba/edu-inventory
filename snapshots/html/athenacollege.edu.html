Athena College of Beauty offers innovative Cosmetology and Cosmetician programs to prepare you for a successful career in the beauty industry.
 
At Athena College of Beauty we offer Cosmetology and Cosmetician programs that combine classroom teaching with hands-on training. We provide extensive state board exam preparation, to help you successfully become licensed as a cosmetologist or cosmetician in the state of California.
 
Athena College of Beauty is proud to be accredited by the National Accrediting Commission of Career Arts & Sciences, Inc. (NACCAS) and approved by the California Board of Barbering and Cosmetology as well as the California Bureau of Private Postsecondary Education (BPPE). We are also a proud partner of Milady so you can be sure you'll be getting the highest-quality Cosmetology  or Cosmetician education possible. In addition to the student curriculum, our educators have diverse knowledge and industry specific specializations.
 

Our student salon and spa offers students the opportunity to work with clients in a professional, salon like environment while studying at Athena College of Beauty.

All our programs are designed to be affordable and convenient. We offer day and evening classes with interest-free, low monthly payment plans to fit your household budget. Monthly payments are structured so your tuition balance is paid off before your completion date. Our zero-interest payment plans allow our students to complete their programs debt-free, without the heavy burden of sizable student loans before entering the workforce.
