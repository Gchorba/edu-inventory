May 1, 2012

“Derby Day” Held at Davis College

On April 27, Davis College celebrated “Derby Day” with students and their families. Activities included pony rides, face painting, crafts, and the "Davis Derby". The "Derby" horserace allowed the College to make $100 donations to the Muliple Sclerosis Society, American Diabetes Association, National Fibromyalgia Society, and the American Heart Association. A $200 donation was made to the American Heart Association. Photos from the event are on the College's Flickr site.

February 16, 2012

Department of Design Appointments

Janet Weber has been appointed Chairperson of the Design Department. She will direct the Department's Graphic Design, Interior Design, Motion and Visual Effects Design, and Website Design programs. Janet has been with Davis College for over 20 years. She teaches foundation design and interior design courses. Janet is the previous Interior Design Program Director. She is also an allied member of the American Society of Interior Designers (ASID) and is LEED certified.

Sally Mielcarek has been appointed Interior Design Program Director. She will work with the department chairperson to ensure that the program curriculum meets the needs of the interior design profession. Sally holds a bachelor's degree in interior design from BGSU. She has taught interior design courses at Davis College for over 15 years.

February 15, 2012

Caregiver Support Group Begins for Davis College Students and Alumni

(Robin Banks, Gwen Kauffman, Melissa Hamblin-Smith (Group Chair))

In the last couple of months, a new student group has formed at Davis to help those who are caregivers. The group is there to support each other because they understand how difficult it can be at times juggling school, life, and taking care of someone else.

This group helps them know they are not alone. Resources have been put together for everything from caring for elderly parents to autism to ADHD. One of the group members is happy to do research for the students. A caregiver resource area has been set aside in the Resource Center for any student who would like to learn more about various disabilities, illnesses, or help that is out there for caregivers.

If you are interested in finding out more about this group, please contact Melissa Hamblin-Smith at hamblinsmithmel@daviscollege.edu. If you need some research done, please contact Gwen Kauffman at gkauffman@daviscollege.edu. This group is open to current students as well as alumni.
