  • Welcome to HGST

    Founded in 1983, Houston Graduate School of Theology is an evangelical, multicultural, and multidenominational seminary that provides high quality theological education for men and women who serve in a wide variety of ministry settings. HGST’s fully accredited degree programs include Master of Theological Studies, Master of Arts in Counseling, Master of Divinity, and Doctor of Ministry. Also offered are graduate level certificate programs in Christian Spirituality, Spiritual Direction, and Missional Theology.

     

     

     

     

     

  • Love HGST? Tell a Friend!

    We are trying to spread the word about the wonderful things happening here at HGST, and we would like your help! If you know of someone who feels called into the ministry or counseling professions, tell them about HGST and get rewarded!

Is God Calling You?

As you consider God’s calling in your life, you are sure to have many questions. At HGST, we seek to guide our students through the process of discerning God’s call. If you feel called into ministry or the counseling professions, HGST can provide an education that will both strengthen and challenge your mind and spirit.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

At HGST, we confess our faith in Jesus Christ and commit our lives to him and to the service of his church. We affirm our faith, not in order to exclude others, but rather to offer a positive witness to the reality of our experience of Christ as Lord and Redeemer within the community of faith that has nurtured us.

Learn More

OUR PROGRAMS

HGST is on the cutting edge of the missional church movement. We offer degree programs in Master of Theological Studies, Master of Arts in Counseling, Master of Divinity, and Doctor of Ministry. We also offer graduate level certificate programs in Christian Spirituality, Spiritual Direction, and Missional Theology.
