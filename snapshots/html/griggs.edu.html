Griggs University/Griggs International Academy

Griggs University/Griggs International Academy

is an accredited, values-based distance education and homeschool provider. Griggs offers both individual courses and complete programs to students for preschool through university. Providing academic stability in a changing world since 1909—formerly Home Study International.
