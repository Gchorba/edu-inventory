Welcome to Los Angeles ORT College

Los Angeles ORT College, affiliated with ORT Operations USA, is a Technical Vocational Facility dedicated to providing affordable technology based education and English as a Second Language (ESL) instruction to the community.
Our Computer and Medical Technology Programs and ESL Programs are designed to provide graduates with the training they need to secure jobs when they graduate. Employment Preparation courses are included in each program to ensure that our graduates are given the skills required to perform successfully in job interviews.
Our Los Angeles Campus is conveniently located just east of Beverly Hills. Our San Fernando Valley Campus is located in the Van Nuys.

Los Angeles ORT is part of ORT Worldwide

ORT: 135 years of Global History

ORT is one of the largest non-governmental education and training organizations in the world with activities in more than 60 countries, past and present, with current operations in Israel, North America, Latin America, Europe, Africa, Asia and the Pacific.
