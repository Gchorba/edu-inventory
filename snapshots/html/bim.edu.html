Mr. Ragavendra Nath
Managing Director
Ladderup Wealth Management Private Ltd


Raghvendra leads the private wealth management business with a vision to make it the finest in India. He brings with him more than 18 years of corporate experience and a deep knowledge of the financial markets. He was earlier the chief marketing officer at Birla Sun Life AMC Ltd. Prior to that he also headed the Strategy and Business Development team at BSLAMC. Before joining Ladderup, Nath was the head of sales & distribution at Aditya Birla Money Ltd, a leading equity and commodity broking firm. He was instrumental in the successful takeover of the broking outfit and then affecting a turnaround.
