About JIU

  • Driven by the vision to enable more students to experience the transformative power of education, JIU became the first fully online university to be granted the highest form of accreditation in the industry.
  • Students' evolving personal and academic needs are at the core of our policies, learning platforms and services.
  • Unlike traditional schools, our curriculum is specifically designed for online learning success.
  • Our Gold Standard® model offers the highest quality education in a flexible, supportive community where students gain the most relevant, real-world knowledge to help them transform their lives
