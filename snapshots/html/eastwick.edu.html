  • Exploring Eastwick
    • Hands-on training is a major focus at Eastwick College and the HoHoKus Schools. Students train in unique classroom environments, from ultrasound labs to welding workstations, that feature the same equipment and procedures they will encounter on the job once they start their careers.

      This video gives you a quick glimpse into the wide variety of programs that Eastwick has to offer. To learn more about a specific program or career field, call one of our schools or check out a full list of our programs.
  • Healthcare programs
    • • Seven of the fastest-growing occupations are in healthcare.
      • Three million new healthcare jobs are expected by 2018.
      • Eastwick offers medical programs you can complete in as little as six months.

      Programs Offered: • Associate Degree in Applied Science in Health Science • Associate Degree in Diagnostic Cardiovascular Sonography • Associate Degree in Health Science, Health Information Technology Concentration (HIT) • Associate Degree in Registered Nursing • Associate Degree in Surgical Technology • Diploma in Bilingual Licensed Practical Nursing • Diploma in Licensed Practical Nursing • Diploma in Vascular Sonography • Diploma or Certificate in Medical Assisting • Diploma or Certificate in Medical Billing and Coding
  • Trade programs
    • • Do you like working with your hands? Consider a career in trades or construction.
      • Employment in construction is expected to rise 19 percent by 2018, according to the Department of Labor.
      • If you're excited by the sparks and power tools, call HoHoKus-Trades at 800-646-WELD

      Programs Offered: • Building Maintenance Superintendent Diploma • Diploma in Electrician Apprenticeship • Diploma in Manufacturing Technology • Diploma in Plumber Apprenticeship • Diploma in Structural Plate Welding
  • Technology programs
    • • Employment in computer systems design and related services is expected to increase by 45 percent by 2018.
      • Study advanced electronic and computer technology, including the study of computer networking, hardware, software, fiber optics and wireless technology.
      • If installing and repairing computer and electronic systems is something that interests you, call HoHoKus-RETS at 973-661-0600.

      Programs Offered: • Computer Certification Courses • Diploma in Computer Electronics Technician • Diploma in Heating, Ventilation, Air Conditioning Technology (HVAC)
  • Business programs
    • • Employment in administrative services is expected to increase 18 percent by 2018 according to a recent report by the US Department of Labor.
      • The business courses at the HoHoKus Schools are recognized at over 1500 colleges in the nation and are evaluated by the American Council on Education.
      • If you'd like to learn more about the opportunities you can pursue, call one of our schools today.

      Programs Offered: • Certificate in Business Administration with Computerized Accounting/Bookkeeping • Certificate in Business Operations • Certificate in Computer Concepts and Applications
