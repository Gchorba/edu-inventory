Upcoming Events        

May 7: SAP Convocation

(4:00 PM) Celebrating the end of the prep year for our Service Academy Program completers. Location: MMI Chapel. BBQ supper for SAP cadets and their families immediately following at the President's Quarters.  

May 11: Graduation Picnic & Honors Convocation

The MMI Alumni Brigade will host graduating cadets and their families for a cookout at the Paul B. Robinson Excess House (4:00-6:00 PM). Honors Convocation will follow in the Chapel (7:00 PM). If you plan to attend the picnic, please RSVP your name, cadet's name and total number attending to Shawna Baker.   

May 12: Commencement & Commissioning

(10:00 AM & 1:00 PM) Saturday morning's Commencement Ceremony will take place in the Ireland Center, followed by a reception at the President's Quarters and the afternoon Army ROTC Commissioning Ceremony. U.S. Senator Jeff Sessions  (AL) will give the commencement address. *Tickets are NOT required for either event.

June 24-30 & June 29-July 5: Tiger Camp

Tiger Camp, offered in two cycles, prepares prospective cadets for the Leaders Training Course (LTC) at Ft. Knox, Kentucky. It enables MMI to attract, select and retain the highest caliber cadets and ensure their success in the Early Commissioning Program.

News/Announcements  

Olympic Wrestling Family Tradition

5.3.12   Freestyle wrestler Sam Hazewinkel is following in his father and uncle's footsteps by heading to the Olympic Games in London. Our MMI wrestling coaches, Dave Hazewinkel & Jim Hazewinkel, both wrestled for the U.S. Olympic Team in 1968 (Mexico City) and 1972 (Munich). 

Men's Tennis Conference Champs, Headed to Nationals

4.24.12 The MMI men’s tennis team won the 2012 NJCAA Region XXII (ACCC Conference) Championship, defeating Gadsden State CC 8-1. They will participate in the NJCAA National Tournament in Plano, Texas, May 13-18. Team Coach Chuck Wright was also named "Coach of the Year."

USAFA Falcon Scholars Continue Flight Training

4.6.12 Four of MMI's Falcon Scholars in the Service Academy Program are receiving certified flight training locally at Vaiden Field with the assistance of Aviation Scholarships.
