Event Calendar   

2012 Organized Crime Symposium -- May 8-10
Sign up now for the 2012 Organized Crime Symposium at MTSU May 8-10! Sponsored by MTSU's Forensic Institute for Research and Education, the symposium on "Technology, Trafficking and Terrorism" is free and open to the public. Get more details at mtsunews.com/gang-symposium-2012.

Campuswide Power Outage

May 9-10
MTSU will shut down electrical power in phases across campus May 9 and 10 to complete the next step in connecting new underground wiring. The work may result in temporary closing of some offices. Find out more now at http://mtsunews.com/campus-power-outage.

Positive Behavior Support Conference -- May 17
Educators, school administrators and members of the academic community can learn to prevent classroom problems and help their students at MTSU's sixth annual Positive Behavior Support and Inclusion Conference on May 17. Learn more about the free event at mtsunews.com/pbsi-conference-2012.
