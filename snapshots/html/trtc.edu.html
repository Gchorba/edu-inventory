Toledo Restaurant Training Center is Northwest Ohio’s choice for culinary, food safety and restaurant management career training. We offer career diplomas for individuals just starting their culinary careers, professional certifications and professional development classes for those currently working in the foodservice and hospitality industry. Toledo Restaurant Training Center offers training tailored specifically to enhance your skill level; our programs are designed to teach the skills that maximize your employability and expertise in the current market.

Toledo Restaurant Training Center’s professional instructors offer hands-on training, to prepare our students for working and succeeding in a restaurant or food service environment. TRTC offers a range of student services to help our students succeed in today’s competitive job market.

Contact Toledo Restaurant Training Center today and find out more about the Toledo Restaurant Training Center “Where Quality Service Begins.”
