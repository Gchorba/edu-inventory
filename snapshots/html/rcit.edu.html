President’s Welcome Message

Welcome to the Royal Canadian Institute of Technology, located in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.

The Royal Canadian Institute of Technology was founded more than 10 years ago. It is an accredited institution with Private Career Training Institutions Agency of the Government of British Columbia, Canada. It offers Certificate and Diploma Programs in Bank Teller, Information Technology (one and two years), Business Administration (one and two years), Health Care Nursing Assistant, Health Care Attendant, Office Administration and Business Finance. Our college is proud to offer programs and experiences designed to develop in both domestic and international students a lifetime commitment to learning.

Our talented, well-respected, and real-world experienced faculty and adjunct professors hold Bachelor, Master and Doctoral Degrees from Canadian, American and European universities.

Our primary goal is to provide students with the skills required for successful employment and career. We want every student at the Royal Canadian Institute of Technology to graduate with the skills they need to acquire employment in their area of training.

On behalf of all our staff, faculty and students at Royal Canadian Institute of Technology, I welcome you to our college.

Dr. Yourie Pankratz, President
