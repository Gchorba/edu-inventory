NTI Partnering with Globe University

Since 1957, students have looked to NTI for the skills they need for success in the drafting and design industry. And, employers in the field have relied on NTI for a pool of talented and well-trained job candidates. We are pleased to announce that we have joined forces with Globe University—high-quality drafting and design education has never been more accessible!
