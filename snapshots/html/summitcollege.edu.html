  • Provide Care as a Certified Nursing Assistant

    /Programs/Healthcare/Certified-Nursing-Assistant.aspx
    Be there for patients when they need help with personal care, mobility, getting exercise, measuring vital signs and much more. Get your foot in the door and provide care before becoming a nurse. Start your career as a Certified Nursing Assistant today!

  • Get CPR Certified

    /Programs/Healthcare/CPR.aspx
    Become certified in cardiopulmonary resuscitation after this short course, required for most healthcare providers. Sign up for the CPR course today!

  • Assist Dentists as a Dental Assistant

    /Programs/Healthcare/Dental-Assistant.aspx
    Learn to be versatile in the dental setting and gain the skills that will help you play a key role in patient care, the laboratory and the front office. Become a highly trained Dental Assistant and start your new career and your new life! *Coronal Polishing Program - Coming Soon!

  • Join a Growing Field in Electronic Health Records

    /Programs/Healthcare/Electronic-Health-Records.aspx
    In the coming years the process of “Paper Files” will transition into “Electronic Health Records” Systems. Get the hands-on training you will need and start your new career today!

  • Get Certified in IV Therapy & Blood Withdrawal

    /Programs/Healthcare/IV-Therapy.aspx
    Get training and certification in IV Therapy and Blood Withdrawal and learn to start an IV infusion and withdraw blood for testing. This course is specially designed for LVNs. Enroll today!

  • Make a Difference as a Licensed Vocational Nurse

    /Programs/Healthcare/Licensed-Vocational-Nurse.aspx
    Through our accredited Licensed Vocational Nurse (LVN) program, gain nursing skills and knowledge that are in high demand. Enrich your life by caring for others and join a growing field!

  • Join a Growing Field as a Medical Assistant

    /Programs/Healthcare/Medical-Assistant-Clinical---Administrative.aspx
    Play an important role in the healthcare setting as a Medical Assistant trained to handle front office and clinical duties. From computer operations to EKG and UA lab procedures, gain valuable new skills and start your new career!

  • Become a Medical Billing and Coding Assistant

    /Programs/Healthcare/Medical-Billing-and-Coding.aspx
    Help medical offices with the specialized task of medical billing and insurance coding. Get the hands-on training you need to help doctors and hospitals bill and receive insurance reimbursements. Start your new career today!

  • Make a Career of Clarity

    /Programs/Healthcare/Optical-Dispensing-Technician.aspx
    Join an exciting and integral profession in eye care. As an Optical Dispensing Technician, you'll have the broad knowledge and versatility to work in an office or lab setting. In 10 months, you'll gain the knowledge, skills, and support to succeed in an exciting field!

  • Gain Worksite Flexibility with Bookkeeping & Accounting Skills

    /Programs/Business/Accounting-Assistant.aspx
    Gain bookkeeping, office and computer skills in one training program. Learn to use the most current software to help businesses manage their finances. Start your new career as an Accounting Assistant today!

  • Work in a Professional Environment as a Business Information Assistant

    /Programs/Business/Business-Information-Assistant.aspx
    Get hands-on computer and information processing training and gain the skills to excel in modern business settings. Start your new office career now!

  • Work in a Legal Office as a Legal Administrative Assistant

    /Programs/Business/Legal-Administrative-Assistant.aspx
    From computer software to court procedures and client management, get well-rounded training that will help you become a skilled Legal Administrative Assistant. Start your new career and learn to thrive in any legal office setting!

  • Be a Hands-on Welding Technician

    /Programs/Industrial/Welding-Technician.aspx
    Learn the specialized welding techniques that are needed on major machinery, building and construction and product development sites. Get hands-on training and start your new career as a Welding Technician today!
