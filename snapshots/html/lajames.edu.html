La James College of Cosmetology is located in Mason City, Iowa. We are the original La James school. Some customers refer to our beauty school as La’James or LaJames School but the preferred spelling is La James.

Beauty school, beauty school, and more beauty schools ... how can I decide?

Our cosmetology, massage therapy, esthiology and nail technology programs are all in one choice training center.
La James College (also known as LaJames College) is an internationally recognized beauty school providing world-class beauty training and hair styling. The same excellence in education is provided in massage therapy training, skin care and nail care. After almost 80 years in business, La James is one of the best places to receive professional beauty training in the world. As a result, our cosmetology school and beauty college graduates consistently land the best entry-level jobs in the industry with many opening their own salons!

If you are looking for the best cosmetology school, beauty college, massage therapy training, esthiology, and nail technology program, look no farther than La James College. Compared to any other standard beauty academy, La James College differs dramatically by providing spacious classrooms and training areas. More than 15,000 square feet of classroom and clinical space teach students the art of beauty and cosmetology techniques, hands-on massage therapy applications, and related training in esthiology and nail care technology. The professional environment is both educationally stimulating and personally enjoyable. Each institute area boasts new, state-of-the-art equipment. The cosmetology clinic floor is equipped with the latest European design hair-styling stations. Our massage therapy clinic is a modern, soothing spa-like setting. You will enjoy taking cosmetology beauty courses, massage therapy and our related programs in our first-class artistic institution!

La James College is located at the hub of Mason City, Iowa. Mason City has been voted one of the Top 10 best cities in the country to live in according to CNN, Fortune and Money® (for cities up to 50,000 population). Uniquely, this Iowa beauty school has many students in attendance from surrounding states, including Arkansas, California, Colorado, Florida, Illinois, Iowa, Minnesota, Missouri, Nebraska, New Hampshire, North Dakota, South Dakota, Ohio, Pennsylvania, Texas, West Virginia and Wisconsin. Our international students come from all over the world. We offer affordable dormitory facilities for our students who like or enjoy dorms to further enrich their campus experience. Other housing options are available. Attending La James College will be one of the best experiences of your life.
