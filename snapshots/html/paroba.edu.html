Paroba College

Looking for a place to pursue training as a cosmetologist, barber or esthetician? Paroba College is that place. With unique educational programs and experienced staff, Paroba College will get you ready to for the Washington State Licensing exam and beyond.  Come explore your creative side while learning the skills you need to take the next step towards a future in the beauty industry.Learn more about our Programs >>




Nationally Accredited

Paroba College is nationally accredited by The National Accrediting Commission of Cosmetology Arts and Sciences (NACCAS), recognized by the United States Department of Education (DOE) and licensed by the State of Washington.

Explore Your Beautiful Education Today!

Paroba College is nationally accredited, which means financial aid may be available for students who qualify. Our full-time, 3-day-per-week and evening schedules work well for students who work or have other commitments while attending school.

Classes start regularly.  Give us a call at 425-353-8193 to learn more about enrolling.

Paroba College also offers salon-quality hair, skin and nail services at great prices. Check out our salon/spa page or call us for an appointment. All services are performed by students under the supervision of licensed instructors.
