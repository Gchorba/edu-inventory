Thanks for interest in Potomac College! Just a few questions about your educational goals and you will be on your way!

There's a better you. Someone who is smarter, has a better professional outlook and better job security. Someone who earns more and has a better quality of life. Someone who has a college degree. Potomac College wants to introduce you to that person.


Imagine being able to pursue your degree at a pace and time that works with your busy schedule. Potomac College offers practical and tailored curricula to a diverse community of working adults and students. It is our goal to make education obtainable regardless of a student's schedule and finances. Our academic advisors are trained to assess your educational needs and tailor a curriculum that matches your career goals and expected time of completion. And with two convenient locations in Washington D.C. and Northern Virginia, your view for a better life broadens the moment you sign up.

Online Learning

Potomac College offers students the opportunity to take advantage of the same programs and specializations online that are offered at either of their two physical campuses. This allows students the convenience of flexibility and makes it an ideal institution for fulltime working adults, military persons, or anyone with a busy, demanding schedule seeking to pursue a college degree. In fact, many of our students residing in a close proximity to either our Washington, DC or Herndon, VA campuses have chosen to take both online and on-campus courses for an accommodating blended program.
