Start Your Transformation

We know you have something extraordinary to contribute. What is it?

You'll find out at Pacific Oaks College. And that discovery is part of the journey—part of what will make your time here transformative. You'll emerge empowered to stand up for yourself and others.

We start with the principles of inclusion, social justice, and respect. You'll learn to use those principles to help people from diverse backgrounds—people who have different learning styles and face different challenges—to realize their individual potential. But first, we'll help you uncover yours.

Pacific Oaks offers classes at our main campus in Pasadena, online, and at convenient locations throughout California.
