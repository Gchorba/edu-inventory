University of Mount Union

Students at the University of Mount Union actively engage in our deliberately dynamic private university setting featuring a remarkable campus and facilities. Students benefit from preparation that is personal, practical and pertinent for the 21st century – an education that combines relentlessly relevant academic offerings with experiential and research-focused learning. To ensure success in our global society, our students are encouraged to pursue excellence, and Mount Union mirrors that sentiment with a commitment to be exceptional in all that we do.
