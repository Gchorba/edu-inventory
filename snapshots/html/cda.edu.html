For some people, the lure of the ocean is reason enough to look for an under water career in diving. At Commercial Diving Academy, we know that underwater careers are lucrative, exciting and rewarding options for those who feel drawn to the sea. We help potential students take that very idea from dream to reality with programs that are set up to not only inform students of their underwater career options but also teach them the skills they need to become successful underwater professionals.

Commercial Diving Academy is an internationally recognized commercial diving school located in beautiful Jacksonville, Florida. Known as “the sunshine state,” Florida offers the ideal climate, ocean access and opportunities for students looking to make the most out of their underwater diving school experience. We bring commercial diving certification to the Southeast with programs in underwater diving, underwater welding, and wet welding. Plus, as an international diving academy, we offer students access to the full range of educational experiences and underwater career opportunities.

Commercial Diving Academy is one of the only underwater diving schools in the Southeast to be able to offer courses in underwater welding, commercial diving, wet welding and IMCA training. Commercial Diving Academy also offers underwater inspections and training making us one of the few underwater diving schools in the U.S. to give students all the tools they need for underwater diving careers.

Commercial Diving Academy is the only fully-accredited diver training program with IMCA-recognized training, on-campus housing and a full meal plan. Commercial Diving Academy in Jacksonville, Florida offers the most comprehensive and rigorous (20 weeks) program of all the accredited commercial diver training schools.

IMCA training means that graduates from Commercial Diving Academy are fully prepared to respond to any issue that arises during their underwater careers. Whether a student is looking for dive medical training, hyperbaric chamber training, underwater inspections training, or IMCA training, Commercial Diving Academy helps students get the skills they need for the underwater diving careers that they want!

Our Florida diving school also offers a wide variety of opportunities to get hands on training and placement with qualified underwater diving professionals who mentor and teach students in and out of the classroom.

CDA’s graduates may earn certifications from the Association of Commercial Diving Educators, Association of Diving Contractors International, Diver Certification Board of Canada, National Center for Construction Education and Research, American Petroleum Institute, American Welding Society, Hazwoper Environmental Training, National Board of Diving and Hyperbaric Medical Technicians, National Academy of Scuba Educators, and National Registry Emergency Medical Technician. We also offer certification in commercial diving, underwater welding and IMCA training.

Our underwater welding school is one of the only U.S. underwater welding schools offering an internationally-recognized underwater welding program, Underwater Welding Specialist. This one week advanced underwater welding certification is available to certified commercial divers and offered upon completion of the Air/Mixed Gas program.

Underwater Welding jobs require that commercial divers be trained in underwater welding and our wet welding school is designed to allow students to certify underwater, as well as give them extra time welding underwater in addition to the time during the Air/Mixed Gas program. This level of training allows graduates from Commercial Diving Academy to advance quickly and find underwater welding jobs soon after course completion. Our underwater welding programs also offer underwater welding certification.

Commercial Diving Academy also offers underwater diving education for those interested in receiving hyperbaric chamber training. Our hyperbaric chamber training is designed as a certification beyond a qualified profession such as EMT/DMT. This program meets the NBDHMT requirements to work as a Certified Hyperbaric Technologist (CHT) in a clinical environment and sit for the Board examination after 480 hours of documented preceptorship.

Commercial Diving Academy is also one of the best locations to receive dive medical technician training and our diving school Florida location makes our international diving school one of the best places to learn about becoming a dive medical technician.

Dive Medic Technicians are required on-site in all offshore diving. Commercial Diving Academy students will learn to operate a hyperbaric chamber using treatment tables and O2 to treat dive injuries. All divers who take this program will gain a greater understanding of the science of diving and in turn be smarter, safer divers. The student who successfully completes this program will qualify as a DMT advanced by NBDHMT.

Diver Medic Technician Training Programs are designed to help save the lives of commercial divers who are injured while working on off-shore locations such as oil rigs, archaeological points of interest and oceanographic sites.

Commercial Diving Academy is the best place for commercial diving, commercial diving school, and commercial diving certification. We are one of the only diving school, dive training schools and diving school U.S. to offer careers in diving, IMCA training, wet welding schools, underwater inspections, underwater training or underwater schools and diving school Florida or International diving school.

Commercial Diving Academy offers wet welding schools, underwater welding, hyperbaric chamber training, dive medical technician training and underwater welding schools making us the perfect place to receive hands on, commercial diving training for a career in diving.
