Peace Strengthens

 
Welcome to William Peace University / Peace College! Peace College is William Peace University and doing business as both. Our new website will launch summer 2012!

Learning is a rewarding journey - our goal is to give you a sense of adventure, an eagerness to learn and an open mind to what your future could hold. We have shaped Peace College into an inspiring learning environment where an intelligent and energetic student like you can discover who you are and who you want to become.


Feel free to search our site, review our degrees, clubs and activities, or even apply online today. We'd love to meet you in person, so be sure to get directions and plan to come visit our beautiful campus nestled in downtown Raleigh, North Carolina.

At Peace, we know the challenges that await you and the possibilities that can be attained from an enriching academic experience. We know you have it in you  - you just have to take the next step.
