Welcome to South Florida’s finest Microsoft-authorized computer school. Our small, live, hands-on classes begin weekly and are taught by qualified instructors — each considered to be an industry expert.  Since 1989, PC Professor has been offering computer training classes to those seeking condensed, instructor-led training in a relaxed and professional environment.

As both a licensed Florida school and an Authorized Microsoft IT Academy, PC Professor adheres to the highest of academic standards.  This includes a team of certified instructors, access to exclusive Microsoft material and the use of learning applications unavailable to other learning institutions.  Moreover, both our Boca Raton and West Palm Beach campuses have national testing centers on-site to help our students feel as comfortable as possible.

Just a few of the many benefits of being a PC Professor student include free practice time, complimentary refresher classes, unlimited phone support, job placement assistance and lots more.  We take great pride in the well-being of our students and graduates and it shows.

Call an Admission Representative at your nearest PC Professor campus today to arrange a tour, meet with an instructor, sit through a class, interview some existing students or meet our friendly administration and office staff.  We are confident that you will determine what thousands of South Florida residents have already concluded, namely, that it is for good reason that PC Professor is the established leader in quality, hands-on computer training.

Besides small computer training classes that begin weekly, we can also help you with any computer repairs you may need. Specifically, we offer hourly and flat fee computer repairs, service contracts, hardware/software upgrades, malware removal, system speed optimizations, email setups, network configurations, custom programming and data recovery.  As an authorized reseller and repair center for numerous hardware and software vendors, we offer computer support at either our location or yours with a very unique policy. Pertaining to both computer repair and data recovery, if, for whatever reason, we are unable to fix your computer problem, there will be no charge.
