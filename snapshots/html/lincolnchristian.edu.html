Dr. Marion Henderson (1922-2012)
Funeral Services Announced

Marion Henderson was called to his eternal reward this morning at 5:50 a.m. Dr. Henderson served Lincoln Christian (originally LBI) from 1951-1976 and from 1986-2006. Visitation Sunday, 4-7 p.m. in Henderson Hall. Funeral Monday, 10 a.m. at Lincoln Christian Church.

Dr. Hall Professor Emeritus

Dr. Gary Hall was awarded the rank of Professor Emeritus of Old Testament on April 16, by Lincoln Christian University. Colleagues, family and friends gathered for a retirement dinner at the LCU Laughlin Center to honor Dr. Hall's distinguished service as a member of the Seminary faculty. Read more…

LCU Offers MDiv Distance Learning

LCU is pleased to announce a new long distance option for the completion of the Master of Divinity (MDiv) degree. This exciting initiative joins the Seminary Master of Arts degree as an alternative for students wanting to complete their education without relocating to Lincoln, Illinois. Read more.

New Professor Teaches Old Testament

Lincoln Christian University (LCU) has called Dr. Paul Kissling to serve the Seminary as Professor of Old Testament effective June 1, 2012. Dr. Kissling graduated from our Seminary with a Master of Divinity in 1984 and returns now to fill the vacancy created by Dr. Gary Hall's retirement at the end of this year.

2012 Commencement Services

April 25, 2012
LCU will hold Commencement Services on May 12, 2012 in the Earl C Hargrove Chapel on the Lincoln, IL campus. All undergraduate degrees will be awarded at the 10 a.m. service and master's and DMin degrees will be awarded at the 1 p.m. ceremony. A reception for family and friends is scheduled from 11 a.m.-1 p.m.
