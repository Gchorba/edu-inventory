Acupuncture Training in NYC

Tri-State College of Acupuncture is one of the oldest acupuncture and Oriental medicine schools in the United States. Located in New York City, we offer in-depth professional Master's degree programs, featuring hands-on training in three styles of acupuncture and two styles of herbal medicine. Tri-State College's senior faculty members are among the most experienced practitioners in the country.

Chinese Herbology Program

The two-year Chinese Herbology program is open to licensed acupuncturists and physicians certified in acupuncture, and leads to an MSOM degree (for TSCA graduates) or an Advanced Certificate in Chinese Herbology (for non-TSCA graduates and physicians). More on Oriental Medicine >

Integrative Medicine for Doctors

The 300-hour Acupuncture Integrative Medicine Program provides abbreviated acupuncture training for physicians and dentists. Graduates are eligible to take national certification examinations administered by the American Academy of Medical Acupuncture. More on Acupuncture for Doctors >

Affordable Community Clinics

Our Community Clinics offer reasonably priced acupuncture treatment and herbal counseling, performed by student interns. Treatments are by appointment only. More on Affordable Acupuncture >
