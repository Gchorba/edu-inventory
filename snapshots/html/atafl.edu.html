Your college for medical careers

ATA Career Education has provided quality instruction, training, and career placement for men and women in the allied health fields for over ten years. Accredited by ABHES, ATA Career Education is dedicated to helping students achieve success.

We offer valuable, hands-on instruction that equips you with real-world skills and lets you step into an in-demand career in the healthcare field. In addition, we offer day and evening classes, financial aid for those who qualify, and graduate career services to help you get exactly where you want to go in your career.
