The Sonoran Desert Institute, or SDI to our students and faculty, is an accredited online school providing undergraduate degree, diploma and certificate programs in allied health, real estate and construction and firearms technology.

We create a unique academic program just for you. Pursue your professional or vocational interests, receive personalized guidance from Faculty members, and schedule learning at your own pace. Many distance education schools mandate weekly attendance, campus residency, and rigid, inflexible program requirements and term schedules. Not SDI! Balance your education with career demands and needs for personal and family time.

Seeking to advance professionally, achieve your dreams and balance personal life with education and career? The Sonoran Desert Institute is the school for you.

We look forward to helping you succeed in achieving your educational goals. And, if you find yourself in Scottsdale, Arizona please stop in and visit.
