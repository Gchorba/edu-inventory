Welcome to Hamrick School!

Get hands-on career training at our Medina career training school.

Are you exploring the ways a quality education can change your life? Then Hamrick School is the right place for you. Just a short drive from Cleveland or Akron, Hamrick School offers career training programs at our Ohio career school.

At Hamrick School, we prepare students for careers that allow them to do independent, rewarding work while serving others. Our school has over 30 years of experience in career training, helping students meet their career goals. Our instructors and staff offer students the knowledge, skills and support necessary to gain the confidence and succeed in a new career.

Are you ready to take control of your future?

The training programs at Hamrick School are both fast and comprehensive. You can gain all the skills your new career requires without dragging your education out any longer than necessary. If you live in Ohio near the Medina, Cleveland or Akron areas and you’re looking for a school that offers truck driving training or massage therapy, the Hamrick School is for you!

Each educational program at Hamrick School is designed to prepare you for a rewarding, new career and the lifestyle you want. Choose from these specialized training programs we offer and find your calling today.
