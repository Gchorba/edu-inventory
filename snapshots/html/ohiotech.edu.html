Welcome to the 2009 School of the Year! Call Today For FREE Info Kit 800-322-7000

Named the 2009 National School of the Year, Ohio Technical College is an accredited private, proprietary, post secondary Cleveland technical college. If you’re looking for a challenging and rewarding career in modern mechanics, we are dedicated to providing the premier automotive training students need. Ohio Technical College is one of the technical schools in Ohio that prides itself on rich tradition, performance strength and dedication to automotive training and education for employment’s sake.
