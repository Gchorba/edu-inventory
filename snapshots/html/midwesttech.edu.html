Welcome to Your Future

 

Whether you’re just beginning your career education or looking to enhance your current professional position, Midwest Technical Institute (MTI) can help. MTI provides a diverse mixture of technical and educational programs, and has earned an excellent reputation for meeting the needs of the people of central Illinois.

 

Our small class sizes combined with extensive "hands-on" training creates a personalized education that assures our graduates are well prepared for success in today’s job market.

We invite you to visit and tour our campuses in Springfield, IL , East Peoria, IL & Moline, IL. Our instructors, staff, and current students are always willing to share with you the many benefits of an MTI education.
