Marquette, Wheaton Franciscan to build clinical simulation center

Marquette’s College of Nursing has announced that it will partner with Wheaton Franciscan Healthcare to build a clinical simulation center on campus. It is the first clinical simulation center partnership of its type in Wisconsin.

Made possible in part by a $1 million investment over five years from Wheaton Franciscan, the $4 million simulation center will combine the expertise of Marquette’s nursing faculty with the real world experience of Wheaton’s nurses and clinical care providers.
