Diesel Driving Academy – The Leader in CDL Training

Since 1972, Diesel Driving Academy has been training and placing men and women in exciting, high paying jobs in the trucking industry.

The top truck driving school in Louisiana and Arkansas, Diesel Driving Academy offers the best truck driver training programs available in Shreveport, Baton Rouge, Little Rock and surrounding areas.

Our training programs are designed to provide you with the skills and endorsements major transportation companies are looking for in their drivers.
