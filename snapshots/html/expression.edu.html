Welcome to Ex’pression College

If you want to launch a career in the field of your dreams, Ex’pression College is the place to make your passion your profession. The Ex’pression Bachelor’s degree is accelerated, and may be earned in just over 2 ½ years. Our graduates are able to be out of school, doing what they love, sooner. Ex’pression College offers Bachelor’s degrees in six programs: Animation & Visual Effects, Digital Filmmaking , Game Art & Design, Interactive Audio, Motion Graphic Design, and Sound Arts.

Ex’pression’s training in 3D animation, gaming, motion graphic, sound arts, and film, video or music production is landing graduates positions in digital arts entertainment companies.

Class sizes are limited, taught by industry professionals and instructors from universities, and the equipment is professional-grade. See what’s made in Emeryville – step inside.
