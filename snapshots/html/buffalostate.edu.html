Finals Week Begins Monday, May 7

You're almost there! Just a few more days of studying before the spring 2012 semester is history. Keep the following items in mind while preparing to wrap up the semester:

  • Critique and Evaluation Period (CEP) is Monday, May 7, to Thursday, May 10
  • E. H. Butler Library has late-night study hours
  • The bookstore will buy your unwanted textbooks
  • Looking for work? Check out the Career Development Center's employment and internship listings
  • Registration for Summer Session is open

News

  • Commencement 2012: May 12

    Buffalo State will confer degrees on more than 1,960 undergraduate and 630 graduate students during its 140th Commencement celebration, Saturday, May 12, in the Sports Arena.

  • The Anne Frank Project: Making the World Seem Smaller
