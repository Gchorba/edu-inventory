Why We "R" Special

 

At the Beauty Institute:

• As a family owned school that has been in existence for over a decade, we train today’s students for tomorrow’s success through modern and innovative education.

• We utilize scientific methods and natural products, together with professional hairstyling ingenuity, which results in the Cosmetologist becoming a respected, highly technical craftsperson.

• We support the theory that the Cosmetologist’s desire has always been to empower people everywhere, both men and women, to look and feel beautiful. With new scientific methods, this goal can be more easily accomplished.

• We believe that being a part of making your fellow human beings feel better about themselves is one of the most gratifying experiences one can have.

Our teachers are focused on the students’ success in a personalized hands-on training program, where they teach the necessary skills and encourage each student to unleash their creative abilities. By doing so, each one can reach his or her full potential and then gain employment in the exciting and lucrative field of Cosmetology/Barbering Arts and Sciences. Our staff has been recognized with great praise for its tireless effort to exceed your expectations.
