What is the eBridge?


Crown College is a private, four-year Christian college offering a personal, biblically based education. Located near Minneapolis, Minnesota, Crown offers degree-completion programs and graduate degrees, and over thirty-five undergraduate degrees such as nursing, communication, business, and social entrepreneurship. Crown gives students the chance to make an impact--both local and global.
