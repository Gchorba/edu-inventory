    Key program information

    • Teaching students professional massage and bodywork since 1986
    • Advanced training with a 900 classroom hour program
    • Financial Aid & In-House Financing available for those who qualify
    • Program Accredited by the Commission on Massage Therapy Accreditation (COMTA)
    • MTIC is a member in good standing of the Associated Bodyworkers and Massage Professionals (ABMP) organization

    There are vast differences between the massage therapy educational programs offered in the Denver marketplace. Choosing the right school and associated curriculum is an important decision, one which very well may affect your success in this exciting career. To help you understand, and actually experience our unique program offerings, we encourage all potential students to schedule a personal school tour. This is a fun, interactive, no pressure introduction for us to get to know one another.


    Come by and see our unique facility (massage clinic, herbal pharmacy, 8000+ book library, Izba spa, and more…), the passion of our dedicated instructors, and the overall learning experience our students enjoy on a daily basis (you can even sit in on a class if you like). Schedule your visit with us today.
