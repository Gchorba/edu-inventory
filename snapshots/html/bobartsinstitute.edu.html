This Premier ReggaeMusicExpo© for Songwriters, Composers and Producers,
Open to all.

The ReggaeMusicExpo©  puts you face-to-face with some of the world's most successful songwriters, composers, producers and music business leaders, all who willingly share their knowledge and expertise and give you the know-how to take your music to the next level.

The ReggaeMusicExpo© is the first and only national conference dedicated to Reggae. The EXPO immediately provided a unique opportunity for songwriters, composers, publishers, producers - and those in the industry that support them - to come together in an unprecedented way to share their knowledge and expertise.

In its short history, the innovative programming offered to attendees has grown to include celebrity Q&A's, master classes, songwriting and composing workshops, publisher and business panels, One-on-One sessions, Do It Yourself career building workshops, showcases and performances, song feedback panels, state-of-the-art technology demos as well as leading music industry exhibitors.

Some of the biggest names in music, both on the creative and business side are invited to participate at ReggaeMusicExpo©.

February is when Jamaica welcomes the world to Reggae in Jamaica. Celebrate with us as we Reggae in the next 50 years of our Culture “REGGAE”. Thousands of music industry professionals will gather in Falmouth Jamaica for music's most anticipated event. ReggaeMusicExpo© 2012 is regarded as the singular networking event in the Reggae Industry, attracting professionals from different countries. With its high concentration of top international artists, DJs and industry professionals, ReggaeMusicExpo© permeates the international press and is thereby one of the most publicized events in the business. Every aspect of the industry will be represented including the top technological innovators, artists, DJs, producers, radio and video programmers, retailers, distributors, audio manufacturers and many more. There are countless reasons why people from all over the world will come for this event, the most outstanding one being that there is nothing else in the world like it.

Industry Development

The event has helped bring the Reggae Music world together in a very focused, productive and meaningful manner thanks to its friendly, forward-thinking and intimate setting. The event has also helped its attendees broker many new business and creative opportunities, allowed them to develop invaluable new relationships via its unparalleled networking platforms and provided them with insight and access into the evolution of the global music business, which is essential in today’s rapidly changing musical ecosystem. In addition, ReggaeMusicExpo© has provided great outcomes for emerging showcase Reggae talent from around the world.

Our mandate has always been to inspire those with a true passion for music and focus on doing new business worldwide and to create and explore new ways to work together for the common good of our Reggae Industry. ReggaeMusicExpo© will 2012 introduce our International Songwriting Summit, which will provide roundtable face-to-face meetings with some of the most influential songwriters, music supervisors, gaming companies, ad agencies, brands, publishers and content creators from around the world.
