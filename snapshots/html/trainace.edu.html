Career Training for Computer and IT Professionals

The Academy of Computer Education (ACE) is a professional career training institution which offers courses designed to help prepare current and prospective Information Technology (IT) professionals for various industry certifications.

Note: This website will be used for future initiatives. If you are interested in learning more about our training center or our computer certification courses please visit our main website at http://www.trainace.com.

Our course work is specifically designed to accomplish two goals:  To help give students the understanding and core knowledge needed to achieve industry certification, and to help prepare our students with the skills and knowledge they need to be successful in today's IT marketplace. Our hands-on training helps make prepare our students to be the best IT support consultants in the area and our students help make up the employee base of many DC, Virginia and Maryland IT companies.

Classes we feature focus on a variety of popular industry certifications, including Microsoft and Cisco Networking, Information Security, and Productivity Software.  Our school features small class sizes, flexible schedules, knowledgeable instructors and affordable pricing.  We are conveniently located in Greenbelt, Maryland - overlooking the Washington DC Capital Beltway. We can offer training at our location or on site anywhere in the Washington DC / Baltimore area.
