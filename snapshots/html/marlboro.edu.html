Why Marlboro?

If you passionately explore ideas, demand independent thinking with your liberal arts education, and respect the responsibility and freedom that comes from creating your own curriculum with a trusted faculty advisor, then Marlboro may be the place for you. Listen to a few Marlboro students tell how they discovered Marlboro. And how Marlboro discovered them.
