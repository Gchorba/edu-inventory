Welcome

Every Day is Career Day

Baker College is the largest independent college in Michigan with the most focused approach to education and training available. Our mission is to prepare you for meaningful employment. We’re proud to have a 97 percent employment rate among our available graduates. In addition, all our graduates receive Lifetime Employment Assistance—free and forever.

Creating bright futures for U.S. servicemembers.

Whether you’re on duty, on leave, or retired from military service, Baker College can help you make the most of your military education benefits. Our programs are military friendly and are available at 14 Michigan locations or online worldwide. Best of all, Baker College graduates receive Lifetime Employment Assistance—free and forever.
