Congratulations Band Members
11 LCC band students have been accepted
into the 2012 Ohio High School Honors Band at Ohio Northern University!
Freshmen Bethany Jordan-Squire (flute), Meredith Bruno (clarinet), Kacey
Bradshaw (bari sax), Natalie Hunt (trumpet), and Vincent Schreck
(trombone); Sophomores Elizabeth McNamara (flute), Jane Knapke (trumpet),
and Sophia DePalma (euphonium); Junior Kyle Ferris (percussion), and
Seniors Janina Bradshaw (clarinet) and Michael Shanahan (percussion), will
join high school musicians from across the state to spend 2 days of
intensive rehearsals on the ONU Campus, April 28 & 29, ending with a
public performance at the Freed Center for the Performing Arts at 4:00pm
on the 29th.
This is the sixth honors band LCC band students have been accepted into
during the 2011-2012 academic year, with 80% of the band students
participating in at least 1 honors band!
Congrats to all on a successful year in music

May 21-22: Senior Exams (Block Days)

May 23-25: Underclassmen Exams
