Knox Theological Seminary exists to equip servant leaders for effective Reformed ministry that is Christ Centered, gospel driven, and mission focused.

  • CHRIST CENTERED - At Knox, we teach that the whole Bible points to Jesus—it is one story about one savior and tells of God’s one plan.

  • GOSPEL DRIVEN - The gospel directs and drives the Christian's life, shapes and sustains our ministry, and founds and fuels the church.

  • MISSION FOCUSED - Knox is passionate about evangelism and discipleship as well as patterns of justice and mercy in the city.
