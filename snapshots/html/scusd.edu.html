Parents as Partners in Schools (PAPS)
Sutterville Elementary School

March 15, 2012 - 5:30pm - 7:30pmMarch 22, 2012 - 5:30pm - 7:30pmMarch 29, 2012 - 5:30pm - 7:30pmApril 12, 2012 - 5:30pm - 7:30pmApril 17, 2012 - 5:30pm - 7:30pmApril 26, 2012 - 5:30pm - 7:30pmMay 3, 2012 - 5:30pm - 7:30pmMay 10, 2012 - 5:30pm - 7:30pmMay 17, 2012 - 5:30pm - 7:30pm Sutterville

Parents as Partners in Schools (PAPS) consist of nine, highly interactive workshops lasting two hours each.  The training, childcare, food, materials, and graduation certificate are provided free of charge for parents with children enrolled in the school.  The program goals are as follows:

• Learn how to help your child succeed in school.
• Be a part of a parent network to improve your child’s school.
• Communicate with ease with school staff for your child’s educational needs.
• Develop leadership skills and the feeling of join ownership with schools.
• Gain a better understanding of the school system.
