Denver | Colorado | USA | Welcome!

Denver is considered one of the United States' leading cities. It is an international transportation hub and hosts many conventions year 'round. It is a moderately sized, modern city, where you can find anything you need. It is well known for its friendly, open-minded people, abundance of parks, recreation, and safety. You will also find a well developed performing arts complex, nightlife, diverse culture, and architecture, as well as major league football, baseball, basketball, hockey and soccer teams.
Denver lies just outside the foothills of the Rocky Mountains which is home to internationally known ski/snowboard resorts such as Vail, Aspen, Breckenridge, Keystone and Winter Park. Personal Academic Advisor & Experienced Teachers

At CSE, a personal academic advisor will help you define your goals, monitor your progress, and offer you the assistance you need. He will tailor a program that fits your exact needs.

Meeting Your Individual Goals with our Experienced Teachers
  Communicative approach
  Intensive & accelerated academic program
Colorado School of English strives to meet the individual needs of each and every student. With our emphasis on conversation and self-expression, you will develop confidence and actually enjoy learning English in the USA. Our classes are accelerated, and you may move to the next level as soon as you are ready; it's not necessary to wait until the beginning of a new term. At Colorado School of English, our highly qualified staff of teachers have experience teaching outside of the United States and are sensitive to cultural differences. If you should ever have a problem, our teachers will work with you to resolve it immediately. Spoken English is emphasized in all classes. You will practice using grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation and idioms, until you feel confident expressing yourself in English.
