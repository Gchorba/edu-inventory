Don't just make a living - make a life.

Are you looking for a job or do you want a career? Perry Technical Institute has more than 70 years of experience launching successful careers. Explore the possibilities at Perry Tech. Perry is a private, non-profit technical school in Yakima, Washington, offering 10 fast-track programs for in-demand careers. Learn real-world skills through hands-on education. At Perry, you'll develop top-notch technical skills while you build a strong work ethic. And when you graduate, you'll carry Perry's track record for success into the job market. Your qualifications backed by Perry Tech's reputation... the perfect combination for launching a successful career.
