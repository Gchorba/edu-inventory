Mouse over an IRIS Program for more information:

Data Management System
The IRIS Data Management System is charged with ensuring long-term archiving and distribution of all IRIS generated data.

Education and Public Outreach
Education and Public Outreach develops and implements programs to foster seismology education in the classroom and for the general public.

Global Seismographic Network
The GSN is a 150+ station, globally distributed, state-of-the-art permanent digital seismic network providing free, realtime, open access data through the IRIS DMS.

PASSCAL
Program for Array Seismic Studies of the Continental Lithosphere (PASSCAL) is a facilities program for the seismic research community supported by the NSF and DOE.

USArray
USArray is the seismic and magnetotelluric component of EarthScope, an earth science program to explore the structure and evolution of the North American continent.
