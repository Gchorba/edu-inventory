If you love music, movies, art, news, politics or sports, consider getting an education at Specs Howard School of Media Arts. We offer diploma programs in Radio, Video & Film, Graphic Design and Digital Media Arts and a certificate program in Radio, Video & Film to provide you with the skills you need to qualify for entry-level employment in the media and communications industries.

Whether you see yourself holding a camera, putting your gift of gab to work on a microphone, designing artwork for an advertising campaign, or creating videos to post on the web, our experienced faculty provide a hands-on approach to education, so you get to practice your chosen craft on software and equipment used by professionals.

Today our graduates work in virtually every aspect of the broadcast, communications and media industries. Some are now successful radio personalities, newscasters, play-by-play announcers, television reporters and news anchors. Others are employed in the television and video production industry as camera operators, producers, video editors, production assistants, and video technicians. Many work in marketing, promotions, sales, graphic design, motion graphics, and digital design. Visit our Grad Stories and Hall of Fame for in-depth profiles of some of these successful graduates.
