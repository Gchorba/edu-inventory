THE COLLEGE

Looking for a new career? Join the 1000s of successful professionals who chose Lytle's Redwood Empire Beauty College for their education. Over 35 years of experience and the latest trends in skin care, hair design and education are the reasons Spas and Salons want our grads! Lytle's Redwood Empire Beauty College is a premiere school of Cosmetology Arts & Sciences located in Northern California, and the only nationally accredited beauty college in Sonoma County.
