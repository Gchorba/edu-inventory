Dallas Nursing Institute has been developed with the premise that students deserve a quality institution as they begin their quest for a professional career. At DNI, we take great pride in being open, friendly, caring, culturally sensitive and service oriented.

The school recognizes the need to graduate quality students. The instructors are well qualified and dedicated to making your educational experience a positive one. Through their guidance, they will be providing you with the skills and knowledge that you will need to succeed in your chosen field.

Our vocational nursing program is based on the latest technology and state of the industry equipment. Curriculum is designed to be of the highest quality, academically challenging and relevant to current industry standards. Every effort has been made to make this program financially affordable to all who desire to attend. DNI provides continuous job placement support to all our graduates.

We have the experience, the tools, the instructors and the student support and benefits to help you change your life and earn a new career! Make today “the first day of the rest of your life” – make the call that will change your life and get you on the right track toward a rewarding future.
