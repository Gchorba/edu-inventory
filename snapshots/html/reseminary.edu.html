Welcome to RES on the Web

Founded by the Reformed Episcopal Church in 1887, the Reformed Episcopal Seminary educates and trains Christians for lay and ordained ministries in the church of Jesus Christ.

We are distinguished by our strong commitment to the inerrancy of God's Word, adherence to reformed theology and evangelical beliefs, worship and polity in the Anglican tradition, and an emphasis on pastoral ministry training. Read more...

FULL SCHOLARSHIPS AVAILABLE!
RES is pleased to announce that 10 full paid scholarships have been made available for new students.

To qualify, applicants must have a bachelor's degree from a recognized college. They should also be prepared to enter into full time study in the three year program leading to the Master of Divinity degree at the Seminary's Blue Bell, PA campus.

To apply, contact the Seminary at (610) 292-9852 or registrar@reseminary.edu
