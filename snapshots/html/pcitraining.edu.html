Welcome PCI: Offering hands-on career training programs! 

  • [View]

We offer convenient technical training school locations in South Kansas City, Missouri; North Kansas City, Missouri; and Lawrence, Kansas, as well as online programs. PCI offers the flexibility our students need to make sure they receive the best quality education available. Students know once they graduate, they can secure the kind of career they’ve always wanted. Our career education gives our students the tools to excel in the marketplace.

Since 1953, PCI career education schools have been training students to reach the "pinnacle" of professional opportunities. We do so by offering career training programs in competitive and expanding industries. Pinnacle Career Institute offers something for everyone.

Pinnacle Career Institute strives to train people to become better professionals who are team players, excellent communicators and valued employees. With our career training programs, you can enter the business, electronics, alternative energy, health and wellness, medical, or trades and technical field feeling confident in your skills.

As times continue to change, so does PCI. We offer online career training programs to students in Kansas City, Lawrence and beyond. This gives students the chance to take classes around their schedules – not PCI’s. Find out more about PCI’s online training programs here.

The career training programs at Pinnacle Career Institute make the difference between a job and a career in today’s employment market. Whatever field you may be entering, training from a technical school like Pinnacle Career Institute will look great on your resumé. Our superior programs and the dedication to our students make Pinnacle Career Institute your best choice for career training programs in Kansas City, Lawrence and Online Education.
