Lexington Theological Seminary's mission is to prepare faithful leaders for the church. We seek to prepare
men and women for ministry in congregations through innovative instruction, flexible curriculum, congregational experience and compassionate engagement with the needs of society. LTS offers an online program that is competency-based, congregation-centered and developmental in its commitment to prepare people for leadership in congregational ministry.
