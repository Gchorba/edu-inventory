AGREEMENT WITH WINONA STATE AND CROSSROADS - Monday, July 11, 2011

Crossroads College is pleased to announce an articulation agreement now in place with Winona State University, which allows for undergraduate credit transfer and recognition of baccalaureate degrees between the two institutions.

Signed and effective July 1, 2011, this agreement states “Winona State University agrees to accept all non-remedial Crossroads College courses in transfer upon admission to the University.”  And further, “Winona State University recognizes… Crossroads College baccalaureate degrees in totality,” and “Winona State University will no longer require a second baccalaureate degree from WSU for students seeking enrollment into post-baccalaureate programs.”

Thanks to the excellent work by teams at both institutions, and relationships developed between the two colleges, this agreement formalizes our partnership in higher education.  Special thanks to Professor Kimber Schletty, Director of Admission Chris Williams, and Registrar Robert Damon for their work on this agreement, and for the encouragement of Presidents Mike Kilgallin of Crossroads College and Judith Ramaley of Winona State University.
