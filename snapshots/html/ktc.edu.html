Practical Nursing
Practical Nursing is now taking applications for the 2012 school year. Hurry, the deadline is April 15, 2012. Get your Application.

Practical nursing training may be the first step in a career ladder concept in the nursing profession. The KTC Practical Nursing Program is a post-secondary program designed to provide students training in academics and clinical skills. This program will prepare students to work as a highly skilled member of the healthcare team. Earn your diploma now to increase your earning potential. Financial aid is available to qualified applicants. Contact a campus near you.
