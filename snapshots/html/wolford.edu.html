Come and take the challenge!  Expand your horizon and reach new heights in your nursing career as a CRNA.

Become a Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetist.

A professional career as a CRNA is rewarding, challenging and in high demand. Wolford College offers a 28 month curriculum leading to a Master's Degree in Nurse Anesthesia with a full scope of nurse anesthesia practice.

Advance your Knowledge and Career!

Wolford College has received approval to offer a post-Master's Doctor of Nurse Anesthesia Practice (DNAP). This is a 24 month curriculum offered entirely online. Any questions, please call Wolford College at (239) 513-1135.

Wolford College, located in Naples Florida, is an independent co-educational institution of higher learning and is accredited by the Council of Accreditation of Nurse Anesthesia Educational Programs having received the maximum accreditation of 10 years and licensed by the Florida Department of Education Commission of Independent Education.

Wolford College's Nurse Anesthesia Program has received institutional accreditation from the Council on Accreditation of Nurse Anesthesia Educational Programs, a recognized U.S. Department of Education agency, however, credit in this program may or may not be transferable to other universities.
