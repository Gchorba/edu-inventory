    San Diego, CA

    United States University, San Diego campus, is an accredited university located in Chula Vista, and southeast of downtown San Diego, CA easily accessible by the 805 and 5 freeways.

    United States University
    Orange County, CA

    United States University’s newest campus is located in Orange County, about an hour’s drive north of San Diego and south of Los Angeles, California.

    United States University, Online

    Not in San Diego or Orange County, California? Many United States University courses are available online, so you can get your degree from anywhere in the world.

U.S. University Offers A SUPERIOR EDUCATION

Founded in 1997, United States University (USU) in San Diego, in Cypress, and Online offers students from all backgrounds a quality education and degree programs in business and management, nursing, health sciences, education, degree completion in general studies as well as California teaching credential programs.

U.S. University is:

  • > Accredited by Western Association of Schools and Colleges
  • > Approved of the Board of Registered Nurses
  • > Accredited by the California Commission on Teacher Credentialing (CCTC)

You deserve a superior educational experience.
