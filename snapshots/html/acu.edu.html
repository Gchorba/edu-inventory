Spotlight

Elise (Smith '83) Mitchell

Elise Mitchell could have gone to college close to her southern Illinois home. But then she wouldn't have found herself doing other people's laundry on a recent Friday night. Mitchell chose to follow her older brother to ACU instead, turning her public relations degree into a nearly 30-year career that now sees her in charge of her own company, serving clients as large as Walmart and Tyson Foods. Elise is one of several 2011 alumni award recipients.

Partner in the Journey

Farron Salley is among hundreds of ACU students who needed a partner to help them on their journey to ACU. Without the support of committed friends and alumni, students such as Farron could not afford the premier Christian higher education ACU provides. With that in mind, we have begun the Partnering in the Journey Student Affordability Campaign, raising $50 million for endowed scholarships that will help generations of students fulfill the mission God gives them. Learn more about the campaign or partner with a student today!
