DISCERNMENT
The calling on or movement of the Holy Spirit to lead or give direction on a matter. It is how the Spirit shows the Church or its people what God wants them to do and be.

Go in prayer about God leading you to get prepared through Providence Bible College & Theological Seminary (PBC&TS).


We want to thank God for all He does for us, give Him the Glory, and never forget who gives us the breathe of life.  That so much of His Love goes out to every man, woman, boy, and girl that we cannot even begin to fathom the depth of His Love.

Come join us even if all your answers are not YES yet!
