American Healthcare Institute (AHI) has been the leader in professional continuing education for nurses and mental health professionals since 1984. At AHI, our primary mission is to provide you with the latest and most reliable resources available to help you continue your process of professional education.

When you select AHI as your continuing education provider, you are assured of getting cutting-edge and clinically useful information designed to help you improve and refine your skills. AHI's live seminars, conferences, audio programs, and on-site customized training are all delivered by highly experienced and well-qualified faculty experts who are active in the field and continually informed by up-to-date practice and research. 

For almost three decades, AHI has been the preferred choice for health care professional continuing education across the United States.   We look forward to having the opportunity to serve you and support your educational growth well into the future.

Easy Four Step Audio Process:

  1. Simply review the listing of AHI Audio Library programs and choose your selection(s).
  2. When we receive your order, we'll get your CDs right to you via Regular Mail or RUSH shipping.
  3. Listen to the program.  Multiple CDs  come to you automatically in both 'Listen Anywhere'  and MP3 formats, packaged in an attractive and durable vinyl case, with handouts on disc.  Listen when and where it's convenient for you.
  4. For CE testing: Simply fax or mail your answer sheet to AHI.
