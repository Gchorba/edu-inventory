Presidential Finalists Visit Campus

Sharon L. Hanson, Chair of the Presidential Search Committee, announces the selection of three finalists for the next phase of the Presidential search. The candidates will be on campus April 30-May 2 meeting with the college community.

Trocaire Unveils New Vision Statement

Our Vision: "Trocaire is the college where lives are transformed. Our graduates will be the first choice of employers."

Main Campus Expands to Seneca Street

Trocaire adds over 7,500 square feet of new classroom space for the Massage Therapy program, Massage Clinic and General Studies courses. Just two minutes away from Trocaire on Choate Avenue.

Apply to Trocaire College

Applying to Trocaire College is easy and free! Just click below to apply online, follow the instructions, complete the forms and then click 'submit my application'. Then you must request academic transcripts from the high school and any colleges that you attended.

New: Healthcare Informatics

Trocaire’s newest program blends healthcare, business & information technology to train students in the skills & technology needed to enhance patient care and manage healthcare data for systems improvement. Available as a certificate, A.A.S. degree or B.S. degree program, Healthcare Informatics is available in an evening/weekend format beginning this fall 2012.
