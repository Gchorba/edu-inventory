The School of New Jersey for Health Programs

The Institute for Health Education has been Training Students in the Allied Health Field for over 20 years. We continue to develop and offer programs that will yield the best opportunities for exciting careers. Our Graduates are trained by practicing professionals in each program.


Our goal is to promote the development of self-esteem, self confidence and respect for diversity through the commitment of education. We provide a solid foundation for the appreciation and learning within the health-care professions with  programs that the School for Health Education at New Jersey offers. We believe our placement service is the moral compass that drives our success. The insight and skills students receive from the quality faculty and staff will enable our graduates to be a "team player" and asset to any employer.
