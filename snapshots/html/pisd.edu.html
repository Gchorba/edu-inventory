Vision Statement

Plano ISD graduates will be empowered to:

Proactively adapt to new learning opportunities throughout their lives.

Collaborate with and contribute to the global community.

Be both creative and disciplined in their thinking.

Richard Matkin Named by Trustees as Lone Finalist for Position of Superintendent of Schools

The Plano ISD Board of Trustees on April 12 named Interim Superintendent Richard Matkin as the lone finalist for the position of superintendent of schools. Mr. Matkin joined Plano ISD as associate superintendent for business services in 2001. His thirty-six years of experience have yielded him the reputation as a school finance authority.

Teacher of the Year Gala May 10

Plano ISD's 2012 Teacher of the Year nominees and Beginning Teacher of the Year nominees will be celebrated for teaching excellence at Plano ISD's annual Teacher of the Year Gala to be held the evening of Thursday, May 10, at Plano Centre. At the gala, 71 experienced teacher nominees and 50 beginning teacher nominees will be honored for "Teaching by Design," the theme for this year's gala community event.

Graduation June 4 in Dallas

Plano ISD will celebrate graduation on Monday, June 4, at the Dallas Convention Center. At each of three ceremonies, hundreds of graduates will be honored, while more than 7,000 family members and friends fill the convention center to commemorate the occasion. Plano ISD is working with WFAA to produce a live television broadcast and a live webcast of each commencement ceremony, so that friends and family near and far will be able to take part in this event.

New School Administrators Announced for Year 2012-13

Several administrative and principal appointments were announced at the May 1 meeting of the Plano ISD Board of Trustees. Appointments included Renee Godi as the first principal to lead Plano ISD's new PBL Academy, new principals at Jasper and Shepton High Schools and Frankford, Murphy and Otto Middle Schools and at Hunt and Weatherford Elementary Schools.

'Thrill of the Grill 5k' Proceeds
to Benefit Education Foundation

The 4th Annual Central Market Thrill of the Grill 5k benefiting the Plano ISD Education Foundation was held on April 21 at Central Market Plano and was a sizzling success. View our post-event video , and help us congratulate Haun Elementary School for winning the top award for group participation. Other top groups included Plano ISD Bands, Schimelpfenig Middle School, Hedgcoxe Elementary School and Plano East Senior High School. Register early for Central Market Thrill of the Grill 5k 2013.
