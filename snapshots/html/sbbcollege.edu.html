Santa Barbara Business College

Wouldn't you have more employment possibilities if you had information technology or healthcare training? Maybe you're interested in paralegal schools or business colleges in California or you have been looking at California medical office programs. Whatever the case, Santa Barbara Business College offers programs and career training that can help make you a marketable prospect - and get you headed down a promising career path.

SBBCollege is a California technical school that helps people just like you learn marketable career skills with bachelor's degree, associate's degree and career training programs in Business Administration, Paralegal, Criminal Justice, Pharmacy Technology, Medical Assisting, or many other fields.

What makes SBBCollege so special?

SBBCollege has been providing California business school students with career training for more than 120 years. We know what a quality education can do for you. The Santa Barbara Business College bachelor's and associate's degree programs give you the option to enter the job market in your field while you continue your training â€“ and increase your job opportunities.

We're not just in Santa Barbara...

SBBCollege has multiple campuses throughout Southern California, making it possible for students to receive technical training and career education at their local community college campus or online from any location. In addition to the Santa Barbara campus, SBBCollege has campuses in Santa Maria, Bakersfield, Rancho Mirage and Ventura, California.

SBBCollege offers online courses...

The SBBCollege online school serves students who desire a quality online education, whether they live nearby or outside of Southern California. It's perfect for students with busy lives and others who make the decision to go to college online.

Five reasons to choose SBBCollege...
They actually get to know you and help you.
SBBCollege is a small college, allowing instructors and staff to devote time and attention to each student.
Founded in 1888, SBBCollege has trained and educated generations of students in a supportive family environment.

Get info about attending SBBCollege. SBBCollege is accredited. Learn more about SBBCollege.
More
They strive to make college affordable.
Wondering where you'll get the money to pay for college or job training? Well, stop worrying. SBBCollege can help.
As an accredited college, SBBCollege is able to offer financial aid to those who qualify, including grants, scholarships, and student loans. If you are serious about going to school, SBBCollege will work hard to help you afford it.

Learn more about financial aid at SBBCollege.
More
They offer job-placement assistance!
At SBBCollege, students get more than just a great education. Your SBBCollege tuition includes career services, such as job placement assistance, resume review, and interview preparation...
You see, some schools just teach classes. SBBCollege goes farther, giving graduates the tools they need to get started in their career!

Learn more about career services at SBBCollege.
More
Students get on-the-job training while still in school.
In addition to classroom education, SBBCollege students learn what it's like in the real world.
To gain valuable on-the-job experience, most SBBCollege students go to work on externships in their last term before graduating. The College's externship experience gives students the opportunity to reinforce what they've learned in class and learn what it's like on the job... all this while enhancing their resume.

They have a program for you.
