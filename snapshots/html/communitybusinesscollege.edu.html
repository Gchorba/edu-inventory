Welcome to Community Business College

Community Business College (CBC) invites you to explore our Web site and school community. CBC offers career training in select fields: Office Administration and Technology, HVAC/R Specialist, Solar Photovoltaic Specialist, Property Management, Accounting, Legal Secretary, and Medical Office Management through our Computer Office Specialist program. 

All programs are designed specifically for career development with an emphasis on current technology. Federal financial aid in the form of grants and student loans is available to qualified students. Financial assistance from third parties is also available to qualified students for job training.

Employers and alumni are invited to participate in our online community and visit our career training center. The success of our job training programs is tied directly to a viable career. Alumni are always welcome back to Community Business College for additional training to refine their skills and further their career development. Contact us today!
