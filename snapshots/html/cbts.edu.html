MY EXPERIENCE

Jennifer Harris Dault

College music major and journalist Jennifer Harris Dault was recruited for create by Central President Molly T. Marshall, who told her that they were “ looking for creative, entrepreneurial, ‘outside-the-box’ thinkers,” for an experimental cohort-model Master of Divinity program.

“I knew those were the type of people I wanted to surround myself with,”says Jennifer, who was a news writer for Word & Way . A native of Pineville, Louisiana, Jennifer lives in St. Louis. “My passion is people – engaging and understanding them. I feel the urge to go beyond the surface, find needs, and find ways to resource together to meet them.”
