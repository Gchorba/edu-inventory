“THE BEST CAREER COLLEGE IN THE SAN GABRIEL VALLEY FOR 2012”

Come and experience Premiere Career College’s educational services, now celebrating 20 years of the highest quality education and successful careers in Vocational Nursing, Surgical Technology, Hospital Central Service Technician, Medical Assisting with Coding & Billing, General Office, and Financial Records Processing. 

Vocational Nursing Modules have starts every 3 months; with morning, afternoon and evening sessions. Premiere Career College also offers Financial Aid resources to the deserving students and also accepts foreign applicants. With its high caliber faculty, consisting mostly of doctors and nurses, dedicated and highly qualified administrative staff, state-of-the-art equipment and a well kept comfortable building complex in a conductive-to-learning environment in Irwindale, California, Premiere Career College continues to become truly a premiere site of learning and ranked as "The Best Career College In The San Gabriel Valley for 2012"
