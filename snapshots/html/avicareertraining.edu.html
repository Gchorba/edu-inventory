Welcome to Avi Career Training. We are an accredited school offering programs in Esthetics, Master Esthetics, Electrolysis, Massage Therapy, Laser hair removal, Permanent Make Up, Cosmetology and Instructor Program. Conveniently located in Virginia, we serve students in the Northern Virginia, Washington DC and Maryland areas.

Our school is recognized both in the United States and internationally. Our Northern Virginia Massage Therapy School has been approved by the National Certification Board of Massage, Bodywork for Massage Therapy training and continuing education. The Department of Professional Regulation has approved our programs in Virginia, Maryland and Washington DC, including our Aesthetics school, Make up artist school, skin care school, Cosmetology/nail and permanent make up school. Students from around the world travel to attain the benefits of our training programs.

As a result of our high standards and accreditation, we are able to offer our students federal financial aid, including Pell Grants and Stafford Loans, and Direct Loan student loans. In addition to the various loan programs, we offer affordable tuition and payment plans to help our students realize their dreams.

Esthetics, skin care, beauty therapy, Cosmetology, massage and permanent make up are growing fields with great potential. Our flexible scheduling makes it possible for students to attend classes at their convenience. We offer day, evening and weekend classes to fit the needs of our students. This enables you to take your Esthetician Classes, skin care education, Cosmetology, Nail or massage therapy training at your convenience. Get in touch with us to take the first step on the road to your new career.
