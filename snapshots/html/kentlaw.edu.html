  • The C-K Experience

    • Extraordinary Faculty
      At IIT Chicago-Kent, you'll study and collaborate with one of the most productive and influential groups of legal scholars in the country.
      Learn more...
    • Dynamic Student Body
      Our students come from 40 states and 16 countries, with backgrounds in biochemistry, journalism, engineering, teaching, fine arts and more. They are interesting people leading interesting lives.
      Learn more...
    • Intensive Skills Training
      We combine rigorous theoretical course work with first-rate training in lawyering skills, legal writing and advocacy.
      Learn more...
    • Individual Career Planning
      Beginning in your first term, you're assigned a career counselor who helps you create a career development plan that fits your personal and professional goals.
      Learn more...
    • Top Bar Pass Rate in Illinois
      At 97 percent, our grads earned the highest pass rate in Illinois on the July 2011 bar exam, surpassing Illinois' eight other law schools. Learn more...
