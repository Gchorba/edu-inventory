Vatterott can prepare you to be successful in the ever-changing 21st century workforce.  Since 1969, Vatterott has been one of the most innovative and respected providers of career and technical education.

In addition to our many campuses located throughout the Midwest, we offer distance education programs that will allow you to earn credits toward your degree or diploma through online classes.

 Vatterott understands that relevant career skills are the key to a successful professional career. Vatterott offers career-focused education in a wide variety of degree-granting programs in high-demand fields like Business, Technology, Healthcare, Court Reporting, Trades, Culinary and Legal. Whether earning a diploma or one of several different degrees, Vatterott students graduate with credentials from an accredited institution.

Vatterott students receive hands-on training from expert instructors, flexible scheduling, and financial aid for those who qualify and career service assistance. Whether you are starting a new career, getting back into the workforce, or advancing your current career, Vatterott provides the training necessary to take the next step professionally.

 Programs at Vatterott campuses include fields in Business, Medical, Technical, Trades, Court Reporting, Culinary and Legal. All programs are industry-relevant and designed to support the Vatterott mission of providing career skills for a better life for graduates.
