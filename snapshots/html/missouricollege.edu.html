At Missouri College, We're Focused On Your Future

In today's society, healthcare is important; a large industry requiring a wide range of educated and trained practitioners, technical workers and business professionals to keep up with a growing demand for services. In fact, ten of the fastest growing occupations are healthcare related.* This has created opportunities for motivated individuals focused on a future helping others.

At Missouri College, we have been offering quality career training for over 45 years, introducing thousands of professionally focused students to exciting, challenging roles and responsibilities. Our programs in the fields of Business Administration, Healthcare, Dental and Massage Therapy are designed to help students gain relevant career and life skills.

Missouri College wants you to be successful in your chosen profession, so from the day you start classes, you'll be treated like a professional. You'll study in professional environments that replicate the modern workplace. You can learn from instructors with relevant experience and applicable, first-hand perspectives on what you can expect in your chosen field. You can gain confidence from the support and encouragement you receive from our collaborative community as you acquire the knowledge and skills to pursue your personal and professional goals.

We teach professional development. We encourage personal growth. Welcome to Missouri College.

To learn more about Missouri College, our programs or how to apply for admission, please contact us for more information.

*Source: http://www.bls.gov/oco/cg/cgs035.htm (visited August 15. 2011)

At Missouri College, We're Here to help You Sort It All Out Anytime

How to pay for an education is one of the first and foremost considerations any student must make, and the financial aid process can seem overwhelming and stressful to many. At Missouri College, we want you to be excited about going to school; not stressed out about completing all the right paperwork. That's why we have an experienced staff of Financial Aid representatives, ready to help you with everything from tuition planning to any questions that you may have.

At Missouri College, your education is a collaborative effort, from the time you enroll through the day you graduate, and beyond. Our Financial Aid representatives are ready to do what they do best so you can get on with what you do best... preparing for an exciting future in business or healthcare.

At Missouri College, You'll Get the Support You Need

The Career Services Department at Missouri College functions as a liaison between students and employers. From the day you begin classes, you will be supported and mentored by a team of dedicated professionals focused on your future after graduation.

The Missouri College Career Services staff can help you with every aspect of your career search. Our Career Services team is ready, willing and able to help you prepare for professional career opportunities in healthcare and other exciting industries.

Visit Missouri College Career Services

At Missouri College, Virtual Training Helps Prepare You For Real-World Situations

Many of today's professional opportunities in healthcare involve everyday interaction with patients. To help prepare for the roles and responsibilities in your chosen field, Missouri College employs the use of the Simpro Virtual Trainer.

As you complete your core curriculum coursework in your chosen area of healthcare training, your studies will be enhanced by interactive components designed to replicate situations you are likely to encounter in the real world. From streaming video to 3-D anatomy renderings and clinical simulations, it's virtually amazing!
