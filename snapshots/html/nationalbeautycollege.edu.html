...to National Beauty College and thank you for selecting us to assist you in obtaining your training in the field of cosmetology arts and sciences. You are now entering an expanding field that offers great potential for success in the world of beauty.

National Beauty College is an independent educational institution that started in December 2001. We offer courses in Cosmetology, Hairstyling, Esthetics and Manicuring. We train students to be eligible to sit for the state licensing exam. We want to instill a sense of professionalism in each student so we place great emphasis on how to be successful in the marketplace while providing professional services. Located in a residential area that is surrounded by different businesses, students have more opportunity to interact with clients and use the skills learned in class in a salon-like environment.

The school is equipped with a full service beauty salon, nail shop, and facial clinic to provide the best learning experience for students. We have excellent facilities with all of the amenities you would expect from a beauty college. Classes are taught in English. Tutoring sessions are available in English-Vietnamese and English-Spanish to accommodate for each student’s level of understanding.
