Digital Media Arts College // Boca Raton, FL

Digital Media Arts College (DMAC) is designed to energize your creativity and refine your artistic skills as it readies you for a career in the ever-changing design industry. DMAC offers specialized Bachelor of Fine Arts degrees in Computer Animation, Graphic Design, and Advertising Design as well as Master of Fine Arts degrees in Visual Effects Animation and Web Design.
