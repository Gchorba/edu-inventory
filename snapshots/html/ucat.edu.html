About the Utah College of Applied Technology | Your Future. Your Choice.

Whether you’re an adult needing to prepare for or upgrade your career, a high school student looking for hands-on education you can take to work, or an employer seeking a competitive workforce, UCAT offers choices at its eight regional Applied Technology Colleges (ATCs). UCAT was established by the State of Utah to provide market-driven technical education that meets the needs of Utah’s employers for skilled workers. Come in today. It’s your future, and you’re in charge!
