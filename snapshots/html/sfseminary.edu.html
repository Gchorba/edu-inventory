Welcome to Sioux Falls Seminary.
Sioux Falls Seminary has been equipping servant leaders who engage the mission of Jesus Christ since 1858.  In addition to our heritage as a Baptist seminary, we also serve the needs of our region.

Our contextual learning approach offers students much more than our renowned programs, including the Master of Divinity.  Study at SFS will equip you for ministry and transform you.  You’ll not only receive a quality theological education, but also serve in real-world ministry settings.  We invite you to explore all that Sioux Falls Seminary has to offer.
