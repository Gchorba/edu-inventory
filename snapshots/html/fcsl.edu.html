Celebrating commencement and preparing for the summer

The Florida Coastal School of Law Alumni Association will host its annual Graduation Open House on Friday, May 11 from 5:30-7:30 p.m. at the school. The event provides students the opportunity to welcome family and friends to the campus for tours, refreshments, graduation portraits and more to celebrate their commencement. Pictures will be made available on the school's Flickr account shortly thereafter.

Whether you're graduating or not, the summer provides an excellent opportunity to gain legal experience. Follow this link to find helpful tips from the Career Services Department on how to make your summer and productive one.

  • Professor Roger Groves

Professor Roger Groves is published in the Creighton Law Review. The thesis involves cutting edge issues of using algorithms and predictive analytics to identify past and future breaches of fiduciary duties by corporate board of directors. The article is entitled The Implications of a Jeopardy! Computer Named Watson: Beating Corporate Boards of Directors at Fiduciary Duties?

Professor Groves is also presenting at Howard University- IP Institute conference regarding innovative methods for professional athletes to retain their intellectual property. Click through to read the full list.
