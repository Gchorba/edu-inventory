Welcome

Thank you for your interest in the Colorado Institute of Massage Therapy, an owner operated school since 1985. The website is a great place to start your research, but massage therapy is about people connecting with people, so please feel free to contact us with any questions, or to visit us on campus to learn more! CIMT is honored to be the only school in Colorado to include the International Academy of Neuromuscular Therapy (IANMT) certification in our curriculum.
