Advanced Career Training

 Established in 1994, CNI Vocational College offers advanced training at our Surgical Technology School, Massage Therapy School, LVN School, Personal Fitness Training School, Medical Assisting School, Medical Billing and Coding School, MRI Technology School, and Pharmacy Technician School. All of CNI’s career training programs feature extensive courses that will expand your knowledge to perform your job well and allow you to excel in the workplace.

Accreditations and Approvals

CNI College is accredited and approved by numerous associations and agencies, including the Accrediting Bureau of Health Education Schools (ABHES), Commission on Accreditation of Allied Health Education Programs (CAAHEP), Board of Vocational Nursing and Psychiatric Technicians (BVNPT), National Strength and Conditioning Association (NSCA), American Massage Therapy Association (AMTA), American Society of Health Systems Pharmacists (ASHP), American Registry of Magnetic Resonance Imaging Technologists (ARMRIT), California Association of Private Postsecondary Schools (CAPPS), Career College Association (CCA) and Veterans Administration Benefits Training. These affiliations not only demonstrate CNI’s credibility and offerings, but provide eligibility to CNI College graduates to take their specific program’s licensure or certification exam – National Council Licensure Examination-Practical Nurse (NCLEX-PN), National Board of Surgical Technology and Surgical Assisting (NBSTSA) - Certified Surgical Technologist (CST) Examination, National Strength and Conditioning Association - Personal Fitness Training (NSCA-CPT) Certification Examination, National Certification Board for Therapeutic Massage & Bodywork (NCBTMB) exam, Pharmacy Technician Certification Exam (PTCE), Certified Professional Coder (CPC) Exam, American Association of Medical Assistants (AAMA) Exam, American Medical Technologists (AMT) Exam, and American Registry of Magnetic Resonance Imaging Technologists (ARMRIT) National Certification Examination.

Veterans Benefits

CNI College is proud and honored to help our military veterans and dependents in furthering their educational training in the allied healthcare field. CNI College is approved by the Department of Veterans Affairs so military veterans and their dependents can utilize the Montgomery GI Bill and the Post 9/11 GI Bill to help pay for their education.
