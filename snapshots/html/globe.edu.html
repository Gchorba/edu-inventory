Globe Institute of Technology - Your Life, Your Dream, Your College

You know deep inside that you can be so much more. You just need to optimize your potential.

At Globe Institute of Technology, we see you as a complete person with real potential, with untapped talents that can be developed. Through the right Globe degree or certificate, we can help you attain your personal and career goals.

We want to be the training partner that helps you build the educational resources you need now to get the job you want and the earning security your family needs. We want to help keep you sharp and relevant in an ever-changing world.

Globe Institute of Technology features comprehensive and affordable certificate and degree programs in a wide variety of career fields, including:

  • Accounting
  • Banking and Finance
  • Business Management
  • Healthcare Management
  • Hospitality Management
  • Legal Office Management
  • Management Information Systems
  • Sports Science Management
  • Computer Information Systems
  • Computer Programming and Systems Design

Please explore our site for more information about our course offerings and class schedules.
If you're interested in earning your certificate or bachelor's degree in New York, our technology college could be right for you! We encourage you to visit us in our midtown Manhattan location, and speak with our faculty and staff. With Globe you can experience a small-college environment without sacrificing big-school resources. We would love to show it to you.
