SCHOOL OF INTERIOR DESIGN

Discover how a unique interior design school in California can help you gain access to a rewarding career in interior design. Wherever you are in your life - recent high school graduate, transferring college student, or someone searching for a satisfying career - your time is now.

Design Institute of San Diego, founded in 1977, is devoted to a single purpose: professional interior design education.

The CIDA-accredited interior design degree program leads to a BFA in Interior Design.

Sustainability and Green Design practices are the most important issues in interior design today. New ways of doing things will be discovered by your generation. You can, in fact, play a part in this transformation.

You can learn to integrate environmental accountability into your design projects as a student and introduce green practices into your firm when you graduate with an interior design degree.

This is your time. Your Time is Now!
