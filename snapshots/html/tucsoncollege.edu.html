Welcome to Tucson College!

Tucson College provides our students with a comprehensive and current career education. With our modern facilities, instructional materials, and industry experienced instructors, the college provides a friendly, supportive learning environment in which Students Come First.

Our faculty and staff are on hand to guide you to success through comprehensive hands-on training in a friendly, family-like atmosphere. Choose any one of our in-demand career programs:

  • Administrative Assistant/Computer Applications Specialist
  • Certified Nursing Assistant
  • Electrical Technician
  • Medical Assistant
  • Medical Office Billing and Coding Specialist
  • Patient Care Technician
  • Pharmacy Technician
  • Phlebotomy Technician
  • Photovoltaic
