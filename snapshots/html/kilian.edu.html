Located in the heart of the city, near the Falls of the Big Sioux River in downtown Sioux Falls, Kilian is the city’s only community college.

What does that mean for you? It means you have access to an experienced and dedicated faculty teaching at an accredited college.

Established in 1977, Kilian is renowned for its small class sizes and personal attention given to students by experienced faculty and in our Student Success Center. Achieve your goals with a two-year degree, or start your education at Kilian and finish your four-year degree at the university of your choice.

Kilian Community College focuses on helping every student succeed. And because each person defines success differently, each student’s program is unique. Kilian’s faculty and staff help you create your own goals, pathway and direction. And we’ll journey with you until you reach your destination.
