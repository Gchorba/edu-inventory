Attention Financial Aid Students:
 
Pell Grant Recipients
Effective 2012/2013 Award Year, students will only receive a Pell Grant for 12 semesters. The 12 semesters is based on full-time enrollment for each semester enrolled. This applies to ALL Federal Pell Grant eligible students and includes ALL years of receiving Pell Grant funds.
 
 Pell/Loan Refund Checks
Available for pickup on Thursday, April 12, 2012 in McSwain Building (Main Campus) and Shope Building (Graham County Center).  Picture ID is required.

For more information, contact the Financial Aid Office at (828) 837-6810.
