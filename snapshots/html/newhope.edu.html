About New Hope Christian College


New Hope Christian College is a private, four-year Christian college located on a 36-acre campus in Eugene, Oregon. NHCC - Oregon offers a Bachelor's degree Bible with several concentrations including Christian Counseling, Cross Cultural Studies, Business Administration, Music, Worship Leading, Drama and Pastoral Studies. NHCC - Hawaii offers a MA in Christian Ministry.

Dream Releaser Statue Installation

On Wednesday, February 15, New Hope Christian College Oregon – (NHCC) dedicated the Dream Releaser statue. The artist, Dan Chen, created the piece based on Chancellor Wayne Cordeiro’s book, “The Dream Releasers.” Cordeiro’s childhood experience of freeing a bamboo caged bird in Japan and seeing its joy as it took flight emblazed in him the desire to cooperate with God in His plan to tap on the cages of young hearts and encourage the release of their dreams. Cordeiro believes that in the heart of every person is a God-sized dream of what we can be for the kingdom of God.

A New Location

New Hope Christian College (NHCC) is excited to announce that it added an additional location this fall. The former Pacific Rim Christian College in Honolulu, Hawaii has merged with our Eugene, Oregon campus as an added location under the name New Hope Christian College - Hawaii. The two locations affords NHCC increased faculty resources; combined library and financial resources; streamlined administration; and joint missional outreaches to the US and international destinations, including a presence in Asia and the Pacific Rim, with the possibility of additional locations or alliances in all the corners of the world. We are currently working out the implications of this merger of the two colleges as well as a new website for NHCC with information about both campuses.
