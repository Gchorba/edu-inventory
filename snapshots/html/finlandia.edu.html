Hello! Welcome to Finlandia University, a learning community dedicated to academic excellence, spiritual growth, and service. We're glad you've found us. Finlandia is a private, not-for-profit university located in Michigan's beautiful and adventurous Upper Peninsula. Contact us at 906-487-7274, 877-202-5491, or admissions@finlandia.edu.
