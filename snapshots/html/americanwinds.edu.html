Flight Academy

NEW UNIVERSITY ASSOCIATE DEGREE PROGRAM: American Winds, through a license agreement with Akron Institute of Herzing University, now provides career flight training as part of an Associate Degree of Professional Aeronautics degree program. This program gives a career minded student the perfect opportunity to advance their education with a degree while obtaining the required aviation licenses to become a career commercial pilot.

International

American Winds Flight Academy is now SEVIS certified for International Students. AWFA pairs first-class, professional training with one-of-a-kind hospitality to help you build an amazing experience and a home while earning your pilot's license. Unlike our competitors which train hundreds of students each year, AWFA limits its class size so that each student is a priority and receives the personal attention necessary to reach maximum potential.

Read More

 

Charter

You have heard about private aircraft charter, seen an expensive magazine ad promoting aircraft charter, or may have seen a movie with some business executive or celebrity getting on or off an expensive multi-million dollar private jet. This is only one side of private aircraft charter. American Winds Charter is affordable and is a great alternative to commercial airline travel. Private charter aircraft allows you to save time and money by eliminating airport hassles. Call us for a fast quote today!

 

Discovery Flight

Testing Center

Under the guidelines of the FAA, knowledge exams may only be given through certified testing centers. American Winds is an approved testing center. Call (330) 733-2500 to schedule your exam today.
