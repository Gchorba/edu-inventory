At SNHU, the focus is on students. Whether you're just starting to research or are ready to apply, we have the resources to help you make the right decisions and succeed. Take the next step and become a part of the SNHU Community.

Online, on campus or on location, the focus at SNHU is on you. By making it easy to transfer credits into our programs (up to 90 credits for undergraduate students), SNHU includes you in our community and gives you the support and resources for your success.

You have the drive to succeed and SNHU has the programs and support for you to do it here. Online degrees give you the convenience and flexibility to take classes anywhere you're stationed. SNHU is a top military-friendly school, and has served the military for over sixty years.

No matter where you're from, SNHU can help you go places. Our on campus student body includes more than 600 students from 60 countries and many of our faculty members have worked or studied overseas. Come experience SNHU and everything we have to offer.
