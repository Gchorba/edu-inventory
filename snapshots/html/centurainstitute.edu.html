Welcome to Centura Institute

Our campus is centrally located to residents of outlying cities and towns in the Orlando Florida area. Approval to operate Centura Institute was received in 2002 from the Florida Commission for Independent Education. Our career school is dedicated to providing quality education in the fields of massage therapy, medical billing & coding, practical nurse and medical assisting & HVAC.

Centura Institute is our Southern most addition to Centura College Corporate Schools. Our Colleges and Institute are career adult education schools dedicated to career advancement with locations in the Eastern part of the United States. Through our network of Schools, we have the resources & expertise required to bring you the quality education you deserve in Orlando Florida.

Our medical assisting, practical nurse, medical billing & coding, massage therapy & HVAC programs combine basic theory with hands-on learning to prepare you for employment opportunities once you graduate. We work with local businesses to ensure that real world scenarios are brought to you in a classroom environment for analysis and learning. This ensures that businesses hire Centura Institute graduates with the most current knowledge in their field.

If you would like to know more about our school programs please visit the career student request page and a school representative will contact you. We can provide information on financial aid, tuition, courses of study, career outlooks and more.
