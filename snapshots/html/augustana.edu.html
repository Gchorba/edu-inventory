Beloved Dean Brodahl
dies at age 89

Betsey A. Brodahl '44, a dean at Augustana from 1948-1987, died April 20. "Few persons have had the kind of impact Betsey Brodahl had on Augustana during her long association with the college," said Augustana President Steve Bahls. "Her legendary grace and confidence inspired generations of students, including alumni now in their 80s, not to mention innumerable colleagues."(more)
