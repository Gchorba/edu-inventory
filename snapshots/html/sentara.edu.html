Why Sentara College of Health Sciences?

At Sentara College of Health Sciences we will provide you with the hands-on clinical education that will set you apart from all others.  You will become a confident and competent clinical professional through the educational opportunities and experiences you will receive.  We offer a variety of programs and courses: Bachelor of Science in Nursing Degree with four tracks:  Traditional BSN, LPN to BSN, RN to BSN, and Early Admission for High School Seniors; Associate of Occupational Science in Cardiovascular Technology with four, highly specialized tracks:  Invasive, Non-Invasive Vascular Study, Adult Echocardiography, and Cardiac Electrophysiology;  Diploma in Surgical Technology;  ST-CST; Care Partner; Nurse Aide; Monitor Surveillance
