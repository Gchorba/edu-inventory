Welcome

Welcome to Briarcliffe College!

Since 1966, Briarcliffe College has been providing career-focused education to a diverse student population. We are committed to helping you achieve your personal, professional and academic goals by providing you with an unparalleled level of student support.

From the time you apply through the time you graduate, and beyond, you'll belong to a community of dedicated faculty, financial aid advisors, mentors, classmates and alumni. You'll know them by name, and they'll know you. You'll see them at Briarcliffe athletic events and school functions. And you'll rely on them for assistance in helping you successfully complete your chosen program.

Welcome to Briarcliffe... a place where you can go far.

Degree Programs

Career-Focused Programs

At Briarcliffe College, our goal is to provide you with the knowledge, skills and confidence you need to go far in life, as well as in your career. That's why we structure every academic program to help prepare market-ready professionals for satisfying, rewarding career opportunities in specific fields of interest.

We work closely with local businesses, seeking their input and utilizing their expertise to help shape academic programs and ensure our students receive a market-relevant education specifically designed to expand their employment opportunities in today's competitive job market.

We offer career-focused programs in Accounting, Business Administration, Criminal Justice, Graphic Design, Networking & Computer Technology, and Paralegal Studies.

Campus Locations

A Convenient Location Near You

With three convenient locations, Briarcliffe College provides easy access and industry-current facilities to all students living or working in the New York metropolitan area. At our Bethpage, Patchogue and Queens locations, we have expanded our facilities and curricula to meet the growing educational needs of the communities we serve.

Furthermore, Briarcliffe College offers the option to take some courses and some degree programs fully online, adding a level of flexibility that can enable you to fit classes into your current schedule without disrupting your personal and professional responsibilities.

Learn More

Schedule Flexibility

Education That fits Your Current Lifestyle

Briarcliffe College understands that today's busy lifestyles require class schedules to be flexible and convenient, so you can pursue your education on your own terms and without disrupting your current responsibilities. That's why we offer day, evening, weekend and even online classes so you can tailor your studies to fit comfortably into your current day-to-day activities.

At Briarcliffe, you can go to school just two nights each week to maintain fulltime student status. We also offer a Flexible Learning Option that gives you the freedom to take some of your classes online, including some of the general education courses, as well as courses in your chosen concentration.

Online Degree Programs

Online Degree Offerings

As more and more people make the decision to pursue career-focused knowledge and skills, online learning becomes an ideal option for many of them. In fact, practically anything you can accomplish on a traditional college campus can be achieved through the interactive Briarcliffe College online portal, which keeps you in touch with your studies anytime, anywhere as you acquire the knowledge, skills and confidence to pursue your dreams.

From choosing your preferred style of learning and interacting one-on-one with your classmates and instructors to joining social clubs and participating in extracurricular activities, we've made it possible for education to be an active, convenient part of your everyday life. Welcome to the next generation of Briarcliffe College.

Welcome to Briarcliffe College

Briarcliffe College is a career-focused institution of higher learning that provides quality education and support services to a diverse student population. Our standards of excellence and student centered teaching and learning help prepare students for personal and professional growth.

We are committed to providing superior customer service by delivering personal, focused attention and anticipating and responding to students' educational needs. Briarcliffe College also remains committed to students beyond graduation by providing continued career services assistance and instilling the principles of lifelong learning and community involvement.

Tuition & Financial Aid

At Briarcliffe College, a wide variety of financial aid programs are available, including scholarships, grants, loans and work-study programs. Our friendly Financial Aid staff will be happy to help you find the best and most efficient way of paying for your education. STUDENT SUPPORT SERVICES

At Briarcliffe, we understand college life can be an exhilarating and at times challenging experience, both academically and socially. That's why we strive to create an environment where you will feel comfortable, connected and enthusiastic about your education.

Briarcliffe College is committed to providing you with the support and encouragement you need to pursue your dreams and achieve your goals. Online offerings

Briarcliffe College online programs in business and healthcare administration offer you the flexibility to study at home, at work or on the go, wherever you have an Internet connection. So, you can gain industry-current knowledge and skills that can enhance your employment potential while you pursue career opportunities, or work full or part time.

It's amazing how much you can accomplish online these days, including advancing your education. Briarcliffe College's online programs can help you achieve your goals when your schedule and responsibilities make it impractical for you to enroll in traditional classes at one of our Long Island locations.

When you take classes online at Briarcliffe, you'll have the opportunity to interact with your classmates and instructors in many of the same ways you already stay in touch with your friends, family and co-workers. By incorporating chat, email and popular social networking sites into the many ways you can access our online learning community, we've made it possible to make education an active, engaging part of your everyday life. Courses are fully web-based, and most content is also available through a variety of portable devices, offering multiple opportunities to interact with instructors and classmates throughout your learning experience.

In fact, practically anything you can accomplish on a traditional college campus can be achieved through the interactive Briarcliffe College online portal. We are committed to developing quality online programs that can help you prepare for exciting future career opportunities. Learn more

Career Services

Most students at Briarcliffe College have identified long-term professional goals and are seeking the knowledge and skills that can help them get there. Our Career Services Office is committed to helping students and alumni compete and succeed in today's job market through a variety of career development and employment services.

As a student at Briarcliffe, you will have the opportunity to team-up with a program-specific career services representative who will be dedicated to your growth and success. The services offered by these trained professionals include career coaching, resume and cover letter writing, follow-up letter preparation and mock interviews.

Briarcliffe also sponsors on-campus recruitment, which takes place each semester, providing you with opportunities to meet hiring representatives from many leading companies. We work closely with local businesses, seeking their input and utilizing their expertise to help ensure our students receive an industry-current education specifically designed to expand their employment opportunities in today's competitive job market.

Pursuing success means tapping into all your available resources, and Briarcliffe's Career Services team is ready, willing and able to support you with guidance, advice and direction as you pursue your goals. Learn more

Athletics

Success in the real world depends on leadership, teamwork and cooperative skills; qualities that can be learned both in the classroom and on a field of play. Briarcliffe offers a variety of competitive collegiate team sports that can help balance your studies with the camaraderie of extracurricular activities.

Men's sports at Briarcliffe include baseball, lacrosse, bowling and soccer. Women's sports include basketball, bowling and softball. The Bulldogs and Lady Bulldogs compete at a variety of venues and facilities across Long Island. If you are interested in participating in one of Briarcliffe's intercollegiate sports programs, please contact the Athletic Department.

At Briarcliffe, we believe the complete college experience encompasses community and camaraderie as much as it does academics and studying.

That's why we strive to create an environment where you will feel comfortable, connected and enthusiastic about your education. From academics to athletics, focusing on your career and enjoying extracurricular activities, Briarcliffe College is far and away an extraordinary place to find your way in the world. Learn more
