Welcome to Modern Technology School

714-418-9100

Congratulations on making the decision to further your education!

Modern Technology School has been in the education business since 1981. We have graduated many successful X-ray Techs, Ultrasound Techs & Medical Assistants working in the medical field today.

Your education has the potential to be the most important decision you make in your life. Modern Technology School is here to get you started. 

MTS is accredited by the Department of Education, Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges; also accredited by the California Department of Public Health Radiological Health Branch.
