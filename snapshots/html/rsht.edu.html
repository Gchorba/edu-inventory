Mission/Philosophy

The mission of RSHT is to provide educational career programs which prepare students to succeed in selected professions. Students at RSHT are provided the opportunity to develop knowledge and skills; self-discipline and confidence; a professional attitude; and the ability to meet employer expectations in order to enter and advance in the job market. 

Providing a quality educational experience to each individual student is a priority at RSHT. The needs of the students remain in the forefront of the daily activities of each employee. The management of this school requires and expects that quality and prompt service will be provided to each student on a consistent basis.
