  • Dream Big
 
  • For over 60 years, SMCC has had a rich history of educating and preparing Maine people for jobs vital to Maine's economic prosperity. With nearly 7,500 students enrolled, SMCC is not only the largest, oldest and most diverse institution of the Maine Community College System, in 2009 it was also the fastest growing community college in New England.
  • SMCC offers more than a great education. From athletic teams and student organizations to campus activities and trips to Europe, SMCC's students enjoy an active and exciting college experience. The South Portland Campus is located on the beach overlooking Casco Bay and is just minutes from the hundreds of restaurants, galleries, professional sports venues, and nightspots in downtown Portland.
  • The Midcoast Campus at the former Brunswick Naval Air Station opened in 2011. It is a model for higher education and business partnerships preparing Maine people for high-demand, high growth occupations and focusing on composites, engineering, manufacturing, advanced energy technology, heavy equipment and health science.
  • SMCC offers over 40 degree and certificate programs at the most affordable tuition in New England that prepare students to begin an exciting career immediately upon graduation or allow them to continue their education at a four-year college or university.
  • Of the SMCC graduates who immediately begin careers after graduation, 93% secure employment and wages of graduates increase 73% in the first quarter after graduation. Approximately 44% of students continue on to a four-year college or university after graduation.
