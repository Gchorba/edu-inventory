Ohlone College Announcements and Upcoming Events

Summer/Fall 2012

  • Registration: Review Class Schedules, add classes to Preferred Sections List. Continuing students register starting April 26; New and former students start May 14.
  • State Mandated Fee Increase: California increased enrollment fees to $46/unit for all community colleges starting Summer 2012.

Ohlone Highlights

  • Freshman Days: Orientation for High School Seniors, Friday, May 11 or Saturday, May 12. Sign up in advance!
  • 45th Annual Graduation Ceremony, Friday, May 18 at 7:00pm, Epler Gymnasium (Building 9), Fremont campus. Congratulations Graduates!
