Central Valley Christian School


Welcome to Central Valley Christian School!

We hope you will call us today to take a tour
of our wonderful school.

Classes are divided into 4 classrooms...
Preschool, K-2, 3rd-5th, 6-8th.

We are a small school with a family feel that focuses on higher education.
We feel that multi-grade classrooms allow us to teach to kids where they are
and encourage them to stretch farther.

We are located on Hwy 34 - a convenient place for many who travel from Corvallis, Albany or Lebanon.

Click on 'Tuition' and see for yourself that our rates are comprable if not better than other private education.

Your child can't afford to NOT get a quality education for life!
