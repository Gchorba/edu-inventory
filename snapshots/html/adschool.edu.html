PORTFOLIO SCHOOL? ART SCHOOL? GRAPHIC DESIGN SCHOOL?

The Miami Ad School is not just an ad school; we're a network of educational options and cultural experiences in just about every major ad and design capital in the world. We give each student the chance to study advertising in up to five different cities, learning the trends and styles in each culture.

In addition to our advertising schools in Miami, San Francisco, Minneapolis, Hamburg, Madrid, Berlin and Sao Paulo, we have training programs for our students inside agencies such as CP+B, R/GA, Lowe, Dieste, Iris, PIC, Bureau Pindakaas, and Ogilvy - in cities like New York, London or Paris.

 

COPYWRITING

Copywriters brainstorm with art directors on TV commercials, web sites and anything digital. They write dialogue, create characters and stories. If creating the next video game for Burger King or TV commercial for Axe is what you'd like to do, take a look at our Copywriting courses

DIGITAL DESIGN

Digital designers mix color, ideas, motion, space, images, type and interactivity to create almost anything you can think of. So if creating a line of greeting cards for Starbucks, product designs for Joe Boxer, web site for Coke or blog for the gaming industry sounds interesting take a look at our Digital Graphic Design school program

ART DIRECTION

Art directors work in advertising agencies and digital companies. They brainstorm with copywriters to come up with ideas for all sorts of media. Then they collaborate with illustrators, directors, photographers or claymation artists to bring the ideas to life. So if directing a photo shoot for Skittles, creating a Super Bowl spot for Bud Lite or an online game for Volkswagen sounds interesting take a look at our Art Direction courses

GRADUATE MASTERS DEGREE

If you are interested in a graduate program, take a look at the Masters Degree in Art Direction and Copywriting (Masters Degree in Advertising), offered in conjunction with the Florida International University (FIU)

ACCOUNT PLANNING

Account planners use their strong analytical and communication skills to develop brand strategies. So if developing a plan to empower people to quit smoking, moderating focus groups for Clearasil and re-living those awkward, teenage years, hopping the red-eye to Mexico City to discover why twenty something men in bars order Dos Equis, embracing your inner nerd by conducting a quantitative survey on the modern definition of "Fun" for Ritz Crackers or harnessing your inner Jedi power to convince clients to buy daring campaigns sounds interesting (this is the kind of stuff our graduates do), take a look into the Account Planning school

MEDIA PLANNING

Have you noticed that everyone uses their cell phone as their real time piece? Our grads did and shifted Timex, a technology brand, to a fashion brand by linking the watch to preppy couture. They also created urban basketball tournaments for Reebok to make the brand more inner-city. If these jobs our grads have sound interesting take a look at the Communication Planning program
