Science & Technology Innovation Center

UNC’s Science & Technology Innovation Center (STIC) functions as an incubator for medical device businesses that draw on the academic strengths of the University. These strengths include expertise in biomaterials, biomechanics, biomedical engineering, optical and photonic engineering, systems engineering, tissue engineering, human health and medicine, and industrial biotechnology. learn more

Acupuncture Clinic Opening

The University of Northern California is opening a new Acupuncture Clinic in Petaluma. The Clinic’s Director, Dr. Y. King Liu, Ph.D., L.Ac., is a master acupuncturist with over 35 years of practitioner experience. Currently, the clinic specializes in the treatment of chronic pain in general and osteoarthritis of joints. learn more

Fully Funded Tuition Scholarships

UNC provides financial assistance that enables students to meet their tuition and living expenses.  Applicants who are admitted to the Biomedical Engineering PhD program receive full-tuition scholarships plus fellowships and living stipend.

About UNC

UNC is a highly selective graduate research institute that welcomes a small group of qualified students from around the world. UNC’s academic and research focus is on graduate programs in biomedical technology and engineering that emphasize both theory and practical application.
