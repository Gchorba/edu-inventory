Merrell University of Beauty Arts and Science is a post secondary school providing education in arts and sciences of cosmetology and esthetics.  The school is dedicated to “professional excellence” enabling our graduates to be highly competitive in the job market. Our Instructors are licensed by the Missouri State Board of Cosmetology and continuously keep abreast with the new trends and products
through educational seminars and workshops.

A skilled licensed cosmetologist or esthetician has a large selection of professional avenues for personal growth and success. The demand for hair stylists, platform artists, skin care specialists, sales representatives and sculptured nail technicians continues to grow each year. A recent study made by Clairol Company and released by the National Accrediting Commission of Cosmetology Arts and Sciences found that presently there is a need for 40,000 additional cosmetologists. Being an employee is only a portion of the job opportunities available in the beauty business. In our free enterprise system there are unlimited opportunities for management and ownership.

I am personally excited about having you join our fine student body. If you want to become a professional cosmetologist or esthetician, have visions for the future, and a strong desire to succeed, together we will work toward the accomplishment of those goals. Your success is limited only to your imagination and your willingness to work hard.
