Reduce, Reuse & Recycle With 'Leave it Behind'

From May 2-14, the residence halls on campus will be collecting items for the Leave it Behind program. School supplies, clothing, bikes and furniture items will be sorted and available to the community at the CSU Surplus tent sale June 2.

EnvironMentors Compete in National Science

Four students from CSU's EnvironMentors chapter will compete in the organization's 2012 National Science Fair in Washington, D.C. EnvironMentors is a national initiative connecting high school students with environmental science learning.

Renovated LSC Theatre Hosts Commencement Concert

The class of 2012 will get a special treat for graduation this spring - a performance by Trevor Hall, Post Paradise and Nathan Worthey in the newly renovated Lory Student Center Theatre.
