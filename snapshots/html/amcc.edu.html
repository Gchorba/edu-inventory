    Over 30 Years of Experience

    Anderson Medical Career College (AMCC) was first established in 1999 by founder Blanca E. Lozano. Ms. Lozano served the community for over 30 years as a Registered Nurse (RN) for Kaiser Permanente in Panorama City and Granada Hills Community Hospital in the San Fernando Valley area. Ms.Lozano opened Anderson College to give other individuals like herself the same opportunity by providing training in the Nursing field.

    Welcome to Anderson Medical Career College!

    Our mission is to provide the best, yet affordable hands-on training programs in the field of Nursing. Our goal is to see our students succeed in their chosen career path. We strive on perfection and hold the distinct honor of knowing that our students leave our campus well-prepared and fully-trained for a successful nursing career.
