Academy of Art University transforms aspiring students into professional artists and designers. Building a strong foundation, instructors at the art school guide students to cultivate their individual style and prepare them to do what they love for a living.
Learn More About Who We Are.

Recognized for its state-of-the-art facilities, Academy of Art University fosters innovation through a faculty of industry professionals. Together students, faculty and staff create a vibrant community within the art school, enriched by the progressive urban culture of San Francisco and more than 80 years of inspiration.
Learn More About What We Offer.

Open to everyone with a passion for art, a desire to grow and a drive to succeed, Academy of Art University is one of the top art schools in the nation. The Academy's art school programs guide students to achieve their creative potential and start professional art and design futures.

Areas of Study

More than a traditional art college, Academy of Art University offers accredited degrees, continuing art education and pre-college scholarship programs in 18 areas of art and design. Diverse art school programs offer incredible opportunities for collaborative work, emulating the environment of real-world companies.
Explore 18 areas of art and design.

Classes Online & in San Francisco

Offering classes online and in San Francisco, Academy of Art University sets the gold standard for flexible learning among art colleges. Students receive the same world-class preparation for their creative future regardless of how they complete art school courses. Dedication to hands-on, professional art programs makes Academy of Art University a top art school.
