Welcome to Sleep Studies Inc., Sleep School @ Rockdale, Allied Health Training Center

Sleep Studies Inc., Sleep School @ Rockdale, Allied Health Training Center is an institution of learning whose sole purpose is to provide our students with superior medical careers training skills. We strive to acquire a reputation of trust and reliability within the medical community, which will make our graduates the preferred choice of healthcare organizations nationwide.

Sleep Studies Inc., Sleep School @ Rockdale, Allied Health Training Center offers courses in clinical principles of sleep medicine and clinical polysomnography.
