Our Philosophy

Career College of California’s mission is to help job seekers and career changers build brighter futures. We believe that education is all about one thing: a single, individual student--you.

  • When you are here, you are not a number...you are a person.
  • Everything we do is designed to ensure that you have the opportunity to change your life in a positive way.
  • We will train you so you have the knowledge and skills for a career in a thriving, high-demand field.
  • When you succeed, we succeed.

Medical Careers

In our Medical Front Office with Billing & Coding program, you’ll learn the practical administrative and technical skills needed to help manage the day-to-day operations of everything from a small local dentist’s office to a major research hospital. You’ll learn the core skills that every healthcare organization needs to thrive.More Info

Business Careers

When you understand the fundamentals of Business Office Administration and have the skills and training necessary to help manage a businesses success and growth, you are a valuable asset anywhere you choose to go. Combined with our job placement expertise , this means a world of opportunities for you to explore!More Info

Legal Careers

America’s legal system is the most balanced, fair and sophisticated one the world has ever known. It is one of the cornerstones of what makes our country great.  If you’ve dreamed of being a part of it, of working alongside attorneys, judges, and law enforcement, then this is your opportunity.  Together, we’ll make it happen!More Info

Medical Assistant Career
