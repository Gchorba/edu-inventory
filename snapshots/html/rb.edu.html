Royal Beauty Careers is located in the prestigious Champions area in Northwest Houston at 5020 FM 1960 West, Suite A-12, Houston, Texas 77069. The school was approved by the Texas Cosmetology Commission in 1985, approved by the Texas Department of Health in 1999, and accredited by the Council on Occupational Education in 1994, and re-accredited in 1999. The school is designed to best accommodate three areas of instruction: Theory, Practical, and Clinical work for both day and night classes. All areas are fully equipped with furniture and fixtures that are required by the Texas Cosmetology Commission. more
