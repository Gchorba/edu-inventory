Alameda Beauty College

The Bay Area's Premier Beauty College & School Of Cosmetology

Alameda Beauty College is much more than a beauty school. ABC is the the Bay Area's leading school of cosmetology for people serious about obtaining the education necessary to advance a career in beauty.

Alameda Beauty College delivers group and one-on-one instruction by some of the beauty industry's most devoted and knowledgeable cosmetology instructors.

Get the knowledge, experience and expertise you need to launch and advance your professional career in the rapidly growing field of cosmetology.

As an accredited beauty school, Alameda Beauty College is able to help you, if you qualify, to access financial aid such as grants and low-interest government loans.

Courses are available for both part-time and full-time schedules.

Set in Alameda's attractive, busy and historical downtown area, public parking, shops, restaurants, public transportation and entertainment are all conveniently nearby.

Alameda Beauty College also serves nearby San Leandro, Oakland, Berkeley, Castro Valley, Hayward, Fremont, Richmond, and more. ABC is easily accessible by all forms of public transit: BART and AC Transit.
