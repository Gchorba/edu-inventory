Located in Seattle, Washington's Madison Valley, The Bush School is the city's oldest K-12 independent, coeducational day school offering rigorous academics along with robust programs in the arts, experiential education, and athletics. Small class sizes and our committed faculty make the educational experience at Bush one that stays with students forever.
