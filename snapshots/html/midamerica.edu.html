2012 Commencement

Mid-America's 2012 Commencement will be held on Thursday, May 10 at 7:30 pm. You are invited to the ceremony, which will be held at Lynwood United Reformed Church in Lynwood, IL. The speaker will be Rev. Craig Troxel. Seniors graduating are: Andrew DeYoung, Nathan Tomlinson, Robert Van der Woerd, and Ananda Jayawardene. Reception to follow.

Alumni Profile: Brian Allred

Brian Allred taught for nine years in the Department of Physiology and Health Science at Ball State University prior to attending Mid-America Reformed Seminary. Following his graduation in 2007, he taught Greek at the Seminary for two years. He has found his teaching experience was good preparation for the educational aspects of his current preaching ministry.

Summer Greek

This year's summer Greek classes will begin on July 5 and end on August 21. The classes will meet from 8:00 am to 11:20 am on Mondays, Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays, with exceptions of the first full week (July 9- 13) and the last full week (August 13-17), meeting on Mondays through Thursday with Fridays off.
