SU entrusted with late Nuncio's scholarship scheme 
The University has been entrusted with €30,000 to set up an endowment fund in honour of the Late Archbishop Clemente Faccani. An endowment fund is a type of an investment fund set up by an institution in which regular withdrawals from the invested capital are used. Returns from the fund will be given as scholarships.
