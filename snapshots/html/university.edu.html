CapStone is a U.S. non-profit university established to educate, equip and empower qualified underprivileged students around the world through high quality interactive business education at low cost. CapStone’s humanitarian mission continues to attract the finest faculty from top business schools to make a meaningful contribution to prepare our students for the global market.CapStone’s innovative low cost structure is made possible by two factors:
  • Volunteer efforts of our academic faculty from premier U.S. and European business schools who are passionate about our vision and mission;
  • Leveraging cost-effective state-of-the-art technology to provide a flexible program that is easily accessible to our students.
