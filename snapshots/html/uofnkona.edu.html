Awaken YWAM-DTS

A school that will change your life

The Awaken DTS this summer is the integration of all the very best things God has called the Discipleship Training School to be. Our prayer is that it will awaken hearts to the first commandment of loving God, overflowing into the second commandment of loving others... it’s a movement of people who are consumed with a passion to intimately know Jesus and translate His life into every setting they find themselves in.

The Awaken DTS will include key elements such as:

  • Learning to hear and obey God's voice through the Word of God and the inspiration of the Holy Spirit.
  • Understanding the world we live in and the importance of worship, intercessory prayer, community and spiritual warfare.
  • Discovering callings and gifts.

The M1 arts & media event was amazing... but it's not over yet!

PDF | Print | E-mail

Share |

The M1 Event stretched our Ohana Court beyond capacity with the seating area completely filled and the areas above and around the court stacked with 3 rows or more of onlookers.  The warm Kona night air was filled with the sounds of laughter, singers, violins, rappers, dancers' footsteps, drums, prayers and cheers... as well as the smells of flower leis, burning fire-knife fuel and wet paint. Our YWAM Kona ohana (family) felt our hearts filled with new passion, joy, wonder, determination and faith.   

      

The Hawaiian word Lokahi means unity expressed in harmony - a fitting name for these incredible facilities that will house the numerous present and future arts & media ministries at YWAM Kona. Artists poured out their offerings of praise and shared words of faith as the vision of the new "LŌKAHI Studios" was revealed... see the  presentation video & artist interviews here:   

 

 

LŌKAHI Studios' vision is to facilitate, empower and train a community of artists, technicians and storytellers for reformation in the spheres of influence.  read more about LŌKAHI Studios (pdf)   

  

OUR GOAL: to raise one million dollars to fund the materials for studio A - the first phase of the 4.9 million dollar multi-purpose facility Lokahi Studios. Donated professional labor and materials have already brought the cost of this incredible facility down by $2.5 million.

At the March 1 event we were able to raise approximately $250,000.00.  Would you consider giving a gift for LŌKAHI Studios to help us reach the one million dollar goal?

Regardless of the amount of your gift we would like to say thank you by giving you access to:  ywamboutique.com to receive a gift from us.


You too can still participate by: donating online here  or donate by text here
