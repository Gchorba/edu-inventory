  • A Legacy of Proven Leadership
    For over 40 years, AIM has continuously produced outstanding leaders who run companies and governments around the world. By continuously adapting and evolving its management programs to higher levels of excellence, AIM has educated and shaped men and women to do more than respond to rapid global changes; they create the foundations for a strong, dynamic future.
  • Master in Business Administration (MBA)
    Everything you need to be a globally-competitive professional. Emphasis is placed on strong core business management skills and competence in both Western and Asian management styles. Students have the option to major in finance, marketing or entrepreneurship
  • Master in Management (MM)
    Learn to develop multiple strategy skills and leadership perspectives. A must for executives seeking more mature leadership skills, greater responsibilities and higher management positions.
  • Master in Development Management (MDM)
    Train to be an agent of change. Be part of a select group of leaders whose far-reaching policies and programs build sustainable institutions that can have maximum, positive impact on developing societies.
  • Executive MBA (EMBA)
    You never stop, even when you’re at the top. Sharpen your entrepreneurial skills and learn to see opportunities all over Asia and around the world. This unique program is a three-way learning partnership between the AIM faculty, the students and their organizations.
  • Centers of Excellence
    The Centers of Excellence are AIM’s research and advocacy facilities that expand the field of knowledge on social development, responsible corporate governance, and the management of nonprofit organizations.
  • ADB- AIM Knowledge Hub
    Be fully knowledgeable about ASEAN economic development and regional integration efforts. This program is a collaboration between the Asian Development Bank and the Asian Institute of Management.
  • AIM Team Wins Business Plan Competition
    An AIM MBA team beat contingents from 10 prestigious business schools to become the regional champions of the 3rd Annual Hult Global Case Challenge (Hult GCC) held in Shanghai on February 25, 2012.
