California Healing Arts College is a Los Angeles Massage school and Personal Training school located in West Los Angeles, California, two blocks from Santa Monica and only ten minutes drive to the Pacific ocean and Santa Monica pier. This Los Angeles based massage therapy and personal fitness training school was founded in 1988 by Dr. Paul Schwinghamer, a chiropractor and educator devoted to massage therapy training, and to expanding general public awareness of therapeutic massage and personal training. In 1999, the school was listed in the popular book "Massage for Dummies" as one of the top ten massage therapy schools in the United States. In 2011, California Healing Arts College became the first ACCSC member accredited school in Californi to offer a 900 hour Personal Fitness Trainer program based upon the National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM) curriculum.

Our philosophy at California Healing Arts College is that learning is an enjoyable and mentally stimulating process where students and teachers work closely together and benefit alike. Our purpose is to promote Massage Therapy and Personal Fitness Training as non-invasive forms of healing to complement traditional medicine and alternative healing arts. Our focus, therefore, is both scientific and intuitive, with emphasis upon the practical consideration of gaining employment upon graduation.

Federal Financial Aid is Available to Students who Qualify.
California Healing Arts College is an ACCSC accredited institution, eligible to participate in Title IV Federal Financial Assistance programs, including Federal Pell Grants and Federal Stafford and PLUS loans. As a Los Angeles Massage School dedicated to quality Massage Therapy Programs since 1988 and Personal Fitness Training since 2011. Our most popular Massage Therapy program is our Massage Therapist - Master CN650 program, which includes Swedish Massage, Oscillation Massage, Shiatsu Massage, Deep Tissue Massage, Jade Stone Therapy Massage, In-Office Chair Massage and Foot Reflexology Massage, as well as anatomy and physiology training and extensive practical experience working on massage therapy clients in a live clinical setting.
