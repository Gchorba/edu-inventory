Dubai Pharmacy College 
Allied health professionals of outstanding quality are vital for holistic healthcare . With this in view Dubai Pharmacy College was established in 1992 by the astute vision of Founder and Chairman , Haji Saeed Al Lootah. The first Pharmacy College in the Gulf region exclusively dedicated to educate aspiring Muslim girls in the field of pharmacy sciences, is accorded recognition by Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research to award First Degree in Pharmacy vide its decision No:151 of 1998.
