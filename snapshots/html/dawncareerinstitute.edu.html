Begin your journey toward a brighter future right here with our quality career education.

 

At Dawn Career Institute, in Wilmington, Delaware, and through our adult education online programs, we specialize in preparing you for a new career, or advancement in the allied health, wellness and business fields.
 

With over 30 years of experience in instructing adult learners, Dawn Career Institute, formerly known as Dawn Training Centre, understands your priorities.

We know you want to gain the specialized knowledge and skills to prepare for a new career quickly and conveniently. As a trusted adult education school in Delaware, our day and evening classes and online career education programs can offer you the freedom to fit education into your busy schedule. You can pick the time frame and the format your program is offered in.

And, our applied theory plus hands-on training can give you skills to achieve excellence in the workplace. We strive to provide career-specific programs, which means you won’t have to take any unnecessary courses and you can focus strictly on the knowledge and skills that you can apply to your future career.  Choose from our career education programs in the allied health, business and wellness fields, or work toward gaining the advanced skills you need in our continuing education or certification classes.

 

At Dawn Career Institute, we not only help you choose the career education that is perfect for your personal growth, we support you at every step along the journey.

Our adult education programs are led by friendly staff who stand ready to answer any question you have. And as a student, you’ll discover that our qualified instructors are your resource to help you succeed in your classes and find the satisfaction that comes with using your knowledge in an enjoyable career.

As a student at Dawn Career Institute, you can also enjoy our job placement assistance, which can help you make connections with local employers. We offer a job board, resume reviews, interview coaching and other career search training to help you have a competitive edge when you step into today’s job market. 

Located in Wilmington, Del., Dawn Career Institute is proud to serve students in the tri-state area.  Our campus offers a convenient drive from Dover Air Force Base; Atlantic City, N.J.; Baltimore, Md.; and Philadelphia and Lancaster, Penn.

We walk through each step of the admissions process with you and offer on-site financial aid available to those who qualify.  We participate in a wide variety of financial aid programs that you may qualify for, including grants, private scholarships, loans and veterans benefits.  Our financial aid representatives will work to see which options you qualify for and may benefit from. 

 

Dawn Career Institute is an adult education school committed to helping you reach your professional goals.

Ready to make a career change?  We’re ready to help!  Enrolling is simple at Dawn Career Institute.  Don’t let the opportunity to work toward a new career pass you by! Learn more today.
