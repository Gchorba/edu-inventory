An Opportunity to Excel

Trinity College of Nursing & Health Sciences has highly respected Bachelor and Associate degree programs that focus on the individual student. Through coursework and clinical experiences students learn the critical thinking and practice skills necessary to function as entry level health care professionals. The College is fully accredited by the Higher Learning Commission of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools, and each program is accredited by its professional accrediting organization.

Life-like Settings. Life-like Community.

As a student at Trinity College of Nursing & Health Sciences, you will be part of a select group of students who is pursuing a career in the health professions. You will have access to the tools and resources necessary to achieve your goals. The faculty and staff at the college look forward to assisting you down your educational path.

The Success of Our Students Begins With You.

As highly qualified and trained professionals, you bring a level of knowledge and expertise to our programs that elevates them to our high academic standards.
