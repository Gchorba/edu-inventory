WELCOME TO ANTONELLI INSTITUTE

Welcome to Antonelli Institute, where our mission is to prepare motivated students for entry-level positions leading to careers in the photographic and graphic design professions. Since 1938, Antonelli’s distinct, accelerated visual arts programs have combined small classes, interactive lecture, and hands-on learning.

Antonelli Institute is accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges.

Learn more about how you can use your creativity in a career by exploring our applied associate degree programs in photography and graphic design.

Visit on-line student galleries for photography and graphic design.

Register for an upcoming workshop for high school students or visit the gallery for the Spring 2010 High School Contest winners.

We encourage you to visit us on our 15-acre campus in a quiet suburb of Philadelphia.
