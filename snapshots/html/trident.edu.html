Accredited Online University

Trident University International is a 100% online university accredited by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC), which is one of six regional accrediting associations in the United States. Accredited online universities offer flexible distance learning and cutting edge military university programs, including: Military Degrees, Bachelor Degree, Masters Degree, MBA, PhD online.

 

Gainful Employment Disclosure

For more about Trident University's programs, including the graduation rates for each program, median debt of students who completed each program, and other important information, please click here. In order to help students make informed decisions about academic choices, Trident University provides this information for all of its programs.

 

Online Degree Programs

At Trident University, online degrees are designed with a commitment to help you achieve your college, career, or military education goals. Our online university faculty provides you with the high quality online degrees and individual support to help enhance your academic experience. Over 90% of our faculty have doctoral degrees.

 

Academic Online Colleges

Trident University offers distinguished online colleges and military education including PhD, Bachelors, MBA, Masters Degree. Online education tools are provided to help you succeed regardless of location, a hallmark of our military university programs. Online degrees are supported by our university online faculty and staff to help each step of the way toward earning online degrees. TUI provides a unique opportunity for accredited university degrees in Business Administration, Health Sciences, Information Systems, or Education.

 

University Online Flexibility

Online universities, military colleges, and degree programs from online colleges give you broad flexibility to complete the university courses based on your schedule. Accredited online universities can provide an ideal academic opportunity for working professionals, members of the military and their families, and others who need the flexibility offered by online college courses. Choosing a university degree online can save you both time and money.

 

Educational Programs Designed For Those in the Military

An online university degree at TUI is designed for active duty military personnel and their families seeking a degree from an accredited online university that offers undergraduate and graduate degrees online in a wide range of professional disciplines. Trident University International is a leader among online universities, providing education and career advancement tools for your military career or for a new professional career.

 

Publicity Statement
