Creative. Connected. Personal.

Welcome to the public liberal arts university of the West. SOU offers a campus experience you won't find anywhere else.

Here you'll connect with faculty, fellow students, and the Rogue Valley community. Our small classes and creative approaches prepare you for success. Rich cultural resources and glorious surroundings provide inspiration beyond the classroom.

If you're searching for a creative, personalized approach to learning, SOU may be the choice for you. Learn more.
