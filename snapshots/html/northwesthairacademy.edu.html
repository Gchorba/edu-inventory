The Northwest Hair Academy is committed to providing you with an experience as much as an education.

 

You will find in each of our locations a culture and curriculum that is designed to support and motivate you to study, develop your creative skills, practice and gain the confidence you desire and will need for a successful career. A career that is really about helping people feel better about themselves.

 

These experiences are designed to inspire you to explore your passion and creativity, and help you learn the business that will make your career in the beauty industry fun and rewarding.

 

We are dedicated to curriculum updates that will expand on the student's abilities to develop a 'creative thought process' in respect to cutting, coloring, texture and styling hair.  While all Future Professionals must learn a strong foundation in these skills, it is the creative and artistic opportunities that draw most people into our profession.

 

Prospective employers have recognized the Northwest Hair Academy's commitment to quality and our reputation for innovation and excellence as evidenced by the success of our graduates such as William Blair.

 

At the Northwest Hair Academy our Future Professionals are our most important clients.
