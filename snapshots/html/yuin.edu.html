FROM THE DESK OF PRESIDENT
PRESIDENT’S MESSAGE

As the book of Galatians Chapter 4 Verse 9 said “Let us not become weary in doing good, for at the proper time we will reap a harvest if we do not give up.” (NIV), there are God’s appointed times for sowing, working and reaping. Our Yuin University is no exception. Our founding President Dr. Henry Yu, gave all of his past thirty-five years for Yuin University. He then graciously and humbly passed the leadership baton to me as the new President. As the recipient of his most gracious and generous act, I am humbled by his pure act of generosity and love for the family of Yuin University.

When Yuin University has recently gone through some stinging trials and hardships. Thanks to those experience, Yuin University has become stronger and mature as an educational institution. It is now high time to reveal its true value as one of very few comprehensive graduate and post graduate institution in our ethnic communities. It also has the untapped pool of alumni from its near four decade existence.
As we look forward to achieving our near and long term goals for our Yuin University, we should count all those recent trials and hardships as blessings that will propel us to go farther in improving the lives of those in our communities through academic excellence and acts of love to those in need.

Sincerely,
Andrew K. Kim, J.D.
President
