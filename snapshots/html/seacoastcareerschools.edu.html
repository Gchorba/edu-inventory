Welcome to Seacoast Career Schools, a leader in career-focused education.

Is a career-focused education right for you? If you’re ready to begin a new, high-growth career sooner rather than later, your education from Seacoast Career Schools is the best place to begin. programs in:

• Health Claims Specialist
• Massage Therapy
• Dental Assistant (Manchester, NH)
