Events and Announcements

  • Kindergarten Parent Coffee

    On Wednesday, May 9, from 8:00 to 8:30 a.m. there will be a World Language Coffee in the Dodge Writing Center for parents of Kindergarten students.

  • Grade Two Hoedown on Wednesday

    The annual Grade Two Hoedown will take place from 8:30 to 9:15 a.m. in front courtyard, weather permitting. Festivities will move indoors to the School Meeting House if the weather is bad.

  • Two days of Special Event Dress

    Students should wear Special Event Dress on Thursday (for the Declamation Contest) and on Friday (for Grandparents Day) this week.

  • 2012 Harold W. Wise Declamation Contest

    The Declamation Contest, a long-running, well-loved tradition at Broowkood, will be held on Thursday, May 10, at 9:15 a.m. in the School Meeting House.

  • Grandparents' Reception

    The annual Grandparents' Reception will be held on Thursday, May 10 at 6:30 p.m.

  • Grandparents Day on Friday

    In honor of Grandparents' Day there will be a special School Meeting on Friday, May 11 at 11:00 a.m.

  • Noon Dismissal on Friday

    One Friday, May 11, there will be a noon dismissal for all students. There will be no afternoon bus service, no REACH or After School Programs, and no afternoon music or combo group lessons.
