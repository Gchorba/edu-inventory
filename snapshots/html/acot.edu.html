Accreditation and Recognition

  • ACOT is an Accredited Member of the (DETC) Distance Education and Training Council. DETC is recognized by the Council for Higher Education Accreditation and the U.S. Department of Education.
  • ACOT is recognized by the United States Department of Education.
  • ACOT is approved to operate by the Missouri Department of Higher Education.

Voted Military Friendly

  • ACOT is an Approved Provider for the United States Department of Defense, and is a Partner Education Institution in cooperation with the new DOD MOU.
  • ACOT accepts all chapters of the GI BILL.
  • Active Duty, Veterans, Spouses and Dependents can take advantage of Military Friendly Tuition rates and may qualify for discounts and scholarships.
  • ACOT was voted Military Friendly by GI JOBS.com in 2010, 2011 and 2012.
