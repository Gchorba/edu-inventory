What Does It Mean To Be Human in the 21st Century?

Explore the possibilities while earning your masters or doctoral degrees in leadership, organizational systems, psychology, human science or mind-body medicine at one of our three graduate colleges.

Saybrook is a not-for-profit, accredited graduate school.
Take advantage of our:

  • scholarship and financial aid programs
  • flexible schedules for working professionals
  • hybrid online and residential courses
  • 40 years of academic tradition

If you’re a self-directed, independent thinker, you’ll enjoy our stimulating community of 600 learners committed to practicing humanistic values. Choose the graduate school that’s best for you.

  • LIOS Graduate College
    Enhance your skills as a counselor, coach, or consultant. Innovative masters degrees in psychology, systems counseling, or leadership & organization development. Seattle-based residential program.
  • College of Psychology and Humanistic Studies
    Advance or start your career in management, teaching, research, or clinical practice with a low-residency graduate degree in psychology, organizational systems, or human science.
  • College of Mind-Body Medicine
    Pursue professional training in “the new medicine” to enrich your career as a medical practitioner, educator, researcher, or administrator. Help transform healthcare systems in the 21st century.
