FEES

Once you have accepted an offer, paying your fees is the next step to becoming a Sheridan student. Your fee invoice indicates your amount owing and your payment due date. It also includes an itemized list of your charges and your payment options. View your online fee invoice through myStudent Centre. For more information, visit the links below.

Faculties

Faculty of Animation, Arts and Design

Create your future here

Sheridan Faculty of Animation, Arts and Design is recognized as a top-choice destination among creative arts colleges. Sheridan is world-renowned as an animation school and has a stellar reputation broadly within the creative industries. Explore our  art, media, communications or design college programs  to discover which is right for your talents.
EXPLORE

Faculty of Applied Health and Community Studies

Make a difference while you build your career

Sheridan's Faculty of Applied Health and Community Studies excels in preparing people for community service sectors such as health care, nursing, education, social services, child and youth care, public safety and security. Browse our programs and find out how a social services college education can change your future - and the future of your community.
EXPLORE

Faculty of Applied Science and Technology

Practical skills for a rewarding career

The Faculty of Applied Science and Technology offers specialized education in nearly 30 programs, including computer science, applied computing, skilled trades, and the engineering sciences. Be prepared for the excellent and rapidly increasing job opportunities that exist in the emerging technology sectors. Browse the list of programs below to find the one that's right for you.
EXPLORE

Faculty of Business

Put your business career on the fast-track

Graduates from Sheridan Faculty of Business can look forward to outstanding job opportunities, satisfied employers and career success.   Our business school students tackle real-world challenges and develop job-specific skills, gaining a competitive advantage in the employment market. Leading edge in its design, services and programming, our Hazel McCallion Campus brings Business to Mississauga and reflects Sheridan’s Creative Campus concept.
EXPLORE

Faculty of Liberal Arts and Access

Programs and Resources for Immigrants and Newcomers

Sheridan's Faculty of Liberal Arts and Access offers a variety of programs and resources for Canadian newcomers that are specific to immigrant education and job placement.  Our experienced staff can help get you started on the right path, so that you can become successful in the shortest time possible.
