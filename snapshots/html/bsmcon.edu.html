The Bon Secours Relationship

Welcome to the Bon Secours Memorial College of Nursing! Bon Secours offers a unique educational experience for the student seeking to become a Registered Nurse. Rooted in the tradition of the Sisters of Bon Secours who founded our organization in the 1800s, the College, by educating tomorrow's nurses, extends the Mission of providing "good help" to those in need. In fact, "Bon Secours" is French for "good help."
