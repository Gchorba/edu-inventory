Bringing Learning to Life

Alumna Angela Hawse (2001) uses her education to achieve her wildest dreams. She was recently awarded 2011 Guide of the Year by the American Mountain Guide Association (AMGA), as the second woman in history to hold this honor. Angela is also the sixth woman in the U.S. to be certified by the International Federation of Mountain Guides Association.

A Passion for Conservation

If you would have asked Abram Fleishman (2008) what he wanted to do with his life when he first came to Prescott College, he would have said something about climbing mountains and skiing powder. Now, after extensive research at the Prescott College Kino Bay Center, Abram proclaims: "Prescott College introduced me to the ocean in a formal way and changed my path forever."

New Green Living on Campus

Prescott College is walking the talk with constructing the new sustainable housing for new residential students. Opening fall 2012, Prescott College Campus Village Apartments will be home to new freshmen and is designed to create a true living-learning community complete with the latest in green architecture and technology.
