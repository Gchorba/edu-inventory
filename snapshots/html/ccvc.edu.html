  • Ultrasound Technologist

    • Graduate as a Ultrasound Technologist:

    Use special equipment to direct high frequency sound waves into areas of the patient's body. Sonographers/Ultrasound Techs operate the equipment that collects reflected echoes and forms an image that may be videotaped, transmitted or photographed for interpretation and diagnosis by a Physician.

  • Medical Assistant

    • Graduate as a Medical Assistant:

    This is a healthcare specialty that offers a set of unique challenges while assisting the Physician in examinations, diagnostic care and in minor surgery. You will help patients ranging from newborns to senior citizens. Clinical duties commonly include: taking and recording vital signs and medical histories; explaining treatment procedures to patients, preparing patients for examination and assisting during the examination.

  • Dental Assistant

    • Graduate as a Dental Assistant:

    Most Dental Assistants work in private dental offices. Others work in dental schools, hospital dental departments and in state and local public health departments. They usually work near the dental chair and arrange instruments and assists with the dentist's needs.

  • Administration Billing & Coding

    • Graduate as a Administration Billing & Coding Specialist:

    These specialists work in pleasant and comfortable offices. The efficiency of a Medical Biller & Coder is vital to the healthcare industry as they perform procedural & diagnostic coding and determine insurance claim reimbursements.

  • MRI Technologist

    • Graduate as a MRI Technologist:

    Magnetic Resonance Imaging Technicians, or MRI Techs, use an MRI Machine to get an internal look at both hard and soft tissues within the human body. The machine makes use of a giant magnet and radio waves to generate internal images of these tissues. MRI Techs work in hospitals, imaging centers, and urgent care facilities.
