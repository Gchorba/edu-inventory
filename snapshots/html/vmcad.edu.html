VIRGINIA MARTI COLLEGE OF ART AND DESIGN

Virginia Marti College of Art and Design, located in Lakewood, Ohio on Cleveland’s west side border, is an art and design college with a big difference. We’re an art entrepreneurship school where ambitious students get to learn the business of art from professionals in the field. VMCAD is also committed to providing academic excellence for students who want to specialize in selected areas of the design industry for successful artistic careers. We specialize in:

  • Providing nationally accredited art and design degree programs
  • Empowering students with practical business and design knowledge & skills
  • Hands-on experience with detailed instructor assistance
  • Instructors and staff who are involved & committed to your success
  • Art & design degree programs that are coordinated to accommodate family & work schedules
  • Employment networking and internships

HOW WE CAN HELP MAKE YOUR CREATIVE DREAMS A REALITY

As an art and design school, Virginia Marti is one of the regional leaders in entrepreneurial education, featuring practical, hands-on learning through our professional networking organization and our experiential Internship program.

VMCAD students learn in the classroom, AND learn through work internships in their field of interest. Our faculty is comprised of practicing professionals from the art & design industries, strongly committed to developing the talent and technical design ability of our students in all five program areas.
