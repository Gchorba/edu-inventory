Rolling Registration + Orientation

RETURNING STUDENTS:

Summer and Fall Registration is open. However, summer registration will be shut down from April 30 at 4:30 p.m. until May 8 at 8:00 a.m. for end of term processing and billing. Fall registration will remain open during this time. Summer classes start May 14. Fall registration will stay open until Friday, August 24. Fall classes start August 25.

To register for classes go to myKirtland, log in with your student ID and password, and click on the Student tab. Make sure you select the semester you're interested in searching.

NEW STUDENTS:

All new students to Kirtland are required to sign up for a day long Orientation/Advising/Registration Day (OAR) to ensure a smooth transition into college. You will register for classes during your OAR.

Please read more about OAR Day and sign up for an OAR session so you'll be ready for summer/fall registration.

KIRTLAND REGIONAL POLICE ACADEMY HONORS GRADUATING CLASS (May 2)
Kirtland Regional Police Academy will honor its thirty-fourth graduating class with ceremonies in the Performing Arts Center at Kirtland Community College on Friday, May 4, 2012. Graduation begins at 11 a.m.; a reception will follow in the Student Center.

Kirtland Student Receives Roscommon DDA Award (April 24)
Kirtland Community College graphic arts student Rose White has been selected by the Roscommon Downtown Development Authority for her winning design submission in a spring-themed streetlamp banner contest. The Roscommon DDA, under the direction of Jim Anderson, approached Kirtland in February and collaborated to sponsor a design contest with a prize of $100 for the winning entry. White's design, which depicts a Kirtland's Warbler on a pine branch, will be printed on banners and will hang from lamps lining the streets of downtown Roscommon.

Kirtland Holds 43rd Commencement Ceremony (April 20)
Kirtland Community College will honor 140 graduates at its 43rd commencement ceremony on Friday, April 27 at 7 p.m. Ferris State University President Dr. David L. Eisler will deliver the commencement address to the Class of 2012, and former Kirtland Community College graduate Carlee Allen will be recognized as Outstanding Alumna.

M-TEC at Kirtland-Gaylord Partners to Offer Welding Training (April 18)
The M-TEC at Kirtland-Gaylord will partner with Petoskey Michigan Works! and North Central Michigan College to offer high-demand welding training to eligible individuals using Kirtland's Mobile Welding Lab. This training program is organized by the Northwest Michigan Council of Governments and will give people an opportunity to receive free training in gas metal arc welding (GMAW), specifically metal inert gas (MIG) welding.

Kirtland to Host Various Art Exhibitions (April 12)
Kirtland Community College's Art Department will host its annual Winter Semester Student Art Exhibit/Reception from 6-8 p.m. on Thursday, April 26 at the Art Building on the main campus near Roscommon. Collectively, the works of more than 300 art students from classes including animation, ceramics, computer generated images, drawing, design, illustration, photography, sculpture, painting, digital communications, studio graphics and portfolio will be featured. In addition, local residents are encouraged to watch coverage of Kirtland's Student Art exhibit featured on TV 9&10 and Fox 33's Michigan This Morning segments between 6-9 a.m. on Thursday, April 26 with reporter/journalist Alex Jokich.
