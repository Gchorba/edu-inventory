What we do

Bellingham Beauty School provides a comprehensive education with an emphasis on hair design, hair coloring, esthetics, and manicuring. With our program you will attain the necessary skills to step into the hair industry with confidence.

What you should do

Come to Bellingham Beauty School where you can receive the finest hands-on training available. With our world class training you will be able to build a foundation for a career to last you a lifetime. Cosmetology can take you anywhere!

Be Prepared

Q: What kind of qualifications do I need to start Cosmetology School?

A: Bellingham Beauty School requires a GED or High School diploma. You must also present two letters of recommendation from individuals who believes in your success as a student and in the beauty industry. A student should be committed to their education, a positive attitude, enjoy working for people, and believe in practice makes perfect.

Our Philosophy

"Every decision we make about this School; from setting the Student Dress Code, to inviting visiting Guest Artists; from selecting Coaches who'll help prepare Students for Competition, to providing Wi-Fi access for Students' laptop computers - whether it be a rule, a class, an activity, or a convenience - absolutely everything exists only if we can answer "Yes" to either of these two questions:.

1) Will it help the Student Pass the WA State Examinations? or,.

2) Will it help the Student become successful as a working Cosmetologist?
