How do you know if you're an Auggie?

If something is exciting, invigorating, and completely unexpected, you want to be right in the middle of it. For you, the best way to learn about something is to do it -- in the classroom, on the playing field, on the theater stage, and out in the community.

>> If this describes you, you might be an Auggie! <<

Meeting the needs of adult students

For more than 25 years, Augsburg College has been offering weekend and evening degree programs designed to meet the needs of adult students. Today we offer 18 majors including business, communication studies, education, and nursing (bachelor's completion) on our Minneapolis and Rochester campuses. See how we can make getting your degree possible.

>> Get your degree at Augsburg <<

A degree. And so much more.

Augsburg offers graduate degrees in business, education, social work, leadership, nursing, and physician assistant studies. Classes are offered on the Minneapolis main campus, in downtown Minneapolis, and in Rochester. Learn from faculty with years of practical experience in their fields and side-by-side with other students who will become colleagues and friends. It's all part of an outstanding Augsburg graduate education.

Educational Excellence

An Augsburg education prepares students to take on the challenges of our world. Through their experiences in the classroom, in the city, and abroad, they become informed citizens, thoughtful stewards, responsible leaders, and critical thinkers. It's all part of the outstanding education Augsburg provides to learners of all ages.

AUGGIES PLAY TO WIN

Being an Auggie athlete means success both on and off the field. When Auggies aren’t setting school records or winning sportsmanship awards, they’re busily racking up academic honors. And between classes and practice, they’re out serving as mentors to young athletes or lending a hand in the community. Wherever they are, you can bet they are active and involved.

>> Be an Auggie <<

Auggies love life in the city

As a college in the city, Augsburg is a great place to learn and explore. Student organizations provide opportunities for you to share your talents and interests. Get involved in environmental issues, exercise your talent as a journalist, deepen your faith, or play a little competitive ultimate Frisbee. And our location in the heart of the city gives you access to sports and entertainment as well as internships and important career connections.

ALUMNI & PARENTS

Whether you're an alumnus, a parent, or both, you want to know what is happening on campus. Find a way to share your gifts, talent, and time by volunteering. Log-in to NetCommunity and share your story. Or find an event to attend such as Advent Vespers or athletic events. And don't forget Homecoming! There are so many ways to be involved and to stay connected to life at Augsburg!

IN THE HEART OF THE CITY

When you come to Augsburg, you’ll be right in the middle of everything. Our campus is set in a vibrant neighborhood just minutes from downtown Minneapolis. There you can find internships, museums, theaters, shopping, and sporting events. Or you can take the light rail train south from campus to the world-famous Mall of America, beautiful Minnehaha Falls, or the Minneapolis-St. Paul International airport.
