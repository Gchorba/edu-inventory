Military Students

Military Students

As one of the top 15% military-friendly institutions in the United States, ACHS is here when you need us.

With ACHS, you can:

  • Maximize your education benefits
  • Get the 24/7 support you need
  • Access dynamic online classrooms
  • Use cutting-edge technology for a stimulating, interactive learning experience.
  • Achieve your educational goals
  • Become a leader in your chosen field

From active duty military preparing herbal remedies over a camp stove in Afghanistan to military spouses providing wellness coaching to base communities, ACHS’s holistic health training is serving U.S. servicemembers, spouses and veterans worldwide.
