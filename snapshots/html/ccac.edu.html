First Summer 2012 Tuition Payment Due Date May 9

Students who registered for first summer 2012 credit classes between March 15 and May 9, please be reminded that payment in full is required by Wednesday, May 9 to avoid being deregistered from classes. Keep your preferred schedule and finalize tuition payments via CCAC Central e-Services or via cash or check at the campus of your choice. Learn more.


Visiting Students: Earn Summer
Savings, Credits at CCAC

With tuition of $95.50 for Allegheny County residents and reasonable tuition for others, hundreds of students home for the summer take one or more CCAC credit courses to transfer back to their universities. Benefits include small class sizes, cost savings and access to courses or class times not available during the year. Learn more, view tuition and fees and search for classes.


Attend the Big Read Closing Ceremony

Join us for the last of a series of outreach events designed to promote literacy, reading and open dialogue within our community. The Big Read closing ceremony will be held Sunday, May 6, 2012 at 3:00 p.m. at the August Wilson Center for African American Culture and will feature student contest winners, documentary film viewing, presentations and the Pittsburgh Gospel Choir.
