News

Methodist College of Nursing is now Methodist College.  The announcement was made on April 4, 2012.  To see pictures from the event click here.

 

Apply now!

Qualified nurses are in high demand all across the country. At Methodist College, we're dedicated to providing students with the knowledge and experience they need to take advantage of that demand.

Our curriculum combines quality educational programs with real-world clinical experience to provide students with a comprehensive health care education. We are accredited by the Higher Learning Commission, North Central Association, the National League of Nursing Accrediting Commission, Inc. (NLNAC) and CCNE.
