Fast Facts

Celebrating 65 years of transforming lives
Rated 16th Fastest Growing Campus in 2011 Chronicle of Higher Ed Almanac
Admitted to the President’s National Community Service Honor Roll
Career-oriented, co-ed liberal arts college
Distinguished faculty and small class sizes
Seventeen NCAA Division III varsity sports
A Fifth Year Option leading to a master's degree
Study abroad programs and a wide range of internship opportunities
Fully accredited by the New England Association of Schools and Colleges
192-acre campus located in the heart of New England
