Lake Land College Alumni Association selling cookbooks for scholarship fund

The Lake Land College Alumni Association is selling cookbooks in order to raise funds for the Alumni Association Scholarship. The cookbook is a collection of recipes compiled by the Lake Land College Alumni Association from alumni and friends of alumni.  All proceeds will support the scholarship which is awarded to a family member of a Lake Land College alumnus.

Lake Land College’s Alumni Association inducts four into the 2012 Distinguished Alumni Society

Lake Land College recently inducted the Distinguished Alumni Society (DAS) Class of 2012.  The inductees were Dave Storm (class of 1975) of Effingham; R. Rush Record (class of 1987) of Champaign; Julie Ann Janssen (class of 1976) of Springfield; and Tracie Goodnight-Egger (class of 1992) of Delphi, Ind. The Distinguished Alumni Society recognizes alumni who have utilized the Lake Land experience to make an imprint on his or her profession or community.

LLC student named 2012 Coca-Cola Community College Academic Team Silver Scholar

Michelle Clark, Lake Land College sophomore from Lovington, has been named a 2012 Coca-Cola Community College Academic Team Silver Scholar.  The Coca-Cola Scholars Foundation sponsors the Coca-Cola Community College Academic Team program by recognizing 50 Gold, 50 Silver and 50 Bronze Scholars, and providing nearly $187,500 in scholarships annually. Silver Scholars each receive a $1,250 scholarship and a special medallion.
