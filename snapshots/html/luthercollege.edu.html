Luther College

Welcome to Luther College in Regina, Saskatchewan, Canada.

Luther College operates a historical, independent high school and a liberal arts university campus that is federated with the University of Regina.

HIGH SCHOOL

Luther College's high school campus offers a quality education in a Christian context.

Luther College high school places a strong emphasis on academics and welcomes students from Regina, other parts of Canada, and around the world.

UNIVERSITY

Luther College at the University of Regina offers a liberal arts education that centres on individual attention, teaching excellence, and a global perspective in learning.

In addition, the campus features a residence that is open to all post-secondary students in Regina.
