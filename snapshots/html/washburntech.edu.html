Welcome to Washburn Institute of Technology!

  

At Washburn Institute of Technology, we are committed to helping high school, adult students and business/industry students develop the skills and knowledge to pursue many opportunities in today's career fields, including building and industrial technology, business technology, design technology, electronic technology, computer repair and networking, graphics technology, health occupations, mechanical technology and metal technology.

Washburn Tech is affiliated with Washburn University and is accredited or approved by Higher Learning Commission: A Commission of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools.  Washburn Tech is also accredited by the National League for Nursing Accrediting Commission, contact: 3343 Peachtree Road NE, Suite 850, Atlanta, Georgia 30326, phone 404.975.5000, fax 404.975.5020, www.nlnac.org.
