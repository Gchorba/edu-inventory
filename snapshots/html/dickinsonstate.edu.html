DSU News

5/3/2012
TRHLP students selected to present in Boston
Ten students in the Theodore Roosevelt Honors Leadership Program at Dickinson State University have been selected to present proposals at the National Collegiate Honors Conference in Boston. The conference will take place Nov. 14-18, 2012.
5/3/2012
DSU students win North Dakota Professional Communicators awards for publications
The 2012 College Communications Contest sponsored by The North Dakota Professional Communicators, recognized Dickinson State University's literary magazine, "Impressions" and the contributions of student editors and writers for "The Hawk" and other DSU student publications.
5/3/2012
Faculty members receive promotion/tenure at DSU
A group of faculty members have been granted tenure and/or given promotions at Dickinson State University. Promotion at DSU acknowledges and rewards faculty members for professional competence and service to both the institution and the community.
