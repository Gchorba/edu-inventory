Founded in 1879, Curry College is a private, four-year, liberal arts-based institution located on a wooded 135-acre campus in Milton, MA, just seven miles from downtown Boston. We offer 20 undergraduate majors, over 65 minors and concentrations, as well as four graduate programs.

Future students at Curry College can apply for admission and choose from quality academic programs, including undergraduate majors, graduate studies and continuing education classes delivered by committed and caring faculty at our campus locations in Milton and Plymouth, Massachusetts.

Curry College welcomes adult and graduate students into quality academic programs to achieve bachelor’s degrees, master’s/graduate degrees and certificates or pursue continuing education courses, corporate training and professional development opportunities.

More than 10,000 Curry College alumni stay connected with our dynamic community through online access, campus events, publications, our National Alumni Council, volunteering, naming opportunities and ways to give that help provide quality academic experiences and rewarding outcomes.

Curry College welcomes our parents as important partners with our caring and committed faculty and staff in creating successful, individualized and empowering academic and personal opportunities and outcomes for motivated and passionate undergraduate students.

Curry College welcomes our local community to visit campus, enjoy events from theatre to athletics, take advantage of conference and event services, corporate training, our Early Childhood Center, and programs and courses for undergraduates, continuing education and graduate students.
