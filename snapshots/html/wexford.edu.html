Wexford University accepts students from around the world. If you are a non-U.S. student, you will be given information specific to foreign students.

All degree programs are delivered online through the university's LMS (learning management system) with supplemental textbooks for some courses. Degree programs start every 3 months (quarterly format).

CREDIT TRANSFER INFORMATION: Wexford University will accept transferable credits from state-licensed, nationally accredited, and regionally accredited colleges and universities. All foreign students will need to have their prior credentials reviewed by a third party credential review service. Details will be provided well in advance of our reopening.

Graduates of the Personal Fitness Trainer Certification from National Exercise & Sports Trainers Association (NESTA) can transfer credits into the Associate of Arts Degree in Fitness Training (AA).

Graduates of NPTI (National Personal Trainer Institute) can transfer credits from NPTI into the Associate of Arts Degree in Fitness Training (AA).
Graduates of other NCCA-accredited personal trainer certification programs can transfer credits into the Associate of Arts Degree in Fitness Training (AA).
