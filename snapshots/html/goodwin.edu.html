About Goodwin

Specializing in career-focused education, Goodwin College attracts students from a broad range of backgrounds, from recent high school graduates to those who have attended college in the past. Many graduates find successful employment in their fields of choice with the help of the College's counseling and career services staff.

Goodwin College is a proud member of the Hartford Consortium.
