Home 

IntelliTec Medical Institute is an institute of higher education in Colorado Springs dedicated to providing excellent healthcare training.

We believe the key to building a successful healthcare school is providing you the opportunity to learn and fine-tune your skills in classrooms built just like working laboratories.

At IMI, you can develop your medical career under the guidance of expert in-the-field professional instructors. We've maintained this strategy for healthcare career training for more than 40 years and it has been fundamental in our ability to help thousands of people enjoy their successful medical careers.
