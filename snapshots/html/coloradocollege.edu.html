Winona LaDuke, a former vice-presidential candidate of the Green Party and member of the Anishinaabeg tribe, presented the 2012 Timothy C. Linnemann Lecture at CC’s “First Mondays” event. LaDuke has worked on behalf of environmental, Native American, and women’s issues and is a recent inductee into the National Women’s Hall of Fame. As executive director of Honor the Earth, LaDuke has supported environmental sustainability in Native American communities. Photo by Stanley Sigalov '13.
