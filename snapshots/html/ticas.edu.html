If you're looking for an exciting career working with people, a career in  Cosmetology is a great field to enter. To succeed, you'll need an education that offers quality curriculum, accreditation and licensing that will enhance your talent and creative artistry. If you're willing to devote your time and energy to being a success, Technical Institute of Cosmetology Arts & Sciences is the college for you.

Educational Objectives

The objectives of our Institute is to provide a vocational course of study in all  phases of Basic Cosmetology that will qualify a student to pass the State Board Examination and to prepare the student for maximum job placement potential.  We seek the highest level of educational information, tools and methods to accomplish these objectives.  We seek to produce a well-rounded graduate, with a firm foundation in the cosmetology sciences as well as the creative cosmetology arts.  We are aware that well trained graduates benefit themselves, our profession, and the community.
