Registration for Spring Semester 2012 is now OPEN! Fall 2011 is well under way, and registration for Spring semester is now open! Schools may send a spreadsheet for enrollment purposes, or individuals may register at Enrollment Form. Courses are $250/ea for individuals, $225/ea for 10-49 student seats, $200/ea for 50-99 student seats, and $180/ea for more than 100 seats. International students may qualify for full scholarships under grant-support.

 

 

Welcome to PLC! PLC isn't just an online school, but a place to collaborate, learn, and explore while we build our future. PLC and its staff are dedicated to bringing the highest quality, lowest cost education to students around the world so that students everywhere can join the global cultural and economic community. If you would like to earn a high school diploma with a global emphasis, please explore further!
