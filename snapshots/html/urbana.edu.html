Welcome to Urbana University

Urbana University, located in Urbana, Ohio, focuses on building close connections between faculty and students while providing cutting-edge programs that help students develop the skills necessary to succeed in this global economy. Visit our Admissions Department to learn how you can become a Blue Knight.

 

Our first priority is academic excellence, preparing our graduates to make a difference in their careers as well as their communities. Each of our academic majors prepares students for success in their chosen career or graduate school through a combination of personal classroom instruction, real world experiences, community partnerships and technology.

Mendenhall, Risner Named Blue Knights of the Year

Apr. 27th, 2012

Urbana University held its second annual All-Sports Awards Banquet on Saturday night in the Sara Landess Room on campus. Each of the University's 15 intercollegiate athletic programs recognized outstanding performances from the 2011-12 school year, and at the end of the night, the athletic department handed out its most prestigious awards. Sophomore QB D.J. Mendenhall and senior softball player Megan Risner took home the night's top awards, the Blue Knight Student-Athletes of the Year. Read More
