Welcome

The Professional School of Psychology (PSP) is a distinctive graduate school that emphasizes high quality yet affordable education for mature, accomplished adults and experienced human service professionals from all regions of the world.

Through its unique Residential and Tutorial modes of education delivery at the Masters degree and Doctoral degree levels, PSP effectively serves those who face the challenge of working full-time and meeting substantive family responsibilities.

Under its Authorization from the State of California, PSP offers advanced degrees in psychology, including Clinical Master Degree and Organizational Masters Degree programs as well as Clinical Doctoral Degree and Organizational Doctoral Degree programs.

Gail Johnson Vaughan is Winner of Prestigious Purpose Prize

The Professional School of Psychology is very pleased (and honored) to announce that one of our doctoral students, Gail Johnson Vaughan, has just received a major award—the Purpose Prize (which is the USA’s only large-scale investment in people over 60 who are combining their passion and experience for social good). Mission Focused Solutions, which Gail founded in 2008, has worked with local policymakers to identify budgetary savings that can then be reinvested in programs that place youth age 11-18 in permanent homes. Gail Johnson Vaughan hopes to take her advocacy nationwide to overcome what she calls “systemic barriers” to helping older youth find loving families. Congratulations Gail ... and BRAVO for your extraordinary work.
