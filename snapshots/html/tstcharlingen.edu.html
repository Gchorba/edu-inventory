Child Care Services Registration Day
Workforce Solutions Cameron Child Care Management Services (WFS-CCM) will be on campus from 1:00 PM to 4:30 PM Wednesday May 16, 2012 to certify TSTC students for childcare services. WFS-CCM will only meet with registered TSTC students who received CCMS services for the spring semester and are registered for the summer semester. Certification will be determined upon meeting with WFS-CCM. Students are required to sign up for an apppointment in advance and in person at the Support Services Office (Building P). Reservations are limited to 50 appointments and advance sign-ups will close once capacity has reached 50 or by 12 PM on May 15.

Summer Semester Registration Information
 The cashiers windows will be located in the Learning Resource Center Orientation Room from 8:30 a.m. to 6 p.m. May 7 through May 10 and 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. May 11. The cashiers also will be in the LRC from 8 a.m. to 12 p.m. May 12 and 8 a.m. to 6 p.m. May 14. The 15-week summer semester begins May 7. The 12-week summer semester will start May 14 and students must pay tuition or make payment arrangements by 3 p.m. May 9. Textbooks may be purchased at the Bookstore using financial aid through May 12 for 15-week classes and from May 7 through May 19 for 12-week classes. Financial aid credit balance refunds will be transmitted to the HigherOne TechOne Card after 7 p.m. May 21 for the 15-week summer semester and on May 29 for the 12-week summer semester.

Registered Nursing Information Sessions
Anyone who plans to seek admission into the new Registered Nurse Program scheduled to open at TSTC for fall semester 2012, pending Southern Association of Colleges and Schools (SACS) approval, must attend an information session to receive a program application. The information sessions will be held in the Senator Eddie Lucio Jr. Health Science Technology Building (Building U), Room U-126, from 5:30 PM to 6:30 PM Tuesdays through May. The application deadline will be 4 PM May 31. After May, the information meetings will be held on a monthly basis. All information sessions are open to the public. More information is available on the TSTC home Web site or please phone Rachel Wilson at 956.364.4983.
