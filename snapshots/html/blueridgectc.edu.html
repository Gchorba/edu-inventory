Welcome to Blue Ridge Community and Technical College

At Blue Ridge Community and Technical College, our goal is to lead our students on their path to success. Get a jump start on your education, complete a degree you’ve already started, or expand your knowledge and skills. With programs to give high school students an edge, over 40 collegiate level associate degree and certificate programs, and results-oriented workforce development and training solutions, Blue Ridge Community and Technical College provides the education you need to succeed.
