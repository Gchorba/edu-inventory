Community Service

The Touro University California community feels blessed with the beauty of the campus on Mare Island and the rich cultures and community from which we come. We support and encourage Touro faculty, staff and students to serve as a vehicle for personal growth and guidance; to lead in our community and create a difference; and to teach their scope of experience through service and support. Service is a fundamental value on the Touro campus. Health clinics, health fares, public health programs, and educational mentoring of K-12 students are all in the repertoire of Touro’s students, faculty and administration.

Alumni

Graduates of the College of Osteopathic Medicine, College of Pharmacy, Graduate School of Education, Masters of Public Health program, and Physician Assistant Studies program come together at Touro University California to share their experience and themselves with current students.  The Alumni section provides information on alumni events, professional positions available, and campus news.  Contact information can also be updated by visiting and logging on to the Alumni Section (available soon).

Library

Touro University Library actively supports teaching, research and the learning goals of the university. The Library’s collection focuses on education, medicine, pharmacy and health sciences and includes an extensive collection of e-books, e-journals, and databases. In addition to the collection, the Library also offers a variety of services to TUC students, faculty, and staff including instruction in database searching, citation management, and evaluation of resources.
