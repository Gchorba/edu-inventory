NCAD: The Creative Spirit

A creative life today explores traditional image-making combined with what seems to be unlimited technology. It is truly an incredible time to be an artist and designer.

We believe in you and can help you become the creative professional you know you can be.

NCAD: Art is both a Business and a Passion

At NCAD, professional artists are created through a sequential and comprehensive curriculum; a knowledgeable and professional faculty; a campus environment that is inspiring and supportive to the creative spirit and a dual emphasis on individualism and job placement.

NCAD: Artists Must Make Art

Artists must make art. We must be knowledgeable, develop a strong sense of curiosity, be aware of both traditional techniques and current technology, and have the courage to be truly original.

— Craig Freeman

Three Great Reasons to Apply to NCAD Now!

There are lots of reasons why students choose NCAD, and here are just a few of the ones they say matter most to them:

1We Emphasize Career Training Aligned With Your Personal Goals

At NCAD, we put great emphasis on your unique and personal creative career goals, and help you to direct your choices and to use your time within our program to best help you achieve your goals. At NCAD, it is all about your unique dream for your creative life.

2We Offer Great Value at a Very Reasonable Cost of Tuition

College is one of the most imporant investments in your future, and you both want and deserve a good return on that investment. At NCAD, we want you to acheive your dream of a college art education, and our lower tuition rate helps make that dream a reality.

3We Focus on the Individual, and Keep our Class Sizes Small

Classes at NCAD are kept intentionally small (maximum 30 students per instructor in a typical class setting) to ensure a personal approach to education. NCAD also welcomes returning students who are ready to fulfill their creative ambitions.
