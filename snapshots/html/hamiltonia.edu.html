Kaplan University iPad App

We are committed to providing you with extraordinary convenience. The KU Campus iPad app offers several unique capabilities not available via the iPhone® and iPod® apps offered by other universities.
  These features include:

  • Classes—attend classes, access archived seminars, and review course content and course structure.
  • Course information—the syllabus, course materials, unit information and links, and activities are available.
  • Discussion boards—join the conversation on the discussion boards and participate in an interactive dialogue with professors and classmates as part of the learning experience.
  • Grades and credits—view assignment and final grades, credits earned for each course, as well as cumulative credits and GPA.
  • Financial aid information—view charges, payments, and balances by term as well as a summary of financial aid or scholarship information.
  • Alerts—access notifications, alerts, and appointments in the message center.
  • Connect with classmates—find links to Kaplan University’s social networks on Facebook, LinkedIn, and YouTube to connect with other students and alumni.

The Kaplan University Campus app, available as a free download at the iTunes® Store, was developed specifically for use on the iPad®.  Download it here.
