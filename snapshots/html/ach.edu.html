Welcome to American College of Healthcare

Do You Want a Rewarding Career in Healthcare?

If you answered "yes", then we can help you start your healthcare career training today! American College of Healthcare (ACH) is an established career training school in Riverside that offers industry driven health education programs to individuals that are seeking to establish a foundation that leads to a rewarding career or to enhance the continued development of an existing career in a health related field.

ACH offers career training programs for Medical Assistant, Medical Administrative Assistant, Medical Billing and Coding, Pharmacy Technician, Surgical Technology, and Massage/Physical Therapy Aide. The programs at ACH are designed to meet the changing needs of diverse business environments.

The staff and faculty at this vocational trade school believe that long-term career success relies on continued education and personal enrichment. Not only are we highly motivated to help you join American College of Healthcare for career training, but in helping you find a job as quickly as possible.

Are you worried about cost? Our expert financial aid staff is here to help you find the right funding for your education.
