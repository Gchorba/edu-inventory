Expand Your Horizons Online

California National University for Advanced Studies was founded in 1993 offers accredited Bachelor and Master degrees completely online. Self-paced, with no campus visits you can study at the time, place, and pace that works best for your lifestyle. Our online programs cover fields such as engineering, business administration, quality assurance, computer science and human resources. California National University is one of the only Universities that offer an accredited Bachelor and Master of Science in Engineering degree program completely online.
