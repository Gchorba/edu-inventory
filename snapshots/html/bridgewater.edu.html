One Of The Best Liberal Arts Colleges Private and Small

Bridgewater College was named one of the best liberal arts colleges in the southeast by Princeton Review. BC is a private, small liberal arts college that is recognized for its strong academics and student-centered atmosphere. With more than 60 majors and minors, multiple concentrations, pre-professional programs such as medicine and law, dual degree programs and teacher certification you're sure to find the bachelor of arts or science degree that's right for you.

Student-Centered Small Liberal Arts Colleges

Bridgewater College is an independent, small, private college that enrolls more than 1650 students, has a faculty-student ratio of 14:1 and average class size of 19.
