Brandman University, a part of the Chapman University System, is a private, non-profit institution accredited by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC) and has been serving the needs of working adults since 1958. We offer over 52 bachelor's, master's and certificate programs in a blended format, combining online and in-class instruction at 26 campuses in California and Washington. Fully-online programs are also available for added flexibility.
