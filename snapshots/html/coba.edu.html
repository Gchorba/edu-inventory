Since 1965, exclusive salons and the entertainment industry in Southern California and beyond have sought out COBA Academy graduates. As a licensed cosmetology professional, you will be prepared for a career in beauty, make-up, nail tech and hair design.

Salons know that COBA graduates have:

  • First Class Training in hair style and cutting, make up, nail care and ear piercing
  • High Standards that exceed state licensing requirements – We have a 93% Pass Rate.
  • Personal Pride
  • Dedication
  • A Desire to Succeed!

National Surveys show that there are more jobs than graduates! So your successful Cosmetology Career is within reach.

As a licensed COBA graduate, you have a whole world of new career possibilities available to you.  We are not just a beauty school.  Our cosmetology training will prepare you for:

  • Salon – providing cuts, perms and colors. Ownership. Management.
  • Entertainment – doing hair and makeup for movies and TV.
  • Fashion Industry - creating looks for runway models and print ads.

Cometologists can specialize in color, texturizing, makeup, spa services, facials, nail technology or any other segment of the exploding beauty industry.
