  The Washington Campus, founded in 1978, has served as the principal foundation in Washington for educating business students on the practices and realities of public policy. Consortium members include some of the leading business schools in the United States. As keynote speaker and honoree at a dinner commemorating the consortium's 25th Anniversary, Vice President Dick Cheney gave the following assessment:
 
        “… The Washington Campus program has been so important. They're helping bridge that gap between the world of business and the world of politics. Business
leaders learn to see the world as Washington policymakers see it, and policymakers often gain a better understanding of the realities of business life."
 		  
  Located in Washington at the L. William and Sally Seidman Center, the Campus has had the privilege of educating some of the brightest minds in business. We look forward to continuing this tradition of serving corporate leaders by equipping them with the tools to navigate Washington DC. Join us at The Washington Campus.
