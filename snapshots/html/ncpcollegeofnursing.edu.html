Welcome! NCP is a California State approved College of Nursing that serves the San Francisco Bay Area. Our nursing programs are attended by many students from nearby of San Francisco, Daly City, San Bruno, Pacifica, Millbrae, Brisbane, San Mateo, Burlingame, Hayward, San Leandro, Oakland, Emeryville, Berkeley, Castro Valley, Union City, Fremont, Concord and San Jose.

 

The Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC) recognizes NCP College of Nursing South San Francisco and NCP College of Nursing Hayward as accredited institutions. The ACCSC is listed by the U.S. Department of Education (DOE) as a nationally recognized accrediting agency.

 

NCP College of Nursing is pleased to announce that it has received eligibility from the U.S. Department of Education to offer Title IV Financial Aid for the Vocational Nursing Program.     Please log on to www.fafsa.gov to apply using the NCP College of Nursing Federal School Code 041954 for the South San Francisco Campus or E02091 for the Hayward Campus.   For more information, please contact our South San Francisco Campus at 650-871-0701 or our Hayward Campus at 510-785-0454.   

 

Congratulations to the NCP College of Nursing LVN graduates who achieved a 100% NCLEX pass rate at So. San Francisco Campus (Q1 2010), 94% (Q4 2009) and 94% pass rate at the Hayward Campus (Q4 2009) and 89% (Q1 2010) !  California annual pass rate for 2010 is 76%.
