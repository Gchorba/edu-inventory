Full Calendar		 5/4/2012
Three Rivers to receive SEMA funding for two new buildings
Three Rivers College announces the receipt of over $3.3 million in grant funding from the State Emergency Management Association to be used for two new building projects.

5/3/2012
Enrollment continues for "Maymester" at Three Rivers
Open enrollment for Maymester - an accelerated academic term that comes between the Spring semester and Summer sessions - continues at Three Rivers through May 18.

5/1/2012
Three Rivers bands to close year with concert
The Three Rivers College Jazz and Symphonic bands will host a free music concert titled "Gems of the Concert Stage" at the Tinnin Fine Arts Center on Tuesday, May 8 at 7 p.m.

4/27/2012
Neal E. Boyd to perform at Three Rivers Commencement
Singer and celebrity Neal E. Boyd will perform at the 2012 Commencement ceremonies for Three Rivers College on May 20 at 4 p.m. at the Black River Coliseum.

4/23/2012
PBFD to conduct special simulations at Merchant's Showcase
The Poplar Bluff Fire Department, in cooperation with the college's fire science program, will demonstrate the effectiveness of new residential fire-fighting technology. 		myTRCC
