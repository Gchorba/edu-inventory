Welcome to GSST

Golden State School of Theology has been preparing men and women for Christian ministry since 1979. Institutions in the Philippines, Ukraine, and Malaysia are using GSST methodology and expertise to train their own people for service. Our Directed Internet Studies (DIS) methodology allows students to receive qualitative and quantitative Bible centered education regardless of where they live. GSST is a world wide school, and we prepare men and women to change the world for Jesus.

GSST has totally embraced the educational opportunities God has provided through the internet. If you have ever wanted to involve yourself in Bible centered education to complete a degree or for personal enrichment, GSST is THE school of OPPORTUNITY for YOU!! We provide degree programs and courses without high tuition or residency requirements. Our Mentors cause each program to be as personalized as it could possibly be.

GSST's Tuition Rates are among the most student friendly you'll find anywhere. We invite you to review our Most Commonly Asked Questions to discover even more how and why GSST can help you achieve your Bible centered educational wants and needs.
