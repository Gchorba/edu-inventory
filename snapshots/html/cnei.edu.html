California Nurses Educational Institute

Welcome to the California Nurses Educational Institute’s web site!

 

The College has moved! 

We have relocated to brand new expanded facilities at 5200 E. Ramon Road, Building I in Palm Springs.   We are on the corner of Ramon and Gene Autry.

 

We appreciate your interest in our nursing education programs.  We are committed to preparing students who will provide caring, compassionate and competent health care in our diverse community and will be successful in the nursing profession and in life.

 

CNEI offers several nursing programs including Licensed Vocational Nurse, Certified Nurse Assistant, Home Health Aide, Phlebotomy, and Psychiatric Technician.  In addition, we offer State of California approved CPR and Continuing Education Units for CNAs.  Our educational progams maintain high standards for nursing practice and enjoy an excellent reputation in our community. 

 

Our graduates are in high demand for employment in a variety of healthcare settings.  There is a great potential for nursing in the rapidly changing health care system, and our outstanding nursing faculty is committed to preparing graduates who will be leaders of the future.  Nursing is the nation's largest health care field.  Many exciting and rewarding job opportunities can be found in nursing.  The U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics projects that employment for nurses will grow faster than any other occupation through 2015.

 

Nurses, in collaboration with those in other health disciplines, deliver preventive, restorative, and adaptive health care to people in a wide variety of acute care and community settings, such as hospitals, outpatient clinics, extended care facilities, private homes, public health departments, school settings, and surgery centers.   Nursing offers a wide variety of opportunities for service to society, for personal growth, and for professional development.

 

The information in this web site is designed to assist you in learning about our program, providing answers to FAQ and outlining the steps of the application process. The faculty and staff of CNEI hope this information will be beneficial to you as you embark on your nursing career.

 

We are looking forward to hearing from you and answering questions you may have related to your preparation for a career in nursing.  We hope you'll decide to be part of the future in nursing.
