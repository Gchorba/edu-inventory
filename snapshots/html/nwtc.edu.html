    Tractor Safety offered for youth age 12-16

    Area youth who work on farms can learn how to safely operate tractors and farm machinery through upcoming classes in Door County, Shawano and Green Bay.

  •                                                                                                                                                                                                 

    Get plants and garden tips from Horticulture students on May 4

    Get expert assistance with your plants and select new plants for your gardens during the Horticulture Club Plant Sale and Open House on Friday, May 4.

  •                                                                                                                                                                                                 

    Car Show & Swap Meet set for May 6

    NWTC is revving up for the 26th Annual NWTC Auto Club Car Show and Swap Meet. This family event features fun for everyone!

  •                                                                                                                                                                                                 

    Celebrate Life art exhibit on view now through May 23

    The latest exhibit at the NWTC Art Gallery celebrates the lives of over 50 children who are facing life threatening illnesses.

  •                                                                                                                                                                                                 

    NWTC Foundation to raise funds for student and program needs

    Volunteers are visiting area businesses to raise funds for the sixth annual A Day for NWTC campaign. This year's contributions will support the NWTC Fund which helps meet immediate student and program needs.

  •                                                                                                                                                                                                 

    NWTC honored for community service

    NWTC has once again been admitted to the 2012 President's Higher Education Community Service Honor Roll for engaging its students, faculty and staff in meaningful service that achieves measurable results in the community.

  •                                                                                                                                                                                                 

    NWTC student named New Century Scholar

    Port Lor, a student in NWTC's Leadership Development program, is one of 50 community college students from the United States, Canada, and Guam who have been named 2012 New Century Scholars.
