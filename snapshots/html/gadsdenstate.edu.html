Gadsden State Community College

Welcome

Welcome to the Gadsden State Community College Web site. As home to more than 8,000 students from across Northeast Alabama and the world, as well as approximately 800 faculty and staff, Gadsden State is the ultimate college experience. Our Web site keeps you connected with events and developments across six campuses.

Whether you are alumni, current student, prospective student or citizen of one of the many communities we serve, we invite you to explore gadsdenstate.edu and discover how Gadsden State can help you to choose your direction!
