College Overview
Lancaster General College of Nursing & Health Sciences is a private college dedicated to the education of healthcare workers. Accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education, the College is located in Lancaster, PA.  Admissions is open to students with the interest, desire and ability to pursue a healthcare program. The College seeks individuals who demonstrate qualities of scholarship, motivation and commitment to academic and personal growth.  Academic Programs include Cardiovascular Electrophysiology Specialty, Cardiovascular Invasive Specialty, Clinical Laboratory Science, Diagnostic Medical Sonography, Emergency Medical Technician, General Education, Nuclear Medicine Technology, Nursing, Paramedic, Radiography, Respiratory Care, and Surgical Technology.
