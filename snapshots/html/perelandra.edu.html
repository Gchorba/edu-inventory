Superior Education

The Perelandra College Bachelor of Arts in Writing and Master of Arts in Creative Writing programs equip students with skills and encourage them to write their unique stories, powered by their originality and guided by their beliefs.

Affordable Degrees

The college is non-profit, its only purpose to serve our students by offering quality education and the opportunity to earn respected, accredited degrees without sinking into debt. Our online programs and dedication to simplicity and thrift allow us to deliver both affordable clases and degrees and mentoring by dedicated faculty.

Self-Paced Classes and Programs

In respect of the different goals, circumstances, and learning styles of writers, we offer open scheduling. While we encourage students to proceed through classes and programs at a steady pace, they start each class when ready, and end when the work is completed.

We are a Servicemembers Opportunity College and work with veterans to access the benefits of the GI Bill.
