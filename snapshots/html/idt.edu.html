IDT is Los Angeles County's premier provider of information technology IT training and certification. Our offerings feature authorized courses from the most renowned vendors. Microsoft, IBM, Oracle, Novell, Comptia, Planet3 Wireless, as well as a broad range of non authorized courses from Cisco®, Redhat, Macromedia and more.
