Introduction

Passion for Beauty Education

At ABC, we provide solid fundamental knowledge, incorporate advanced skills with hands-on practice not only to assist you to pass the licensure exam but also to become a successful professional. We focus on how to help you to achieve your goals – to be successful in the market place – we create an environment where inspiration of creation and dedication to practice is paramount – you will discover your own ability to transform your life. Come to visit us.

Since 1978, ABC has been accredited by National Accrediting Commission of Career Arts & Sciences (NACCAS) - as an accredited institution for more than 30 years, our instructors always emphasize continuing education to improve their teaching effectiveness, our staffs constantly dedicate to student’s learning and motivational needs, and our graduates are well known of their outstanding performance and highly respected in the beauty industry. Come and talk to our alumni.

Financial Aid

Alhambra Beauty College is approved by Department of Education to participate in Title IV Financial Aid programs.  Please call us for more information, or, visit http://www.fafsa.ed.gov/ to know more about the different Federal Financial Aid Programs.

International Students

Foreign students are welcome.  We are Certified by Student and Exchange Visitor Information System (SEVIS) to issue I-20 M1 Student Visas.  Counseling and assistance are available in various languages.  Please contact us by email or phone for more information.

Accreditation

Alhambra Beauty College is Accredited By National Accrediting Commission Of Career Arts & Sciences (NACCAS) since 1978.

Job Placement

At ABC, hands-on practice at our great location significantly strengthen our students' ability to adapt into real-world envrionment for employment in the beauty industry - through our extensive contacts with future employers in the entire beauty industry, we assist our graduates to successfully achieve their career goals.
