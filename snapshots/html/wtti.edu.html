Welder Training and Testing Institute

For 42 years, Welder Training & Testing Institute (WTTI) has offered students the necessary occupational training and certifications that can lead to employment in the welding field.  WTTI is accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC) and licensed by the Pennsylvania State Board of Private Licensed Schools.
