Why Juniata?

  • Juniata offers a 4-year graduation guarantee. 95% of students who graduate do so in four years or fewer.
  • Juniata offers a great education, within reach.
  • Design your area of study through an individualized Programs of Emphasis.

There's a host of reasons for applying!

Why Juniata?

  • Two faculty advisors assist you in graduating in four combined years.
  • Academic scholarships and financial aid are awarded on the same basis as freshmen.
  • International or internship experience can be part of your education.

There's a host of reasons for applying!

Why Juniata?

  • Study with students from over 35 countries at a top US college.
  • Airport pick-up, academic advising and English language support
  • Experience life in a safe, small American town... all within proximity to major cites.
