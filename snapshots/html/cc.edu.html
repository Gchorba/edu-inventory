Carrington College

Carrington College: Founded in 1976, Carrington College offers certificate and associate degree programs that help students find success in new careers throughout the health care field.

With 10 campuses across the Western United States we are continuing to make it easier for students to access exciting new careers in the medical, dental, veterinary, health and fitness fields. Take a look at our programs and find out what is right for you!
