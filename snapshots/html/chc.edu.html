HISTORIC ROOTS.
MODERN REACH.

Founded on the philosophy of the Sisters of Saint Joseph more than 80 years ago, Chestnut Hill College still holds true to its founding principles. At the core of this philosophy lies the Sisters' commitment to work and live amongst the people they are called to serve. This commitment to helping others ensures that graduates are prepared to improve not only their careers, but their communities as well.

YOU MIGHT BE SITTING DOWN WHEN YOU DISCOVER WHAT YOU STAND FOR.

At Chestnut Hill College, we believe that a curriculum should go beyond the classroom because education occurs on many levels. And while our coursework is always evolving to stay one step ahead of our ever-changing world, it's based on a tradition of values. This holistic approach inspires a passion for learning, builds a collaborative learning community, and creates well-rounded individuals, prepared to excel in all aspects of their lives.

WELCOME TO
A 75-ACRE TEXTBOOK.

Strolling through Chestnut Hill College's campus, you can't help but feel the history. French Gothic and Italian Romanesque architecture transports you to another time, and hallways seem to echo with past scholarly discussion. You'll cross tree-lined groves perfect for outdoor studying and wide-open fields full of coeds shouldering backpacks or chucking Frisbees. Once you step foot on campus you'll quickly discover that you'll be hard pressed to find another college campus that's equally, well, collegiate.

THE BEST OF BOTH WORLDS.

We're a city school. We're a suburban school. Truth be told, we're a little bit of both. At Chestnut Hill College, you can enjoy the benefits of a geographic identity crisis: a quaint, safe, charming campus on the outskirts of one of America's largest cities and cultural epicenters. From the hiking trails of Fairmount Park to the cafes of the Chestnut Hill neighborhood to the museums of Philadelphia, you'll be well occupied.

HOW WILL YOU GET INVOLVED?

At Chestnut Hill College, it's no exaggeration when we say that campus life extends well beyond the classroom. With a wealth of clubs, organizations and sports, students find an outlet for every interest while still managing to put in some solid time exploring Philadelphia's music scene, or discussing Arrested Development over a double cheese pizza at Cosimo's.

INVEST IN YOUR FUTURE.

At Chestnut Hill College, we offer abundant financial aid and scholarship opportunities to help our students reach their educational goals. Our Financial Aid office offers individualized assistance, so that you can understand all of your financial options. A Chestnut Hill education is a sound investment, and allows you to graduate in a timely manner ensuring both professional and personal success.

HERE, THERE'S NO SUCH THING AS
A TYPICAL STUDENT.

At Chestnut Hill College, the only thing you can say for certain about our students is that they don't fit into any pre-cast stereotypes. They are smart, confident, interesting, spiritual, worldly, and well-rounded people. They come from different backgrounds, and bring with them unique world views. Chestnut Hill College has created an environment that both teaches and fosters this kind of versatile individuality, and our student body reflects this fact.
