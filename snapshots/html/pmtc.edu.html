Continuing Education

At PMTC, we believe that massage therapists should continue to educate themselves throughout their career. With new developments and therapy concepts arising every year, it is important to stay up-to-date in order to deliver the best care possible. PMTC makes such education easily available to our graduates by offering monthly continuing education classes.
