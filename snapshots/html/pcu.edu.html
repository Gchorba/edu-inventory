Welcome to Pass Christian University

Pass Christian University offers you hundreds of engaging online courses for adults, covering every topic from Anatomy to Web Design.

Each Pass Christian University course comes equipped with a patient and caring instructor, lively discussions with your fellow students, and plenty of practical information that you can put to immediate use. All courses run for six weeks (with a two-week grace period at the end). Courses are project-oriented and include lessons, quizzes, hands-on assignments, discussion areas, supplementary links, and more. You can complete any of these courses entirely from your home or office and at any time of the day or night Click here to view Pass Christian University's expanding catalog of more than 300 online courses and life development resources.
