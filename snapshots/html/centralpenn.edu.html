Central Penn College is a career-oriented, baccalaureate, residential college in the suburbs of Harrisburg and offsite locations in Lancaster and Bethlehem, Pennsylvania. Central Penn College offers bachelor's and associate degree programs for both traditional and continuing education students by providing hands-on and online distance education taught by experienced, caring faculty.
