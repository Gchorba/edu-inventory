"The Best Decision I Ever Made."

We hear it all the time. FPU students know their degree will help them succeed as they start careers or apply to top graduate programs—including our own. Challenging, relevant academics and expert, committed faculty make us good value. The region’s highest four-year graduation rate and generous financial aid—98 percent of our traditional undergraduate students receive assistance—make us accessible and affordable.

Adults completing bachelor’s degrees or earning master’s degrees can choose among five campuses throughout the Central Valley as well as online options. We also serve more than 10,000 professionals in education and business across the country through continuing education and professional development courses.

Seminary 

Fresno Pacific Biblical Seminary is uniquely positioned to serve your needs. If you’re looking to expand your ministry and counseling toolbox, our programs are what you’re looking for.

International Students

The International Programs and Services Office is an international student's home away from home. Being international ourselves, we know what it's like to live and study abroad. Our goal is to help you integrate into the Fresno Pacific University community and American culture.

Our Faculty

Ready to help you succeed.

More than 80% of Fresno Pacific professors hold doctorates and are committed to research and publishing in their field of expertise. They also serve as mentors to students, engaging them outside of class and helping them develop professionally, ethically and personally.

Athletics

Success in action

FPU competes in 16 sports and our Sunbirds have won national championships in women’s volleyball, men’s and women’s tennis and men’s swimming. Visit the athletics website
 

The Arts

The arts at FPU.

Our music, drama and visual art programs cover the spectrum from classical to contemporary. Ensembles and activities are open to students from all majors and levels of experience.
