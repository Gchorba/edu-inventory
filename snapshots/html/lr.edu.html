Viaje a España

Nine LR students led by Dr. Eric Schramm and his wife, Christine, visited several areas of Spain, including Madrid, Seville, and Cordoba as part of the Broyhill Leadership Institute.

The program focused on Spain's multi-cultural past and present, with special emphasis on Roman and Arabic influences.

On the rocks

Students in Biology 106 visited the Blue Ridge Parkway for their final lab of the semester. They are shown here looking at ecological succession on granite outcrops by measuring changes in vegetation type and height with changes in soil depth.
