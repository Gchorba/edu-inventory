Events

Sunday May 06  03:00 PM

YCP Community Orchestra

A full symphonic orchestra, the YCP Community Orchestra includes music majors, non-music majors, and musicians form York and the surrounding area. The program will feature music by American composers Aaron Copland and Morton Gould, and will conclude with Russian Eastern Overture by Rimsky-Korsakov. As a special feature, the Orchestra Concerto Competition winner, percussionist Jon Mrenna, a senior general music education major from Sterling, Va., will perform Concerto for Percussion and Orchestra by Milhaud.
