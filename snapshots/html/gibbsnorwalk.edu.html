Sanford-Brown College - Farmington

Thank you for your interest in Sanford-Brown College in Farmington, Connecticut! If you are ready to unlock your true potential, we are ready to help. Sanford-Brown College – Farmington can be the key to positioning yourself for a future in exciting healthcare and business fields.

Our career-oriented school became Sanford-Brown College – Farmington in 2010. Previously, the school was known as Gibbs College – Farmington, one of two institutions remaining of Katharine Gibbs Schools which was originally founded in Rhode Island in 1911. Sanford-Brown College – Farmington is located on Farmington Ave., less than two miles north of the Two Mile Road exit of I-84 in northeast Farmington. We provide students with a quality curriculum by embracing traditional practices and combining them with contemporary ideas.

The goal of Sanford-Brown College is to offer focused, quality, short-term, career-oriented programs for the purpose of enabling each student to:

  • Successfully complete his/her program of study
  • Gain the professional attitude, competitive skills, and reliable performance that the community needs and employers demand
  • Build self-respect and confidence
  • Develop a foundation for pursuing success in future educational endeavors

At Sanford-Brown College – Farmington, we stand by our commitment to academic excellence.
