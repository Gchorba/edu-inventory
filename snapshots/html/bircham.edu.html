More Information:

Bircham International University is an independent provider of adult degree programs leading to a distance education university degree at professional, undergraduate and graduate levels through sound updated curricula and an innovative distance learning method of instruction.
Bircham International University commitment is to fulfill the needs of the adult professional student. BIU online university courses and each distance learning university degree is simultaneously registered in the USA , the European Union & the Commonwealth. A BIU online university degree can therefore be issued and legalized in any of these countries.

Bircham International University offers a distance learning degree, Bachelor's (B.B.A., B.S., B.A.), Master's (M.B.A., M.S., M.A.), online doctorate Ph.D., and professional specialist & expert online diploma through a full distance learning program. A distance learning university degree is completed entirely by correspondence. BIU will mail some textbooks and will require you to write reports as a way of earning the online university degree. Bircham International University adult degree programs accept some credit transferred from prior learning and professional experience. The curriculum is designed to meet each student needs. BIU offers a distance education university degree in over 150 disciplines: management, communications, humanities, arts, psychology. natural health, therapies, computers, sciences and engineering among many others.

Online university courses and adult degree programs are not for everyone, or for all purposes. It is meant for busy working professionals. Bircham International University offers an alternative to higher education to adults from all countries and cultures who choose not to attend a traditional on-campus university or who cannot find a distance learning university degree that meets their specialization requirements. BIU helps you to reinforce your professional career and personal goals. Since 1992, many of our graduates have boosted their professional career. A distance learning degree of Bachelor's (B.B.A., B.S., B.A.), Master's (M.B.A., M.S., M.A.), online doctorate Ph.D., and professional specialist & expert online diploma are a lifetime asset.

Bircham International University distance learning degree is valid for many purposes but not for all. Acceptance of a distance education university degree is always the prerogative of the receiving institution or employer.
