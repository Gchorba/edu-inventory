During it's illustrious history as an accredited college, American Bible College and Seminary trained as many as thirteen thousand students, many of whom have gone on to  become some of today's outstanding pastors, missionaries, evangelists and teachers. The mission and vision of ABCS continues on through the legacy of its alumni, to take the Gospel of Jesus Christ to the vast corners of the earth.


Alumni of American Bible College and Seminary have taken their place in ministries and vocations around the globe, and have founded churches, organizations and missions that have touched the world for Christ. We are currently assembling links and profiles of some the outstanding leaders that have emerged from the alumni of this outstanding institution. If you are a past graduate of ABCS, please register your current data with our alumni association.



We are now preparing to offer the continuing education seminar, Lifestyle Evangelism, to be conducted in Nashville, Tennessee. This program will teach believers how they can help bring others into a relationship with Christ, simply by living their faith for Jesus, without necessarily using other more direct methods.
