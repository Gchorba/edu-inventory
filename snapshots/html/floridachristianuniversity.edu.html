Who we are?



Since 1985, Florida Christian University is a global leader in Bible-based high education. We offer a range of degrees from Associates to Post Doctoral in more than 30 different programs. Our Global Reach Vision has extended FCU courses in more tha 50 countries around the world. Be part of the history!
