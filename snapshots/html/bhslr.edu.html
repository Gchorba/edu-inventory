Since 1921, Baptist Health Schools Little Rock has served the needs of residents throughout Arkansas and the region by providing quality clinical-focused healthcare education in the areas of nursing and allied health.

Fast Facts about BHSLR

  • Nine programs of study in both direct and indirect patient care
  • Certificate and diploma programs of study
  • Associate and bachelor degree options for some programs through affiliate colleges and universities
  • Largest registered nursing and practical nursing programs in Arkansas
  • Institutional accreditation and national programmatic accreditations for all areas of study
  • Approximately 900 students served each semester
