SANS Technology Institute

The Premier Skills-Based Cyber Security Graduate School

If you are looking for a Master of Science in Information Security Program that entails more than just taking and passing courses, SANS Technology Institute is the answer. STI offers two degree programs:

  • Master of Science in Information Security Engineering
  • Master of Science in Information Security Management

In addition to courses, STI offers students a unique opportunity to learn leadership skills, share knowledge and research with the information security industry and build a solid network of peers.

Click here to learn more about STI's Master's Degree Programs.

The STI Difference

With an STI degree you will:

  • Build the necessary technical, leadership and communication skills needed in the information security industry
  • Earn more than five highly revered Global Information Assurance Certifications (GIAC)
  • Build your reputation in the industry through the publication of papers and projects

Introducing Dr. Toby Gouker

STI would like to introduce Dr. Toby Gouker as the new Chancellor and Chief Operating Officer for the Institute. Toby brings both scholar and practitioner skills to his administrative role at STI. Combining his recent roles as CSO and COO in the federal contracting IT services industry with his roles as Vice President for University Affairs at American Graduate University, and Executive Director of National Technological University, he will help advance the Institute's mission to prepare managers and technical leaders by building and maintaining a back office infrastructure that facilitates an outstanding learning environment for STI students. In addition to his PhD in Applied Management and Decision Sciences, Toby holds a GIAC GSLC certification.
