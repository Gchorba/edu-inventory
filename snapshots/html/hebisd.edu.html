Adopter Shares Exotic Animals with Stonegate Students

Bridges Work, a Stonegate Elementary campus adopter, hosted "Talk to the Animals" for Stonegate students on Saturday, April 21. Students were introduced to over seventy different exotic animal species by David Allison, the animal science teacher at Dawson Middle School in Southlake-Carroll ISD and the owner and operator of a summer safari camp in Southlake. Everyone discussed the care, responsibility, and relationship that people should have with our animal companions.
