Welcome to Something Different —the amazing and unique
world of Classical Five-Element Acupuncture.

Classical Five-Element Acupuncture (CF-EA) is the Taoist healing tradition brought to the west by the late Professor J. R. Worsley. This oral tradition, taught to close-knit student groups produces practitioners of great understanding, knowledge and depth, and who work with the body, mind and the spirit.

The Institute of Taoist Education and Acupuncture (ITEA) offers the degree of Master of Acupuncture in Classical Five-Element Acupuncture. In June we will offer a Master of Acupuncture in CF-EA Acupuncture through our Acupuncture Degree Program for Licensed Healthcare Practitioners.


ITEA supports each student on an individual path of learning by allowing each to progress at his or her own pace, and by maximizing each student’s individual potential. Our international faculty are experts in their field, and all CF-EA faculty have been expertly trained to teach this oral tradition.

ITEA is looking for students who are unique!

Our program is for students who take responsibility for learning — students who are not only eager to learn academically, but who are also eager to learn through the senses, through their own personal progress, through relationship with others and through all aspects of nature.
