We Build Careers...Let us build one for you.

Founded in 1888, Mountain State College is committed to the educational and personal enrichment of each student. In today’s competitive job market, an education is an investment in your future. It gives you the opportunity to have a career in a field that you enjoy.

At Mountain State College, we offer career programs, taught by professionals. We can help you build the future you deserve.

Mountain State College is accredited by the Accrediting Council for Independent
Colleges and Schools (ACICS). ACICS is designated as a nationally-recognized
accrediting agency by the US Department of Education.
Mountain State College is also approved for training of veterans.
