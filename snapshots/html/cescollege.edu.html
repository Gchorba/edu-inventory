CES College

Community Enhancement Services (CES) College has been offering educational programs since 1995. CES COLLEGE is an accredited non-profit school. The CES College’s purpose is to "offer education opportunities to address the unique needs of individuals and communities served by CES by providing adults with the knowledge and skills necessary for employment, skill upgrades, career changes and/or advanced education.”

Why Choose Us?

CES College is approved by BICE to enroll non-immigrant foreign students and issue I-20 for F-1 student visa. CES College is accredited by  Council on Occupational Education (COE) and Western Association for Schools and Colleges (WASC). CES College is approved by the US Department of education to offer financial aid to those who qualify.
CES College has been helping people succeed since 1995. CES COLLEGE is a non-profit school.
