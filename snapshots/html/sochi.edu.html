Massage Therapy Program

SOCHi's Advance Professional Massage Therapy Program provides the student an integrated approach to health and wellness through massage therapy. Courses include:

Swedish Massage
Deep Tissue Massage
Hot Stone Massage
Sports Massage
Chair Massage
Shiatsu & Thai Massage

Physical Therapy Aide / Sports Rehab Program

SOCHi's Physical Therapy Aide / Sports Rehab Program provides the student an integrated approach to health and wellness by incorporating medical massage and physical therapy modalities. Courses include:

Swedish Massage
Deep Tissue Massage
Sports Massage
Physical Therapy Modalities
Therapeutic Stretching
Therapeutic Exercise
