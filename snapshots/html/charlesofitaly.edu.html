Charles of Italy Beauty College and School of Massage Therapy and School of Massage Therapy would like to welcome you to the profession of cosmetology. No matter which of the many possible career paths you may choose, our instructors and staff are here to open the doors to your future.

Charles of Italy offers training programs in

 

  • Cosmetology
  • Nail Technology
  • Massage Therapy (Lake Havasu only)
  • Instructor Training.

 

Entrance requirements for each of these programs as well as specific information about them and our school are contained in our downloadable guide.

 

 

Mission

Our mission is to provide professional cosmetology education that meets the current demands of the industry and to prepare its graduates to enter their chosen career with the necessary tools to be successful.

 

 

Educational Goals

In an effort to meet the growing demands for well-trained professionals, Charles of Italy Beauty College and School of Massage Therapy and School of Massage Therapy offers a variety of instructional programs each designed to lead the graduate to a variety of career options in the cosmetology industry. All programs are taught by combining classroom theory and practical clinical experience. The programs are offered in an environment that approximates as closely as possible to the working environment graduates may encounter after completion and licensure.
