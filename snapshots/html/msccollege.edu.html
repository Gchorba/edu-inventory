About MSC

Minnesota School of Cosmetology serves the Minneapolis-St. Paul area at campuses in Plymouth and Woodbury.

 Our history and accreditation assures students of excellent cosmetology training; our commitment to quality assures clients of premium services at reasonable prices.

Read more...

Program Information

Prepare for a career in beauty. Classes provide training in hair design, nail technology and skin care, skills you will need to excel in a creative industry. Study beauty school basics, practice advanced techniques and get plenty of practice on real clients in a totally hands-on environment.

Read More...

Client Services

MSC provides a variety of beauty services in professional salon settings. Experience great service at an affordable price as you try out a new hairstyle, a manicure or pedicure, high-end hair products or the latest spa techniques for your skin.

Follow Us

MSC is a nationally accredited cosmetology school, with campuses in Plymouth and Woodbury. We serve students and clients in Minneapolis-St. Paul area communities including New Hope, Maple Grove, Wayzata, Medicine Lake, Brooklyn Park, Brooklyn Center, Crystal, Champlin, Minnetonka, Golden Valley, Cottage Grove, Inver Grove Heights, Newport, Eagan, Stillwater and Oakdale, MN, and Hudson and River Falls, WI.
