Welcome to American School of Technology

At AST, we believe it's critical to have the right skills for the right job. That's why we offer comprehensive career training for some of today's most popular careers in the Business, Heating Ventilation Air Conditioning/Refrigeration, IT and Medical Fields.

The training you get at AST will prepare you for an exciting new career. Find out how our experienced faculty can train you for a rewarding career in less than 1 year!

AST is committed to meeting each student’s individual needs. We understand the challenge of training for a new career and the hard work that goes into it. That’s why we offer smaller class sizes so you benefit from more personal attention from faculty and staff.
