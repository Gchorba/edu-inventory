Who Are We?

We Are...

  • The New York Institute of Photography
  • The oldest photography school in the world (founded in 1910)
  • The largest photography school in the world (Thousands of new students enroll every year)
  • The only home-study photography school where real-life professional photographers will look at your work and suggest ways that you can make it better
