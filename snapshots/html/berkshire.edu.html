Salt and light.

Whether you are pursuing a full-time career in Christian service or strengthening your knowledge and faith in Jesus Christ, Berkshire can help prepare you to become salt and light in a needy world.

 

"Berkshire Christian College Day" is May 6

The first Sunday of May is set aside in the denominational calendar for Advent Christian Churches in North America to learn about the programs of Berkshire and renew their commitment to supporting its ministry with prayer and financial support. The school has just begun its 116th year of training workers for the Advent Christian Church and many other Christian groups worldwide. What can you do to support Berkshire on "Berkshire Christian College Day"? 

  • • Please pray for us in your worship service on May 6th.
  • • Please consider sending a gift to support our programs.

A Supportive, Personalized Christian Education

Berkshire offers a highly supportive, personalized experience that is theologically sound and relevant to today's world. We have been preparing men and women for Christian service for over 100 years. Today, our academic opportunties include online courses, distance learning, and partnerships with Eastern Univeristy and Gordon-Conwell Theological Seminary. An educational institution of the Advent Christian church, we are dedicated to training another generation of Christian leaders who are filled with hope, love, and servanthood.

Educational opportunities include:

Undergraduate Degree >

Get your college degree with specialized courses in Advent Christian studies through Eastern University in St. Davids, Pennsylvania.

Seminary Degree >

Obtain your seminary degree with an emphasis on Advent Christian studies through Gordon-Conwell Theological Seminary. Campuses are located in Hamilton and Boston, Massachusetts; Charlotte, North Carolina; and Jacksonville, Florida.

eBerkshire Online Courses >

Take selected courses online towards a certificate or for personal growth from the comfort of your home through eBerkshire.

Distance Education & Learning >

Berkshire offers numerous opportunities to develop your Christian leadership abilities, whether you are a pastor, church leader, layperson, BCC alumni, or simply want to pursue educational enrichment in a Christian setting. This can be done by taking classes through eBerkshire (on-line), local learning centers, or a combined delivery method. If you are interested in earning a certificate in Pastoral Studies or Leadership Development we have developed a constellation of courses for each of these to aid you in your ministry and growth goals.
