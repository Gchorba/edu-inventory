Sanford-Brown College - Tysons Corner

Whether you are a current healthcare professional looking one day to take your skills to the next level or a motivated individual focused on one day pursuing a new direction, we are here to help you make your one day today! Sanford-Brown offers the short-term programs and hands-on training you need to pursue your goals.

The healthcare industry is always in need of confident, qualified professionals to fill vital positions in a wide range of health-related fields. That's why Sanford-Brown College – Tysons Corner helps students gain knowledge, practical experience and training in the healthcare field.

Discovering your calling in life is great feeling. Making a difference in the lives of others can bring a lifetime of personal and professional satisfaction.

Turn one day into day one at Sanford-Brown College – Tysons Corner . A healthy new future can start right here!

Turn one day into day one with training in:

  • Business Administration
  • Criminal Justice
  • Graphic Design
  • Medical Assistant
  • Medical Billing and Coding
  • Nursing
  • Veterinary Technology
