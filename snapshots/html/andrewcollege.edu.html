Andrew College Cares

 

If Success is your Choice, Andrew is your College. We offer a vibrant two-year liberal arts foundation that prepares you for anything — and takes you anywhere. Our premier academic programs, caring faculty, staff,  and students, and strong athletic program may draw you. Whatever the reason, you will have the opportunity to lead, serve and even travel.

At Andrew, life focuses on adjusting to college, making solid grades and earning your associate's degree. Our dynamic education program prepares you for your next step forward. Andrew graduates consistently transfer to bigger campuses such as Auburn University, the University of Georgia, LaGrange College, Emory University, Valdosta State University and Georgia Southwestern State University as well as Florida State University, University of Florida,  and University of Alabama. If you want success, Andrew is your best option.

The Welkin Ring Literary Magazine

Our pleasant 40-acre campus is a place for big dreams. Our relaxed rural setting eliminates the distractions of larger universities, so you will love it during your important freshman and sophomore years. You'll benefit from the personal attention of your own faculty advisor and a close-knit group of friends who become like your family. Our small class sizes, with just 12 or 13 students, offers you the extra guidance and academic support for which Andrew is known.

Students, faculty and staff make their own fun at Andrew. From Homecoming and the Late Night Breakfast to Bed Races and Andrew's Got Talent, there is an array of traditions that you will come to love. (Where will the Mystery Van take you? Who will be crowned Miss Andrew? What happens during Tiger Spirit Week?)

And our buildings and sports facilities bear the name of proud alumni who have all enjoyed successful careers. The Pitts, Suarez and Hord facilities stand as gifts from individuals like you, the future proud alumni of Andrew College.

We invite you to explore Andrew College because success is your only option.
