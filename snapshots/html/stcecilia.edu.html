St. Cecilia is a private, Catholic, college preparatory school for high school young women, owned and administered by the Dominican Sisters of the St. Cecilia Congregation in Nashville, Tennessee. Founded in 1860, St. Cecilia encourages within each student academic excellence, creativity and development of leadership. The Academy prepares young women to meet the challenges of modern life, to contribute in a positive way to the society in which they live, and extends to students of all faiths the principles of Christian life in the Roman Catholic tradition.
