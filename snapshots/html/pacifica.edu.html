Welcome to Pacifica Graduate Institute


Pacifica Graduate Institute, with two beautiful campuses located between the coastal foothills and the Pacific Ocean a few miles south of Santa Barbara, California, is an accredited graduate school offering masters and doctoral degree programs in depth psychology, clinical psychology, counseling psychology, and mythologicial studies.

Pacifica has developed a convenient education format that enhances rigorous academic study while meeting the needs of individuals who wish to pursue graduate education while continuing their existing professional and personal commitments. 

Pacifica is currently accepting applications for fall 2012 enrollment. For admissions information, please call 805.969.3626, ext. 305.
