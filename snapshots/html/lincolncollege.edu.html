Two campuses. Two unique opportunities.

We provide students with the best educational experience possible and an opportunity to succeed. What makes Lincoln College special is our staff and their willingness to meet the individual needs of each and every student.

Lincoln College at Lincoln is a two-year private college offering a liberal arts foundation as well as athletic and fine arts programs. Lincoln College at Normal also offers associate degree programs as well as four-year programs and a cosmetology school. Both central Illinois campuses provide low student to faculty ratios and complete supportive services.

Take some time to explore all Lincoln College has to offer. We look forward to providing you with an exciting college experience!
