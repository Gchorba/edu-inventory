The mission of SES is to "train and prepare men and women based on the inerrant and infallible Word of God to evangelize the world and defend the historic Christian faith, with courtesy and respect." Central to this purpose is the provision of a biblical basis and an academic understanding of our commitment to Christ. While others are wavering on timeless truths, SES is standing strong for “the faith once delivered to the saints” (Jude3). This education will enable learners to fulfill their personal and professional goals as Christian leaders and as active citizens in the society in which they live. We invite you to join other SES students and graduates who are making an eternal impact for Christ. At SES you will be trained to think clearly, critically, and rationally and to engage people in meaningful dialogue. We have professors who will challenge your mind, strengthen your faith, and warm your hearts.  We take you seriously and hope you will take a serious look at SES.
