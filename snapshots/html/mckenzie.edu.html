Request information! McKenzie College is now accepting applications for Visual Arts Foundation, Graphic Design, Game Development and Photography & Video courses. Get your application or request more information.
Game Development This 10 month program will provide students with the skills necessary to build flash games and publish them online. You will learn how to design games, produce graphics, integrate sound and program inter-activity. You will also explore topics including game concept development, documentation, storyboarding and more! Find out more.
Graphic Design This 10 month program will help you develop your design and visual communication skills. You will learn how to use various Macintosh software programs to create brochures, corporate identities, product packaging, and newsletters for print, web and multimedia presentations. You will also learn how to develop an integrated visual identity package for a publicity and marketing campaign. Learn more.
Visual Arts Foundation This program is for aspiring artists and designers who would like to pursue visual arts programs and have an interest in developing their skills in drawing, painting, and design concepts. Students will learn by working in a creative studio environment that allows them to develop critical and creative thinking. Learn more.

Welcome to McKenzie College

We are Atlantic Canada's Art and Design school and the perfect place to find your creative voice. Located in the center of the Maritimes, our downtown Moncton campus is part of a vibrant bilingual arts community. We have a contemporary art gallery and ground-floor wheelchair accessible facilities. Highfield Square shopping center is only a block away, and we are on all major bus routes..
