Career training

The International College of Broadcasting offers Degrees in Radio / Television and Recording / Video, as well as a Broadcasting certificate program. Training in these fields leads to exciting career opportunities, such as disc jockey, sound engineer, production engineer, camera operator, video editor, contract writer, production assistant, and more!
