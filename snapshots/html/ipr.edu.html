IPR: Minneapolis Music Production & Recording School

At the Institute of Production and Recording (IPR) in Minneapolis, college is an experience, not a process. You will have the creative control you need to achieve your unique success in the music industry. Work when you're inspired—you'll have 24/7 studio access. Personalize your program with electives. Be part of the rich Minneapolis music scene and learn from industry pros. At IPR, your dream can become your life. 

Degree programs and Avid and Apple courses

Our Associate in Applied Science (AAS) in Media Arts 2-year degree programs provide strong foundations for careers in music business, audio engineering, sound art and related fields. We also offer single courses for Apple and Avid certification.

  • AAS: Audio Production and Engineering degree
  • AAS: Music and Entertainment Business degree
  • AAS: Sound Design for Visual Media degree
  • Avid Pro Tools courses
  • Apple Logic Pro courses

Contact us today to learn more

Live close by? Call toll-free: 1-866-477-4840 and schedule a tour of our Minneapolis, MN, campus. Far away? Check out our virtual tour, then call and ask about travel reimbursement. Or request more information from admissions.
