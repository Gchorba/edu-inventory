EXPECT MORE (You Should!)

What do you expect in your university experience? Here's what you can expect at MBU: We believe in in the importance and cultivation of character. We strive for excellence. We are serious and intentional about our Christian faith. We will freely and responsibly search for truth. We believe in social change through service and leadership.

(Your Life) CENTER STAGE

At MBU, the arts have earned a reputation for excellence. Life is extraordinary and so are the opportunities for expressing yourself. We believe in being faithful with more than just our beliefs and actions; we believe in using the gifts and talents with which God has uniquely gifted each person. We all play a part. Express yourself.

(Get in the) SPIRIT

Spartans are more than just champions; we are part of a community of integrity in competition. Spartans strive for excellence both in season and out of season as Champions of Character. Each team collaborates in a local service project annually. Why be a spectator, when you can experience the heat of competition?

(Know) BE KNOWN

Life and college are what you make of them. You get as much out of your time at MBU as you care to get out of it. The experience is completely a matter of what you want from it. If you want to experience new things, to be involved, and to grow as a person, then you will at MBU...and you'll have plenty of new friends for your personal journey.
