Leading photography school, New England School of Photography (NESOP) offers a full—time, two-year intensive Professional Photography Program for those seeking a career in photography and photographic workshops in a variety of disciplines at all levels-from basic camera instruction to advanced digital imaging. NESOP's Professional Photography Program is a balanced, comprehensive program focusing exclusively on photography to provide practical technical and business photographic training while nurturing the students's creative development. NESOP graduates enter the industry with two cohesive, professional portfolios (a major and a minor)-a competitive advantage unique to the NESOP education when compared to other photography schools. NESOP offers both digital photography and film photography classes and facilities.
