Why Choose Eastern International College?

Home

  • We offer programs designed to meet the needs of the competitive job market that allow you to receive your Associate in Applied Science degree (A.A.S.) and prepare you for the jobs that are in demand

  • Our students have the privilege of learning from seasoned faculty that have trained nationally and internationally, and are experts in their field 

  • With an impressive 10:1 student/faculty ratio, you’ll have immediate access to your administration and receive one-on-one attention from your professors in and out of the classroom

  • Our classrooms and laboratories are equipped with the latest state-of-the-art technology and supplies

  • Through our clinical externship program, you’ll receive hands-on training in a real-world setting and be exposed to potential employers

  • We have experienced financial aid representatives ready and waiting to help you determine how to pay for your education*

  • We embrace the “students first” philosophy and ensure that your education and success is our number one priority

*financial aid is available for those who qualify
