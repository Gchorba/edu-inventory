Start a new career

Austin's School of Spa Technology (formerly Austin Beauty School) has offered comprehensive training in the area of appearance enhancement for nearly 80 years. The Austin’s programs focus on development of the knowledge bases and skills that our students need, not only to pass the New York State Licensing exams, but also to become exemplary professionals in their chosen fields.

The cosmetology, barbering, nail technology or esthetics programs will allow you to pursue a career that is as individual and creative as you are. If you are looking for a program that values the practical as well as the artistic, the tried-and-true as well as the trendy, and the foundational as well as the fashionable, the Austin's programs are for you.
