Welcome

When you tour a Paul Mitchell School, you’ll experience a positive learning environment everywhere you look and a culture that inspires confidence and success. Our learning communities provide opportunities to develop essential skills while exploring your talents and creativity.

Why Paul Mitchell?

Thanks to the life and teachings of Paul Mitchell, the legendary hair designer, product innovator and salon educator, careers in hair design today can be both personally and financially rewarding. As a Paul Mitchell-trained cosmetology professional, you can realize your artistic dreams and work almost anywhere in the world.

About The Lab

Established 30 years ago, the Paul Mitchell network is nearly 100,000 salons strong and spans 81 countries worldwide, and every professional hairdresser knows the Paul Mitchell name. Now, with the arrival of Paul Mitchell beauty schools, we've taken the world of cosmetology education by storm.

Say YES to your future at a Paul Mitchell Cosmetology School!

Over the years, the staffs at Paul Mitchell Schools have built strong working relationships with salons around the world. Prospective employers recognize our commitment to quality and our reputation for innovation and excellence, with Paul Mitchell—trained hair designers working in virtually every corner of the world.

The Lab Ewing-NJ was designed to teach you the cosmetology skills you'll need, inspire you to explore your passion and creativity, and help you learn the business that will make your career in the beauty industry fun and rewarding.
