employment, and life-long learning. We seek to create a caring environment

LCC offering summer program for high school students

Published: Thursday - April 12, 2012 | Jesse Gallegos

program for upcoming classified juniors and seniors. For several years, LCC
has offered the Student Academic Advancement Institute (SAAI). This year, it
will run from Monday, June 4 to Friday, July 27.
