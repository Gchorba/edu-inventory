Healthcare Training and Degree Programs

Educating Students to Grow in the Healthcare Field

At Ultimate Medical Academy (UMA), healthcare training is all we do.
Since 1994, UMA has trained thousands of students for careers in the allied health field. Today, UMA continues to expand its wide-ranging portfolio of healthcare degrees and diploma programs, concentrating on the curricula, skills and certifications essential to meet the growing demand for qualified healthcare professionals.

According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics*, 10 of the 20 fastest-growing occupations are in healthcare. UMA‘s exclusive focus on short-term diplomas and associate degrees means that you can embark on a new healthcare career faster than you ever thought possible.

With healthcare training programs that feature the job skills you need, financial aid for those who qualify and a Career Services team, UMA can help you quickly achieve your healthcare education goals.

Career-Focused Courses, Online or on Campus

Whether you’re looking for the convenience of online healthcare training or the interaction of a traditional classroom, UMA has a variety of programs and schedules to meet your needs.

Located in Tampa and Clearwater, Florida, UMA’s campus-based healthcare degree and diploma programs offer convenient day, evening and weekend classes; while its flexible online learning platform allows students worldwide to access quality healthcare training – anytime and anywhere

Launch Your Healthcare Career Today

If you aspire to a professional and rewarding career in healthcare, UMA is a great place to start. UMA is accredited by the Accrediting Bureau of Health Education Schools (ABHES) – the only agency recognized by the U.S. Department of Education as both an institutional and specialized accreditor focused on healthcare training and education.

In addition to awarding a valuable degree or diploma, many of UMA’s programs prepare you to sit for an industry-recognized certification that may enhance your qualifications. Depending on your specialization, your program may also include an externship at a healthcare facility – providing you with hands-on experience that can distinguish you from other applicants.

Online Healthcare Programs for Military Families

UMA salutes the dedicated men and women of the U.S. military, and is committed to helping them achieve the brightest possible future. If you’re searching for a military-friendly school with online healthcare programs, you’ve found it. UMA makes it easy to continue your education and prepare for job opportunities in the allied health field, whether you’re looking to advance within the military or pursue a civilian career.

Contact UMA today to learn about the many campus-based and online healthcare training programs available. The path to your allied health career begins here and now!
