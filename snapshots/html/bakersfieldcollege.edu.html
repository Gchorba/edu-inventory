Pool Open for Swimming

Get fit in a fun and cool way before summer hits. Bakersfield College's pool is open to students and the community for Summer swimming. Check the pool's website for a full list of available times and appropriate fees.

Tour of California Coming

The AMGEN Tour of California is coming to Bakersfield College on Thursday, May 17. The Time Trials portion of the race will be hosted by Bakersfield College. Check our AMGEN Tour of California website for details on road closures, parking lot closures, and festivities information.
