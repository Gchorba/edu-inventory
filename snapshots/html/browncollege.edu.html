Welcome To Brown College

At Brown College we help students find their voice, create their future, and pursue success.
For over 60 years Brown College has been providing students with the technical knowledge, skills and hands-on training needed to stand out in their chosen fields. For example, 23 Brown College alumni have been inducted into the Pavek Broadcasting Hall of Fame. Those results show that radio broadcasting school can help take your career to new heights. Brown College is proud of the legacy that our radio broadcasting school has created. We invite you to join us as you are setting your own career goals.

From its origins, Brown College has grown its academic offerings to include programs in Design, Technology, Business Management, Criminal Justice and now Healthcare. With the exciting evolution of Brown College into the growing healthcare field, we are responding to the marketplace and providing additional opportunities for our students.* Today more than ever, Brown College is recognized for its personal commitment to its students and their success.
