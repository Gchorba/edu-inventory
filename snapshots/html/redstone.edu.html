combat college expenses

Redstone accepts a number of government-supported student aid programs on behalf of the military, like the Post-9/11 GI Bill, including its Yellow Ribbon Program provision.

Dedicated student advisors with military backgrounds can guide you through the student financing process, so you can focus your attention on our career training.

With over 1/3 of our student population as U.S. veteran or reserve, you'll be among friends.
