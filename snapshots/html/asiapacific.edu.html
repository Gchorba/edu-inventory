  • Established in 1996, Asia-Pacific Institute of Management ranks among the top Business schools in the Delhi region. Perceiving the tough challenges faced in the fast integrating global economy, the Institute strives to groom market leaders in different areas of management. With highly experienced faculty and world class infrastructure comparable to the best MBA colleges in India, Asia-Pacific aims to create a stimulated learning atmosphere conducive for overall development of its students. The Institute offers the best management courses to help realize managerial potential of each student.
