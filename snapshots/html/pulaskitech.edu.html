Follow Pulaski Tech:

 

Welcome to Pulaski Technical College, where student success is the highest priority.

Student success occurs when students accomplish their educational goals at Pulaski Tech and finish what they start. Pulaski Tech demonstrates its commitment to student success by offering a personalized, quality college education featuring small classes, cutting-edge technology and dedicated faculty and staff. Courses and programs are offered at seven locations throughout central Arkansas and online.

Students may choose from more than 76 associate degree and certificate programs designed for students who plan to transfer to four-year colleges and universities or to enter the workforce with marketable skills relevant to central Arkansas.
