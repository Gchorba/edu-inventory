The goal of The Paralegal Institute at Brighton College is to provide quality education to students seeking preparation for a career in their chosen field as a paralegal, a legal nurse consultant, criminal justice professions, or in an allied heath field. The programs of The Paralegal Institute at Brighton College will prepare you to begin your new career; not just to pass a certification exam. Your instructors are practicing professionals, specializing in their respective area and will share their expertise and experience ensuring that what you are learning is relevant and current in todayâ€™s world.

Programs are self-paced so you set your schedule. Open enrollment allows you to start your studies immediately. Lower costs and affordable tuition that include interest free payment plans allow you to pay as you go.

By enrolling in one of our programs, students will acquire competencies in one of these selected fields of study:

  • Paralegal Studies
  • Criminal Justice
  • Legal Nurse Consultant
  • Allied Health Programs

Allied health programs are offered through our main website and include medical coding, medical billing, pharmacy technician, medical records specialist, and an Associate degree in Health Services Management
