The Girl,s Fencing team defeated Staten Island Tech yesterday in both foil and epee to bring the current season record to 5-1.
Girl's Volleyball
There will be a mandatory meeting for all girls who are interested in trying out for the girls volleyball team for the 2012-2013 season. Please go to the 8th floor gymnasium after 9th period on Thursday May 3rd. If you are an incoming 9th grader please complete a medical and parental consent which can be downloaded off of the BTHS.edu website under athletics/forms. All information must be completed by your doctor and or parent before you can attend tryouts in August. There will be another post for incoming 9th graders with further details.
Boy's Tennis
