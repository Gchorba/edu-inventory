Welcome to Education Management Corporation. Our schools are dedicated to giving students the skills, tools and confidence they need for a lifetime of success. From preparing graduates for their first, exciting foray into the business world to helping busy professionals broaden their career possibilities, we provide flexible curriculum and scheduling, smaller classes with hands-on instruction and abundant personal attention and, most importantly, a practical education.
