Why Choose Genesis?

Genesis Career College was founded to provide an unparalleled educational experience to all students, equipping each with the skills they need to pursue a fulfilling and rewarding career. Our students are educated by accomplished professionals in a supportive environment that inspires confidence and success. Genesis Career College programs offer a well-rounded education by incorporating extensive hands-on learning with thorough classroom training.

At Genesis Career College, our goal is to provide the foundation of success by meeting the needs of every one of our students.

Accreditation

We take accreditation very seriously, find out more here.

Genesis Career College is authorized by the Tennessee Higher Education Commission. This authorization must be renewed each year and is based on an evaluation by minimum standards concerning quality of education, ethical business practices, health and safety, and fiscal responsibility.
