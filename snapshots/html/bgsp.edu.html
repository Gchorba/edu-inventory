Boston Graduate School of Psychoanalysis

BGSP is the only regionally accredited, degree granting, independent psychoanalytic graduate school in the country offering Master of Arts in Psychoanalysis, Master of Arts in Psychoanalytic Counseling, Doctoral programs in psychoanalysis, and programs in Psychoanalysis and Culture.  It is one of the few such institutes in the world.  BGSP also offers a One–Year Program through its continuing education department. This program is designed to enrich the work of clinical practitioners and professionals who are employed in various disciplines.

As a result of the revolutionary work of BGSP’s founders, access to a psychoanalytic education in this country no longer requires prior training in any other profession. BGSP’s doors are open to any capable and motivated post-bachelor’s student who is interested in understanding unconscious forces. More about Boston Graduate School of Psychoanalysis.
