Welcome to Moreno Valley College!

On March 1, 2010 the Board of Governors officially recognized Moreno Valley College as the 111th Community College in the state of California. Having received initial accreditation on January 29, 2010 from the Association for Community and Junior Colleges of the Western Association of Colleges, Moreno Valley College met the standards of an independent two year college. Moreno Valley College is now part of a multi college district of the Riverside Community College District alongside Riverside City College and Norco College.

The vision, hard work, and dedication of faculty, staff, students and the community has made this a reality. I am proud to be the President of the Moreno Valley College. We will strengthen our commitment and presence in Moreno Valley and the Inland Empire.

Learn more about Moreno Valley College on the About Us page.

MVC President

 

Accreditation

ACCJC notes that MVC has resolved Recommendations 1 and 3, from the Follow-Up Visit Report from November 2010.

We are eager to have the follow up visit in early November, 2010 to demonstrate our continued commitment to institutional assessment and excellence in student services. Visit the Accreditation Site for more information.

 

Mission Statement

Responsive to the educational needs of its region, Moreno Valley College offers academic programs and student support services which include baccalaureate transfer, professional, pre-professional, and pre-collegiate curricula for all who can benefit from them. Life-long learning opportunities are provided, especially, in health and public service preparation.
