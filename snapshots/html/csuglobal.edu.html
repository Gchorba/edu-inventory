CSU-Global Campus: A Public Online University

Before investing your time and money in an online degree, make sure you choose the right university, one that will open doors and advance your career. Colorado State University-Global Campus offers you something you can’t get from other online universities, a degree from the world class Colorado State University System. Employers will know you have a solid foundation of knowledge, the skills to grow with the company, and the passion to succeed. CSU-Global Campus is uniquely focused on 100% online university degree programs for working professionals. Classes start every four weeks and the enrollment process is streamlined so you can get started right away. Plus, as long as you are enrolled in courses for your degree, your tuition never goes up, guaranteed.

We are now enrolling for Spring 2012. Apply now to get started on earning your degree!

Online Bachelor’s Degrees and Online Master's Programs

Earning your Bachelor’s Degree online from CSU-Global will give you the advantages you need to compete in today's workforce. CSU-Global focuses on degree completion and will accept transfer credits from many regionally accredited colleges or universities.  Bachelor Degree Programs from CSU-Global provide relevant coursework and specialized training in a broad range of disciplines including Business Management, Information Technology, and many more online undergraduate degree programs currently available.

Obtaining a graduate degree from CSU-Global gives you the tools you need to take your career to the next level. Gaining education through online master’s degree programs demonstrates the advanced expertise and professional skills that current and future employers are looking for in their partners. Give yourself the professional edge with a Master’s Degree in Management, Organizational Leadership, Teaching & Learning, or one of the many other specializations from the leading online public university with CSU-Global. 

University Admissions

Starting the application process is easy! In most cases, you can be accepted into the online degree program of your choice and start taking online university courses when the next eight-week session begins. Bachelor’s completion programs as well as Certificates of Completion and Master’s programs are available in a number of specialized fields, and CSU-Global Campus has a dedicated team of advisors to help in all phases of your university career.  

Tuition Rates and Financial Assistance

At CSU-Global Campus, we offer competitive guaranteed tuition rates that won’t increase as long as you are enrolled in your degree program, and there is no out-of-state tuition hikes as CSU-Global is 100% online. As a not-for-profit, public university that is a part of the renowned Colorado State University System, CSU-Global has received independent regional  accreditation by the Higher Learning Commission (HLC) to ensure the best possible education for you. Invest in your future with the affordable, intelligent choice in CSU-Global.

Students of CSU-Global Campus’ online university programs may be eligible for Financial Aid to help pay the costs associated with continuing your education. Scholarships, Grants, Loans, and other financial aid programs are available to help reduce overall costs and defer payments until after you complete your degree.

Mission Statement

CSU-Global Campus is committed to advancing student success in a global society, investing in human capital, expanding the state economy, and enhancing the quality of life for citizens in the state of Colorado and beyond by providing access to dynamic degree programs characterized by academic excellence, innovative delivery technologies, and strong stakeholder engagement.

Our Core Values: Demonstrate Inclusiveness and Diversity

Public Statement on the Value of Diversity: CSU-Global Campus recognizes the indivisible tie between the diversity of the University’s administrators, faculty, staff, and student body and the experience of integrity of the organization. Diversity is not an abstract concept for CSU-Global; our people represent diversity of ethnicity; age; gender; and socio-economic background.
