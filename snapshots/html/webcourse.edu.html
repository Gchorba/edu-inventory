Free Access for Pennsylvania Schools

As part of a special program, WebCourse is being made available, at no cost, to a limited number of Pennsylvania high schools and middle schools starting January 2012. If your school is interested in setting up an account, please contact us at free@WebCourse.edu.

WebCourse has several built-in features that were developed specifically for high school students and teachers. We look forward to working with you.
