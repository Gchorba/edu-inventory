Wall Maximizes Time in High School with Dual Enrollment at JSCC

Humboldt native now Milan resident, Luke Wall, is maximizing his time as a Dual Enrollment student at Jackson State Community College (JSCC). The Dual Enrollment plan allows students to earn college credits that count toward both a college degree and their high school diploma.
