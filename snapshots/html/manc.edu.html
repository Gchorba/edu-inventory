May 5, 6

Commencement 2012

Join us on May 5 & 6, 2012, in College Church of the Nazarene Sanctuary for the 41st Commencement and to celebrate the Class of 2012. Undergraduates account for 467 graduates while graduate and post-grad certifications account for 213 making a total of 680. Congratulations, seniors!
