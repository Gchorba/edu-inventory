News and Events



New Dean of Applied Technologies a Former CNM Student
John Bronisz, who first experienced CNM classrooms as a student, has been named the new dean of the School of Applied Technologies. Read more.

Communication, Humanities & Social Sciences Names New Dean
Erica Volkers has been named the new dean of the CNM School of Communication, Humanities & Social Sciences (CHSS). She enters in to her new role after serving as associate dean of CHSS since 2011. Read more.

Student Leader Earns Scholarship for Service and Academics
Cesar Silva was recently awarded a $1,000 scholarship from the New Mexico National Guard Enlisted Association to further his education. Silva will be celebrating his graduation from CNM on Saturday (April 28) at noon during CNM’s Spring Graduation Ceremony at Tingley Coliseum, then he'll transfer to the University of New Mexico to pursue a degree in Electrical Engineering. Read more.

Graduates Honored at Spring Ceremony
On April 28, CNM celebrated 525 students as they became graduates, setting a record for the largest spring graduating class seen by the college. To watch a video recapping the graduation ceremony, click here.
