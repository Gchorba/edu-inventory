About Tyndale

Tyndale Theological Seminary & Biblical Institute is located in the Dallas/Fort Worth Metroplex in Texas. We have several learning centers worldwide and offer multiple learning formats to make Biblical teaching available to everyone.

Our mission is to prepare godly believers for the ministry of exegetical and expository teaching of the word of God for effective and spiritually mature leadership and service worldwide.
