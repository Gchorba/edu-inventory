    Which do you prefer?

    If you chose classroom-based learning, please visit one of our Academic Partners.

    If you prefer to be an independent scholar, you've come to the right place. At Warnborough, you can make the way you learn fit the lifestyle you want. With books, motivation, and the Internet, you can gain knowledge and earn a qualification at your own pace without any lapse in quality. Learn more.

     

    New to Distance Learning?

    Here are some reasons for studying this way.

    Distance Learning Excellence

    Whether you are looking for online learning or just learning at a distance, Warnborough College provides you with a variety of quality programs.

    Some of the features include:

    • Start on the first of any month
    • Options to tailor-make your own program
    • Research, projects, modules, or publication options
    • Flexible tuition payment schemes
    • Top faculty who care about your learning experience

    450 Vocational and Technical Short Courses

    Warnborough College UK has spun off its vocational and technical short courses to the Academy for Distance Learning (ADL) and to Short Courses Ireland. ADL and SCI offer over 450 short courses, certificates, diplomas and other other qualifications by home study and distance learning.
