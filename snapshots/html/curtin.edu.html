Perth, Australia

Our main campus is in Bentley, Western Australia, 6km south-east of the state’s capital, Perth. Our other Western Australian locations include the Graduate School of Business in central Perth, and several regional locations.

Sydney, Australia

Our Sydney campus gives students the opportunity to live and study in one of the most beautiful cities in the world.

Singapore

Curtin Singapore gives students access to a flexible, superb education in one of Asia’s most important business centres.

Sarawak, Malaysia

Our Malaysian campus is located in Sarawak, on the northwest coast of the island of Borneo.

Offshore partner institutions

In addition to the above locations, we have partner institutions in China, Hong Kong, Malaysia, Mauritius, Singapore, Sri Lanka and Vietnam where you can study a Curtin degree. Learn more.
