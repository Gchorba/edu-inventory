Prospective Student Guide

Southern States University (SSU) is a private American university founded in 1983. With several campuses in San Diego and one in Newport Beach, SSU is focused on training business-oriented professionals from around the world. The University offers a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) degree, a Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree and a Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages (TESOL) Certificate Program.

In addition, SSU offers TOEFL test preparation course and intensive beginner through advanced English courses for students who do not yet possess the English skill level necessary to begin their university studies, as well as for those non-native English speakers who wish to improve their English proficiency.

To assist those students whose intention is to pursue a certificate or degree, SSU can customize a program that includes degree program classes plus intensive English study, to adequately prepare them to succeed with their higher education studies.

The University received national accreditation from the Accrediting Council for Independent Colleges and Schools (ACICS) in 2010. Southern States University is authorized to issue the legal documents required to obtain a student Visa (SEVIS approved I-20 Form) by the Department of Homeland Security (DHS).

NEW ACADEMIC STUDY CENTER!

Southern States University’s move to its new Fashion Valley location is remarkable for affording its students an opportunity to experience learning and take classes in a more campus-like environment.  In addition to more spacious classrooms, delineated administrative offices and improved library facilities SSU provides students with improved access to both University personnel and resources.   It is in this spirit of recognizing SSU’s commitment and dedication to its students’ betterment through education that SSU is pleased to announce the opening of a new Academic Study Center at its new Fashion Valley campus.

This academic area contains both separate study booths for SSU students to work independently as well as tables for groups of students to study and work together.   Equally important, wireless Internet access is available, so that students may freely access University resources such as the University’s electronic library subscription databases and the University’s learning portal, Moodle.  SSU’s Chief Academic Officer, Dr. Stephanie Dieringer, cannot be more excited about the opening of this new space, “With the dedication of this space SSU affirms its focus on assisting and supporting students in their academic studies.  As the University continues to grow, we are committed to enhancing the culture of academic excellence that was pivotal in SSU achieving accreditation. ”

Located in room 307, this study area is open Monday thru Thursday from 8.30 am to 9:30 pm, Friday from 8:30 am to 5:30 pm, and Saturday from 9 am to 6 pm, making it a very convenient place for SSU students to get prepared for their classes.
