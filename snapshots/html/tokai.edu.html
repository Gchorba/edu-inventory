Pursuing Advancements in Research and Education

The Tokai University Educational System (TES) has three universities: Tokai University, Kyushu Tokai University and Hokkaido Tokai University; three junior colleges in Japan and an American junior college in Hawaii. Read More

Head Office of Intellectual Property Management

In 2003, Tokai University, Kyushu Tokai University, and Hokkaido Tokai University were chosen by the Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology as model schools at which the ministry could establish the University Intellectual Property Headquarters. Read More

Overseas Education Cruise (OSEC)
