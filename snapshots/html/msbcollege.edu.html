Minnesota Business School and Career College

Minnesota School of Business and Globe University have, since the 19th century, provided students with tools for professional success. The colleges today work together to prepare students for careers in business, health, technology, legal and creative fields. Programs are offered online and at campuses across the Midwest.

On campus and online degree programs

We meet the needs of students at various points in their careers, offering master’s, bachelor’s and associate degrees and diplomas. Every program provides employer-requested career education. Choose from more than 50 options, including the following:

  • Business: accounting, business administration, business management
  • Creative media: digital video, music business, graphic design
  • Health science: massage therapy, medical assisting, veterinary technology, personal trainer education 
  • Technology: networking, security, web applications, server administration, game design, architectural and engineering drafting and design 
  • Legal science: criminal justice, paralegal

130 years of experience, focused on your success

It is our goal to help you achieve personal and professional success. Our formula is time-tested and simple: small class sizes, hands-on learning, faculty with high professional and academic credentials, and above all, personal attention. We invite you to request more information or call 1-877-655-7676 to arrange a visit to one of our convenient campuses.

Globe University/Minnesota School of Business is accredited by Accrediting Council for Independent Colleges and Schools to award doctoral degrees, master’s degrees, bachelor of fine arts degrees, bachelor of science degrees, associate in applied science degrees and diplomas.

Working collaboratively with fellow Globe Education Network member Minnesota School of Business, the university offers college degree programs online and on campus in locations in the Minneapolis-St. Paul region (the IDS Center, Blaine, Brooklyn Center, Plymouth, Richfield, Elk River, Lakeville and Woodbury), Rochester, Shakopee, St. Cloud and Moorhead, Minnesota (MN); Appleton, Eau Claire, Green Bay, La Crosse, Middleton, Madison and Wausau, Wisconsin (WI); and Sioux Falls, South Dakota (SD). Our online program is world-wide, and our campuses serve students from Upper Midwest communities including Fargo, North Dakota (ND) and Bloomington, Eagan, White Bear Lake, Coon Rapids, Eden Prairie, Burnsville, Roseville, Maplewood, Minnetonka and Maple Grove, MN.
