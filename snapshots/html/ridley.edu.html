Welcome to Ridley-Lowell

Ridley-Lowell offers career training programs to get you started in a new career, designed around the skills employers want! Our New York and Connecticut business, technical, and trade schools can help transition you to a new career in:

  • Accounting
  • Cosmetology
  • Criminal Justice
  • Electrical
  • Esthetics
  • IT - Information Technology
  • Legal Assisting
  • Massage Therapy
  • Medical Administration
  • Medical Assisting
  • Medical Billing and Coding
  • Office Administration

With our long-standing history in the community within the technical and business fields, our graduates benefit from an education at Ridley-Lowell Business & Technical Institute.
