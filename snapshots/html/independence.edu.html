Distance Education for Working Adults

For more than 30 years, the distance learning programs at Independence University have helped working adults like you to get the educational credentials they need. We offer distance learning courses in both undergraduate and graduate level programs in healthcare and business. Our experienced staff is prepared to assist you throughout your distance education experience.
