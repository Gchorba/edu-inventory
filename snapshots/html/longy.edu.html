Longy School of Music of Bard College

Perform. Teach. Think.

Located in vibrant Harvard Square, Longy School of Music is a degree-granting Conservatory that also offers Community Programs for children and adults. Our mission: Preparing musicians to make a difference in the world.

Along with classes and degree programs, Longy’s public concert schedule includes dozens of programs each year ranging from baroque to jazz. 
SPOTLIGHT

Annie composer Charles Strouse visits Longy

Simon Linn-Gerstein (MM '13) awarded Creativity Foundation’s 2012 Legacy Medal for exceptional creative promise as a cellist and composer. Choreographer Mark Morris presented the award to Linn-Gerstein in Washington, DC this month.

Violinists Yura Lee and Jesse Mills join Longy's faculty.

Daniela DeMatos (MM '13) named winner of the inaugural Radius-Longy Composition Competition.
