Accredited University Serving the Mountain States

Broadview University is committed to helping students realize their professional goals. We focus on career-specific education, preparing students for successful futures in a wide variety of business, medical, legal, technology and creative fields. Contact us today for more information.

Expanding horizons through education

Success in the workplace can be measured by degrees—master’s, bachelor’s, associate—which one has your name on it? We offer over 30 programs that can help you get into a new career quickly or improve your skills for advancement in your current profession. Now is the time to expand your horizons.

Flexible, affordable and convenient

Concerns about class schedules, commutes, time commitments, and finances are often cited as obstacles to continuing education. As a career college, we know how to accommodate the busy lives of students:

  • Flexibility: we offer classes days, evenings and online. Degree programs can be completed on campus or through distance learning.
  • Convenience: we have multiple campus locations and an online learning division. Going to school couldn’t be easier.
  • Affordability: our financial aid department will help you find scholarship, grant and loan opportunities to create a reasonable payment plan.

Are you ready to take the next step?

We're proud to carry on a 30-year tradition of career-education excellence. If you’re ready to continue your education or if you need more information, call Broadview University today at 1-877-480-3335 or contact a representative.

Broadview University (formerly Utah Career College) is nationally accredited by Accrediting Council for Independent Colleges and Schools to award master's degrees, bachelor of science degrees, bachelor of fine arts degrees, associate in applied science degrees and diplomas.

We serve students and employers from communities throughout the Mountain States region, including Salt Lake City, West Jordan, Orem, Layton, Ogden, Provo, Sandy, Taylorsville, West Valley City and Bountiful, Utah.
