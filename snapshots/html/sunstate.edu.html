If you're a creative, imaginative person who likes being around people, why not seek cosmetology, massage therapy or nail technician training?

For over 35 years, Sunstate Academy has provided graduates cosmetology training in the state of Florida. Sunstate Academy is a Redken Premier School with qualified instructors who have Redken professional training and salon experience.

A combination of theory and hands-on training teaches you what you need to know to begin a career as a professional hair stylist, massage therapist or nail technician. Qualified, experienced instructors at Sunstate Academy can teach you how to capture your vision and turn your enthusiasm into a rewarding career.
