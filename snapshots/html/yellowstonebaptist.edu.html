Welcome to Yellowstone Baptist College!

Yellowstone Baptist College (YBC) is an exciting place with a bright and promising future. Located in Billings, Montana, in the middle of the Big Sky Country about 100 miles northeast of Yellowstone National Park, the school is the newest college in the Southern Baptist family.
