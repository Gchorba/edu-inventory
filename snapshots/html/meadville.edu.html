At Meadville Lombard Theological School, we educate students in the
Unitarian Universalist tradition to embody liberal religious ministry
in Unitarian Universalist congregations
and wherever else they are called to serve.

We do this in order to take into the world our Unitarian Universalist vision
of justice, equity and compassion.
