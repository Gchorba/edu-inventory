As a student at the Vet Tech Institute of Houston, located in Houston, TX, you can earn your Associate of Applied Science in Veterinary Technician degree (AAS) in only 18 months (for those attending full time).

During the veterinary technician program, you'll:

-Attend a veterinary technician program that is accredited by the American Veterinary Medical Association (AVMA) Committee on Veterinary Technician Education and Activities (CVTEA) .

-Have on-going access to veterinary facilities that include a full kennel (housing a range of animals), diagnostic and radiological labs, and prep and surgical rooms.

-Spend time at a large animal farm so you can practice working with large animals such as horses, cows, and sheep.

-Benefit from the Vet Tech Institute’s student services such as financial aid, housing assistance, and career placement.

Learn more about how you can begin your career as a veterinary technician at the Vet Tech Institute at Houston.
