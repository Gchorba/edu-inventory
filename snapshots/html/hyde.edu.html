Adventures Await! Hyde’s Summer Sessions are Open for Registration

Hyde Schools run summer programs on both the Bath, Maine and Woodstock, Connecticut campuses. This summer there are 18 different sessions to choose from, including White Water Rafting, Songwriting, and Community Service. The programs run from July 1 – 29 and students can sign up for just one week or all four. CHOOSE YOUR SESSIONS

Meet Hyde in Bath, ME or Woodstock, CT

Hyde will be holding Open Houses on our Bath, Maine campus on May 12th and on our Woodstock, Connecticut campus on May 19th. Open Houses are from 1:00 P.M. to 3:00 P.M. They are excellent opportunities to meet our admission staff, faculty, and students, as well as learn about our adventurous summer programs for students ages 13-18. REGISTER TODAY

‘Life Long Journey’ – An Exploration of Our Unique Potential

Members of the Hyde Parent Alumni Association (HAPA) and parents of senior Hyde students are invited to attend a parent summer retreat July 13-15 on the Bath campus or July 20-22 on the Woodstock campus. Join us for this opportunity to step out of your busy life, meet new friends, rekindle old friendships, and reflect on life’s purpose and journey. REGISTRATION & MORE INFORMATION

Hyde Woodstock Reunion May 25, 26 & 27

All Woodstock alumni take note! You are invited to the 2012 Woodstock reunion. We are celebrating the class of 1997′s fifteen year reunion, the class of 2002′s ten year reunion, and the class of 2007′s five year reunion, but all alumni are welcome to attend. REGISTER today!

    Hyde Schools is a network of college preparatory schools dedicated to the development of character and leadership in students and families. The Hyde New England boarding schools, located in Woodstock, Connecticut and Bath, Maine, focus on helping students in grades 9 - 12 and post-graduate studies, reveal their character and develop the confidence they need through self-discovery, rigorous academics, and parental involvement. Hyde School students are uniquely prepared to navigate the tough choices they face in everyday life.
