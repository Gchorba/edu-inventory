JP Morgan Scholarship

Every year JP Morgan offers scholarship to the bright finance students of SIMS. JPMorgan's Launching Leaders is about recognizing and rewarding exceptional students - people with unique experiences and diverse backgrounds. Selection is based on academic performance and a case study presentation and only those students are selected who demonstrate intelligence, ambition and a serious interest in global finance.
Deloitte Human Capital Case Study Competition

Deloitte conducts an annual case study competition at SIMS wherein senior business leaders working across the globe come and judge the caliber of our student managers.

Here, students are encouraged to think and work under the conditions prevalent in the consulting industry pertaining to challenges, mergers, acquisitions, divestitures and change management.
Centre for Corporate Governance

SIMS is the only accredited Centre for Corporate Governance of the National Foundation for Corporate Governance in Pune. The NFCG has been established by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs and CII with support from ICAI and ICSI.

The Centres singular focus is to enhance knowledge on important aspects of good Corporate Governance norms amongst the senior management and boards of companies through dissemination of appropriate programs in association with experienced persons practicing in the field... More

SPSS
