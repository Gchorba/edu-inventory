The Atlanta University Center is a non-profit organization created to provide services and programs to its member institutions. These institutions; Clark Atlanta University, Morehouse College, Morehouse School of Medicine, and Spelman College share a common mission as members of the Consortium to provide quality education for African Americans and other students of diversity.
