Welcome to Hood

What is your call to ministry?

Hood Theological Seminary is a graduate school that prepares men and women for transformational leadership in Christian ministry. This site describes programs that can help you to...
      ...pastor a local congregation (MDiv)

      ...serve in a specialized ministry such as Christian Education or Counseling (MTS)

      ...explore theological questions for personal enrichment (MTS)

      ...lay a foundation for a PhD program elsewhere (MDiv or MTS)

      ...retool yourself for more senior church leadership (DMin)

     

Hood is an ecumenical seminary in the African Methodist Episcopal Zion tradition. Our students and faculty represent all the pan-Methodist churches, as well as many other Christian traditions.

If you are employed full-time, our Track II weekend program can provide a theological education that will suit your schedule. If you do not have a bachelor's degree, you may wish to explore our Diploma in Christian Ministry program.

Your Gift to Hood
Your financial support for Hood Theological Seminary conveys a spirit of confidence, friendship and appreciation for the mission of Hood Theological Seminary. The Seminary depends on the abiding generosity of alumni, friends, foundations and the AME Zion Church. Your financial contribution, regardless of the dollar amount, is greatly appreciated by Hood Theological Seminary. You may wish to support the Seminary's annual fund, endowment and scholarship giving, capital campaigns or consider leaving a bequest to Hood Theological Seminary through planned giving. Click on the links to learn how the Seminary benefits from your gift in each of these areas.
