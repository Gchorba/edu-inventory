Overview
A mainstay in the culinary field since its founding in 1975, The Institute of Culinary Education is one of the most active and engaged culinary schools in New York City. A trusted resource and profound influencer in the culinary arts, ICE offers a wide range of culinary courses, including culinary career programs and recreational cooking classes, as well as continuing culinary education and professional development in the culinary arts. From career-minded aspiring culinary professionals to novice chefs, ICE's cooking schools and programs have something delicious to offer everyone. ICE is proud to have been recognized with the IACP Award of Excellence in 2008 for its dedication to the culinary arts, and it is honored to be counted among America's top culinary schools.
