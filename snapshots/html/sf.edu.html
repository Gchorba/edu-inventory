Lecture Series Launched

Hundreds of people gathered in the new USF Performing Arts Center in downtown Fort Wayne for "The Future of Nursing," which launched a series of free educational lectures in the facility. Nursing and medical professionals from across the state contributed to an expert panel, a USF Sim Lab demonstration took place and a health careers job fair was available.

SOCA students Shine

Students in the School of Creative Arts won 37 ADDY awards, including two prestigious Judges’ Choice awards, from the American Ad Federation of Fort Wayne at the organization’s award ceremony.

Softball in full swing

USF's softball team is batting over .500 in wins for the season. Check out the action on the athletics website, or drop by the field to take in a game!

Steel Magnolias

USF raised the curtain on its first show in the new USF Performing Arts Center, "Steel Magnolias," March 23-24 and March 30-April 1. Theater-goers experienced laughter through tears during the show, with their enjoyment enhanced greatly by the beautiful auditorium of the former Scottish Rite Center.
