Interior Design Senior Projects


Seniors majoring in interior design at Park University will exhibit their projects and portfolios during "First Fridays" on Friday, May 4, from 5 to 7 p.m. at Encompas, 1512 Grand in Kansas City's Crossroads Arts District.
ICM Piano Recitals


Park University's International Center for Music will host a series of recital concerts featuring piano students of Stanislav Ioudenitch, Friday-Monday, May 4-7, in Graham Tyler Memorial Chapel on the Parkville Campus. All of the recitals begin at 7:30 p.m., and are free and open to the public. More
Ibragimov Cello Recital


Sunnat Ibragimov will hold his graduate cello recital on Wednesday, May 9, starting at 7 p.m. in Graham Tyler Memorial Chapel on the Parkville Campus. The concert is free and open to the public. More
