Our Mission Statement

Hays Academy of Hair Design strives to provide a progressive cosmetology education that will prepare students for a career in cosmetology while enhancing maximum personal potential.

Get Started Today!

More than you ever imagined in a beauty school

Advanced Hair Color, Advanced Cutting & Finishing, Business Training

Hays Academy has developed a reputation from the east coast to the west coast for being a top notch cosmetology school. Our students are experiencing tremendous success in the salon from the start. Many people think that a top notch education can't be delivered in such a quaint community, however we have proved the skeptics wrong! We challenge you to compare us to the others as we are confident that you will be excited about what you learn. We have partnered with some of the premier names in the industry as we challenge ourselves to produce some of the TOP graduates in the country! We are passionate about what we do and it all starts with YOU, the student! Come in and see us today for your personal tour of our facility.

Hays Academy of Hair Design

opened its doors on April 1st, 1981. In January of 2003, Summer Melvin and Danielle Markley (sisters) purchased the school with the vision of offering the community professional salon services while providing the students with an up to date education that will enable them to thrive in the beauty industry.
