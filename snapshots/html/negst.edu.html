Welcome to Africa International University. AIU is a christian chartered University in Kenya and is the successor of Nairobi Evangelical Graduate School of Theology, NEGST. AIU has three schools namely, NEGST, ISAR and SPS.AIU is located on a 54 acre campus along Karen - Dagoretti Road, Kenya.
