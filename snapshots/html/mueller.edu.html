Mueller College is an accredited instiutution that has specialized in the training of holistic health practitioners for over 30 years. The College offers an alcohol and drug counseling program, personal fitness trainer program, massage therapy programs and a holistic health practitioner program featuring a wide range of holistic health subjects such as herbology, reflexology, Reiki, nutrition, and Eastern and Asian modalities (Shiatsu, Tuina, Ayurvedic therapies, Thai massage and more).

Students come to Mueller College to become successful, independent practitioners in as little as nine months, and graduate with all the tools to start - or change - their careers. From an emphasis on business and career development to assistance with placement in our Community Wellness Center, Mueller College graduates are prepared for real-world experience.

In private practice and cruise ships, spa, gyms and doctors' offices, our graduates use their skills to help others. Mueller College also offers one of the only hospital-based training programs in the U.S. for students interested in a traditional healthcare setting.

DID YOU KNOW?

The Mueller College personal fitness trainer program includes an exciting boot camp experience in Las Vegas, Nevada where students learn from well-known industry professionals and train for the NSCA national certifcation examination!

Mueller College offers a Complementary and Integrative Health (CIH) course - one of the few in the nation to offer holistic health students an opportunity to gain experience in a traditional hospital setting!

Mueller College has classes in a wide range of Asian bodywork modalities such as tuina, shiatsu, chi nei tsang, Thai massage and more! You can learn these skills by taking individual classess or in our Holistic Health Practitioner program.

Mueller College’s Community Wellness Center gives students a chance to work with the public, and gives graduates a place to work and build a clientele!

Mueller College offers a wide variety of events, lectures, workshops and seminars - many of which can be used for continuing education units (CEU)!
