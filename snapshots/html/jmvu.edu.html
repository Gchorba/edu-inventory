Education for a Changing World
Education for a Global Society


  

José María Vargas University is part of the "Complejo Educativo Parra Díaz," a higher education multiplex committed to the development of bilingual professionals for the world. Its strength lies in a common vision to provide education that promotes pluralism, solidarity, responsibility, honesty and integrity.

Dual Language Instruction at JMVU!

   

 At JMVU academic instruction is presented in both English and Spanish preparing students for a successful future in the multicultural world in which we live.
