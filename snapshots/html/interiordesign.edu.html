Harrington College of Design is an integrated, collaborative, professional design school within the cultural and architectural capital of Chicago. Offering undergraduate and graduate degrees in interior design, graphic design, communication design, and commercial photography, Harrington employs a faculty of experienced professionals to prepare graduates to hit the ground running in these dynamic fields. Our school is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and a member of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools.
