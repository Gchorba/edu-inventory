Be extraordinary. That’s our motto at Medtech. What do we mean by extraordinary? We believe in going beyond the expected to deliver an exceptional health care education experience that can be truly life-changing, for your career and for you.

If you’re ready to be extraordinary, choose the experts. At Medtech, health care education is what we’re all about. Within our community of technical schools across Indianapolis, Indiana; Kentucky; Atlanta, Georgia; Washington, D.C.; Maryland; and Virginia, you’ll discover outstanding allied health programs like medical assisting, phlebotomy and nursing, and even English as a Second Language classes (escuelas de ingles), all designed to prepare you for a position in healthcare that allows your skills to shine.

At Medtech, you’re always a person with unique needs – not just a name or a number. From the start, you can experience our nurturing, dynamic environment, focused on helping you attain your goals. As a Medtech student, you can expect small, intimate classroom settings and experienced instructors who take care to ensure all of your questions are answered. Not to mention our accessible campus locations, friendly staff and financial aid specialists. Some Medtech College and Medtech Institute locations are formerly Sanz School, Sanz College and Javelin Technical Training Center. However, each campus shares the same unique values that make Medtech extraordinary.

Whether you are looking for nursing schools, healthcare schools, ESL schools, or are just looking for a technical school that specializes in medical training, consider Medtech! You’ll learn by doing – hands-on – giving you the knowledge and confidence to succeed in your career. At our nursing schools and healthcare schools, we not only teach, we also learn your hopes and plans – so as you complete your education, we can provide personalized assistance with your search for a career that is in demand.

You can create the future you want for yourself and your loved ones. At Medtech, our medical training prepares you for more than just a career; we give you the life-building tools to be extraordinary. So why be ordinary in your career, when you can be extraordinary? Request more information or call us today at 1-877-287-1612 to learn more about our extraordinary healthcare education!
