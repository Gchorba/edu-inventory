Important Notices

Attention All Registered Students!

The College has "gone green" and is no longer mailing paper tuition bills and schedules. View your bill through your email icon on delaGATE. You can pay by check or credit card online, too. Summer I payment is due May 9.

Click here

Registration for Summer I, Summer II and Fall 2012 is now open

Class sections fill quickly so register today. New students must apply for admission first. Students who took classes within the last year can register via delaGATE. Current students should contact their advisor as soon as possible to ensure they’re taking the right classes.
