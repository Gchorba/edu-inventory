ICC Hawaii offers a variety of programs for those seeking to learn or improve their English. Whether pursuing admission into a US college or university or simply seeking to improve your English Language skills, ICC’s small classes, well qualified teachers, friendly staff and comfortable learning environment offers programs to meet the needs of most students.

Our most popular courses are offered under our General English for Communication program which has 5 levels. Through our Cambridge test preparation programs, students can earn the prestigious Cambridge certificates which are recognized worldwide. Our English for Business program is taught by weekly topics and can be brought to any location around the world as a one week course or a weekend seminar. Finally, ESL teachers can upgrade their instructional skills through the Cambridge CELTA teacher training program.

From registration to graduation, ICC strives to give students the best English study experience in Hawaii. Our school is located just minutes from Iolani Palace, Aloha Tower, the Hawaii State Library, and the
Hawaii State Art Museum. Students can enjoy the beautiful scenery and unique culture of Hawaii with our fun weekly activities. Also, ICC Hawaii is authorized to issue I-20 forms for visa purposes.

This safe, friendly environment is perfect for students from all over the world to prepare for academic or professional success!
