History of Florida Metropolitan University

Everest University has a long history in the Sunshine State. The oldest Everest University campus can trace its roots to 1890 when the Tampa campus became the first business college in Florida, under the name Tampa College. It became Florida Metropolitan University in 1996 and ultimately Everest University in 2007. The name change allowed the Florida campuses to become part of a nationally recognized name. Everest offers programs at more than 85 campuses throughout the United States.

The Everest University Difference

Flexible Class Schedules: Many of our campuses offer morning, afternoon, evening and weekend schedules,* which means you can take the courses that fit your lifestyle. We understand how hectic life can be and how little time there is in the day to get things done. Flexible classroom training encourages participation and teamwork interaction from each of our students.

Hands-on Experience: We think it's critical that you practice what you're learning. This is why it's paramount for our programs to incorporate and provide hands-on training. We're confident that you'll learn better and quicker this way.

Professional Instructors: Everest instructors are men and women who've been there and done that! You'll benefit tremendously from the personal experience and knowledge they bring to the classroom each day. We don't just hire any instructor. We require them to be qualified with industry-specific knowledge and expertise. This means you'll have access to real-world knowledge and the practical insights that count.

Online Classes: Taking online classes can give you the freedom and flexibility to complete class assignments on your own schedule. Pursuing an online degree can help you avoid traffic and other hassles normally associated with getting to and from school. When you're self motivated and in control of when and how you learn—you can actually learn a great deal more.

Business Programs

Many popular business jobs are in sales, office administration, small business management, marketing, and human resources. Through Everest's Business Program, you can acquire skills that may prepare you for a job in these areas.

Learn skills that you can apply directly to the corporate world. Our business program can strengthen your decision-making ability and may help improve your planning and organization skills. With our well-rounded business curriculum, you can get hands-on experience in fields such as computer training and accounting.

The Everest Business Program is not just for those just getting started; it's also for those who want to build on the skills they already have.

The business world is fast-paced and highly competitive, and Everest's Business Program can give you that edge needed to succeed.

Everest can answer your questions. Fill out an application today and be on your way to a successful future.

Paralegal

Help make a difference by helping to defend those in need. Through Everest's Paralegal program, students are taught the skills to perform tasks such as researching legal issues, drafting legal documents, preparing pleadings, summarizing documents, organizing trial notebooks, and interviewing clients.

At Everest, you are given the training to work along side lawyers and in the courtroom. Students can learn legal terminology, and legal activities such as legal research skills and procedures for pleadings and motions.

Everest's Paralegal program can put you on track for a successful future as a legal assistant.

Several Paralegal entry-level career opportunities include:

  • Junior Corporate Paralegal
  • Family Law Paralegal
  • Law Clerk

Online degree courses are also offered in Business, Accounting, Medical Insurance Billing and Coding, Criminal Justice, Paralegal and much more. There's nothing like having the flexibility to learn at your own pace and on your own time and schedule.

Medical Assistant

There is a growing need for medical assistants*, and Everest's Medical Assistant Program helps prepare students for the health care field. This program allows you to complete your training without spending years in medical school.

Students are given quality training so they can be confident in what they do in the office. Students are taught how to properly prepare and administer injections, chart a patient's medical history, and schedule appointments.

Some roles that the medical assistant performs include taking and recording height, weight, temperature, and blood pressure information.

Pharmacy Technician

Become a Pharmacy Technician and work alongside licensed pharmacists. Everest's Pharmacy Technician Program gives students the education and understanding they need to be successful in the workplace.

Students are trained in the classroom and in the lab and gain valuable hands-on experience. They can also become quite familiar with pharmaceutical calculations.

Our main goal for the Pharmacy Technician Program is to provide the student with the skills and knowledge so they can qualify for entry-level positions as a Pharmacy Technician. Everest's program can put you on track for a successful future as a Pharmacy Technician.

Pharmacy technicians often seek employment positions in health care facilities, retail establishments, hospitals, medical centers, outpatient clinics, urgent care centers and pharmacies.

Several entry-level career opportunities may include:

  • Pharmacy Assistant
  • Hospital Pharmacy Technician
  • Retail Pharmacy Technician
