Automotive and Diesel Technology

The Automotive and Diesel Technologies program is designed to train students in the areas of both automotive and diesel technology.

Business Technology

The Business department provides training in a wide range of business and related occupations.

Barbering and Cosmetology Professions

The Barber and Cosmetology Occupations department includes the Barbering, Cosmetology, Esthetician, and Nail Technician programs.

Criminal Justice Professions

The Criminal Justice program is designed to prepare students for a career in Law Enforcement

Early Childhood Professions

The Early Childhood Professions program prepares individuals for various levels of employment in child care centers, public school settings, headstart programs, or other areas where caring for young children is the primary function.

Emergency Medical Professions

Our Emergency Medical Technician program is designed to provide initial and on-going training in emergency medical care for EMS providers.

Healthcare Professions

The Healthcare Professions department includes the Nursing Aide and Practical Nursing programs.

Computer Information Technology

This program is designed to prepare the student for entry-level employment in the Information Technology Industry and to obtain various certifications from CompTIA and Microsoft.

Massage Therapy Professions

This program is designed to help individuals develop the basic entry-level skills needed for a career in massage therapy.

Mining Technology

This program is designed to prepare the student for entry-level employment in the “Green Industry”.

Technical Drafting

The Technical Drafting core program is designed to qualify students for entry-level employment.
