News 

Stritch’s 75th anniversary spring commencement to be held May 20
Cardinal Stritch University will hold two spring 2012 commencement ceremonies on Sunday, May 20. The graduate ceremony will begin at 11 a.m., and the undergraduate ceremony will begin at 3 p.m. Both ceremonies will take place in the ballroom on the first floor at Frontier Airlines Center, 400 W. Wisconsin Ave., Milwaukee.

Update on Spring Phonathon, March Madness for Stritch Giving campaign and iPad winner
 The annual Stritch Spring Phonathon conducted by the University Advancement Office March 18-April 5 raised $22,258 in pledges for the University. The student callers surpassed their goal of $18,775 and raised $5,300 more than last spring.

Top Student Musicians Head to Cardinal Stritch University May 5
Thousands of middle and high school students will head to Cardinal Stritch University on May 5 for their opportunity to perform in a Wisconsin School Music Association (WSMA) State Music Festival - one of the largest student events of its kind in the nation.

Events

B.F.A. Student Art Exhibition
Stritch Visual Art students will exhibit photography, graphic design and ceramics as part of their senior thesis May 11-20 in the Northwestern Mutual Art Gallery.

Theater Dept. to present "Sherlock Holmes: The Final Adventure"
The Theater Department will present the Winner of the 2007 Edgar Award for Best Mystery Play May 5 at 7:30 p.m., and May 6 at 2 p.m. 

Spring Choir Concert
The Stritch Concert Choir, conducted by Michael Batcho will perform in a combined choral concert on May 4 at 8 p.m., at the Cathedral of St. John the Evangelist located at 812 N. Jackson St., Milwaukee.
