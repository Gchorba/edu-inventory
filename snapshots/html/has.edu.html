Principal's Message

Hayward Adult School continues to serve our community in a variety of ways. We have many classes and program certificate offerings.  Our Certified Medical Assistant program will graduate its first class this May as they begin externship opportunities. We look forward to our May 24th graduation which will, for the first time, include High School Diploma, GED and Career Technical Education students.
 
We welcome our new Assistant Principal, Craig Garrison.  Mr. Garrison comes to HAS with many years of administrative experience and exciting ideas for growing our program. One of the projects that he is undertaking is rebranding our school to align with state and federal trends for education. As the education market-place becomes more and more competitive, the adult school is in the process of preparing to better compete in the 21st Century market-place.  Starting with the name, we are in the process of changing the school image.  We believe that a new name will be more in-line with the direction adult education is headed.  Please click here to vote your choice for a new name for the adult school.
 
Our guiding principles for this year, and the coming year, are restructure, re-brand, and diversify.  Through new community and employer collaborations, we will expand services to provide more workforce training.  With this focus in mind, we will continue to build career and higher education pathways to guide students toward even greater success in meeting their goals, earning diplomas and certificates, and preparation for state, national, and professional certification. Through implementation of a program-wide assessment program, HAS continues to better serve student needs and collect data for program improvement and benchmark and grant funding.
 
During 20 years in adult education,  I have come to see the adult school as a lighthouse in the community. Adult schools are the beacons of all of our communities and we strive to shine our light for the community. This year Hayward Adult School proudly celebrates 77 years of service to Hayward.  HAS continues to carry the service forward, remaining the lighthouse for the Hayward community, a guiding light amidst economic and political storms.
 
Everyone at Hayward Adult School has a voice.  Please join our collaboration through the student or staff roundtables or community town hall meetings to shape and support our 2012 – 2013 schoolwide goals. Our schedule and orientation, assessment, advising and registration dates are posted for the Spring session.  Feel free to call if you have any questions. 
 
Ana Weston Solomon, Principal
