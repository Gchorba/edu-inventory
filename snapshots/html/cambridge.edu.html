Cambridge Junior College offers the career training you need to be successful and improve your life!

Cambridge Junior College has two campuses, one in Yuba City and Woodland, Ca. We offer healthcare training programs and business training courses that can help our students succeed in two of the most rapidly growing fields today. Explore our site to learn more about our programs and to find out what great opportunities are just around the corner.

Cambridge Junior College not only gets your career on the right track with the proper education and training, we also offer job placement assistance to help you find work.  Our Job Placement office works closely with prospective employers within the community to assist you in obtaining the best job using your skills and knowledge learned at Cambridge!
