News, Events and Announcements

Cohort MBA begins May 10, 2012

The Rensselaer MBA is an exciting program of study that challenges you to develop and stretch your creativity, imagination, and passion to become a transformative leader in an increasingly global business world. Our MBA is designed to advance your core leadership capabilities and business fundamentals while augmenting them with concentrations in specific areas of study. Learn more.

International Scholars Program (ISP)

The International Scholars Program is a global learning experience in Europe and Asia. This summer term, students will complete four courses toward a Master of Science degree in Management or an Engineering discipline, or an MBA.

Rensselaer's Executive MBA (EMBA)

Beginning in October 2012
Rensselaer's EMBA is an elite program where leaders learn the skills needed to foster innovation, utilize technology, and lead organizations to the forefront of the global marketplace. Participants will join a cohort of executives who change the world every day. The EMBA program can be completed in just 16 months with classes held every other Friday and Saturday. Learn more.
