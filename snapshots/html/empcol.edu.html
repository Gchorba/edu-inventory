Welcome to

Empire College in Santa Rosa, California.

At Empire College, we offer the career training you need to be successful.

Empire College is located in Santa Rosa, California, which is just one hour north of the San Francisco Bay Area in the heart of the wine-growing region of Sonoma County. We offer career training programs for today’s in-demand careers. Explore our site and get to know what Empire College has in store for you.

Over the past 50 years, the School of Business has produced thousands of graduates representing billions of revenue and tax dollars to the local economy. In addition, since 2000, Empire College School of Law has been the top-ranked California Accredited Law School on the California Bar exam and has consistently trophied at the annual statewide Traynor Moot Court Competitions. When searching for Northern California law or business schools, consider Empire College!
