Inside MTSO

Follow us on Twitter
Now @MTSOedu is your source for tweets from campus.
Go directly to our Twitter page here, or visit our social media page to connect with the MTSO family via Twitter, Facebook and other services.

Images from a day devoted to social justice
Do-Love-Walk, organized by MTSO students, drew people committed to a wide variety of social-justice causes for a day of learning, worship and fellowship. Check out photos from the April 28 event.

MACM celebration honors seniors and Lattimore
The MTSO community paid tribute to graduating seniors and Professor Vergel Lattimore III, retiring director of the MACM program. See photos from the April 17 event here. Read President Jay Rundell’s thoughts on Dr. Lattimore’s retirement here.

New ad shares a grad’s success story
Readers of the United Methodist Reporter‘s special UM General Conference supplement this spring are learning about the rebirth of Summit on 16th UMC, a Columbus church led by 2009 MTSO graduate April Blaine.

Wisconsin’s Bishop Lee will address 2012 graduates
Bishop Linda Lee, who presides over the Wisconsin Episcopal Area of the United Methodist Church, will deliver MTSO’s 2012 commencement address May 19. In 2000, Lee became the first African-American woman to be elected bishop in the North Central Jurisdiction.

New issue of Campus View arrives
President Rundell comments on transitions. One student reflects on a life-changing trip to India, and another discusses his theological analysis of vampire literature. Find all that and more in the latest Campus View.
