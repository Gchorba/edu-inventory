Transforming the Professions
of Psychology, Business,
Education, and the Arts

Founded in 1993, Meridian University offers degree programs that educate students to engage the professions of Psychology, Business, Education, and the Arts in creative and transformative ways. These degree programs provide a cumulative, emergent, and integrated curriculum, empowering the student’s transformation both personally and professionally.

Meridian’s programs are offered in Monthly and Blended learning formats which support the transformative learning intent of the curriculum. In addition, these formats provide adult learners, who have significant commitments to family and/or employment, with the opportunity to complete their graduate coursework within a reasonable time frame.

"Meridian University is a gift to this moment in our history. The rare breadth of the intellectual journeys it offers, and its exquisite attention to the ripening of the whole person, set a stunning example for graduate studies in the time of the Great Turning. It grows new and needed disciplines of consciousness, as well as motivation to engage in the transformation of culture."

– Joanna Macy
Author of Coming Back to Life
