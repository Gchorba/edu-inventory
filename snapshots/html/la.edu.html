Variety of College Degree Programs

Mount St. Mary's College, a liberal arts college with two historic college campuses in Los Angeles, offers undergraduate education programs for women, as well as innovative professional and graduate accredited programs for men and women. The Chalon College Campus, located in Brentwood, offers traditional bachelor's degrees in the arts, humanities, natural and social sciences, business administration, and nursing programs. At the Doheny College Campus in downtown Los Angeles, the Mount offers the following degree programs: associate in arts degrees; the undergraduate Weekend College for working adults, which offers both bachelor of art and bachelor of science degrees; and graduate degrees, including master's and teacher credential programs, the MBA program, and a Doctor of Physical Therapy degree. Mount St. Mary's College is affordable, and very competitive among other graduate schools and nursing schools. Our goal is to provide students with all of the advantages of a small, private college institution but at a cost that is realistic in today's changing economy. Merit and other types of scholarships and financial aid are available.

Top Ranking Liberal Arts College

The College consistently ranks among the top liberal arts colleges in California. Over 59% of our graduates with bachelor's degrees go on to earn advanced degrees, either at the Mount or at other leading colleges and universities across the country. Our nursing programs and graduate programs are highly rated among liberal arts colleges and universities.

Leadership and Service

As a Catholic College, the mission of Mount St. Mary's is to provide a superior education enhanced by an emphasis on building leadership skills and fostering a spirit to serve others. For over 80 years, the Mount has prepared its graduates for meaningful professional and personal lives. Our measure of success is college graduates who are committed to using their knowledge, and skills to better themselves, their environments, and the world.
