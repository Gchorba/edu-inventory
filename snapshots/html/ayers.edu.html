Ayers Career College of Shreveport

A Shreveport College focused on preparing students for good-paying, in-demand careers.

Ayers Career College in Shreveport, Louisiana has been preparing the men and women of the Arkansas, Louisiana and Texas areas for successful and in-demand careers since 1953.

Ayers is a career college offering training programs that are in high demand, both locally and throughout the country. All of our technical college programs are available day and evening to fit your busy schedule. With convenient and flexible scheduling options, you can receive:

  • HVAC-R Technician Training
  • Medical Assistant Training
  • Pharmacy Technician Training
  • Home Electronics Technician Training

Ayers Career College of Shreveport also offers financial aid assistance to those who qualify.

All Ayers graduates enjoy the benefits of our full-time job placement assistance staff, all of whom are focused on helping you find the job you want. Find out more about Ayers Career College in Shreveport today. It’s a technical college that gives you options!
