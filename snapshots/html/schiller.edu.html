Welcome to Schiller International University!

Founded in 1964 by Dr. Walter Leibrecht, Schiller International University was created as a private American University dedicated to the active development and encouragement of international understanding. As a philosophy professor in the United States, Dr. Leibrecht was so impressed with the personalized instruction in the American college system that he sought to create something similar back in Europe.

Schiller International University offers Associate, Bachelor and Master's degrees in high demand fields including: Business, International Diplomacy, Financial Planning, Hospitality and more. Schiller International University also offers online courses that are the perfect fit for busy working adults, including online MBA.
