Paths of Paul
Join President David Bernard, Professor Steven Beardsley and guest Professor Jane DeRose Evans on a journey through ancient Asia minor and Greece as you discover the world of the early church. The trip will be June 3-15, 2013 and deadline for registration is May 1, 2012. This trip is available to all seeking an unforgettable experience. Graduate credit is also available.
