Welcome to Antioch University

Antioch University is a bold and enduring source of innovation in higher education. Students' passion to advance their lives, personally and professionally, is developed through academic programs that respond to the world's needs. Together, we form a visionary community that strikes a rare—and essential—balance between idealism and experience.

Alumni

Stay Part of the Community

AULA graduates join an ever expanding network of 20,000+ Antioch University alumni worldwide. Antioch University graduates consistently distinguish themselves within their communities as visionary, adaptable leaders striving for social change. We are incredibly proud of all of our alumni, and invite you to remain engaged, energized, and connected with the AULA community.
