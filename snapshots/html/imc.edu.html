Welcome to the Institute of Medical Careers, Pittsburgh, PA

Begin a Rewarding Career

The Institute of Medical Careers, (formally known as Pittsburgh School of Pain Management, PSPM) is located in Pittsburgh, PA.

Institute of Medical Careers has been educating individuals in careers in the exciting and expanding field of Allied Health since 1995.

We are delighted to introduce you to our career-focused programs in the rapidly growing Allied Health field.

» Massage Therapy » Medical Assisting
» Medical Billing & Coding » Myofascial Trigger Point Therapy

Click here for the Consumer Information on the above programs or go to the Accreditation and Consumer Information tab.

Choose any one of the programs offered and you will find IMC is dedicated to the preparation of men and women in occupational skills necessary to qualify individuals for entry into their chosen career. Programs at IMC provide a combination of clinical training and classroom instruction to develop the abilities for a successful career.

Our programs can have you trained in the skill you need for a rewarding, successful new career in as little as 36 weeks.

IMC provides professional, high quality training to prepare you for the Health Profession of your interest and the skills employers are looking for. We strive to help all of our students become the best they can be with a bright future in the health care industry.

We offer career placement and assistance to help our graduates find a position in the Pittsburgh area or another area of their choice.

Taking the first step is always the hardest! We understand what it takes to get started on a new career path. We encourage you to contact us today to find out how a career in the field in Allied Health can help you put your future… in your hands. Call us today!
