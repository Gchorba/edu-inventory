The New Style of the Florida Barber Academy!

Founded in 1999, the Florida Barber Academy is among the most highly-regarded barber schools in the state of Florida. Our mission is to provide a positive environment where future barbers can be creative and grow into more well-rounded people. Our students are professional, artistic, and have a great desire to find success in the barbering profession. 

The curriculum goes beyond preparing students for the state licensure exam. Our instructors work closely with students, making sure they are familiar with the latest techniques and styling trends. We teach our students the business and customer service skills necessary to be successful in the profession. We produce barbers that are also community leaders.

If you are interested in taking the first step toward a career in barbering, please visit our Admissions page or take a look at our Program Description!
