FHTC Mission Statement

The mission of Flint Hills Technical College, as an associate degree granting institution, is to provide a diverse community of learners with life-long educational opportunities for personal growth and preparation for professional and civic responsibilities that meet the needs of society.




Gainful Employment Information

Flint Hills Technical College has provided information related to gainful employment for each Title IV eligible program of study. Specific information regarding occupations students will be prepared to enter upon program completion, on-time graduation rates, cost, placement rates and median debt incurred can be found on each program specific web page. Under Programs and Majors, click on a program to view this information. This information is provided in compliance with 34 CFR 668.6(b).
