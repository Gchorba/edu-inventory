Hickey College: St. Louis, MO

For over 75 years, Hickey College has helped graduates Finish First. We know that your time, your money, and your future are important to you. That’s why Hickey College’s programs are designed to get you the quality training you need QUICKLY and AFFORDABLY.  Our associate and diploma programs are designed to be completed by full-time students in less than 2 years, depending on the program and options that you choose--and you then can finish your bachelor’s degree while you work.

Hickey College offers a combination a combination of applied associate degrees, bachelor’s degrees, and diplomas in 12 different program areas. Each program’s curriculum has been designed by faculty and industry professionals to emphasize what employers need in their new hires.

Hickey College offers programs in:

  • Business
  • Design and Technology
  • Health Care
  • Culinary


Come visit us and discover how a hands-on, career-focused program can lead to a new, professional you.
