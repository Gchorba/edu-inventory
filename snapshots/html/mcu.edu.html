Welcome To Miami Christian University


EARN YOUR BIBLE THEOLOGY DEGREE ONLINE

Miami Christian University, operated by the Christ Life Center (Miami, FL), is a state authorized educational institution offering theological degrees through the doctoral level. The advent of the Internet has expanded the opportunities in Biblical education for many who do not have the option of attending classes on campus, or who may simply wish to complete their coursework in the privacy of their own residence using their computer and Internet connection. The Internet is easily accessible in most areas of the world, including areas where no Bible colleges or Christian universities even exist.

We're here to assist you on your journey to learning more about God's inspired Word, the Bible. It is only through God's revelation that we learn more about Him and how to become more like His Son, Jesus Christ. Our programs will help you to discover the riches of God's Word and the abundant life that He offers to each of us who trusts in His Son.

If you've been looking for a theology program that will help you to discover more about the Bible, you need look no further! We have a comprehensive program that will take you through both the Old and New Testaments in an interesting and relevant fashion. There is no better day than today to begin your studies.

Take your time and look over our website. We are here to serve you and do everything that we can to make your study both challenging and enjoyable.

"ROLLING REGISTRATION" NOW TAKING PLACE

With MCU's "rolling registration" process, the Internet student may begin a course (or courses) at any time throughout the year. There is no "minimum classroom experience" requirement for the Internet program, and a graduating Internet student may participate with our other students in our annual commencement exercises, should he or she choose to attend.

Simply submit your application form and enrollment fee, then register for the course or courses you wish to begin!

AUDIT ANY COURSE TODAY - NO ENROLLMENT NEEDED!

June29

You do not have to be seeking a degree to take courses at Miami Christian University. Many students take just a course or two. Some register for courses for personal interest, and some for transfer into a degree program at MCU at a later date.

If you are taking courses merely for personal enrichment (auditing), you may simply register for the courses you wish to take, and you will receive all course materials just like other students taking the class -- you just won't be graded for your completed work. If you want credit for the courses you take, you will need to have your work graded, and then officially apply for enrollment in the university before credit can be applied.

STUDY TO EARN YOUR GED ONLINE

August12

Miami Christian University and InstantCert Academy have affiliated to offer students the opportunity to study to earn their General Educational Development (GED) diploma through the GED testing service. GED tests are a group of five tests which (when passed) certifies that the taker has American or Canadian high school-level academic skills.

Getting your GED is the perfect option for working parents, home-schoolers, older students, super-achievers, fast learners, slow learners, returning students, and many others. Students seeking to earn their High School Diploma equivalency before beginning their theological studies at MCU need no longer drive long distances to sit for hours in a lecture hall and plow through hundreds of pages that "pay back" your big investment of time with a small amount of learning!

CHRIST LIFE CENTER OFFERS MCU STUDENTS ORDINATION / LICENSURE

July31

If you are seeking ministerial ordination or licensure, MCU students who complete the 33-credit "Pastoral Ministry" certificate program, which includes writing a 5-10 page book review on a selection of books available in the MCU Online Store, are pre-qualified to apply for a Ministerial License, or to take the Christ Life Center Ordination Exam to receive official ordination.

ACCREDITED LIBERAL ARTS COURSES OFFERED

July26

Miami Christian University has affiliated with InstantCert Academy to offer a completely online study program for accredited liberal arts courses.

Students seeking to earn credit hours to apply towards an accredited degree at a liberal arts college, either apart from or in addition to their theological studies at MCU, need no longer leave home, quit their jobs, move to another city, or even sit in a single classroom for instruction!
