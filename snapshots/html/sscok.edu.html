SSC Baseball Sanction Altered

 

During a third round in the appeal process of the NJCAA Region II ruling prohibiting the participation of Seminole State College in the May 10-13 Region II Tournament, the penalty for the violation has been altered.  The SSC Trojans will be allowed to compete.  The Head Coach will not be allowed to participate in the Regional tournament.

 

Each member college of Region II was allowed one vote after reviewing the evidence regarding a violation of a statistics reporting rule. The schools voted to overturn the ruling.

 

SSC President Dr. Jim Utterback said, "I appreciate our colleagues voting with Seminole State College and Northern Oklahoma College to properly restore the hopes and dreams of the student-athletes who were being inappropriately punished for a clerical oversight of the coaching staff."

 

 

 

 

Croy Selected Vice President for Academic Affairs at SSC

 

Dr. Melanie A.  Croy

 

Seminole State College has announced that after an extensive and inclusive search process, Dr. Melanie A.  Croy has been hired as Vice President for Academic Affairs beginning in the 2012 - 2013 academic year. 

 

Dr. Croy has over 26 years of experience in higher education, 16 of which have been served in an administrative capacity.  She currently serves as Dean of the College of Professional Studies at Sul Ross State University in Alpine, Texas.  As Vice President for Academic Affairs at Seminole State College, she will oversee faculty as well as academic programs of the institution.   (more)
