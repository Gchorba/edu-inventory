Established more than 30 years ago, the Centre for Commercial Law Studies (CCLS) combines excellence in teaching and research, is at the forefront of legal scholarship and law reform, and influences the development and application of commercial law in practice.

CPD and Distance Learning

We offer a range of professional courses, seminars, conferences and workshops, which confer Continuing Professional Development (CPD) points.

International Arbitration

CCLS offers a range of international arbitration courses.
