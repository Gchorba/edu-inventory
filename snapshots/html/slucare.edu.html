Featured Physician
Scott W. Ferreira, M.D., has joined the faculty of Saint Louis University School of Medicine as an assistant professor of internal medicine in the Division of Cardiology. Dr. Ferreira earned his medical degree from Tulane University School of Medicine, followed by a residency at Brown University School of Medicine, a fellowship in cardiovascular disease at Saint Louis University School of Medicine, and a fellowship in clinical cardiac electrophysiology at St. Vincent Hospitals and Health Services in Indianapolis.
