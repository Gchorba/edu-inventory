Sullivan and Cogliano Training Centers

The Training You Need For The Career You Deserve!
At Sullivan and Cogliano Training Centers, a nationally accredited career training school, we offer a unique way to prepare for your new career: focused training that concentrates on developing the skills employers are looking for. If you're ready to take the next step into your future, learn how our career training programs can make the difference for you.

If you're looking for a meaningful career, advancement opportunities or increased skills that can lead to more earnings, learn more about Sullivan and Cogliano Training Centers. We've changed the lives of over 12,000 students since 1993 – now let us change yours.

Career Training In Florida, Massachusetts or Online!

Immediate Start Options. Flexible Scheduling. A School That Fits Your Needs.
We understand that preparing for a new career shouldn't mean putting your life on hold. Whether you're a working adult looking for part-time training in the evenings or weekends, or a recent high school graduate looking to attend full time, Sullivan and Cogliano Training Centers has the right program for you.

With three physical locations in Florida and Massachusetts offering flexible scheduling, and several online programs using our Anytime, Anywhere Learning (AAL) technology, we can meet anyone's scheduling needs.
