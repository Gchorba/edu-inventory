      • Skaggs School of Pharmacy


    • Offers an alternative to big, urban law schools, and a hands-on approach to legal education. We pride ourselves on being a small, student-centered law school with a spirit of cooperation, not competition, among the students.

      The Skaggs School of Pharmacy

      Contains the departments of Biomedical and Pharmaceutical Sciences and the Department of Pharmacy Practice with degrees in neuroscience, toxicology, cardiovascular pharmacology, medicinal chemistry, pharmacogenetics and cancer research including a 6-year Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D.) degree.

      School of Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation Science

      Offers a Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT) degree. Ongoing research efforts within the School include initiatives in the Applied Exercise Physiology, Clinical Kinesiology, and Motor Control laboratories. Visit the School of Physical Therapy & Rehabilitation Science website.
