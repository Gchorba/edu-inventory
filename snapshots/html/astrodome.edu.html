Astrodome Career Centers is a great place for working adults to enhance their current careers or help themselves find new career pathways to better live. We train high school graduates (or individuals with a GED) to enter into the exciting, high-demand Healthcare industry of Dental Assisting.

Our program is focused and centered on empowering you with the skills you need to enter the expanding and rewarding Health Care Industry. Learn only what you need to gain employment, so you can get certified and enter the job market faster.

Astrodome Career Centers also features:

  • Financial Aid available for those who qualify
  • Job placement assistance to graduates
  • Convenient day classes
  • Friendly customer service oriented staff

If you feel you're ready to forge a new direction in your life with better career options in the rewarding Health Care Industry, don't hesitate to find out more about Astrodome Career Centers today!
