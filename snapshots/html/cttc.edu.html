Chisholm Trail Technology Center has 100's of classes available online.  Classes can be taken at your convenience in your home or at any computer with internet access.
Practical Nursing

The Practical Nursing program is designed to provide adult students with the skills and knowledge required for today's licensed practical nurse. The 1500 clock hour program is self-paced allowing the student to maintain part-time employment and/or family responsibilities.

A pre-entrance test (TABE - Test of Adult Basic Education) is a scheduled after an application is received. To be considered for entrance into the program, the applicant should score at or above the 10.0 grade equivalency.


Financial Aid
Chisholm Trail Technology Center assists qualified students in meeting the costs of their education and career training by offering financial assistance in the form of grants, scholarships, and agency funded programs. Financial aid should be considered as supplementary-the student and the family of the student, when applicable, are expected to make a maximum effort to assist with educational expenses.
Business and Industry Services

In today's competitive marketplace, a highly trained workforce can make a significant impact on profits and efficiency. Chisholm Trail Technology Center's Business and Industry Services (BIS) Division has the flexibility to customize training and continuing education to help you or your business maximize resources in the workplace.

Whether your business operation is manufacturing, retail, or service-related, Chisholm Trail's BIS Center will help provide customized solutions in a flexible, affordable way.
