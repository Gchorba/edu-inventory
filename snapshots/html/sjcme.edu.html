Realize the Promise

This year Saint Joseph’s celebrates its 100th birthday, and we honor an educational legacy that is highly personalized and both on campus and online, undergraduate and graduate, liberal arts and professional. The Centennial theme is “Realize the Promise,” because that is what faculty, staff and students have done each day since 1912.
