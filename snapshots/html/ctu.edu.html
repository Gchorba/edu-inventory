"Mayan Procession"

Spring Exhibit features the work of Winifred Godfrey

Join us for "Mayan Procession," the Spring Exhibit at the Mary-Frances and Bill Veeck Gallery at CTU.  The exhibit features the work of artist Winifred Godfrey and will be open April 18 through June 10, 2012, in the gallery on the fourth floor of the CTU Academic and Conference Center.  For more information, click here.

Don't wait until fall - explore CTU Summer Institute 2012

Join us in June and see all that CTU has to offer

You don't have to wait until fall to begin--or continue--your journey at Catholic Theological Union! Summer Institute courses are designed to fit your busy schedule. Nourish your soul, explore your faith, or develop a ministerial skill through short intensive courses that may be audited or taken for credit. Blended courses made up of online and onsite classes allow distance education students to complete requirements. Click to learn more.

Explore a life of service in the Church

Catholics On Call Summer Conference Accepting Applications

Are you, or is someone you know, a young adult considering a life of service in the Church? Attend the Young Adult Summer Conference, July 31 - August 4, 2012 and find "direction for your life." For more information or to apply, click here.
