Siba – Stevens Institute of Business & Arts – St. Louis, Missouri

Stevens Institute of Business & Arts or Siba for short, is located in the vibrant loft district of downtown St. Louis, Missouri in a beautifully renovated historic building, designed for plenty of interaction among classmates and instructors in spacious high tech facilities, with an average class size of just 10 students. Siba offers Fashion & Retail, Interior Design, Business Administration, Tourism & Hospitality and Paralegal degrees.

Our name is new. Our address is new. Our recently expanded curriculum and Bachelor’s degree offerings are new. Our traditions, reputation, and dedication to excellence in education, however, are not new—we started out as Patricia Stevens College in 1947, and have several generations of distinguished and successful PSC alumni who have made us very proud. We carry that pride, reputation and tradition with us as we take the next exciting step in our future.

At SIBA, our focus will continue to be small class size, quality instruction from expert faculty, and accelerated day and evening degree programs in some of the most exciting and fastest-growing fields.
