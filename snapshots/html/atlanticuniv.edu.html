Personal Spirituality and Higher Education meet at Atlantic University. 

Grow toward wholeness in your life while earning your Masters degree! 

Atlantic University is a virtual gathering of dedicated people on a spiritual journey. Not just books and lectures, but meditation, dream work, journaling, intuition, and imagination are your sources of knowledge and wisdom in this academic curriculum. 

Learning alongside like-minded individuals—fostered by faculty/mentors committed to your spiritual growth— awakens not just your intellect, but your emotional and spiritual Self.  

Atlantic University is invested in your quest to embrace your inner knowledge, integrate it into your life, and express it for the betterment of your world.  

Open the door to your next journey and take your spiritual path to the next degree!
