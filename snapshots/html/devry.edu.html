Study on campus, online or both.

95+ locations and online college courses — all designed to fit your life.

When you already have a busy schedule it may seem impossible to find time to attend college, too. That's why we give you the flexibility to take online classes, attend class on campus or a mix of both - whatever best fits your needs. Regardless of where you study you'll receive the same respected education and the personal attention you need to succeed.

Grow your career possibilities with our accounting degree programs.

If you're interested in a career in accounting, DeVry University can help you get there. We offer multiple degree programs that can prepare you to enter this growing field. The career possibilities seem endless and today you'll find accountants working in the entertainment industry, for the nation's leading hospitals, even helping the FBI solve crimes.
