Scholarship

Posted on March 30, 2012 by Mark

Applications are open for the David M. Leach Scholarship for graduate ministerial studies. The purpose of the Leach Graduate Scholarship is to assist students who are members of the Friends Church and who are involved in graduate studies in preparation for ministry. This includes, but is not necessarily limited to, pastoral ministry, missionary service, Christian education and youth ministry. The guidelines and application forms are available online at http://www.friendsyouth.com/efcmaym_leach_scholarship.cfm. Applications must be received by June 1 for the academic year beginning in the fall.

East Asian Mission

Posted on February 22, 2012 by Mark


Dr. Jerry Barber is leaving today for East Asia with a seven member team from Harvest Community Church in Huntersville. Their primary task will be working on a college campus to initiate as many spiritual conversations as possible, share the gospel with prepared hearts, and then connect those who are interested with a team already in place there.

Please keep Dr. Barber and the team in your prayers.
