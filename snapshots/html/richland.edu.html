Tree Campus USA

In honor of its commitment to effective community forestry management, Richland Community College recently received the prestigious “Tree Campus USA” designation from the National Arbor Day Foundation. The award was presented at the Tree City USA Conference and Award Ceremony, held at the Department of Natural Resources building in Springfield on March 27, 2012.

2012 Conference on Diversity and Globalization

The Conference on Diversity and Globalization will be held on Thursday, June 14, and Friday, June 15, at Richland Community College. This year's conference theme, "Serving the World Outside Our Door," asks us to consider how the lessons learned in the classroom must carry into the communities in which we live and work to broaden our individual, community, and world views.

40 Years of Excellence

Richland Community College invites you to join a year-long celebration of its 40th anniversary. In the coming year, the College’s official 40th anniversary logo will be used in place of the current Richland logo. Special events commemorating this milestone will be held beginning August 2011, and will culminate with the President’s Ball in September 2012, and will be targeted toward the College’s community, alumni, and staff.

Tree Planting Workshop

Richland Community College, in conjunction with Southern Illinois University, is hosting a tree planting mini-workshop Wednesday, May 9, at 6 pm. The workshop includes a presentation about tree planting and care, which will be held in SC01 in the Shilling Center. A tree planting demonstration will also be held, and each participant will receive a copy of the Tree Owner’s Manual.

2012 Plant Sale - May 11 & 12

The 2012 Student Farms Plant Sale will be held Friday May 11 from 7 a.m. to 6 p.m. and Saturday May 12 from 7 a.m. to 1 p.m. at the Horticulture Building on the Richland Community College Campus. A large number of plants will be available. To see the full list, download the Plant Sale Flyer.
