WELCOME

FCC is devoted to your success. That is why we
believe that some of the richest educational experi-
ences happen outside of the classroom. We want to
prepare you for the real world by giving you a
comprehensive education which also includes high
caliber instructors, community involvement,
internship and study tours.

Our instructors are respected professionals in their
fields. They not only teach business and technical
skills, but impart the expertise and knowledge
required to be successful in the fashion industry.
We are proud to say that we are a "Fashion Only"
boutique college.
