Clinical Excellence through Graduate Education

The University of St. Augustine is a graduate institution that emphasizes health science education.  Our mission is the professional development of health care providers through innovative and individualized education.  We hold true to our mission by offering unique hands-on curriculums which provides students with the tools it takes to become skilled clinicians and health care providers.

We have campus locations in San Marcos, CA, St. Augustine, FL and Austin, TX.   Our entry-level degree offerings include: Doctor of Physical Therapy Degree (DPT), Master of Occupational Therapy Degree (MOT), Dual Degree Option (MOT/DPT),  Master of Orthopaedic Physician Assistant (OPA) and the Flexible Doctor of Physical Therapy Degree (DPT).  We also offer the Post-Professional Doctor of Occupational Therapy Degree, Transitional Doctor of Physical Therapy Degree, Doctor of Education (EdD),  Doctor of Health Science (DHSc), Orthopaedic Clinical Residency,  Manual Physical Therapy Fellowship Program  and Continuing Education Seminars and Certifications for currently licensed therapists.
