Butler Tech is Ohio's largest career-technical school.

What does that mean to you? It means that we have the resources and the experience to help you build the future you want through state-of-the-art education programs. Here is who we make successful:

  • High school students looking for an education that goes beyond the classroom and gets them doing what they love
  • Adult students who need focused education to get the next promotion or a whole new career
  • Business decision makers who know that educating their workforce is the best investment their company can make

We want to be part of your success story.
