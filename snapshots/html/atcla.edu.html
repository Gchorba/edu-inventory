Welcome to Associated Technical College

Since 1967 we have provided vocational training in fields that are in-demand and ones that will give career opportunities to our graduates. Rest assure that we will do everything we can to make your educational experience memorable. With that in mind, you are now part of a family that will guide you through a path that will lead you to greater heights than you possibly ever imagined.

It is important that you feel comfortable with the decision you made. It is also important that we work together towards a common goal. Your education with us is only one part of the equation; the other part is providing you with a top-notch placement assistance service that is available to you long after you have graduated.

During your seven months, our placement department will regularly visit your classes to give you the latest information about the job market. Contained in this information will be market trends, economic news that will impact you, resume preparation, interviewing techniques, and any other articles or pertinent information we come across that may be useful to you.

As you get closer to graduation, you will participate in small group workshops to hone your interviewing skills and prepare you so that you won't get the “butterflies” when you interview. By participating in these workshops you will learn intimately how to prepare resumes, cover letters, job search, interviewing techniques, and employer/company history research techniques in order to better prepare for the interview. As a result you will feel more confident and ready to tackle the job market because you will know that they have been looking and waiting for you.

So relax, you have chosen a fine school that we will be there with you every step of the way until you are fully trained, confident in your ability, and find the hidden path to your success. Remember we need to work together for your success. Although we cannot guarantee you a job, we can assure you that all of us at the college will work hard until you find your success.

Thank you for choosing us to meet your educational needs.

Sincerely,     

Aram Tomasian

School Director
