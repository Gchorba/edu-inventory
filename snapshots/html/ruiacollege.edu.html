Welcome

Welcome to Ramnarain Ruia College Web Home. This is a comprehensive website maintained primarily to offer a quick overview of useful information, especially for the benefit of students and parents. Here we hope to give you most of the information you may wish to have about our College, including its historical background , a comprehensive view of all its major activities, projected schedules, the courses conducted, admission procedure, examination related information: schedules, timings and results- in short , all the information required by current students as well as their parents for day to day use.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Our Mission

To emerge as a center of higher learning and innovative research to meet the challenges of a global societyMainstreaming the practices that facilitate intellectual, emotional, physical and cultural growth of students.

College Profile

The Ramnarain Ruia College of Arts & Science was established in June 1937; it was the first college in Mumbai to be started by the Shikshana Prasaraka Mandali, Pune. This College is affiliated to University of Mumbai and conducts courses at Undergraduate and Graduate (Postgraduate) level.

Today, Ruia College enjoys the reputation of being one of the finest institutions of higher learning in the country. Its outstanding performance in the field of curricular and co-curricular activities has very considerably enhanced its prestige and brought it substantial financial assistance from the University Grants Commission, the C S I R and many other equally reputed bodies.

Ruia alumni comprise a galaxy of eminent individuals from different walks of life. To cite a few names: Professor Ram Joshi, Dr. K. Kasturirangan, Mr. Dilip Chitre, Justice V. P. Tipnis, Justice Hemant Gokhale, Dr. Prafulla Desai, Dr. K. Ramamoorthy, Dr. Ajit Phadke, Mr. P. K. Dhamdhere, Mr. Nandu Natekar, Mr. Ajit Wadekar, Ms. Shruti Sadolikar, Ms. Ashwini Bhide, Ms. Swati Chitnis, Dr. Snehalata Deshmukh, Mr. Sandeep Patil, Mr. R. H. Mendonca, Mr. P. Narayanswamy and Mr. Manohar Joshi. Ruia College has also consistently had some of the finest members of the academic community on its staff.

From Principals Desk

          Ramnarain Ruia College of Arts & Science is a premier institute which has been contributing to human progress by molding brilliant, creative, committed and caring students who are doing its alma mater proud in different corners of the world and in diverse spheres of human activity.

          Since 1937, this home of learning has offered its students excellent infrastructural facilities along with competent and dedicated faculty who strives for all round development of every student.

         In the context of the changing global scenario, the institute has gone all out to provide its students with state of the art facilities as well as to design innovative courses to meet the demands of the time.

         Alongside, the college regularly organizes innumerable extra-curricular activities to bring out the hidden talent of the students in the form of artistic self expression.

          Above all, the college gladly carries out its social responsibility by serving the physically challenged and socio-economically deprived.

         In the years to come Ramnarain Ruia College will certainly continue to have a cutting edge in the field of education as its moves from strength to strength.
