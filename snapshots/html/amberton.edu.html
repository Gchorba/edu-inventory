Welcome to Amberton University, a private institution that offers a variety of bachelor's and master's programs -- specializing in Business Administration, Counseling, Human Resources & Training, Human Relations & Business, and Project Management. Our flexible schedule of lecture and online courses is uniquely suited for working adults. Amberton University is recognized as one of the most respected, innovative, and affordable universities in the nation.

New Online Bookstore Now Available!

Amberton University has entered into an agreement with eCampus.com to provide a full-service online bookstore to students.  The Amberton University Virtual Bookstore is accessible through the University’s website, www.Amberton.edu.  Just look for the “Bookstore” tab across the top of the home page.  There is also a bookstore link in the Student Portal.

The AU Virtual Bookstore provides an easy to use interface, online buyback of books, and same day shipment of most titles with an average delivery time of 2-3 days depending on the student’s location.  Textbook options include new, used, rental, and electronic media as available.  The new bookstore will accommodate students on financial assistance programs that cover textbook purchases.

Student Services Online
A new technology system has been installed on the e-Course Portal allowing students online access to view, print or save their financial and academic records.  This system is available to new students as well as returning students.  Please click on the link on the top right or lower right corner of this webpage to access the e-Course Portal.
Wireless Network (AU Wi-Fi)
During the interim, the University installed a secured, wireless network at both the Garland and Frisco campuses. This wireless network is campus-wide and available to current students and  faculty to complete research and course work. A network key and acceptance of the University's wireless usage policy are required by all users. The network key can be received from the Learning Resource Center and the Student Services Office in Garland and Frisco. Validation of enrollment is required before obtaining a key.
