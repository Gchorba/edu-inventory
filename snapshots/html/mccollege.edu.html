Overview of the Midwestern Career College
(Formerly Citi College of Allied Health)


Helping All Students Reach Their Full Potential


We are committed to being the best we can be for our students.We put that commitment into practice by earning accreditation from the North Central Association Commission on Accreditation and School Improvement (NCA CASI).


What does this mean to you?


It means you can rest assured that your students are receiving a quality education.
It means that:


  • Students are taught by qualified teachers;

  • Class sizes are reasonable;

  • The curriculum is sound and rigorous;

  • Student services and activity programs are plentiful; and

  • The school is safe.


It means that students earn credit that is recognized by schools and colleges around the world.

It means that we go beyond state requirements and hold ourselves accountable for meeting nationally and internationally recognized standards of excellence.
