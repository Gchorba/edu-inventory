Health and Wellness Career School

Health and Wellness Career School

  • [View]

Kansas City & Lawrence

As a career school dedicated to the field of health and wellness, we've been helping our graduates create exceptional holistic careers for more than 23 years.

When you choose the massage therapy, personal training and wellness or our medical assisting program at WellSpring (formerly MTTI) in Kansas City and Lawrence, you will learn the science and the art of helping clients achieve optimal health and longevity, whether it's as a massage therapist, certified personal trainer, or medical assistant. You will earn more than a diploma when you graduate from WellSpring – you will acquire a depth of understanding about yourself and the know-how to help others "live well."
