Welcome to Shiloh University

Shiloh University is a fully accredited online Bible college and seminary with students throughout the United States and abroad. See our undergraduate and graduate degree programs, and the availability of courses for personal enrichment and ministry development. We are in the process of obtaining authorization from the State of Iowa to extend our accredited programs to individuals beyond The Living Word Fellowship and affiliated churches.
