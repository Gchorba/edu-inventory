TENT CITY 2
Friday, May 4 at 5 p.m.

Show your support for campus housing and enjoy free music, food, and family fun! Are you a high school student exploring your college options? Join students, faculty, and staff who pitch tents on the front lawn to raise awareness about the need for student housing. Free food, games, entertainment, T-shirts, and fun!

CROSS-COUNTRY CALL OUT
Tuesday, May 8 at 3:30 p.m.

IUPUC students are invited to attend an informational meeting about the university's first official athletic teams: men's and women's cross country. Meet the coach, see workout schedules, and meet other students who are interested in cross-country competition. Open to all full-time IUPUC students who meet NCAA requirements. The meeting will be in CC 167.
