Welcome to TTC Memphis

Our Mission

The Technology Centers continue to serve as the premier providers for workforce development throughout the State of Tennessee. The centers fulfill the mission by:

·     Providing competency-based training through traditional and distance learning instructional delivery systems of the highest quality that will qualify individuals for employment and/ or advancement in jobs.

·     Providing high quality training and retraining of employed workers.

·     Providing high quality training that is economical and accessible to all residents of Tennessee, thereby contributing to the economic and community development of the communities we serve.

 

Vision Statement:
The Administrators, faculty, staff and students of the Tennessee Technology Center at Memphis will continuously strive to model excellence in every aspect of their daily endeavors.
 

·     The Tennessee Technology Center at Memphis is a Tennessee Board of Regents Institution

·     The Tennessee Technology Center at Memphis is Accredited by the Council on Occupational Education

 

TTCM is an equal opportunity institution and offers equal opportunity for employment and admission to programs to all qualified persons without regard to race, sex, religion, national origin, age, disability, or veteran status.
