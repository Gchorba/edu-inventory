Welcome to First Coast Technical College! First Coast Technical College faculty and staff are caring individuals who dedicate themselves to making your experience at the College successful. Our technical programs meet needs that have been identified by students, business, industry, and the community. The College prepares prospective employees for their chosen vocation by using equipment, processes, and techniques similar to those used in business and industry.

FCTC provides curricula for both basic and advanced instruction in academic and technical courses. We have designed our courses to build on your strengths and develop competencies, which will make you competitive in the labor force.

We look forward to helping you acquire the skills you need for an exciting career.

Sincerely,

Christine Cothron, Ed. D.
