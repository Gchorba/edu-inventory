Change of Mailing Statements

In effort to “GO GREEN”, beginning with Spring 2012, Southeastern will no longer mail statements/bills. Students may go to Campus Connect, to view and print their statement and/or pay their bill. You may log into CampusConnect and select the "Student Information" tab and then “View/Pay Account”.
