Career Technical College

Welcome to Career Technical College, a chain of technical colleges in Louisiana. The schools are nationally accredited by the Council on Occupational Education.

Career Technical College has locations in Monroe, LA and Shreveport, LA.

Career Technical College offers Certificates, Diplomas, and/or Degrees in such fields as:

Administrative Office Technology | Cosmetology | Criminal Justice | Information Processing  | Management | Massage Therapy | Medical Assisting | Radiography  | Surgical Technology

Programs and level of credentials vary by campus.

Our campuses offer day and evening classes to help you choose a schedule that suits your life.
