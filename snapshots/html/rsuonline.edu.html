Join us in our online learning community!

Rogers State University was the first university in Oklahoma – and one of the first in the nation – to offer bachelor’s and associate’s degrees entirely via the Internet.

Since its inception in 1992, students from a cross Oklahoma – and around the world – have completed classes and degrees through the nationally recognized RSU Online program, without ever stepping foot on one of our campuses.

RSU Online offers highly regarded academic programs at an affordable cost. Financial aid is available to those who qualify.
