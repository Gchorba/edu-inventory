  • Math Club's Success includes three 1st-place finishes

    The Marquette Math Club had a very successful year! Marquette teams took 1st place in the Wisconsin Math League, at MSOE, at the Marquette Fall Math Meet, and at the Wisconsin Math Council State Math Contest. Team leaders included Ben Lowe, Michael Drakopoulos, Joey DeGuire, Jack Kegel, Alex Sprenger, Keegan English and Andrew Puck. Special recognition goes to Ben Lowe, who finished 2nd in the State on the Wisconsin Math League Contests, and Michael Drakopoulos, who tied for 4th. Ben and Michael also scored high on the American Invitational Math Exam. Ben tied for 1st in Wisconsin and Michael tied for 3rd. Freshmen Tyler Nettesheim and John Heffernan also did outstanding work. Tyler solved 28 out of 30 challenging Continental Math League problems, and John finished 7th in Wisconsin out of over 500 9th and 10th graders in on the American Mathematics Competition’s AMC 10 Contest. Math Teacher Mr. Joe Griesbach is the Math Club moderator.
