Today, there is renewed interest in horology.
At the School of Horology, you will learn the art and science of timepiece servicing, repair, restoration, and maintenance.

Watchmaking and Clockmaking represent the past and the future

High-end, ultramodern watches and clocks are found everywhere. Antique timepieces continue to be sought after by collectors—creating a valuable marketplace.

Watchmakers and Clockmakers will always be in high demand

Most watches and clocks will need servicing and repair during their lifetime. That’s where a trained, certified horologist comes in.

More than just a Watchmaker's School or Clockmaker's School

We’re one of only a few schools that offers a complete watchmaker program, and no other U.S. school offers a similar clockmaker program.

Have a successful career as a Watch Technician

Since 1995, we have graduated students into careers with high-end watch companies, as small business owners or employees, and as service technicians with exclusive jewelry retailers.
 

The School of Horology is licensed by Pennsylvania State Board of Private Licensed Schools and accredited by Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC).
