  • DIRECTIONS TO CAL U'S ROADMAN PARK

    From the Cal U entrance, follow Third Street to the traffic light at Wood. Turn left; continue uphill to the stop sign. Bear left slightly to continue on California Road (Route 88). Roadman Park is on your right. For parking, use the entrance just AFTER Adamson Stadium.

    More

  • PARKING REGISTRATION INSTRUCTIONS

    Students, faculty and staff who wish to park at Cal U must register online and obtain a parking permit/hang tag. Permits are required for parking on the main campus and at Roadman Park. Registration is open year ’round. Click "more" for step-by-step instructions.
