Join the Nationwide Effort to Save Student Aid

A college education is one of the most important investments families will ever make, but paying for it can be a real challenge for many. At the College of Saint Elizabeth, students know just how important Pell Grants are in playing a critical role in promoting educational achievement.

Join CSE, and the 60,000+ people nationwide who are signing the “Save Student Aid Statement of Support,” in an effort to save student financial aid programs from additional budget cuts, and help us keep a CSE education within your financial reach.
