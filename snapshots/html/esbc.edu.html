Since 1962, "Preparing graduates for professional careers, through an innovative approach to Cosmetology education"

Welcome to the world of glamour! Take your next step towards an exciting new career with Elaine Steven Beauty College.

We invite you to browse our website to find out more about our programs and the opportunities they can open for you. All of our courses are designed to prepare you for licensing in the state of Missouri. But far from stopping there, our innovative programs will have you ready to take your place as a licensed professional in the salon of your dreams!

Do you work?... have children or other personal obligations?
Our flexible scheduling and 4-day week means that we can accomodate your busy schedule. And with day and evening classes beginning every 4 weeks year round, there's no long wait to get started on your exciting new career!
Elaine Steven Beauty College is proud to have a 50 year history of successfully training Cosmetologists, Manicurists, and Instructors. We are Licensed by the State Board of Cosmetology in Missouri, and accredited by the National Accrediting Commission of Career Arts and Sciences (NACCAS) and offer Federal Financial Aid to those who qualify.
With our unique approach to combining the traditional classroom with realistic practice in our student styling salon, courses at Elaine Steven Beauty College provide students with all the skills and knowledge you need to succeed.
If you are looking for a multi-faceted and dynamic career in the beauty industry then look no further. Elaine Steven Beauty College is focused on your career. Call us today!
