Welcome to Tidewater Tech - Vocational Trades School!

"We prepare unskilled youth and adults to enter the labor force and provide training to help increase employment opportunities and earning income potential."

If you are looking to become a skilled worker then Tidewater Tech Trades may provide you with the education you need to succeed in the workforce. Our goal is ensure student success and mastery of skills you will train for, and instill a positive outlook in your working potential for your chosen trade. We respond to employer's needs so the skills you learn today will be relevant to the trade you are interested in working, now and in the future.

Our vocational school approvals and memberships include, State Council of Higher Education for Virginia (SCHEV), Virginia Committee on Veterans' Education, eligible trade school for Federal Family Education Loan Programs and Pell Grant through the Department of Education, National Automotive Technician Education Foundation, Virginia State Department of Police, Mobile Air Conditioning Society (MACS), ESCO Institute and Hampton Roads Auto Body Association.

For more information about our trade school and your career, please contact us today. You are under no obligation and we may be able to assist you with information on financial aid, career choices, academic needs and much more. You may call us at 757.858.8324 or fill out our student career information request form.
