WELCOME TO TRIBECA FLASHPOINT ACADEMY - CHICAGO'S PREMIER DIGITAL MEDIA ARTS COLLEGE

You rush home afternoons to work on game mods and spend your weekends shooting short films. You loyally post to your animation blog and keep ear buds away from your discerning taste in audio. You love motion and sound, color and design. You’re the next generation of digital artist, and you’re ready to take your passion to the next level.

At Tribeca Flashpoint Media Arts Academy, Chicago’s premier digital media arts college, people just like you are turning a lifelong love of art, storytelling, and digital media from a hobby into something greater. With five intensive, two-year programs to choose from, the very latest in digital technology at your fingertips, and a professional faculty teaching you real-world skills, you’ll earn your Associate’s degree while getting the focused, forward-thinking education to start down the path to career success.

You've found your passion. Make it real at Tribeca Flashpoint Media Arts Academy.
