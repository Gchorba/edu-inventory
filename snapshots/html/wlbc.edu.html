W.L. Bonner College was established in 1995, the reality of a dream envisioned in 1945. The intial purpose of theschool was to prepare pastors, ministers, and missionaries of the church in the organization to perpetuate its educational and evangelistic ministries. The college now enrolls students regardless of their denomination. The college is a fully accredit college approved by the American Association of Biblical Higher Education. It is located in Columbia, South Carolina.
