  • Exhibit Your Art on the Circle

    Notre Dame College and the City of South Euclid invite artists to display and sell their work at the second annual “Art on the NDC Circle” event on Saturday, June 23. Exhibit your artwork surrounded by the beauty of NDC’s serene and picturesque campus and be a part of the College’s fine arts weekend.

    For more information and to apply, visit NotreDameCollege.edu/ArtOnTheCircle.

  • NDC at 90: Looking Ahead While Celebrating the Past!

    On Sept. 18, 1922, Notre Dame College opened its doors to the first 13 students. Over the past 90 years, the College has grown to more than 2,200 current students and 5,500 alumni. Join us as we celebrate our past, present and future with several festivities over the next year!

    Visit NotreDameCollege.edu/90

  • Believing in Notre Dame

    Helen Foose Petersen ’38 was a smart and sophisticated economics student at Notre Dame College. She held several leadership positions on campus and was respected by her classmates. Petersen credited NDC for preparing her for an illustrious career with the State Department. To show her appreciation, she left the College $1.19 million in 2001.
