Positive Signs for Future Beauty Professionals

06 Apr

There is positive economic news about the beauty profession supporting the longterm viability of the industry.

USA Today recently reported that, "Beauty salon sales grew 5.4% in the last two years, vs. a 2.3% sales increase in 2009, according to an analysis of more than 960 financial statements from beauty salons, released Monday from financial analysis company Sageworks."
