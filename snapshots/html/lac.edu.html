For more than 25 years, LA College has been a community for learners seeking to grow in their careers. Many of our students and faculty live across the United States and attend classes to complete their online degree. Others meet in our ground campus classrooms in Los Angeles, California. Wherever our students and faculty meet, we are united in purpose. LA College is unique in providing services-oriented undergraduate degrees and delivering exceptional support to ensure student success. Success at LA College means high levels of academic achievement and increased career potential for every LA College graduate. Apply today and join our growing community at LA College International.

We offer undergraduate degrees in three services-oriented fields: healthcare, criminal justice, and business. LA College has healthcare degree programs that provide knowledge and skills needed in entry level, supervisory, and managerial positions in public and private hospitals, clinics, and care centers. We also offer criminal justice degree programs that prepare you for high-demand jobs in law enforcement, the courts, corrections, forensics, and other security fields. LA College has online degree programs in business management that will help you excel in high-demand supervisory and managerial positions where the latest knowledge of human resources, finance, accounting, and marketing is vital. Explore the online degree program options on our web site, and then call our Admissions Representatives right away. We want you to become a member of our learning community soon.

We understand that busy adults like you have work and family responsibilities and limited time to pursue an education. Our graduation team treats every student as an individual with unique needs for financing their education, developing their academic plan, and meeting their educational objectives. We offer an easy-to-start track of 24 credits in the first academic year, and you may earn more credits if you choose. Our faculty are professionals with career experience in healthcare, criminal justice, and business. In addition, each is a trained educator with special skills in online education. As the leaders in the LA College classroom, our faculty have just one objective â€“ to help you succeed. We also offer help in developing your resume, sharpening your interviewing skills, and locating employers so that you can advance your career while you learn. Call LA College today for more information about your on-campus or online degree or complete the online application for admission. We look forward to helping you!
