Your Future Begins Today

You have goals for your future. You have strong ideas about what you want to do and how you want to live. The problem is the steps to achieve these goals aren't always clear.

Pennco Tech can help you bridge the gap between dreams and reality. We are a technical school with locations in Blackwood, New Jersey and Bristol, Pennsylvania and we work with you to formulate a solid plan, and then give you the training and employer connections you need to accomplish that plan.

What's Your Plan?

Turning Dreams into Reality
Since 1973 Pennco Tech has been helping people achieve their goals. Pennco Tech has a long history of being the stepping stone people use to turn their dreams into reality.

Our Programs
Pennco Tech's career training schools in New Jersey and Pennsylvania employ instructors who know what it takes to achieve career goals; through hands-on and classroom training you will receive the necessary building blocks to succeed. Take a look at our programs and choose the right one that helps you achieve your dreams.

Military Friendly School
At Pennco Tech, we are a military friendly school that offers educational assistance to military members, veterans and/or spouses of veterans. Thanks in large part to the Post-9/11 GI Bill and MyCAA programs, financial constraints on higher education have been nearly eliminated, and we're happy to say that we are doing our part in serving our country's military members' career dreams.

At Pennco Tech, we proudly value your military experience and time spent serving our country. As a military friendly school, we take pleasure in welcoming veteran career-driven training opportunities without the financial burden. Let us help you achieve your academic and professional aspirations. You served our country's needs, we can help in serving yours.
