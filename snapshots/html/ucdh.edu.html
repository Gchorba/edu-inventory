The Utah College of Dental Hygiene (UCDH) at Careers Unlimited was established in 2005 to provide innovative dental hygiene education in the heart of Utah Valley.  Since opening its doors UCDH has had over 325 dental hygiene graduates with over 95%* of our graduates having worked as registered dental hygienists (RDH) in over 30 states!  At UCDH you can earn a Bachelor's Degree in Dental Hygiene (BSDH) from a private college in only twenty (20) months!  This unique 20 month program is the only one of it's kind in Utah.  The College has a 5:1 student to instructor clinical ratio that reflects our ongoing commitment to the success of our students. NOTE:(*New graduates are calculated into our statistics 6 months after graduation).

Why UCDH is a GREAT CHOICE!

1. Sixty (60) Students Accepted Each Class

UCDH is the largest private dental hygiene college in the state of Utah.  The campus covers over 24,000 square feet devoted completely to dental hygiene education.  The dental hygiene clinic is equipped with a total of sixty (60) full dental operatories, two large classrooms with dual color projectors, eighteen (18) X-ray bays, an on-site Learning Resource Center and computer lab with twenty-eight (28) stations, and a fully equipped dental materials lab. The college also has an on site full-time librarian to assist students with class research projects and writing assignments.  Currently the college has a 5:1 student to instructor clinical ratio.    

2. Personalized Financial Aid/Assistance
Getting a valuable college education should be within everyone's grasp. Our dedicated financial services staff can assist you in applying for various types of grants, loans, and scholarships. Financial Aid/Assistance, Title IV federal loans, and grants are available for those who qualify. Please contact the Financial Aid office for details and to see if you qualify. We are dedicated to helping you reach your career goals.  NOTE: Financial Aid is available for those who qualify.  All scholarships are offered by third party organizations not associated with the Utah College of Dental Hygiene at Careers Unlimited.  These scholarships can only be applied for after beginning the program.  

3. A Great Career! (Work in all 50 states)
Attending an accredited dental hygiene program, such as UCDH, allows graduates to apply for licensure throughout the United States and in many other countries around the world. Graduates may also apply to take state and/or regional exams in all 50 states and in many foreign countries. The United States Department of Labor Bureau of Labor Statistics lists the occupational and employment wages in May of 2008 for Dental Hygienist's from the entry level salary range of $44,180 dollars annually to a median salary of $66,570 per year!  (Source: www.bls.gov/oes/current/oes292021.htm ). Current as of 08-19-2009.

Utah Valley truly is a "one of a kind" location.  If you love outdoor recreation, skiing, and hiking then Utah is the place for you!
