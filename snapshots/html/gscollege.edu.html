President's Message

Good Samaritan College of Nursing and Health Science is part of a proud tradition of academic excellence and community service dating back to 1896. Since its start, over 5,500 GSC graduates have served around the globe.

Choosing GSC will offer you the opportunity to expand your knowledge and skills. Our students have the advantage of working with the latest equipment, technology and health care professionals at a state-of-the-art teaching hospital that is recognized throughout the country. The faculty and staff are committed to helping you succeed by providing individualized, systematic learning opportunities, guidance, and development.

At GSC, students gain the quality of knowledge that leads to stimulating, challenging and satisfying professional experiences. Just ask our graduates!

Morris Cohen
President
