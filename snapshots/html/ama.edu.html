Welcome and thank you for choosing American Medical Academy to help you in your effort to pursue a challenging and valuable career within the Medical Field.  American Medical Academy is licensed by the Florida Department of Education, Commission for Independent Education, Florida Department of Health Bureau of Emergency Medical Services Educational Program, Florida Board of Nursing and Accredited by the Accrediting Bureau of Health Education Schools. American Medical Academy is a Training Center for the American Heart Association and the National Association of Emergency Medical Technicians.  Our educators are all dedicated medical professionals, who are eager to share their knowledge and expertise and collectively aspire to cultivate a respect for the medical field of your choice. It is our objective to facilitate the success of every student, so that he/she may graduate confident in their acquired skill and education. American Medical Academy aims to enhance not only your potential but also enrich the community by motivating our student body to truly appreciate the importance of the medical field. We offer nothing, but the highest standards and education towards our EMT, Paramedic, Nursing and Associates Degree in EMS students.

About AMA

American Medical Academy is a student-centered, Healthcare focused institution, conveniently located in Miami. AMA has a commitment to advanced learning and diverse delivery of education in preparing graduates for success and a lifelong career in the healthcare industry.
