Programs We Offer


Bachelor of Theology
The Bachelor of Theology is designed primarily for the man preparing for full-time ministry. 120 semester hours of studying Bible, Theology, Biblical Languages, and other courses to prepare for the pastorate.

Bachelor of Biblical Studies
The Bachelor of Biblical Studies is designed primarily for the Christian preparing for ministry in the local church as teachers, youth leaders and staff positions. 120 semester hours of Bible and Theology courses to prepare for work in the local church.

Associate of Theology
The Associate of Theology is designed primarily for those, who want to increase their bible knowledge and improve their ministry skills. 60 hours of Bible, Christian Education and other courses to improve your work for the Lord.

Sponsoring Church

Illinois Baptist College operates as a mission work of Beverly Manor Missionary Baptist Church in Washington, Illinois. The college was established by the church to prepare Christians biblically, academically, practically and spiritually for a place of ministry that God has prepared for them.

Click here to learn more about Beverly Manor Missionary Baptist Church.

In addition to our sponsoring assembly, the college is supported financially by approximately 30 churches throughout the country, as well as through the prayers of many others.
