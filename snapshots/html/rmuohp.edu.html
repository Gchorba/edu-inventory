Our Mission

The mission of Rocky Mountain University of Health Professions is to educate current and future healthcare professionals for outcomes-oriented, evidence-based practice.

Our Educational Model

For our post-professional graduate programs, RMUoHP's blend of face-to-face and distance education allows students to maintain their home and professional commitments while pursuing advanced degrees which include integration of scholarship, practical experience, and research. The residential, entry-level DPT program incorporates technological and clinical advances as well as contemporary educational theory.
