Distance Education Programs at Brighton College

You want to pursue new career opportunities but it seems like your current level of education is standing in the way. Develop the knowledge and skills that you need to excel at Brighton College.

Brighton offers a number of convenient online distance education programs. We have been training students just like you for over 50 years. You can enroll anytime and learn from anywhere! You can view our accredited distance learning programs 24 hours per day, 7 days per week, and study our online college courses where you want and when you want. We offer distance education diploma programs in the following career fields:

  • Medical Billing
  • Medical Coding
  • Medical Billing & Coding
  • Medical Records Specialist
  • Medical Transcription
  • Medical Transcription & Coding
  • Pharmacy Technician
  • Diploma in Paralegal Studies
  • Criminal Justice Diploma
  • Legal Nurse Consultant

Brighton College also offers associate degree programs in the following fields:

  • Associate Degree in Business Technical Sales - New!
  • Associate Degree in Health Services Management
  • Associate Degree in Paralegal Studies
  • Associate Degree in Criminal Justice

Ready to prepare for a new career? Request more information about our any of our online, accredited distance learning programs, today.

Nationally Accredited
Brighton College is accredited by the Accrediting Commission of the Distance Education and Training Council (DETC), Washington D.C. The Accrediting Commission of the DETC is listed by the U.S. Department of Education as a nationally recognized accrediting agency and is a recognized member of the Council for Higher Education Accreditation.
