Bluewater Bible College and Institute, is a training ground for future ministers and leaders to the world. Since its inception in 1962, Bluewater Bible College & Institute has had a wonderful heritage of ministry in the West Indies and beyond. Students attending our institution have been trained in areas of Christian service vital to the ministries of our regional churches. These areas have included pastoral leadership, evangelistic crusades, church planting, Christian school education, and various children ministries.
