CCTC Employment Referral Database

Database Intent
Provide and maintain a free of charge platform upon which contractors and construction professionals can interact when searching for employment or qualified employees.

Employees / Job Seekers

  • Can post a resume to include work experience, specific skills, wage range and certifications.
  • Can review posted positions/ projects and submit applications.

Employers / Contractors

  • Post positions available and to describe positions and projects conditions, requirements and wages.
  • Search and view posted resumes
  • Accept applications and view associated resumes.

Employer Plus

  • Receives employee suggestions for positions the employer creates based on more than a dozen points of corolations between employee and position.
