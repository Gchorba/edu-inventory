University of America's

The University of Americas has designated the United States Sports Academy as custodian of records.

If you have questions concerning your student records, please contact the Academy Registrar or by phone at 251-626-3303.

If you have questions concerning anything other than student records, please contact the USSA Office of Administration and Finance at 251-447-0331.

The United States Sports Academy is an independent, non-profit, special mission school of sport offering sports-specific residential and online distance learning programs to students, teachers and administrators around the world. The Academy offers degree programs at the bachelor's, master's, and doctoral levels. Programs of study include fitness management, sports coaching, sports management, sports medicine, and sports studies. For more information, please visit http://www.ussa.edu/.
