Welcome

Welcome to Academy of Cosmetology, we invite you to have a look around our website for more about the exciting opportunities available to you in the hair, skin care, nail, and massage industries.

History of Our School:

The Merritt Island school was purchased by its current owner, Jacquilyn Eusanio, in 1986. The school was known then as Advanced & Basic Hair Design Training Center. The owner started the corporation, Sheer Success, Inc., in January, 1987, of which Ms. Eusanio is the president. The school operated as Advanced & Basic Hair Design Training Center until January, 2005, and then changed its name to Academy of Cosmetology.

The Melbourne branch was opened by its current owner, Jacquilyn Eusanio, in 2008.

Facilities

The Academy of Cosmetology is a sophisticated and contemporary 10,000 sq. ft. facility located at 2088 N. Courtenay Parkway, Merritt Island, Florida, 32953. It is fully equipped and accommodates thirty- nine working stations, a reception area, 3 classrooms, 4 manicure and 4 pedicure stations, a dispensary with laundry, a faculty office and an admissions office. There is an inside and outside break area as well as 6 rest rooms.  The Skin Care area has 4 private bays with facial and microderm machines, a large wet spa room, scrub area, laundry room and storage.  The Massage area includes 6 private massage bays, a classroom, scrub area and large storage closet.

The décor of Academy of Cosmetology-Melbourne, located at 2909 W. New Haven Avenue, Melbourne, Florida 32904 is sophisticated and contemporary. It is fully equipped with new equipment, a reception area, a classroom, a facial area, a dispensary, a supply room, a waiting room, a student lounge, offices, and restroom facilities, all consisting of 4, 800 square feet.

Mission Statement

The mission of this school is to prepare and train students to become knowledgeable and skilled and to encourage self-growth, motivation and professionalism in the cosmetology, facial and massage industry.
