National Massage Therapy Institute

We are passionate about massage therapy. To demonstrate that, massage therapy training is the only area of study that we offer. Our Massage Therapy programs are designed for those who seek rewarding massage therapist careers. Whether your interest is in a part-time or full-time career, our school of massage therapy can help you to succeed.

We provide a quality, hands-on education and quality credentials. Attend day, evening or weekend massage therapy courses in four convenient locations: Northeast Philadelphia, PA; Egg Harbor Township/Atlantic City, NJ; Falls Church, VA; and Wilmington, DE.
