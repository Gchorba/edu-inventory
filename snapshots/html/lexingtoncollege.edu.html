Lexington College is the premiere women's hospitality management college in the United States. The College grants a Bachelor of Applied Science (BAS) degree in Hospitality Management and an Associate of Applied Science (AAS) in Hospitality Management degree. Students pursuing the bachelor's degree select a specialization in event planning, hotel/restaurant management, culinary arts, or health care & wellness.
