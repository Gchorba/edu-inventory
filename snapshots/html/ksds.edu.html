  • The KSDS Mission

    We are committed to offering an unparalleled education that shapes young people of strong character and deep commitment to their Jewish identity. Learn More about Us

  • Close-knit Community

    Our atmosphere of warmth and respect promotes student collaboration and the celebration of individual accomplishments. Arrange a Visit

  • Academic Excellence

    Through our dual curriculum in general and Judaic studies, students develop the knowledge, skills and habits of mind to succeed in school and beyond our campus. Learn More about Student Outcomes

  • Jewish Values

    The values we teach are rooted in our history as a people, our study of Torah, and our commitment to the broader communities of family, synagogue and Klal Yisrael. Learn More about Jewish Life

  • Exceptional Results

    The measure of the success of our academic program is students who graduate to the finest independent schools and magnet high schools in the Baltimore area. Learn More about Student Outcomes

  • Dedicated Teachers

    Every member of our faculty is dedicated to teaching in a way that inspires thought, builds confidence and promotes a lifelong love of learning. Meet Our Teachers

  • Bilingual Education

    Our students are immersed in Hebrew as a living language as they explore Jewish texts, culture and tradition. Learn more about Academics

  • Individual Attention

    We recognize the unique qualities of each individual as we educate the whole child – academically, socially and emotionally, spiritually and physically. Admissions Q&A

  • Involved Parents

    The KSDS community extends beyond teachers and students to include parents, grandparents, siblings and friends. Learn More about Our Community

  • Creative Arts

    Art and music inspire creativity and better enable students to grasp the principles taught in every other area of study. Learn More about the Arts at KSDS

  • Team Sports

    Our athletes don't just develop physical fitness and skills. They also learn the value of hard work and sportsmanship. Learn More about Middle School Athletics

  • Technology

    Our students develop 21st century learning skills including adapting technology. Learn More about Technology at KSDS
