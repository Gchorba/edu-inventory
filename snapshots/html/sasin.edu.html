Welcome to Sasin

Welcome to the Sasin Graduate Institute of Business Administration of Chulalongkorn University, located in a park-like setting in the heart of Bangkok, Thailand. We provides education programs in English including MBA, Executive MBA, M.Sc. in Human Resource Management, Ph.D. in Finance & Marketing, Senior Executive Program, Advanced Senior Executive Program, Business English Certificate, and Sasin Management Consulting services, and the Sasin Institute for Global Affairs.

In 1982 the founders of Sasin set out to create a graduate-level management school that would stand side by side with the world’s best management schools. To achieve this, Chulalongkorn University, Thailand's pre-eminent university, collaborated with the Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University and the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania, two of America’s globally-renowned business schools.

On January 7, 2010, it became Thailand's first and only business school to be internationally accredited by the Association to Advance the Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB), the longest serving global accrediting body for business schools. On December 9, 2010, European Foundation for Management Development (EFMD), one of the world’s leading international business education accreditation bodies, has announced its decision to grant its accreditation called EQUIS (European Quality Improvement System) to Sasin.

Students in each class develop strong friendships through campus bonding activities on and off campus. They seamlessly link into Sasin’s alumni network by use of MySasin.Com, developed for the exclusive use of the Sasin Community.

Today, Sasin celebrates an outstanding track record of success in shaping leaders who have already taken, or are now in the process of taking, their places at the top not only in Thailand, but also across Asia and around the world.
