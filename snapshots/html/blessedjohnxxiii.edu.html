Catholic TV, a national cable network, featured Blessed John XXIII National Seminary in two shows - 'Catholic Destinations' and 'Clear Voice.'

On 'Clear Voice' - a magazine-style news show - they are ran an 8-week series including interviews with our rector, faculty and seminarians. Our Seminary was also profiled in an episode of 'Catholic Destinations' - which features places of significance to our Catholic Faith. To view the shows, please visit their website at www.CatholicTV.com/ClearVoice
