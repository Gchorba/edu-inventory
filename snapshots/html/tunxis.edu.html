Start on Your Path to Success at Tunxis Community College
Prepare for the Challenges of a Rapidly Changing World
Over 60 associate’s degrees and certificates designed for transfer to bachelor’s degree programs and direct entry into specialized career fields.
Dedicated faculty with high expectations and real-world experience.
Modern Library, classrooms, laboratories, study areas, and computer labs equipped with the latest technology.
Thousands of Tunxis alumni have transferred to some of the best colleges and universities in the country.
With special guaranteed admissions programs, you can seamlessly transfer credits and start as a junior at UConn or the CT State Universities.
Tunxis helps reduce the shortage of health care workers in Connecticut, offering over a dozen credit and non-credit programs with health care emphases.
Teaching commitment to community involvement that lasts long after graduation through community-based learning experiences and internships.
