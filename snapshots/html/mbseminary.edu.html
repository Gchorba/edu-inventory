Studying at Fresno Pacific Biblical Seminary

One of the major distinctions of Fresno Pacific Biblical Seminary is the relationship of students to faculty. Our typical faculty-student ratio is around 1 to 15 with anywhere from 8 to 30 students enrolled in a course. Our students interact on a first-name basis with leading scholars and teachers who are also committed Christian leaders and mentors.

Our Faculty

The faculty at Fresno Pacific Biblical Seminary are experts committed to academic excellence and student success.

Most have the highest degrees in their field and are active in their discipline. They also serve as mentors to students, engaging them outside of class and helping them develop professionally, ethically and personally.
