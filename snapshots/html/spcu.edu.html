Preparing today's Christian for tomorrow's ministry through Distance Education. We will soon offer classes on-line...be watching for info!

And we pray this in order that you may live a life worthy of the Lord and may please him in every way: bearing fruit in every good work, growing in the knowledge of God, Colossians 1:10

St. Paul Christina University and Covenant Divinity Seminary provide project-based, interdisciplinary theological distance education.

Distinctive Instructional Approach

  • Faculty members facilitate learning by linking students to fresh perspectives and grounded resources.
  • Faculty members coach students beyond knowledge leading them to wisdom.
  • Faculty members mentor student success by networking with them to find best practices in action.
  • Faculty members engage in preparing today’s Christian for tomorrow’s ministry through theological distance education and project-based learningÔ.

Instructional Foci:

  • Certification Subject Practice
  • Associate Subject Exploration
  • Bachelor Subject Breadth
  • Graduate Subject Depth
  • Doctoral Subject Interpretation

Saint Paul Christian University, and its subsidiary schools, exists to provide comprehensive, high quality, educational services, which include consultation, training for the Gospel ministry, the dissemination of information and opportunities for both study and research in the development of the Christian Ministry. Our goal is: "Preparing Today's Christian for Tomorrow's Ministry."

Read what our graduates have to say.....

At the time I enrolled in the post graduate program at SPCU I was employed by a Hospice. The independent research and study format allowed me the flexibility required to maintain a full work and on-call schedule. Since completion of the program I have taken a position as head of the Pastoral Care Department at a cardiac specialty care hospital here in Arizona. My experience with SPCU was a positive one and I would recommend this school to anyone interested in independent study. Rev. Christine A. Van Orden, Ph.D.

"I had been looking for an opportunity to complete my Post Graduate work by Distance Learning. I had been commuting to Harvard University for some time and had accumulated 52 hours towards my doctoral degree. I found SPCU on the internet and discovered them to be a credible instituion.
