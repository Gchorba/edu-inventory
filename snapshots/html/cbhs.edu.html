Welcome to Benedictine High School

Established in 1927 by the Benedictine Monks of Cleveland, our role as an educational community is to model, educate and challenge each student to become a part of the tradition and spirit of St. Benedict.  Benedictine is a community made up of enthusiastic young men who strive academically, spiritually and physically to become a “Man of Benedictine.”    Benedictine High School offers an exciting experience of growth and development for young men from around Northeast Ohio. 

We welcome you to explore our site and encourage you to register for an online account in order for you to better benefit from your website experience by clicking here.
