Antonelli Medical & Professional Institute

Education that works!

Whether you’re fresh out of high school, re-entering the job market, or preparing for a new career in healthcare, Antonelli Medical & Professional Institute will provide a comfortable and supportive learning environment for you to achieve your goal.  Our small classes, caring and professional instructors, and personalized attention at both the academic and administrative levels make going back to school a rewarding experience.
