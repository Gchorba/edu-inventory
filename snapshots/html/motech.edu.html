About MoTech


Missouri Tech has been teaching technology in the St. Louis metro area since 1932. Our goal is to provide you the education needed to succeed in today’s most exciting, in-demand technical careers. The instructors at Missouri Tech are committed to a learning approach which provides real-world experience preparing you to enter your chosen field as quickly as possible. If you are ready to earn what you are worth in a field you love, contact us!
