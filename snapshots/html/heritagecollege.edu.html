    Career Colleges and Technical Institutes

    Career colleges and technical institutes focus on providing career specific education that enables students to finish school quickly and enter their chosen career path as rapidly as possible. A career college or technical institute usually avoids teaching subjects that do not relate directly to the student's career path. Heritage career colleges and technical institutes offer career training designed to prepare students to be ready to start a new career as quickly as possible.

    At Heritage College and Heritage Institute in Denver, Colorado; Columbus, Ohio; Kansas City, Missouri; Oklahoma City, Oklahoma; Fort Myers, Florida; Little Rock, Arkansas; Wichita, Kansas; and Jacksonville, Florida our mission is the career. We train students to be prepared to enter the workforce quickly after graduation. While Heritage cannot guarantee employment, our training is designed to lead to entry level positions in meaningful careers and jobs in such diverse professions as X-Ray Medical Technician® - Medical Assistant, Pharmacy Technician, Esthetician, Surgical Technician, Dental Assistant and Dental Radiologist, Healthcare Administrator, Personal Fitness Trainer, and Massage Therapist.

     

    X-Ray Medical Technician® - Medical Assistant

    The Heritage X-Ray Medical Technician® - Medical Assistant program offers you an opportunity to broaden your opportunities by learning an extra set of skills. At the core of the program lies a curriculum to teach medical assisting. In addition to medical assistant training, the X-Ray Medical Technician® - Medical Assistant program also teaches limited-scope x-ray technician skills that make graduates more valuable in a medical office environment. Be sure to visit the page for our limited-scope x-ray tech school. The X-Ray Medical Technician® - Medical Assistant program is available at our Heritage campuses in Denver, Colorado; Columbus, Ohio; Wichita, Kansas; Jacksonville, Florida; Ft. Myers, Florida; Little Rock, Arkansas; Kansas City, Missouri; and Oklahoma City, Oklahoma.

    A Healthcare Administration School

    At the Heritage Hospital and Health Services Management® (HHSM) school we view hospital administrators as professionals who can make important contributions to the medical care of patients. Our Hospital and Health Services Management® school teaches healthcare administration as a profession. Healthcare administrators can look forward to using their skills in medical records and charting, healthcare management, medical billing, medical coding, medical business management, HIPAA training, and medical office administration. Our Ft. Myers, FL: Jacksonville, FL; Wichita, KS; Columbus, OH; Little Rock, AR; Oklahoma City, OK; and Kansas City, MO healthcare admin schools emphasize careers and the skills necessary for our grads to be qualified for the jobs they seek. Your curriculum at the Heritage Jacksonville, Wichita, Ft. Myers, Columbus, Oklahoma city, or Kansas City Hospital and Health Services Management® program, will include training in medical business management, health information management, medical billing, medical coding, basic medical procedures, medical law and ethics, medical records and charting, HIPAA rules and regulations, technology in a medical office, medical office administration, and more.
