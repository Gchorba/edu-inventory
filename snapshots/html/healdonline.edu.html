Reasons to Choose Heald

Get out quickly, leave prepared

You can complete your associate degree or diploma in 12-24 months and be ready for a new career. Certifications take 6-12 months. You get the experience you need in the shortest practical time. Need a flexible schedule? We offer classes during the day, evening and part-time.

A program for you

Start a new career in an exciting field! Heald College offers career-focused programs in Healthcare, Business, Legal and Technology. We award Associate in Applied Science degrees, Associate of Arts degrees, Diplomas and Certificates. In as little as 6 months, you can be on your way to a new career.

Free career planning and placement

Still not sure which program is right for you? Use our Free Career Planning Tool! Our admissions advisors will guide you through free career planning so you can discover the best path for you. And when you graduate, you are not alone. Heald College also helps you with career services assistance - for life.

A long history of education

Heald College has been preparing students for rewarding careers for over 145 years. Heald is a private career college with a prestigious regional accreditation. This is important! It means you get a quality education with credits that can be transferred if you decide to pursue a bachelor's degree at a college that accepts Heald credits.*

*It is always up to the receiving institution to make the final determination regarding acceptance of transfer credits and class standing.
