Critical Care Transport
Children's Mercy Critical Care Transport is a 24 hour-a-day dedicated pediatric and neonatal critical care transport program, and has been honored as one of the top program in the country by the Association of Air Medical Services. Specially trained registered nurses, respiratory therapists and emergency medical technicians make up the crew for each neonatal/pediatric transport team. Our specially equipped ambulances, helicopter and fixed wing plane offer the most advanced care for neonatal and pediatric patients requiring transport for critical care services.
