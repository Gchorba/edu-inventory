Make Shafston Your Choice

Established in 1996 on the banks of the Brisbane river, Shafston has grown to be the largest college of its type in Australia with over 60,000 students having attended since its inception.

Offering a wide selection of courses in English, Business, TESOL and Hospitality, Shafston prides itself on its high standard of teaching. The friendly staff are highly qualified and motivated to guide their students down a pathway to success.

Watch Video: 10 Reasons to Study At Shafston

For more than 16 years Shafston has provided its students with educational experiences of the highest quality in first class facilities!

Shafston offers you the ability to study in a wide variety of internationally recognised English language courses including; General English, IELTS Preparation, TOEIC Preparation, TOEFL Preparation, Cambridge Exam Preparation and Cambridge Business English. Other courses including Business, TESOL (Teach English to Speakers of Other Languages) and Hospitality. Shafston strives to provide a wide selection of up-to-date, effective courses for students of all levels.

Shafston features three unique campuses; the Brisbane Campus which is centered on 'Shafston House', a heritage-listed building in Brisbane's inner suburbs, L'Academie Hotel, a purpose-built training facility in Brisbane's central business district and the Gold Coast Campus, located amongst the beautiful Queensland beaches.

These campuses allow our students to enjoy a true university campus experience and atmosphere with beautiful outdoor spaces as well as excellent teaching and learning facilities. Shafston also boasts one of the most diverse student populations in the country.
