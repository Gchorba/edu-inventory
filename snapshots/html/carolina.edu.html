CCC Wins Enrollment Growth Award!

Dean Lucas Receiving Growth Award

We did it again! During the 65th annual meeting of the Association of Biblical Higher Education (ABHE), Carolina Christian College was the honored recipient of the Jonathon Thigpen Enrollment Growth Award for the 0-199 quadrant. Carolina Christian College experienced a 53% growth increase from the previous year, which was the highest of all the schools in our class. President Donald Young and Dean Latanya Lucas were on hand to accept the award, which was highlighted by the re-affirmation of the schools accreditation.

Dean Lucas and Dr. Young

The Association for Biblical higher Education is a North American agency, made of approximately 200 postsecondary institutions throughout North America specializing in biblical ministry formation and professional leadership education. CCC received its official accreditation in 2004 and has maintained the highest level of academic rigor since that time. With accreditation, CCC is recognized by the NC and US Dept. of Education as a School of Theology. Furthermore, students have access to federal student aid through Pell grants, Federal Supplement Opportunity Grants, Stafford loans and VA benefits. Outstanding Job CCC team!
