Founded in 1965, Manhattan Area Technical College is a two year HLC accredited public institution of higher education. MATC has a long and vibrant history of providing quality technical and continuing education for individuals who are making lifelong career decisions.

We offer 15 programs of study, each granting an Associate of Applied Science degree or Technical Certificate. These programs are:


  • Advanced Biotechnology Certificate
  • Air Conditioning & Refrigeration
  • Associate Degree Nursing
  • Automotive Collision Repair
  • Automotive Technology
  • Building Trades
  • Business Administration
  • Computer Aided Drafting
  • Dental Hygiene
  • Electric Power & Distribution
  • Information & Network Technology
  • Medical Laboratory Technician
  • Practical Nursing
  • Surgical Technology
  • Welding Technology
