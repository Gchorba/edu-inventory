Since 1842 - Achieving Excellence in the Education of Principled Leaders

The Citadel has been recognized by U.S. News & World Report as the top public college in the south for institutions granting up to a master’s degree.

While the internationally renowned South Carolina Corps of Cadets is the traditional centerpiece for our college, we also operate non-military degree and certification programs in The Citadel Graduate College and the Evening Undergraduate Studies program, which appeal to people interested in furthering their education or careers.
