Welcome to Gutenberg College

Gutenberg is a "Great Books" college providing a B.A. in Liberal Arts. Gutenberg offers a broad-based education in a unique environment. Our goal is to help students develop into mature adults, to equip them to pursue understanding, skill, and wisdom.

The Gutenberg Story: Multimedia resources to tell you about this unique four-year Great Books college.

Becoming a Student: The four-year degree at Gutenberg College—information about academics, student life, and enrollment.

The Institutes of Gutenberg: Gutenberg's institutes are dedicated to biblical studies (McKenzie Study Center), the arts (Art Project), and publishing (Gutenberg College Press).
