ISP Board Scholarship for Excellence Available for TWO IB Diploma Students
A 2-year scholarship is available for two exceptional IB Diploma students. This will be a full two-year scholarship for a student of high academic ability and demonstrated financial need who is entering the first year of the IB Diploma program (Grade 11).
ISP Gala & Auction – Les Années Folles: A Roaring Success!!
Thank you to everyone who participated in last Saturday's Gala & Auction! We were pleased to welcome 140 parents, teachers and other members of the community to the Cercle de l'Union Interalliée for an evening in support of teaching and learning at ISP.
ISP Initiates Exchange with the American British Academy in Muscat
A group of Grade 11 students from the American British Academy in Muscat (Oman) visited ISP in February for an education exchange, and invited ISP students and teachers to visit Oman in return.
