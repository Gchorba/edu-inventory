Simpson University, located in Redding, Calif., is a Christian University offering undergraduate (bachelor degrees), graduate (master degrees), continuing studies (adult education / degree completion) and teaching credential programs. A.W. Tozer Theological Seminary is a graduate school of Simpson University and affilated with the Christian and Missionary Alliance. One of many outstanding Christian colleges on the west coast (California), Simpson University is recognized as "The Christian University of northern California."
