Meet Our Educational Director of Education, Ron Hawkins, a stylist since the age of 14, he has won over 800 styling awards and honors, as well as being inducted into the prestigious National Cosmetology Hall of Fame! Ron is responsible for creating some of the newest design fashions for today's progressive stylists of the world. He has been an International Trainer for many countries throughout the world and a trainer for the USA World Team. Ron is not only a master artist in hair, but is a fashion and creative designer artist as well. Come let Ron Hawkins help you get your career started in Cosmetology at Mitchell's Academy!

Check out his latest video at the 2008 HairWorld International competition where 12 of his team members placed in the top 10 at the finals.

Testimonials
Patrick Hawker, Graduate 2002,  Salon Owner Intaglio Salon  & Educator for Goldwell

>Mitchell's Academy has led the way in shaping and setting the standards for cosmetologists in the Triangle for many years.  As a former student and graduate of Mitchell's Academy I must say that I am still amazed to see the level of consistent, compassionate, & caring instruction still being offered there.  The dedicated team of instructors that were present almost ten years ago are still there to this day.  I am truly grateful for the instruction that I received at Mitchell's!  They helped in building my confidence as a student stylist, which better prepared me for the adventures I would embark upon."

"Now looking back over my career as a stylist, I can truly see just how blessed I was to be a student and to learn the lessons that I learned from the Mitchell's educational team.  Almost ten years later, as a stylist owner of a salon in the Durham area and as an educator for Goldwell, I know that Mitchell's played an instrumental part in forging the path that I am on today."
