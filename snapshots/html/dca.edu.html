Aerosim Flight Academy

A Top-Tier Flight School Created by the Airlines

Aerosim Flight Academy is a top-tier flight school that was originally created by the airlines (Delta/Comair). Because our training structure was developed by the airlines, our instruction is rooted in the discipline required to turn students into successful airline pilots.

The pilot shortage is real, and minimum flight time requirements set by the airlines continue to fluctuate. But one thing remains constant: Airlines continue to hire only the most qualified pilots, which means where you receive your training matters.

At Aerosim Flight Academy:

  • We believe hard work and professionalism are required if students want to become successful airline pilots. Our flight instructors are considered best-in-class and are quickly recruited by leading airlines.
  • We believe skill and safety can never be defined by minimum standards. Our training regimen exceeds FAA requirements to ensure safety and excellence in aviation.
  • We believe in helping place our students and flight instructors at a job within the airlines. Our extensive network with regional airline partners opens the door to more career opportunities for our graduates.

Fly for One of Our Regional Airline Partners

The Academy has partnership agreements with several regional airlines, including Compass, GoJet, American Eagle, Trans State and more. These airlines are so confident in the training provided at Aerosim Flight Academy that they offer conditional job offers to students who qualify BEFORE they even graduate from flight training.

“The transition from being a flight student to an instructor then an airline pilot was smooth and effortless.  The way the curriculum is set up, there are no big surprises when you reach the airlines, so the airline training is rather easy.” − Captain Dipesh Patel, (Atlantic Southeast Airlines).
