Welcome to England.edu

Your premier resource for education in England. Designed for students who wish to study in England, England.edu has worked hard since its launch in 1998 to establish connections with the best universities, institutes, and English language centers. From selecting the right school to setting up convenient travel, England.edu has everything students might need to know about education in England.

Students interesting in distance learning should consider studying online. England online education is particularly beneficial for students who wish to attend university in England, but may not be able to relocate to England for their studies due to family, work or other obligations at the current time. Discover more by starting at the navigation links at the top or to the right in the sidebar to learn more about all the possibilities studying in England.
