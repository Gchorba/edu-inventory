America's Technical College

Announcing New Programs:  Respiratory Care Technology, Veterinary Technology, Cyber Security Technology, Electronic Health Records and RN to BSN Fully On-line Program
                                                                                           

When New England Tech opened its doors in 1940, technology was in its infancy. The family radio was the entertainment center, and computers were only seen in science fiction movies! Back then, we made a commitment to excellence that has remained unchanged in a changing world.

Today, as the undisputed leader in technical career education in Southeastern New England, we take great pride in the thousands of students who have graduated from New England Tech with all the skills necessary for a successful career.

Each of our programs is taught with a powerful combination of technical expertise and hands-on training. Associate’s and Bachelor’s of Science degrees in over 35 different programs all share one important element: dedicated instructors who bring real-world experience to their students.

Most Associate Degree programs can be completed in as little as 18 months, and Bachelor’s of Science programs in as little as 3 years. Our ongoing investment in world-class laboratories and equipment sets the stage for success.

From admissions to financial aid to employment assistance, you can count on all the support you need from New England Tech. There’s no doubt that the world will continue to change. But you can be sure that when you graduate, you’ll leave with a cutting-edge technical education and the critical thinking skills that employers want.
