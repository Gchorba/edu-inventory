Mercyhurst has multimillion dollar impact on Erie economy

Mercyhurst University contributes in excess of $101.1 million annually to Erie County’s economy through direct and indirect spending, according to the recently released Mercyhurst University Community Impact Report.

May 03, 2012

Statue resumes rightful place in Mercyhurst's new Mary Garden

The newly restored statue of Our Lady of Mercy resumed its rightful place on the front of campus today as finishing touches continue on the new Mary Garden.

May 03, 2012

Social media has negative impact on IC hiring, Mercyhurst study shows

Graduate students in Mercyhurst University's applied intelligence program recently presented their controversial study on social media and its impact on the intelligence community's hiring pool in Washington, D.C., before a panel of representatives from U.S. intelligence agencies.

Interior design students unveil capstone projects at Cummings

Eight graduating seniors in Mercyhurst University’s interior design program are exhibiting their capstone projects at the school’s Cummings Art Gallery May 1-22. The students will be honored at a reception on Thursday, May 3, from 7 to 9 p.m.
