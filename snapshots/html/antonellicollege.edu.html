Prospective Students

Join the Antonelli Family

Advance your career

You may come to us to prepare for a successful career, but you will leave with so much more. At Antonelli College, our primary goal is to create an educational experience that will help you grow academically, personally and socially as you not only become a qualified professional but also a well-rounded member of society.

Accelerate your future

As a career-focused school, Antonelli College offers programs in some of today's fastest-growing career fields. Your classes will focus specifically on the knowledge and skills you need to confidently step into your new career, and our flexible courses are tailored to fit your busy lifestyle.

Accomplish your goals

At Antonelli College, we recognize that you are an individual with hopes and dreams, but at the same time, we instantly welcome you to become part of our community. You'll meet engaging faculty members dedicated to your success, and our strong alumni network can be found in communities throughout Ohio and Mississippi.

Attain your dreams

We focus on much more than learning; we're about making tomorrow a better place – to learn, to work and to live. At Antonelli College, you'll have the advantage of lifelong and life-changing relationships while achieving personal, social and academic growth.
