    Veteran & Military Information

    You may know Helms Career Institute’s proven programs in culinary arts. What you may not know are the advantages available to those of you who are making the transition from military life to a new career. 

    Helms Career Institute’s Culinary Arts Diploma Program is approved for enrollment of persons eligible to receive education benefits from the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs.
