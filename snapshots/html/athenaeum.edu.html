The Athenaeum of Ohio, the graduate school of theology sponsored by the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Cincinnati, prepares people to serve the Church

Mount St. Mary's Seminary

 provides spiritual guidance, theological knowledge, practical experience and a prayerful home for the men who are answering the call to go forth and nurture God's people.

Diaconate

 - The Athenaeum is a place alive with seminarians, deacon candidates and lay pastoral ministry students who strive to be open to the Spirit and God's call.
The Athenaeum's

Lay Pastoral Ministry Program

 helps students realize their spiritual gifts and develop the skills they need to share those gifts with their parish.
The Athenaeum's

Master Degree Programs

 help to form minds intellectually and spiritually to be servants of God's church.
