Associate of Science in Health Professions Degree
Major: Health Sciences

Now offering an Associate of Science in Health Professions Degree with a major in Health Sciences!  This degree is similar to an academic transfer degree with an emphasis on the life sciences. 

  • Primarily intended to prepare students to pursue a Baccalaureate Degree in a healthcare field and meets the basic general education requirements for all programs currently offered at BCHS. 
  • Students can choose from a variety of curriculum plans depending on their career goals. 
  • Credits earned in the program are eligible for transfer to other colleges and universities offering health professions programs not available at BCHS.

For more information contact the Admissions Office at:
