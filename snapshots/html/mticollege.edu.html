The Gold Standard

MTI College provides a quality education aimed at putting graduates to work. MTI is a fast-paced, fully-accredited junior college offering diploma and two-year associate degrees.

MTI offers a personalized learning environment. Long lines at the bookstore? At MTI, we deliver your books right to your classroom. Registration hassles? At MTI, your schedule is prepared for you with all the classes you need to graduate at your expected time. Crowded classes? At MTI, all classes are small allowing students to get personal help from our great instructors.

Explore our programs and, when you are ready, contact us to learn how MTI can help you get your career started. We are sure you will find that MTI is different from other colleges - we're the best!

Explore our Programs

Accounting

Professional sports teams, auto shops, and veterinarians. What do these industries have in common? Answer: all need accounting professionals. Start here!

Legal Studies

MTI is proud of our American Bar Association (ABA) approved paralegal program. Study to become a paralegal or legal assistant.

Technology

Learn about the latest technology and earn certifications such as CompTIA A+ and Microsoft MCITP. Become a tech support specialist or a Microsoft network administrator.

Healthcare

MTI offers programs in medical assisting, phlebotomy, and medical billing & coding. For some programs, MTI can help provide valuable externship positions with area hospitals and clinics.

Business

Management, finance, marketing and much more provide our students with skills in demand in today's business workplace. MTI offers diploma and full Associate in Art degrees.

Cosmetology

Paul Mitchell The School MTI College, Sacramento provides an exciting and creative path into the beauty industry. Paul Mitchell The School can provide a fantastic start for your career!

Why MTI College was Voted Best...

  • In the 2011 reporting year, 90% of our graduates secured jobs. Our Career Services department works with hundreds of Sacramento area employers to help our grads secure good jobs.

  • Our programs are aimed at getting you hired. In less than a year, you can earn the credential needed for a good job and in less than two years, you can earn your degree.

  • Since 1965, MTI College has served the Sacramento community as a fully accredited junior college.

  • Personal attention is provided to students through a small student-faculty ratio.

  • MTI is the only local college that stands behind its graduates by offering employers a 30-day salary-back guarantee.

  • Our programs are focused on quickly providing the skills needed for employment. Be job-ready in as little as nine months!

  • MTI College is a Sacramento community-based institution, without any ties to Wall Street-related corporations or investment firms.

  • Free parking, books delivered straight to your classroom, custom class schedules set up for you. At MTI, your focus is on learning.
