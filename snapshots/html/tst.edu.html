Appointment of new TST Registrar

TST is pleased to report the appointment of Diane Henson, M.A., as its new Registrar. She has agreed to begin her new duties on Monday, May 7, 2012. Diane has a graduate degree in Careers Education and Guidance, and twelve years of experience in post-secondary student services, including the positions of Manager of Recruitment and Communications at Centennial College and Director of Admissions and Recruitment at Lakehead University. We're confident that Diane will bring additional strength to our exceptionally able administrative team.

TST Students and Faculty on Program for Annual Canadian Theological Society Meeting

TST is pleased that a seemingly record number of TST students and faculty will be on the program for the annual Canadian Theological Society meeting during the Congress of Humanities and Social Sciences (Wilfrid Laurier University, Waterloo (28-30 May 2012)). Some recognition should also go to Profs. Lee Cormie and Michael Bourgeois, for their work on the executive of the Society.

Let us offer our congratulations to those TST students and faculty who had their papers accepted and express our very best wishes for successful presentations.
