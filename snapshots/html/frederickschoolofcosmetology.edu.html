Supporting the Community

On February 14, 2011, the Frederick School of Cosmetology donated its facilities and services to support the Children of Incarcerated Parents Partnership in Frederick.

Children of Incarcerated Parents Partnership works with local community institutions such as churches and other non-profits to provide support to families coping with family members that are incarcerated.

FSC Students Donate to the Heartly House
Students and staff of the Frederick School of Cosmetology gave back to the community -- comfortably this past October. For the entire month, students and staff paid $2 a day to wear jeans to school instead of standard black slacks.
 
The jean donation program was a success. FSC collected $250 for Heartly House.
 
Heartly House provides comprehensive services for victims and survivors of domestic violence, rape/sexual assault and child abuse.
 
To learn more about Heartly House and/or support their mission, please check out their website.
