Welcome to Forsyth Tech!

Forsyth Tech is your community college. It's your source for affordable college education, offering two-year AAS degrees and college transfer opportunities. It's where you'll find the job training and skills you need for a lasting career or a new start. So look around our site and see what we have to offer. There's sure to be something for you.
