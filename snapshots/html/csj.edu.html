  • CSJ Inducts Five to Kappa Delta Pi

    On April 25th, the CSJ Chapter of Kappa Delta Pi, the International Honor Society in Education, inducted its five newest members.  Toni Christensen, Lauren Davie, Kerrie Kingsley, Charles Schulman, and Corey Smith were all honored at a ceremony in St. Joseph Hal by Dr. Gwen Sussman, Chapter Counselor.  Congratulations to all inductees!
