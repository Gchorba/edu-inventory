Welcome to Northern Michigan University

With over 180 degree programs, a high-tech, high-touch campus and one of America's most liveable communities, NMU is a great place to begin your next adventure.

What's Happening at NMU

Good Luck on Finals
Good luck on your final exams, NMU students.  Exam week is Monday, April 30, through Saturday, May 5. Most exams are held on the first class meeting day that a course is held on during the semester and are scheduled in two-hour blocks of time.   Complete exam week schedule
Faculty Advice on Exams
Commencement is May 5
Congratulations, NMU graduates.  Ada Deer, former assistant secretary for Indian affairs with the U.S. Department of the Interior, will be the keynote speaker at Saturday’s (May 5) commencement ceremony. The ceremony begins at 10:30 a.m. at the Superior Dome and is open to the public.  NMU Commencement
Senior Art Exhibit
This is the final week to see this semester’s senior art exhibit, titled “Some Assembly Required,” at the DeVos Art Museum. A reception will be held Friday evening to close the exhibit, which features the students’ best works and is free and open to the public.  DeVos Museum hours
