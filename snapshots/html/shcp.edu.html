Irish Eyes Smiling: Basketball Wins NorCal Title on St. Paddy's Day
Sacred Heart Cathedral won the CIF Division III Northern California bracket with a 61-57 decision over Bishop O'Dowd on St. Patrick's Day, March 17, in Sacramento. The Irish will now play for the Division III State Championship against Bishop Alemany High School of Mission Hills, outside of Los Angeles. The state title game will be at 2:45 p.m. on March 24.
