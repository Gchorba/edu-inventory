The Medina County Career Center offers both high school students and adult learners academic instruction and career training to meet the demands of today's high standards in education and industry-driven career opportunities.

 
 

The Adult & Continuing Education department provides customized training. Full-time courses provide complete training for new careers. Part-time courses enable you to keep your resume up-to-date and provide you with opportunity to learn new skills.

Start planning your future now!  We have over 25 technical career majors covering practically every career field.  At MCCC, students can begin earning college credit before stepping foot on a college campus - saving time and money.  Many career majors provide our students access to industry-recognized certifications. After graduation, students can go directly into the job market or capitalize on their college credits by continuing their education like 87.6% of our graduates did last year!
