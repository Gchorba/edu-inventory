Become a part of history – this year marks duCret’s 85th year! The duCret School of Art is NJ’s oldest private art institution. Listed among the many who have studied at duCret are nationally acclaimed painters, portrait artists and illustrators, as well as graphic designers who are now affiliated with some of the nation’s most prestigious advertising agencies.
