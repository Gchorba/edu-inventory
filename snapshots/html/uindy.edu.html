On a first-name basis

With a student-to-faculty ratio of 14-to-1 and an average class size of 18, UIndy makes your education personal. Faculty get to know students on a first-name basis and are readily available to help students when needed. In fact, we're so proud of the personal attention that we're having a little fun in a YouTube campaign.

Check out the videos ►

Friendship and opportunity

From the moment you step on campus you can feel the sense of community that is UIndy. You'll find friendship and collegiality as a resident in one of our six residence halls, as an athlete in one of our 21 Division II athletics programs, or as a participant in some of the 50-plus student organizations. There are many ways to get involved!

Get connected ►

Learn by doing

Whether your interest is archeology or visual communication, you'll be an active learner at UIndy. You'll get to do what you love, not just talk about it. And if the classroom doesn't give you enough "hands-on" experience, our location in the nation's 14th largest city provides plenty of internship opportunities.

Learn about UIndy ►

It's affordable

Figuring out how to pay for college can be overwhelming. Let our qualified financial aid counselors demonstrate how affordable UIndy can be. We've kept the reins on tuition while continuing to provide millions of dollars in aid to students based on need and merit. You can afford it; we'll show you!
