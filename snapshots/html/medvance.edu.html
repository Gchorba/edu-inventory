Advance Your Life In About A Year ®

As a healthcare training school, our goal is to make sure you get the best instruction in a supportive environment and help you achieve success in the career you choose.

In as little as a year, you can graduate from MedVance and be prepared to step confidently into your new career. You'll be prepared to make a difference in your workplace and your community.

Best of all, you'll be working in a career you're proud of, and really enjoy. What could be more rewarding than that?

Choose from any of our trade and healthcare schools in Florida to begin receiving the training you deserve!

Looking for our campuses in Louisiana, Tennessee or Texas? Don't worry. They haven't gone anywhere. The MedVance Louisiana, Texas and Tennessee campuses have been rebranded to Fortis. You can read about this transition on the fortis.edu website or click on the campus of your choice in the Locations dropdown menu at the top of the page to find out more!
