Cooperating Libraries in Consortium

CLIC was founded in 1969 to support cooperative collection development, interlibrary loan and document delivery in and among its member libraries. Since then it has expanded its mission to support a wide range of library services, including digitization of selected library collections and access to electronic resources.  CLIC is primarily supported by membership dues.

CLIC Vision
CLIC exists to create a world-class research library experience for its academic communities.

CLIC Mission
CLIC enhances the strengths of its member libraries by
-  Making library users the focus of its collaborative work
-  Sharing best practices, ideas and resources to improve services
-  Engaging library staff in professional development, thereby fostering leadership, creativity and innovation in the pursuit of excellence
