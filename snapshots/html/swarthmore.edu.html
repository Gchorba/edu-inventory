Every spring, the Swarthmore community welcomes a host of visitors, including newly admitted students, prospective students, honors examiners, families who come to celebrate Commencement, and alumni for the annual reunion weekend. Not able to visit the campus in person? Tour the campus online.

    Swarthmore in the City

    Old City

    Explore the most historic square mile in the country! History buffs and political junkies can wander the cobble stone streets to visit Independence Hall, the Liberty Bell, Christ Church, and the National Constitution Center. Art lovers will appreciate the neighborhood's numerous galleries, studios, and performance companies—gallery openings are traditionally held on the first Friday of each month.
