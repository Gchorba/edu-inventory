The College of Visual Arts is a private, accredited, four-year college of art and design offering a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in fashion design, fine arts, graphic design, illustration, and photography. The fine arts degree offers concentrations in drawing, painting, printmaking, and sculpture.

Founded in 1924, the college is located in the thriving urban residential areas of historic Summit Avenue and Ramsey Hill in Saint Paul, Minnesota.
