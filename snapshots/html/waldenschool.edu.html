Located in Pasadena, CA, Walden School is an accredited, progressive independent school for grades pre-kindergarten - 6. Students benefit from a challenging academic program that is balanced with developmental awareness of the whole child and child-led inquiry to promote critical thinking skills as well as a sense of confidence, responsibility and self-direction. Unique to Walden are the Storyteller-in-Residence which adds depth and breadth to the rich language arts program, and a focus on environmental education and awareness.
