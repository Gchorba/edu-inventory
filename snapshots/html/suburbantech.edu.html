Dear Suburban Technical School Students and Alumni:

Suburban Technical Institute (STS) has decided not to renew its lease at 175 Fulton Street, 6th Fl., Hempstead, NY 11550 and, as a result, will be closing. Staff will be available at the campus through March 15, 2012.  
 
Premier Education Group (PEG), the parent company of Suburban Technical School, is committed to providing its students with quality education and support. To this end, STS will facilitate your success, with your assistance, through graduation and placement. PEG is still here to assist you in achieving your academic and career success. 
 
However, any issues or concerns that may arise dealing with your education, placement, or collections may be directed to Sally Ramos at our Branford Hall Career Institute campus in Bohemia, NY, (631) 589-1222. 
 
Should you require additional assistance or should you need a copy of your transcript and/or financial aid documents, you can contact PEG’s corporate offices at 100 South Shore Drive, East Haven, CT 06512. (203) 672-2300 If you would like assistance via the Internet click here
 
The New York State Education Department at: New York State Education Department, 116 West 32nd Street, 5th Floor, New York, NY 10001, Attention: Bureau of Proprietary School Supervision, (212) 643-4760 is also available to answer questions and provide assistance.
