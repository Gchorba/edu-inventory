Phoenix School of Law is committed to market-leading performance in first time bar passage.  With the assistance of a dedicated full-time bar preparation professional, PhoenixLaw students develop and apply effective strategies and skills to create an individualized bar study plan.  In addition, students complete  practice essays and multiple-choice exams with individualized feedback following an embedded review of substantive areas of law.

The Academic Success Program provides workshops, small group, and individual academic support counseling to assist students throughout their law school career.  The Academic Success Program facilitates students in developing strategies in self-learning for success in law school and practice.

The Information Resources Center is committed to a learning experience that is student-centered and creates practice-ready graduates. Our collection of online, audio, and print materials is designed to support the curriculum and research needs of our students and faculty.

Phoenix of Law offers flexible part-time day and evening programs for people with full-time lives.  You don't have to give up your day job to get a law degree.  Learn how you can fit law school into your busy schedule!

 

We believe that in order to create law graduates who are professionally prepared, they must be exposed to practical skills and ethical instruction.  At PhoenixLaw, there are five main areas of focus for experiential learning:  Infusion of Skills and Values; Skills Courses; General Practice Skills; Clinical Programs; and the Externship Program.
