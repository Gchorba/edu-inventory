ABSW’s 8th Annual
Mentor Projects Exhibit

 

 

MONDAY, MAY 7 4-6 PM
Crouch Classroom
ABSW Hobart Hall
1st floor

Advanced students in ABSW’s M.Div. program have been engaged this past year in researching, designing, and implementing major projects in specialized areas of ministry. On Monday, May 7th, 2012, these projects will be on display in ABSW’s Crouch Classroom, and the project designers will be there to dialogue with visitors. Please come, experience, and discuss with us this wide variety of ministry initiatives that are building up the Body of Christ in our churches,neighborhoods, and communities.

2012 Projects by:  Rose Beeson, Mary Breland, Koan Shin Choi, Christiana Felix, Evelyn Glenn, Jae Eun Kim, Bruce Lohmann, Worthemmi Luikham, Loretta Moody, Brenda Pope, Eunkyung Tak.
