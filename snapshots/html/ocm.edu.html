Ohio College of Massotherapy & 
Ohio College of Massotherapy Online 
Since 1973 Ohio College of Massotherapy has been educating students in the field of massage therapy. Ohio College of Massotherapy has one of the best first time passing rates for the State Medical Board Exam and is well respected around the country. 

The Ohio College of Massotherapy Online was started to serve the busy adult learner.  With family, working time commitments, and distance issues OCM Online solves those issues for the student that desires to work as a massage therapist, but can't due to busy life issues.  Study anytime, anywhere to achieve your Associates Degree.  Only attend the college for the "hands on" requirement needed to achieve your goal to become a Licensed Massage Therapist.
