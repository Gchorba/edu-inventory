The Healthcare Training Institute is one of the leading vocational schools in New Jersey. We provide quality education for our students. We specialize in training healthcare careers such as:
  • Medical Assistant Technician
  • Medical Billing Specialist
  • Phlebotomy & EKG Technician
  • Medical Coding & Billing Specialist
  • Certified Nurse Aide
  • Fundamentals of Adult Echocardiography
  • Diagnostic Medical Ultrasound Technologist
  • Patient Care Technician

General basic computer training is also taught as it relates and complements each healthcare discipline area.  We offer high quality training on up-to-date equipment and are committed to providing the best training available for the students as well.

Our highly qualified instructors are professionals in their field of expertise and strongly promote competencies and job readiness.  Equally important is our commitment to teaching effective skills in communication, leadership, professionalism and ethics. Included in our training is basic knowledge in medical jurisprudence as it relates to the healthcare environment.

The goal of Healthcare Training Institute is to match the speed of technology and offer the best training on this technology.

Healthcare Training Institute is approved by the State of New Jersey, accreditated by the A.C.C.S.C (Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges) and approved by the United States Department of Education.
