New Programs Approved

TC3 is now offering three new programs: A.S. degree in Digital Cinema, A.S. degree in Recreation: Exercise Studies, and a certificate in Wine Marketing. All three programs have received final state approvals.

These programs will give students more options that can lead to great transfer options or wonderful job opportunities. The College now offers more than 40 degree programs and more than a dozen certificate programs.

Fall 2011 Dean’s List Released

Provost and Vice President of the College John Conners has released the Fall 2011 Dean’s List, which includes a total of 489 students. The Dean's List recognizes matriculated students who have demonstrated high academic achievement by attaining a GPA of 3.5 or better while completing at least 12 credit hours during the semester. For part-time matriculated students, they are eligible each time they complete a block of 12 hours with a minimum GPA of 3.5.

The Center for Adult Learning and Training


TC3’s Center for Adult Learning and Training is your on ramp to a college education. Our staff specializes in knowing the unique needs of adult students and helping you navigate your way to a successful college experience.

TC3 Pathways Scholarship: Aid and Support for the Adult Student

The TC3 Pathways Scholarship can essentially fund your education at TC3, with awards available for full- and part-time students at the College. If you’re over 24, you may be eligible.

Earn Your Bachelor Degree on the TC3 Campus

TC3 has unique partnerships with three four-year institutions, making it possible to earn a bachelor’s degree without leaving the TC3 campus.

TC3 and SUNY Delhi have developed programs for students of all ages interested in pursuing a baccalaureate degree in Hospitality Management or Business & Technology Management.

TC3 and Keuka College have partnered to offer a B.S. in Social Work through the TC3 campus.

Also, TC3 graduates can continue on locally with Empire State College (ESC)for a variety of four-year degree programs. Directed at self-motivated, adult learners, ESC provides local mentors to assist students in completing their degree.

TC3 Foundation/Giving

The foundation is a separate, not-for-profit, IRS approved 501(c)(3) organization that provides financially for the educational needs of TC3 that cannot be met through state aid or student tuition. Private support is essential for TC3 to thrive, to remain affordable, and to maintain its history of excellence.
