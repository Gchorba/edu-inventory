Get started today on your future career

Are you ready to find a career that is meaningful and rewarding to you? Are you ready to make a change to a more stable and more gratifying career path? Central Career School can help! We offer career-focused training in high-demand fields like Dental Assisting, Medical Assisting, Medical Billing & Coding and Business & Technology.

Central Career School, dental assistant and medical training school in New Jersey, is invested in your success. It is our goal to give you the education and skills you need to succeed in today's challenging workplace. We offer day, afternoon and evening classes and small class sizes so that you receive personalized attention from our experienced instructors. Central Career School also provides job placement to help graduates get on their way and financial aid for those who qualify.

Central Career School is located in South Plainfield, New Jersey and proudly serves students from Middlesex, Somerset and Union counties in New Jersey . Our healthcare career training programs help to prepare you for a career as a Dental Assistant or a Medical Billing & Office Specialist (also, a Medical Insurance Coder, Medical Biller or Medical Administrative Assistant). Located in the heart of Central NJ, our students come from Edison, Plainfield, Raritan, Woodbridge, New Brunswick, Piscataway, Linden, Elizabeth, Scotch Plains, Perth Amboy, South Amboy, Union and other towns and cities in Middlesex, Somerset and Union counties.
