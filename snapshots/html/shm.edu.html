Dear Student

I would like to introduce our school, which, since 1995, has offered Spanish language and cultural courses to students from all over the world. I have dedicated the majority of my career to teaching Spanish and am very proud to present our school. The main characteristics are: a practical and innovative methodology; highly qualified and experienced staff and teachers; youth and enthusiasm combined with years of experience, as well as ample, modern and comfortable facilities in the heart of Granada city centre. We are a young, dynamic and innovative school! Our aim is to ensure that our students maximise their time with us, meeting their language, cultural and experience expectations in the most enjoyable and beneficial way.

We look forward to welcoming you to our school, in the beautiful city of Granada in Andalusia, Southern Spain.
