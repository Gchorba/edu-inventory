Get the career training you need today

You and your future are the focus at FEI. Our people are committed to bringing out the best in you. We know all you need is an opportunity to prove yourself. However, without the right skills your possibilities are severely limited. If your life is not presently what you want it to be, you must consider education as a solution. You can change your life today!

Enrolling Now

Since 1995, FEI has been committed to preparing its graduates for rewarding careers. We are here for you every step of the way. Our career education is designed to teach you the skills you need fast so you can be successful in today's competitive job market.

We are accepting applications now. At FEI, your success is everything!

Finding Work After Graduation - We Can Help

After you graduate, FEI will assist you in finding work in your field of study. Our Career Services staff will work closely with you in getting you that important first job in your field of study. To us, this is really the beginning of your future - and we can help you get there.

After graduation you need to know what to do.

We do many things while you are in school to get you ready for this all-important moment in your life. Once you are a graduation of FEI will continue to work with you and provide you with assistance in getting your career started. We are confident that you will succeed once you have the skills, know how things work in the real world, and follow the road map you have created with our staff and our planning tools. All you need is an opportunity and your continued dedication to your future success.
