Women's Golf Picked To Compete At NCAA West Super Regional

In just its fifth year of existence as a program, the Sonoma State women's golf team has qualified for the 2012 NCAA Division II Women's Golf West Super Regional Tournament for the fourth straight season. The Seawolves, the No. 7 seed in this year's regional, head to Litchfield Park, Ariz. on May 6-8 to compete in the 54-hole event, hosted by top-seeded Grand Canyon University at The Wigwam.


Special Graduation Celebrations Celebrate SSU's Diversity

Members of the University community and general public are invited to take part in and support graduates by attending any of the four cultural graduation ceremonies held to honor African-American, Asian-Pacific, Rainbow and Native American, Latino or Chicano students. These special ceremonies are held in addition to the regular Commencement event.
