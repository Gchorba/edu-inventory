Military Friendly School

For the second year in a row, Davenport University is a top Military Friendly School. The 2012 Military Friendly Schools list honors top colleges that are doing the most to embrace America's military service members and veterans. To see what Davenport has to offer the military and veteran population, please check out our benefits.

Give to Davenport

Charitable gifts to Davenport University are absolutely essential to allow us to focus on priorities that directly and indirectly affect student’s lives and their experience at Davenport.

Career Services

Davenport University provides life-long career services to current students and alumni. Partnering with industry, we connect recruiting needs with the talent of our students and alumni. Our individualized professional development plans prepare our students for life-long professional success.

New Student Orientation

We have developed an orientation program that is designed with all of our students in mind. We want to make sure that students have the necessary tools and skills to be successful so that they can “get where the world is going” when they graduate.
