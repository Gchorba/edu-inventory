Bobfest and More

Commemorating the last day of classes for the 2012 spring semester, students gathered around Lake Bob for a Wednesday afternoon of zip-lining, boating, sidewalk art and ice cream to celebrate the revival of Lake Bobfest, led by Dr. Steven Hake. Afterwards, the student-run "Harmonicomedy" closed out the evening. View gallery.

Commencement, May 12

The over 55 young people who will walk across the stage on May 12 have brought a depth of leadership to PHC during their stay. The Commencement ceremonies, featuring Virginia’s Attorney General Ken Cuccinelli as the speaker, will be webcast live at 11:00 a.m. ET from the Barbara Hodel Student Life Center. Read more.
