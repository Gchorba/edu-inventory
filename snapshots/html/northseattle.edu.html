We’re Military Friendly!

NSCC was named by G.I. Jobs magazine as a Military Friendly School for 2012! Our policies, recruitment and student services are in the top 15% of all colleges and universities nationwide.

Pursue a Career in the Sciences

Get the tools and support you need to earn an Associate of Science degree and prepare to transfer and train for a successful career in fields like biology, chemistry, computer science, engineering, math, physics and nanotechnology.

COMPASS Prep for College Success

The COMPASS test is used to evaluate your skills and place you into appropriate courses. It’s the first important test of your college career. Don’t take it cold - review and prepare at beforeyoutest.org
