Welcome to the Suffolk County Bar Association

The Suffolk County Bar Association is one of the largest voluntary bar associations in the State. We are a private organization representing lawyers of Suffolk County.

For over a century, our organization and the legal profession it represents has assissted the community through charitable services, pro bono representation, scholarship programs and legal education.

Our organization recognizes its mission:
"The purposes and objects for which the Association is established shall be cultivating the science of jurisprudence, promoting reforms in the law, facilitating the administration of justice, elevating the standard of integrity, honor and courtesy in the legal profession and cherishing the spirit of brotherhood among the members thereof." [Circa 1937]
