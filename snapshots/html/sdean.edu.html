“My personal mission is to touch the hearts as well as the minds of those with whom I come into contact.”

 -          Sandra Dean

 

Sandra Dean is an internationally recognized speaker, educator, and author on the topics of:

v     Leadership and Teams

v     School and Community Partnerships

v     Raising Resilient Children

 

Sandra’s guiding principles are:

v     Every human being is important and has value and worth.

v     Every human being has the right to be respected.

v     Every human being has strengths as well as areas for growth and development.

v     Children are capable of wonderful things.  They are precious.  We must nurture them, believe in them, and above all, love them.  They are worth it!

v     Service to fellow human beings brings greatest happiness and joy.
