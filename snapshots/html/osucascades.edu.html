Central Oregon's University

OSU-Cascades is a unique resource for Central Oregon, offering accredited four-year and master’s degrees while making a positive impact in our community, economy and environment.

We offer students small classes and multiple opportunities for hands-on learning. Our faculty and students take advantage of a vast natural laboratory to better understand and protect our environment. We’ve developed unique degree programs to address the region’s economic development needs. Locally focused projects and internships take our students out into the community. And our graduates are making lasting contributions to Central Oregon’s economy and quality of life.

We are OSU-Cascades. Central Oregon’s university.
