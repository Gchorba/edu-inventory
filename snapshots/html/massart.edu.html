Events

  • May 7–12 Spring Sale

    Shop unique gifts – from hand-blown glass, ceramics, oil paintings to jewelry – at this annual sale. Proceeds benefit financial assistance for students. Open 10 a.m.–7 p.m.

  • May 7–June 29 About and Engaged

    This exhibition features work by Genara Banzon, Kathleen Bititti, Gail A. Burton, Michael Dowling, Rinat Harel, and Mario Quiroz. The show highlights socially engaged art practices and is sponsored by CACP.

  • May 16 Industrial Design Spring Show

    This gallery style exhibition of student work will showcase creative problem solving around technology, sustainability, products, and systems. In the Pozen Center from 6-10 p.m. 

  • May 24–25 C3 2012 Creative Careers Conference

    C3 offers useful and practical career information in two days of workshops, panel discussions, and lectures featuring a great line-up of presenters. Open to MassArt students and alumni with registration.
