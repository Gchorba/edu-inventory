Today’s job market requires highly trained, computer-oriented professionals, essential to the nation’s judicial processes. A trained reporter using the latest realtime computer-aided transcription processes remains the fastest, most accurate way to turn spoken information into readable, searchable, permanent text.  These processes are called computer-aided transcription, or CAT, and rapid text entry.

We would like to thank you for visiting our Website.  College of Court Reporting's online program was the first college or school to become certified by the National Court Reporters Association.  Attending college gives many students a newfound sense of freedom that carries equally important responsibilities.  In striving to provide academic excellence, CCR seeks a mature and competent staff, qualified through both education and experience.
