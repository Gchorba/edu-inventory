Welcome to the TUI , the University of Hospitality & Entertainment.

Providing academic and non academic support, TUI is dedicated to preparing you to get the right training, tools and contacts so that you can advance your career in any number of industries.

Relationships have been established with industry partners to develop internships and career placements. Our academic partnerships with other universities add great value to the entire educational experience. New challenges continue to present themselves to those who aspire to be part of the global economy, which offers many opportunities to those who are qualified.

Industries in which our graduates have found rewarding careers include: hotel management, travel, meeting/convention planning, logistics, import/export, and customs brokerage.
