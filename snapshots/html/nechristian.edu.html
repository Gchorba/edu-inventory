  • We Are NCC

    NCC is an accredited Bible college committed to teaching that the way, the truth, and the life is found through Jesus Christ. We are an institution that values thinking biblically, communicating clearly, loving compassionately, and leading courageously in an environment that is spiritually authentic, and ministry focused.


    We are non-denominational, associated with the Stone-Campbell movement...but what we value above all else are hearts that love God, minds that are committed to truth, and hands that are willing to serve. If it is your heart's desire To Know Christ and Make Him Known...join us. Join us as a student, as a partner, or as a prayerful brother or sister in Christ.
