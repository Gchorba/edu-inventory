  • +HEALTH CARE programs

    At a time when a significant percentage of the American population is aging and the health care industry needs professionals with technology, business and leadership skills, American Sentinel offers several online health care degrees designed to meet the evolving demands of the health care sector. Nearly one-half of American Sentinel students are pursuing one of our health care programs. We design our curriculums to be relevant and adaptable based on industry needs.

    more info

  • +BUSINESS PROGRAMS

    The business world is continually becoming more global, more diverse and more interdisciplinary in nature. American Sentinel's online business programs are designed to develop professionals capable of managing and leading in today's contemporary global business environment. We offer several bachelor's and master's degree programs tailored to meet the demands of today's organizations.

    more info

  • +INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PROGRAMS

    Technology has changed the world and is critical for nearly every industry. American Sentinel's online technology degree programs equip students for careers in a number of high-demand fields, including geographic information systems, information security, computer science, software development, information technology, networking and management information systems. Our programs are carefully designed to give students the tools, technologies and skills to better their organizations through technology.

    more info

  • +INFO FOR MILITARY PERSONNEL

    American Sentinel is a military-friendly university, offering military education benefits to active-duty and non-active-duty service members, veterans, reservists, auxiliary and their spouses. We offer many benefits through our military scholarship. We also accept tuition assistance and VA benefits, including the Montgomery G.I. bill, the Post-9/11 G.I. Bill and the Military Spouse Career Advancement Accounts (My CAA). Our flexible class format and frequent enrollment periods are ideal for military members with demanding jobs and frequent relocations.

    more info
