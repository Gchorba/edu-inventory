Welcome to NTPS

The National Test Pilot School (NTPS) is a not-for-profit educational institute incorporated in the State of California . It was established in 1981 to meet the flight test training needs of the US domestic and foreign aerospace industries and governments, both military and civil certification agencies.  NTPS is the only civilian school of the seven largest test pilot schools in the world.  

The primary objective of the NTPS is to educate and train test pilots and flight test engineers to be immediately capable upon graduation of participating in a military acceptance program, an aircraft development program within an aerospace company, or to complete a civil certification of a new or modified aircraft, either fixed-wing or rotary-wing.  NTPS has, since its inception, striven to address a broad constituency. As such we offer 32 unique courses, ranging from one-week, academic-only courses, to the 50 week Professional Course. Beyond that, unique customer requirements may be met by specialized courses tailored to the need if none of the standard courses suffice.

 

Educational Objectives

Educational objectives are defined to be consistent with institutional mission and to meet the needs of the customer. Program outcomes are defined and curricula developed which lead to achievement of these objectives. Outcomes are continuously assessed and objectives are evaluated to assure continuous improvement.
