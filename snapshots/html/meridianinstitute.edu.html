We Can Help

Whether it's answering your questions or assisting with your child care or arranging for your job interview, our friendly, experienced, customer oriented staff will help you reach you goal of a new career.

Meridian Institute will be with you at each step, from your enrollment, through your training and ultimately, being hired at your new job.

The staff and faculty of Meridian Institute are totally dedicated to your success. We have been training people, like yourself, for new careers since 1995.

Once you have decided on a particular program, our admissions representatives will guide you through the enrollment process. All your books and supplies needed for the training will be provided to you so there is no running around to make sure you have everything for class.

Our instructors have both the academic background as well as the work experience in the particular training field. Each training program includes lecture as well as hands-on practice in our labs. Depending on the program selected, externships are also included to provide the student with real life experience.

Our Career Services staff will help develop your resume, practice job interviewing skills with you and provide job leads and arrange job interviews for you. Although we cannot guarantee you a job, we will assist you every step of the way. In the end, it is you who needs to "sell yourself" to the employer.

Call now at 1-800-555-0200 to begin your road to a successful future.
