GET SERIOUS

What Makes Us The Serious U?

Here at AIU, we proudly offer none of the superfluous craziness of traditional campus life. No all-night keggers. No loveable mascot. Not even a football team.

Instead, we offer career-focused degrees, and an award-winning* virtual campus for busy professionals.

And for the truly motivated, we even offer accelerated programs that can be completed in as little as 15 months.**

A Degree That Gets Things Done

Our degree programs are designed to help you stand out in the industry. Whether you want help building skills that you could apply in your current job or pursue a brand new profession, we think you’ll find AIU is as focused and results oriented as you are.

Here's The Reality.

At AIU, there are no inflexible schedules, no wondering where your tuition goes, and no missed opportunities. No filler. Just the real-world curriculum you need to earn a degree and reach your potential in the marketplace. We’re AIU, the Serious U, and if your goals are more about your profession than a frat house, we could be just the place for you.

To learn more about AIU, our degree programs or how to apply for admission, please contact us for more information.

** Graduation date depends upon your chosen program, and the number of transfer-in credits you have. Not all credits are eligible to transfer. See the university catalog regarding AIU Online’s transfer credit policies.

This School Serves You

We understand that a military lifestyle can be unpredictable, and that those in the military have even less time to waste than the average CEO. So we’ve eliminated the fluff and hassle of the traditional college experience.

Earn a degree that could help you in military life or the civilian job market, on a timeline that fits your busy schedule. AIU offers quality, and adaptability that are suited to the realities of today’s military professional.
