Student Stories

As a member of the HPU chapel band, Jose Lopez has been able to "learn how to be a leader and to lead others in worship."
Being involved in the Baptist Student Ministry at HPU was a great fit for theater major Darren McElroy. "It allowed me to reach out to the community and reach out towards others for Christ," he said.
A business major and captain of the football team, Cody Hambrick has found time to be involved in a number of organizations on campus. He says, "One thing you have to do when you are here at Howard Payne is you have to get involved."
Earning a triple major in youth ministry, political science and the Academy of Freedom, Brittany Lunsford says, "Howard Payne has definitely helped me figure out what I want to do in life and where I want to go."
Chase Woodhouse, a Christian education major, has found that Howard Payne has really prepared him for life outside of school.
Art education major Amanda Hendrix has found that HPU challenges her both academically and spiritually.
Political science major Chandler Raine has found that the Academy of Freedom is helping him hone his skills and prepare for graduate school.
Courtney Wilmoth is an English major studying in London for the spring 2012 semester. She works for HPU’s Office of University Marketing and Communications and will blog about her experiences. Click the link for Courtney's blog!

Howard Payne is a university for those who want a hands-on, practical learning experience. At HPU we invest in one another’s success and growth through teaching, learning and service. When you leave, you’ll continue your journey with a sense of purpose and a home away from home that will remain part of you forever. Apply now and begin your journey today.
Howard Payne University is committed to keeping costs affordable. Click here for a Net Price Caculator. More than 90 percent of our students receive financial assistance. HPU offers many opportunities to help finance your education and our knowledgeable staff is ready to discuss your options such as grants, loans and numerous scholarships. Click here to learn more. 
The best way to experience campus life at HPU is through an organized group event like Yellow Jacket Monday held twice each fall and spring semester, Stinger Daze held each spring or you may schedule an individual campus tour.
