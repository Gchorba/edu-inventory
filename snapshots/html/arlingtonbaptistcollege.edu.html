God Can!

“The things which are impossible with men are possible with God.” (Luke 18:27)

I do not think we could have words of greater encouragement than these as we face the complexities of “doing life.”  When we have God as our refuge, the things that we consider to be “impossible” to do or handle, change into “possibilities” by the grace and power of Him.

As you read through the following pages, you will become more familiar with life on campus of Arlington Baptist College.  God may very well use this website to begin stirring your soul with a desire to become a part of what He is doing at ABC…God Can!  If He does, then there will be no acceptable excuse or obstacle too big to keep you from being able to accomplish His will for you…God Can!

I would ask you to open your eyes, ears, and heart to the will of God as you read through this information.  Those things that may seem exciting, but yet unreachable are not out of the realm of possibility for you…God Can!

I hope to hear from you soon.  Straight Ahead!
Dr. D.L. Moody
President
