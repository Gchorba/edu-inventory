          _Northwest Academy - Charlevoix, Michigan

          A Tuition-Free Public School Academy in Northern Michigan

          Northwest Academy's dedicated staff have been educating children in northern Michigan since 1996.  The school offers a challenging curriculum based on Michigan standards, Spanish program, and an award-winning band program for grades5-12.
          Parents are encouraged to join our parent organization, Friends of Northwest Academy to become actively involved in their children's education.
          Please feel free to contact us at (231) 547-9000 or via email at info@nwa.edu to discuss any questions.
