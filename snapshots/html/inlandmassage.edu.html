Inland Massage Institute is the oldest and most respected massage therapy school in the Inland Northwest. Many consider Inland Massage Institute the best massage therapy school because of the excellent massage therapist education and training, the real-world experience provided by the massage school, the dedicated staff, the exclusive massage therapy focus and the lifetime massage career services. Whether your dream is to work on a cruise ship, at a resort spa, or a day spa or salon close to home, the education you receive at the Inland Massage can help get you there. You can even start your own private practice! You will find the information you need in the School section of our site.

Inland Massage Institute also has a full time clinic with licensed massage therapists. All of our therapists on staff have years of experience working with most major insurance companies to help our clients recover from injuries and manage their pain. We also offer a variety of other services such as relaxation treatments, sports massage, and prenatal massage. You will find all the information you need in the Clinic section of our site.
