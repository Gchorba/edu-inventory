Welcome to Oceanside College of Beauty!

Oceanside College of Beauty has been providing the next generation of beauty professionals with high-quality, hands-on training for over 40 years. Our cosmetology training provides students with the training they need to become licensed cosmetologists, with a focus on job placement and career success. At Oceanside College of Beauty, we offer the following programs:

Cosmetology Training

If you’re ready to express your creativity every day, help people look and feel their best, and get the training you need to begin a new career in the exciting beauty industry, click here to learn more about the cosmetology training programs available at Oceanside College of Beauty!

Cosmetology Teacher Training

We also offer a teacher training program for licensed cosmetologists who are interested in sharing their knowledge with future beauty professionals. Click here to learn more about our special teacher training program.

 
Oceanside College of Beauty can help you begin training for a new career in the beauty industry today!
