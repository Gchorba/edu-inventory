Are you considering a career in health care?

Acupuncture College & Massage School
We are one of the oldest colleges of Oriental Medicine and Massage Therapy in the State of Florida. Accredited as a degree granting institution by the Accreditation Commision for Acupuncture & Oriental Medicine (ACAOM).

AMC offers:

Oriental Medicine
36-month Master of Oriental Medicine Degree program.

Oriental Bodywork
8-1/2 month Massage Therapy program with a Shiatsu specialization.

Miami School
Our campus is based in Miami, conveniently located and accessible from all area of Greater Miami and the Beaches. You can contact us to learn more Oriental therapeutic modalities, or to request more information.
