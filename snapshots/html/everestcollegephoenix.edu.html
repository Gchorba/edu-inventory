Welcome to Everest College Phoenix!

Here you can acquire the academic foundation and real-world skills you need to succeed in today's professional work environment.

Our Mission
Everest College Phoenix provides students the opportunity to seek career-oriented learning with a general education background and to develop intellectually and socially in their careers and as citizens in a world of increasing technological and cultural diversity.

Here are just a few reasons why you should attend Everest College Phoenix:

Distinguished Faculty
Everest College Phoenix professors are skilled educators with strong academic backgrounds, and most also have years of professional work experience in the fields they teach.  This unique combination of perspectives provides you with a rich learning experience.

Career-Focused Education
Our programs are designed to provide you with the knowledge, skills and experience you need to compete successfully for positions in the career field of your choice.  We take a practical approach to education, striving to prepare you for the professional and personal demands of the working world.

Career Services
Our career services representatives can help you prepare a winning resume, rehearse effective interviewing skills, select appropriate business attire and contact employers in your area.  Our reputation for educating high quality employees in the fields of health care, business, criminal justice and paralegal could be the edge you need to start your career.

Online Education
With the convenience and flexibility of online learning, you can get your degree from Everest College Phoenix anytime, from anywhere, on your own schedule! It's learning that fits your life. If you’re ready to learn more about how our online degree programs can benefit you, simply complete our easy online form.  It just takes a moment, and we’ll provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision.
