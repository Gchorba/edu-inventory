Degree Programs – Real World Education

Stamford offers career-based areas of studies to develop students for exciting careers. Our international degree programs are designed to ensure ensure you learn and experience what it takes to be successful in the real world.

CampusLife @Stamford – Real Fun

Stamford offers many extra-curricular and social activities to enhance our students’ learning experience. From academic, sports to cultural activities, our students enjoy and excel in their student life.

Multi-cultural community

Stamford students and faculty came from more than 40 countries. We welcome students from around the world to be part of the Stamford’s multi-cultural community.

Laureate Hospitality Program

A dual award program with Blue Mountains International Hotel Management School – the no. 1 international hotel management school in Australasia

Stamford Academics

Stamford has achieved a score of “Excellent” in internal quality assessment by the Commission on Higher Education for two consecutive years. This has placed us one of the top Thai international universities.

What’s the difference?

At Stamford, the difference is a global network of students, faculty and thought leaders working together to prepare you for real-world success.

Our Campuses

Located in Bangkok, Thailand’s capital city, as well as in Hua-Hin, a popular resort destination with an international population. Stamford campuses offer conducive learning environment.
