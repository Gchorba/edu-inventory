Austin Graduate School of Theology is a seminary associated with Churches of Christ and in conversation with all who confess Jesus as Lord. Our mission is to promote knowledge, understanding, and practice of the Christian faith by equipping Christians and churches for service in the Kingdom of God. 

Donations are the lifeblood of our ministry. Click the "Donate Online" icon above to contribute through secure online credit card processing. We welcome checks, money orders or credit card numbers mailed to our physical address, and accept credit cards over the phone at 1-866-AUS-GRAD (287-4723), or 512-476-2772.

NOTE: Many corporations match charitable giving. Be sure to specify if your gift is eligible for corporate matching!

Is Ministry Calling? Austin Graduate School of Theology is a great place to start!  Whether you earn a B.A. in Christian Studies, an M.A. in Theological Studies or simply audit courses, Austin Grad prepares you for a fruitful life of service in His Kingdom. Our alumni hold ministry and church leadership positions across the country and around the world.  Contact us today to learn how easy it is to get started! Email: Celeste Scarborough

Attention Undergraduates!  Austin Grad can accept up to 69 hours of general education coursework from an accredited institution toward a Bachelor of Arts in Christian Studies.  Associate and undergraduate degree holders can complete the plan in as little as 18 months!  For admissions information, contact Celeste Scarborough.


ACCREDITATION

Austin Graduate School of Theology is accredited by the Commission on Colleges of the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools to award bachelor and master degrees. Contact the Commission on Colleges at 1866 Southern Lane, Decatur, Georgia 30033-4097 or call 404-679-4500 for questions regarding the school's accreditation status, to file a third-party comment at the time of its decennial review, or to file a complaint for alleged non-compliance with accreditation standards or requirements. Please direct all other inquiries to Austin Graduate School of Theology.
