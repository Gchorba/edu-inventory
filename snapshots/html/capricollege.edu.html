Capri College could prepare you for a better future! We've created a unique educational experience and every day we strive to stay current by frequently educating our instructors, bringing in guest artists, and teaching you new trends in hair, skin care, and massage. We are proud to be a Sassoon Connection Member School (Davenport Campus) and we teach our students in depth business training with the Nuts and Bolts Business Training Courses.  We are not a chain or franchise and we are not limited to one product line. We supply top of the line products like Redken, Purology, Chi, Big Sexy, Biotone and Dermalogica.  We strive for educational excellence and care about your future. Because we know our reputation depends on your success!

 

Capri College is a school held to precise standards such as honest recruitment practices, minimum three years instructor experience, and student satisfaction by ACCSC accreditation. Our skilled, passionate, caring educators will teach you the skills and knowledge you need to get your license. Our goal is to provide the most comprehensive, hands-on education possible and to offer the knowledge and technical skills you need to be confident and hopefully successful!

 

Nothing compares to the hands-on experience with real clients we provide. Improved technical skills, people skills, and confidence are the result. One visit to our salon-like practical training clinic and you will see the difference real experience makes!

 

Capri College has long-standing relationships with salon and spa employers. We listen to them for ways we can improve, to keep our education current. Our reputation, community involvement, and long-standing relationships allow us to assist you with as many employment options as possible.
