BioTechnology Technician

Students prepare to work in the growing bioscience industry in the areas of research and development, quality systems, production, clinical testing and diagnostic work.

Pharmacy Technician

The Pharmacy Technician program prepares students for positions assisting the pharmacist in his or her practice. Curriculum includes theory, laboratory instruction and clinical experience in area pharmacies.

Medical Assistant

The Medical Assistant program prepares students to perform essential functions, to maintain records, Insurance laws and Transcription.

Business Administration

Business Administration training encompasses a thorough understanding of the duties, responsibilities, skills, and knowledge required of an administrative assistant.

BioHealth College - Tomorrow's Medical Technology Today

BioHealth College is Nationally recognized by the US Department of Education and Accredited by the ACCET (The Accrediting Council for Continuing Education and Training).

Biohealth College offers focused career oriented training in BioTechnology, Pharmacy Tech, Medical Assisting, and Business Administration. Financial Aid is available for qualified candidates.

We can help you launch your new career in 7 months in the fields of BioTechnology, Pharmacy, Medical Assisting, and Business Administration. BioHealth College's experienced faculty believe in the 'hands-on' approach. All our courses are 100% instructor-led and class sizes are small to ensure individual attention.
