    Earn A Diploma or Degree

    In today's world, you can only succeed by equipping yourself with an education. To become gainfully employed, credentials are not only preferred, but required. No matter what your interest, GTCC has a program to offer you!

    • Business

      Business

      Is the corporate world where you would like to be? Does either business administration, human resources management, computer information technology excite you?

    • College Transfer

      College Transfer

      Did you know that your GTCC course credits will seamlessly transfer to any of the 16 UNC system universities? Perhaps you would like to go to one of the outstanding private institutions in the area? Your classes will transfer there as well.

    • Health Careers

      Health Careers

      Showcasing a variety of programs designed to prepare healthcare professionals, many of these programs are limited enrollment, stress high expectations and are student learning and success based.

    • Public Safety

      Public Safety

      The Division of Public Safety curriculum is designed to provide individuals with the technical and professional knowledge necessary to become employed in the public safety arena as a paramedic, entry-level law enforcement officer with state, county, or municipal governments, or to provide individuals with the knowledge necessary to make decisions regarding fire protection.

    • Service Careers

      Service Careers

      Serving others can be a very satisfying feeling. GTCC offers many different service career programs ranging from cosmetology to culinary to basic law enforcement.

    • Trade Careers

      Trade Careers

      Employees with technical skills are in demand. Whether you wish to be a carpenter, electrician, HVAC, welding or another technical trade, GTCC has the training for you!

    • Transportation

      Transportation

      Learn to work on airplane engines, get flight training to become a pilot, or learn the business side of things through aviation management. You can also jumpstart your career in Automotive, Collision Repair, or Heavy Equipment Transport Technologies.

    • Search Programs

      Search Programs

      Are you interested in finding out about different types of academic programs? You may be considering several different fields of study. Learn more about all of the curriculum (credit) programs that GTCC has to offer here!

    • APPLY NOW

      APPLY NOW

      Education opens doors and can take you to the next step in life. No matter if you are working to finish your GED, high school diploma or associate degree, you are moving in the right direction. What are you waiting for? Go to cfnc.org and apply to GTCC today!

  • Earn My GED, Adult HS Diploma or Basic Skills

    Earn My GED, Adult HS Diploma or Basic Skills

    Sometimes life sidetracks us with commitments and we don't finish what we started. In today's competitive world, an education is a must and it is never too late to finish what you started. No matter where you are in life, GTCC can take you further!

    • Adult High School

      Adult High School

      Designed with the needs of working adults in mind, our adult high school completion programs are intended for adult learners who do not have a high school diploma from the U.S. or another country.

    • Basic Skills

      Basic Skills

      Guilford Tech can help you learn to read, improve math skills, finish a high school diploma, earn a GED diploma, improve English skills, develop computer skills and prepare for college.

    • Compensatory Education

      Compensatory Education

      CED is a community college program designed specifically for adults with mental retardation. CED programs focus on helping the individual become as independent and self-directed as possible through acquiring basic and life skills needed to function independently.

    • English as a Second Language

      English as a Second Language

      English as a Second Language (ES0L) is a program of instruction designed to help adults, who are limited English proficient, to achieve competence in the English language.

    • General Education Development

      General Education Development

      This flexible, self-paced program prepares adults 18 years and older who want to earn a high school equivalency diploma. Students must pass five separate tests: Reading, Writing, Social Studies and Mathematics.
