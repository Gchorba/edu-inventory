                      Summer 2012 Payment Deadline

                      Payment for Summer 2012 tuition must be received in full in the Cashier's Office no later than Friday, May 4, 2012 for registered students. Your courses are subject to cancellation if tuition is not paid in full. You can view your balance by logging into your myMCTC Self-Service. Failure to receive a billing statement will not be accepted as a reason for missing the payment deadline.

                      Tuition can be paid by cash, check, or money order at the windows of the Cashier's Office; credit card payments can be made by logging onto your myMCTC Self-Service; check payments can also be mailed to: MCTC Business Services, Attn: Cashier's Office, P.O. Box 1539, Huntington, WV 25716-1539.

                      If your tuition should be covered by financial aid or a third party, please ensure with the Cashier's Office that your tuition has been covered to avoid cancellation of your courses. If you no longer wish to attend Mountwest Community & Technical College for the Summer semester, it is your responsibility to meet with an Academic Advisor to drop your courses in order to avoid being charged for those courses. If you have any questions regarding your account, please contact the Cashier's Office at 304-696-3868 or by emailing cashier@mctc.edu.

                      May 2012 Graduation

                      The Mountwest Community and Technical College commencement ceremony will be held on Friday, May 11th, 2012, at 7 p.m., at the Keith Albee Performing Arts Center in downtown, Huntington, West Virginia. Graduates should be present by 6:15 p.m. and must be attired in academic caps and robes. These may be purchased at Stadium Bookstore located at 1949 Fifth Avenue, Huntington, WV 304-529-2665. Stadium will also have formal graduation invitations you can order.

                      At the conclusion of the ceremony, refreshments will be served in the lobby of The Fredrick, across the street from the Keith Albee. The faculty and staff are looking forward to helping you celebrate your academic achievement.

                      Attention Mountwest Students

                      Beginning in January 2012, Mountwest Community & Technical College will be partnering with Higher One, a financial services company focused solely on higher education, to bring you a new option for receiving your refunds: the EasyRefund card. Please do not throw away this important card since it is your ticket to a faster refund. Simply put, your new EasyRefund card is designed to provide you with increased choices for your Financial Aid or school refunds.

                      These options include:

                      1. A same day deposit to the OneAccount, an FDIC Insured, no monthly fee, no minimum balance checking account. With the OneAccount, you receive your funds the same day they are released by Higher One. The easiest and fastest choice available.

                      2. Transfer to another account.

                      Please activate your card as soon as you receive it.

                      Remember, even if you are not currently expecting a refund from Mountwest CTC, we may have a refund for you in the future.

                      In order to ensure that Mountwest CTC has your current mailing address, please take a moment to visit myMCTC Self-Service to verify and update, if necessary, your address information today. You can also choose to update your address by going to the Registrar's Office in Room 110 of the MCTC building.

                      Your new EasyRefund card will be mailed to the address you currently have on file with Mountwest CTC. Therefore, it is critical that you verify your mailing address as soon as possible. Please note, it is extremely important that you verify your address is entered correctly as any errors could delay you receiving any future financial aid refunds.

                      You can visit https://mountwest.higheroneaccount.com to learn more about all the great benefits that accompany your all-new EasyRefund card! Please feel free to contact the Cashier's Office at cashier@mctc.edu with any other questions you may have regarding this.
