SPECIALIZED PROGRAMS GET YOU INTO A LIFETIME CAREER - FASTER!
Lawyer's Assistant School provides superior education to students who have chosen a career as a legal secretary, paralegal, or business assistant. LASD provides on-point skills training by career legal secretaries, Certified Paralegals, Licensed Attorneys and Applications Trainers. The academic and technical courses are consistent with current standards of a law firm, legal department, government offices and businesses. Graduates possess the skills and knowledge necessary to attain entry-level employment in their field of study.

Job Placement Assistance is available to all Qualified Graduates
