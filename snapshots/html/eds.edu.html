Welcome

Episcopal Divinity School (EDS) is a respected and progressive center for study and spiritual formation for lay and ordained leaders and welcomes students from many denominations. Committed to a mission of social justice and inclusive education and grounded in the Anglican tradition, EDS balances a respect for the past with an orientation of innovation.
 
Located on a beautiful campus in Cambridge, Massachusetts, EDS provides easy access to some of the best educational resources in the world.
