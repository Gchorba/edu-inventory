President's Message

Dear Student,

You enter school with your talents and career goals. At The Art Institute of Dallas, we see our role as providing those education services that will help you to develop your potential.

At The Art Institute of Dallas, education is not a process where we hand you skills and knowledge. It is an interactive process where you take responsibility and apply your knowledge.

In addition to developing the right skills for the future, college is also about having the “college experience.” It’s about meeting new people (friends, potential employers, job leads), joining clubs, and volunteering for special projects. It’s about having fun and enjoying life as you develop into a professional.
