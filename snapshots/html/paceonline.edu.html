Athletics

Pacebook

Pace students John Robb and Peter Franceschini won first place at the Eighth Annual Pace Pitch Contest for their mobile app, Candoo, developed to help the elderly use Android tablets with ease. They were also named finalists in the GSMA Mobile Health University Challenge and will compete in South Africa later this month.

Pace student Dominique Ulino '12 was awarded the prestigious Melissa Riggio Award for Community Service by AHRC New York City, a nonprofit that helps individuals with developmental and intellectual disabilities, for her work mentoring students in Professor Jim Lawler's Catching a Dream Program.
