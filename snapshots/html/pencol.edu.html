Presidential Search
The Peninsula College Board of Trustees has named Dr. Luke Robins as the sixth president of Peninsula College.

Dr. Robin’s appointment is the result of a national search conducted by the Peninsula College Board of Trustees.

“We are very pleased to have found a candidate with the wide range of demonstrated and effective leadership skills that Dr. Robins has,” says Julie McCulloch, Board Chair.

Peninsula College expects to officially welcome Dr. Robins and his family at a special community reception when school commences in the fall.
