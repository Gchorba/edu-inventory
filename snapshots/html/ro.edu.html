Sunday, 06th May 2012.

Welcome to Romania.org!

Romania.org is proud to be the #1 web spot about Romania since almost 15 years. We are the first portal site ever set up on the global Internet, focussing exclusively on Romania- a land flowing with culture, fascinating history and wonderful landscapes. Since our start in 1996 we delivered billions of pages and currently serve with our group of sites more than 1.5 Million unique visitors per month.

Romania is proud to be a full Member of the European Union since 2007. Take a virtual visit to Romania and you'll be left amazed. So why not treat yourself to a real holiday in Romania?! Ruggedly beautiful Romania offers the interested traveler a romantic glimpse of both, traditional and modern ways of life. Heading north from Bucharest to the villages of Brasov area you will find friendly people, proud of their customs and folklore with a timeless rural way of life. Then cruise through the myriad channels of the Danube Delta, one of the world's greatest wildlife sanctuaries. For Low cost European holiday insurance use InsureandGo.
