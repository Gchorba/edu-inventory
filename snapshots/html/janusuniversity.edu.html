General Education


General Education

General Education courses are required courses that students take on their way to earning a Bachelor's degree. General Education courses are a set of core courses where students study: English, Humanities, Sciences, Mathematics, Arts and Social Sciences. General Education courses expose students to a variety of topics and create a backdrop to elaborate on other academic topics.

Additional Admission Requirements for International Applicants

WES (World Education Services)

A third party course-by-course transcript evaluation of all foreign transcripts is required for admittance into the University. WES reviews your transcripts and prepares an evaluation report to send to the institutions you are applying to. WES evaluation reports compare your education from any country in the world to the U.S. system and are widely recognized by U.S. institutions.
