Quality English Language Schools
in San Diego and San Francisco

Converse International School of Languages (CISL) is an excellent choice to learn English and foreign languages in San Diego and San Francisco, California. Our language schools offer programs and courses designed to provide you with the best options to study English (ESL), as well as TOEFL and Cambridge exam preparation. Maintaining a balanced mix of nationalities is a top priority at CISL. Click here to view our 2011 nationality mix.

With almost 40 years of experience, CISL's English language schools in San Diego and San Francisco are internationally recognized as leading centers for effective language training.

Whether your interest is to study English for personal enrichment, professional, or academic purposes, our language schools provide challenging and intensive English courses and classes designed to help you achieve maximum results quickly so you can begin communicating effectively and with confidence!
