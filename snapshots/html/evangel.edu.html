Preparing You to Change the World

Evangel is an accredited university with more than 100 academic programs. Our faculty is made up of Christian professionals who are distinguished in their fields. Students get plenty of personal attention by professors dedicated to the development of tomorrow’s Christian leaders.

Find a Program

So Much More Than Academics

College is meant to be an experience. One that will change your life. It happens in the residence halls, at athletic events, in the classrooms, on the Quad … everywhere you go at Evangel. Through the perfect balance of big-school amenities and small-scale attention, Evangel offers an experience you won’t find anywhere else.

Faith, Learning and Life

Faith isn't just about Sunday School; it’s about life. Everything from career to relationships. At Evangel we are committed to teaching you a whole lot more than equations and principles. We’re dedicated to the integration of faith, learning and life — helping you grow into the complete person God created you to be.

About Evangel

  • Evangel is an accredited, liberal arts university with more than 100 academic programs on the cutting edge of today's professional fields. Our commitment to the integration of faith, learning and life attracts students from a wide variety of Christian denominational backgrounds who have a strong commitment to academics with a desire to combine their Christian faith with every aspect of their lives.

Mission and Values

  • Evangel's theme, "Christ is Lord," touches every aspect of campus life — spiritual, intellectual, social and physical. Our staff, curriculum and student life are centered on helping students integrate their Christian faith into their chosen career fields.
