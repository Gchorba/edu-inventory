    Teaching Sites

    Pursuing a theological education has never been easier! The King's offers courses online, which can be accessed anywhere in the world with an Internet connection. Additionally, we are excited to offer courses at the following locations:

    • Van Nuys, CA - Housed on the campus of The Church on The Way in Van Nuys, CA, The King's University campus provides educational opportunities all year round.
    • Colorado Springs, CO - The King'sUniversity is pleased to provide courses at it's teaching site in Colorado Springs, hosted on the grounds of New Life Church, a large, non-denominational church led by Pastor Brady Boyd.
    • Modesto, CA - The King's University, Modesto teaching site, is located on the 26 acre campus of Calvary Temple Worship Center, a dynamic growing church led by Senior Pastor Glen Berteau.
    • Fontana, CA - Our Fontana teaching site is hosted by Pastor Danny Carroll and the Water of Life Community Church. We are excited to offer courses at this young, non-denominational evangelical charismatic mega-church.

    If you would like more information about taking courses from The King's University at any of these locations or through our Online Education system, please contact the Admissions department today. Please find contact information for the Admissions department and other departments at The King's at the bottom of this web page.
