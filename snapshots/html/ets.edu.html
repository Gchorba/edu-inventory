Evangel Theological Seminary has a long history of providing a Biblically based, theologically sound curriculum offering a quality education to a wide spectrum of students. To that end, we offer both degree and non-degree programs. Our goal is to provide an affordable theological education that is easily accessible and flexible for busy ministers and lay people.

With our 30th academic year underway, we renew our commitment to the mission God has entrusted to us. By God's grace we will continue to effectively:

Teach
Biblically and Practically
Minister
Spirit Empowered Refreshment
Serve
the Church Globally.

If you are looking for enriching theological programs that are unique in their approach to minister to you personally, expand your horizons and revitalize the Church then we invite you to contact Evangel to review your options for a deeper and fuller learning experience.
