 Welcome to the Fort Myers Institute of Technology (FMIT)! We are glad you have found us. (On February 21st, the School Board approved the school's name change. Over the next few months we will be transitioning to the new name.) 

Fort Myers Tech is a public postsecondary career and technical education center serving the students of Lee County, as well as the surrounding counties of Hendry, Glades, Collier, and Charlotte.

Currently, Fort Myers Tech offers over 30 career training programs in a wide variety of occupational clusters including Construction Trades, Industrial Technology, Health Sciences, Family & Consumer Science, Business Technology, Information Technology, and Public Service.

Our tuition rates are extremely affordable, classes are small, and most programs can be completed in a year or less. Some of the hallmarks of our offerings include:

►Hands-on Training ►Bright Futures Scholarships Accepted
►Small Class Size ►Job Placement Assistance
►Convenient Schedules ►Financial Aid is Available
►Affordable Tuition for Adults  


Fort Myers Tech operates under the governance of the State of Florida Department of Education (DOE), Division of Workforce Development, and The School District of Lee County. Fort Myers Tech is accredited by the Council on Occupational Education, and has maintained that accreditation for over 35 years.

We hope you take the time to explore our school through this Web site, and come visit our campus any day between 8 a.m. and 3 p.m. to speak to an admissions counselor.
