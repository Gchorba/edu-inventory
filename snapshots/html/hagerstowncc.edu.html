Veterans' Information Session held May 4

HCC will host an information session for active duty military, veterans, and their families on Fri., May 4, at 10 a.m. in the Elliott Center. This event is free and open to the public.

Health Care Career Expo held May 14

The Health Care Career Expo will be held on Mon., May 14, from 10 a.m. to 1 p.m., in the ARCC. Expo exhibits will include health care employers, educational and training programs, and career counseling.

Grant-Writing Workshop held May 21-23

HCC's Continuing Education and Community Services Division will once again host nationally-known instructor Denise Schlegel, who will offer her grant-writing workshop, “Grant Writing – Show Me the Money ©.”

Red, White and Blue Concerts begin June 7

HCC's Alumni Association will hold its eighth annual Red, White and Blue Summer Concert Series from June 7 through Sept. 8, 2012. All concerts are free and are open to the public.

Book Buyback held April 26 - May 7

Book Buyback will be held from April 26 through May 7 at the HCC Bookstore. Hours will vary by day. Please note that only books purchased through the HCC Bookstore may be sold back.
