In the Spotlight

Summer School Perks

If you are looking for something productive to do this summer, enrolling in classes is a great option.

There are many benefits to taking summer classes, like getting into high-demand classes and choosing the professors and science labs you want.

Additional perks of summer school include the opportunity to earn credits quickly. Six-week online and hybrid sessions are available and are easy to fit in with your other summer plans.

Lighten your fall or spring course load by taking prerequisites now so you have a more manageable schedule later.

Get a challenging course out of the way so you can focus without the pressure of a full course load or get ahead and take classes so you can reach graduation faster!

Registration for summer 2012 classes is under way. If you haven’t done so already, click here to get started.

Day and evening classes for the first 6-week and 12-week session begin on May 16. The second 6-week session starts on June 27.

Remember that you can pay your tuition and fees using the NOVA Payment Plan.

Make sure you have an enjoyable and enlightening summer at NOVA.
