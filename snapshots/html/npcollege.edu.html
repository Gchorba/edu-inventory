Media Technical Production

The structure of this course combines introduction to medical/patient care skills, physics, math, medical terminology, human anatomy, cross sectional anatomy, theory, laboratory, computer skills. and a clinical externship..

MRI Technologist

The structure of this course combines introduction to medical/patient care skills, physics, math, medical terminology, human anatomy, cross sectional anatomy, theory, laboratory, computer skills. and a clinical externship.

Diagnostic Medical Sonography

The Diagnostic Medical Sonography Program is designed to prepare the student, through quality lecture, laboratory, and clinical externship in the theoretical knowledge, and skills, required to work in the medical field as an entry-level general sonographer.

Criminal Justice

The Criminal Justice (CJ) Program is designed to thoroughly prepare the student, through lecture, in the theoretical knowledge, skills, and responsibilities required to work in the criminal justice field as an entry-level Private, Public and Non-Profit Security Personnel.

Medical/Dental Office Management

Upon completion of all courses with a grade point no less than 70%, and after satisfactorily completing all practical skills, knowledge assessments, and competencies in the externship portion of the program, the successful graduate will receive a Diploma.

Medical Assistant

This stimulating Medical Assistant program trains students to perform both the clinical and administrative responsibilities that allow medical workplaces and other healthcare practitioner offices to run effectively and smoothly.

Dental Assistant

This exciting hands-on Dental Assistant program successfully prepares the student in a profession to work as a vital member in a dental office team in both a front and back office position.

Massage Therapy

This fast-track Massage Therapy program trains the student in the vital skills needed for success in this rapidly growing profession.

 

Computerized Accounting

This rapid certification training successfully readies a student for a profession in the dynamic field of Computer Accounting and business finance.
