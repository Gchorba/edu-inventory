InovaTech College of Business & Technology

At InovaTech College, you can earn your Professional Certification or Diploma in Information Technology or Business through a combination of academically innovative methods. InovaTech's attention to the learning requirements of adults is legendary and our new flexible programs develop valuable skills and knowledge in areas required by all employees in today's workplace, be it Government or Private Industry.

InovaTech is an accredited College, having been recognized as an institute of higher learning through the Accrediting Council of Continuing Education and Training, (ACCET), under the U.S. Department of Education. This attests to InovaTech's ability in maintaining those standards requisite for our graduates to gain admission to other reputable institutions of higher learning or to achieve credentials for professional practice.
