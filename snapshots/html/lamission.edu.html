  • New Course: Theater 405 - Costume Design!

    Spring 2012: THEATER 405 Costume Design (3 units), Tuesday 3:30 – 6:40 p.m. Class introduces future professionals to the world of costumes, this hands on course is a must for all who love fashion. Register NOW or contact Deborah Paulsen Chair of Arts/Media/Humanities (818) 364-7738 paulsedr@lamission.edu for more questions.

  • Early Childhood Home Visitation Course!

    Spring 2012 CD 055 HOME VISITATION IN EARLY CHILDHOOD PROGRAMS (CSU) 3.00 UNITS Mondays 6:50 PM-10:00 PM : Prepares present and future professionals to conduct home visitations in a variety of contexts: early intervention, family support systems and ECE programs. Register NOW or contact Assistant Professor Gloria Daims at daimsgs@lamission.edu for more questions.

  • You Made it to LAMC, Now Prepare for Success!

    Get the Information you Need to Succeed in College And Receive 1 Transferable Unit to Any CSU in the Process! PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT 017, Section 0423, FALL 2011. Only 7 Weeks! 9/14/2011 - 10/26/2011. Wednesdays 12:15 - 2:35 PM

  • Green Is In!

    Green is in, and tomorrow's jobs will require that you have key skills in sustainable business practices. Learn all about Green Marketing by taking Marketing 22, Section 0805, T-TH 10:40 -12:05. For more information contact Jason Bordbar at bordbajk@lamission.edu. CSU transferable.

  • Choir - Make Your Voice Heard!

    Vacancies now being filled for Fall choir, MUS 501: a one-credit course that is repeatable. We rehearse Mondays from 7-10 pm and will have opportunities to perform on campus. UC/CSU transferable; perfect balance of work and fun. Register now or contact Dr. Tobin Sparfeld at sparfetc@lamission.edu for more questions.
