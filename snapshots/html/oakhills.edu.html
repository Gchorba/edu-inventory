OHCC Graduation

Please join us as we celebrate God’s work in the lives of our graduates. Commencement will take place at 10:00 am on Saturday, May 5, at the Evangelical Free Church of Bemidji (115 Carr Lake Rd SW). We will also be honoring the graduates of Mokahum Ministry Center (a ministry branch of Oak Hills located in Cass Lake). A short reception will follow the ceremony.
