City College – A Leader in Career-Focused Education for More Than 25 Years

You control your destiny, and City College helps you get there.  Our educational degree and diploma programs put you on the fast track to an in-demand career.  All taught by instructors who know a thing or two about what’s needed to succeed these days. Plus hands-on training in real-world situations, so you know just what to expect. Open new doors, reinvent yourself or gain a competitive edge over co-workers in as little as a few months.  From Private Investigation to Medical Assisting, City College works hard to prepare you well for a successful career.
