Welcome to
Redwood Christian Schools

Redwood Christian Schools (RCS) serves many students in grades K-12. RCS also works with over 60 children in the Concept Help program which is designed to assist children with specific learning disabilities and other challenges that prevent them from functioning in our regular program.

Our Christian Schools are located in the SF East Bay Area, 25 miles southeast of San Francisco. Redwood Christian Schools (RCS) currently has two East Bay Christian School campuses: one elementary Christian School,  Redwood Christian Elementary, in Castro Valley, and a Christian Middle School & High School located in San Lorenzo, California. RCS also has students from around the world including China, Korea, Japan, and other parts of Asia, as well as South America. RCS is authorized under federal law to enroll non-immigrant alien students and issue an I-20 for an F1 visa.

We maintain our identity as an interdenominational Christ-centered school serving hundreds of families throughout the San Francisco East Bay area. Our students and their families represent more than 130 churches, and over 30 Christian denominations. More about our Christian Schools here

Redwood Christian Schools (RCS) Mission Statement:
The mission of Redwood Christian Schools is to provide a Christ-centered education which is able to equip students for daily living and eternal life.

 Why Redwood Christian Schools?

 The cumulative results of our students' test scores indicates that our classes are one to two years ahead of national norms. Students matriculate to some of the finest universities and colleges in the United States. (A current list of where our graduates are currently attending is available upon request.)

The Redwood Christian Chapter of the California Scholarship Federation is based on outstanding high school scholastic achievement and good citizenship. Members are given specific privileges and opportunities.

Extracurricular activities include computers, science fairs, sports, speech meets, academic meets and student government.

The textbooks provided by our schools are selected from the best available. Excellence in content and harmony with a Christian philosophy are important considerations when choosing an education for your children.

 RCS Doctrinal Statement:

As a Christian School, we believe that God is an eternal personal being of absolute knowledge, power, goodness and righteousness. He is ultimate reality, the source and foundation of all reality and truth.We believe that Jesus Christ, God’s only Son, is creator, designer and preserver of all things. In Him the entire universe has its origin, energy, control and final destiny.
