Welcome to the MECR Web Site!

Founded in 1978, the Montessori Education Center of the Rockies offers three American Montessori Society teacher preparation courses and also responds to growing numbers of requests for information and workshops on Montessori education in the Rocky Mountain area. There is renewed interest in the benefits of Montessori education in Colorado and throughout the country, and a demand for excellent teachers. MECR strives to meet that need. 

Montessori Education Center of the Rockies is affiliated with the American Montessori Society, www.amshq.org. Our certification courses are accredited by the Montessori Accreditation Council for Teacher Education, www.macte.org
