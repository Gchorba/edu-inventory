UNCW Professor Part of Major NSF Grant for Climate Change Research
The Earth is warming. Determining what this will mean for future generations is one of the greatest challenges in modern science, and UNCW environmental science professor Paul Hearty has been tapped as one of a world-class team of scientists working to provide answers to this question.

Public History Students Create Exhibit to Help Tidal Creek Cooperative Market Celebrate 30 Years Serving the Wilmington Community
When Tidal Creek Co-op needed help creating a historical exhibit to commemorate its 30th anniversary, history professor Tammy Gordon and her students in the Public History Program were up to the challenge. The exhibit is on permanent display in the retail store, making shoppers part of the history.

May 5 Conference to Focus on the Human Side of Healthcare
Sponsored by the College of Health & Human Services, the conference will bring healthcare professionals together to discuss a "strengths-based" approach that builds on client and community strengths, focusing on the "person beyond the paperwork." 8:30 a.m. to 5 p.m. May 5 in McNeill Hall.
