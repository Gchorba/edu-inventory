CDE Career Institute

A Technical Career Training School in Pennsylvania

Investing in education is an extremely important decision. Training that can assist in increasing your earning potential and expand on your employment opportunities is definitely a worthwhile investment in your future. At CDE Career Institute, in addition to serving as a technical career training school to our students, we help them develop a greater sense of responsibility, which makes them better learners.

CDE Career Institute is a Pennsylvania career school that has established a long-term relationship with area employers near Tannersville and Wilkes-Barre, Pennsylvania – enabling us to keep our finger on the pulse of new developments in the labor force. We stay abreast of the job skills employers are looking for, and make adjustments to our training to keep our students’ abilities always up-to-date. Our goal is to provide short-term training for long-term employment – all in a supportive and enjoyable atmosphere.

At CDE, we realize that you may not be sure what career to pursue, what skills you need to enter or advance in a career, or what your earning potential will be when you acquire these skills. We thoroughly evaluate your present job skills and determine the additional new skills you will need to enter into the career path of your choice. Regardless of the certificate program you choose, CDE Career Institute delivers high-quality instruction and guidance for students in Pennsylvania.

Our faculty and staff provide the motivation and backing to help all students understand the importance of fully applying themselves so they can develop skills to stay ahead of their competition. We derive tremendous satisfaction in seeing each student achieve his or her career goals. We sincerely hope you will become a part of our happy family so we can help you realize your full economic and professional potential.
