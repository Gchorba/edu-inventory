40 years of success!

Columbia college provides comprehensive, student  centered academic and vocational education programs, which help prepare career paths and ultimately, increase future opportunities. We offer Associate in Arts Degrees, Associate in Science Degrees, Associate in Science (Occupational Education) Degrees, and Certificates of Achievement that can be completed in two years. The quality of education at our institution can be attributed to dedicated faculty and staff, low student-instructor ratios, and a nurturing learning environment.
