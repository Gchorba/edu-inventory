Northwest Health Careers Las Vegas, Massage
School, Dental Assistant School,
Medical Assistant School

Northwest Health Careers has been educating individuals who are interested in a career in the exciting and expanding field of medical and health care for over 15 years. We pride our school on the variety of programs we offer. We have a wide selection of programs everyone can be interested in including: massage therapy, dental assistant training, medical assisting, medical billing, phlebotomy, CPR training, GED & continuing educations. We strive to help all of our students become the best they can be with a bright future in the health care industry. We offer career placement and assistance to help our graduates find a position in the Las Vegas Valley and beyond.

We encourage you to contact an admissions counselor today to find out how a health career education can help you put your future in your hands. Come take a tour of our beautiful campus in Las Vegas.
