Welcome to George T. Baker Aviation School
Welcome to Baker Aviation!

George T. Baker Aviation School is located at 3275 N.W. 42 Avenue, adjacent to the Miami International Airport. The school is a public, tax-supported institution authorized by the Florida Department of Education and operated by the Miami-Dade County Public School System. It is accredited by the Council on Occupational Education (COE) and the National Center for Aircraft Technician Training (NCATT) and is certificated by the Federal Aviation Administration under Part 147 of the Federal Aviation Regulations. Certificate # CT9T072R.

New System to Streamline Registration
Written by Administrator   
Monday, 26 March 2012 10:38

Baker Aviation has instituted a new system to improve the efficiency and equity of current student registration.


Starting for the 2011-3 trimester, ALL current students must make an appointment online in order to see the registrar to register for his/her classes.

The process is simple:


1.    Visit www.bakeraviation.edu/registrationappointment
2.    Create a user account (do ASAP!)
a.    Upon initial creation of your account, an email will be sent to you to verify.
b.    Be sure to VERIFY your email – or you will not be able to make an appointment!
3.    After you have successfully created your account, you will be able to login and see the appointment system.
4.    On Saturday, March 31st at 0730, you will be able to login and select your appointment to come in and register.
5.    You must bring your registration paperwork (stamped either “REGULAR” or “PRIORITY”, appointment notification, and Baker ID with you to your appointment in the main office or you will not be able to register and will have to wait till April 16th, 2012.
6.    If you make more than one appointment, all of your appointments will be canceled and you will need to re-enter your appointment at whatever time is still available or wait till April 16th, 2012.
7.    If you miss your appointment, you will have to wait till April 16th, 2012, to register.

Baker Receives $10K grant from ISTAT Foundation
Written by Administrator   
Wednesday, 04 January 2012 07:49

Mr. Carter does it again!  He recently entered into a competitive grant competition with the International Society of Transport Aircraft Trading (ISTAT) Foundation (http://www.istat.org/) and was awarded $10,000.  ISTAT stated that it, "finds your cause of great importance and we are happy to support your institution.  We believe you are an important member of the aviation community and are pleased to assist you in your continuing efforts."  Mr. Carter states that, "through the grant, high school students from each of our programs will combine their knowledge in the building of a FAA Level 3 approved full motion X-Plane Flight Simulator which will assist Baker Aviation in the achieving new programmatic objectives as set by industry."
