For 40 years , Tennessee Technology Center at Pulaski has been proud to serve the community by providing relevant, quality training and education, giving our students the opportunity to develop the necessary skills to successfully compete in today's global job market. We have always believed that technical colleges are not only in the education and training business, but in the "Changing Lives" business as well. Students come to the Tennessee Technology Center at Pulaski for a new and successful start on life.

We Encourage you to find the career training that interests you. Then, take the time to talk with one of our counselors who can help determine the best route to reach your goals. Keep in mind that there is a variety of financial assistance available to those who qualify. Above all remember this: We are here to help you succeed in life.

Our faculty and staff are all committed to help you achieve your dreams and career goals. Our center features a "hands-on" learning environment, small class sizes, and numerous, affordable career options in high demand occupational fields.

Each year, approximately 1,200 students come to TTCP to learn new a new skill or upgrade their existing skills. During the day, you can find these students enrolled in one of our 16 full time programs located throughout our campuses in Pulaski, Lawrenceburg, Loretto, or Lewisburg.

Our goal is to see you succeed. Come join us and become a part of the Tennessee Technology Center at Pulaski family.
