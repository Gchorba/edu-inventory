Seminary for a Day
May 11


Seminary for a Day is the perfect opportunity to check out our historic grounds, attend lectures and worship. SFTS President Jim McDonald and Associate Professor of Old Testament Dr. Annette Schellenberg will be the lecturers this year.
2012 Distinguished Alumni
May 19


Rev. Dr. ChangBok Chung and Rev. Dr. Joseph Kang, the 2012 Distinguished Alumni, will address graduates at the San Francisco Theological Seminary commencement ceremony. Graduation is Saturday, May 19, at 9:30 a.m. at Bouick Field.
 Preachers Retreat
July 23-27


Study and pray the 2012-13 lectionary year in our unique Preachers Retreat, which blends sermon preparation with spiritual formation. Contemplative worship complements our scholarly and prayerful approach to the lectionary texts from Advent to Easter.
