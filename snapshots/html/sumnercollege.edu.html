Areas of study: Corrections, Court Reporting, Paralegal Studies and Nursing

Since 1974, Sumner College has been successfully preparing students in the Portland area for exciting and rewarding careers.

Sumner College career programs include practical learning experiences and professional instruction specifically focused on your chosen career field.  Instructors have real world experience providing you with the necessary knowledge to truly understand the skills required in your chosen field of study.  As a student, you'll have the opportunity to achieve the skills and confidence necessary to pursue positions in the legal or healthcare field.

We appreciate and commend you on your commitment to yourself.  At Sumner College, we're dedicated to supporting you in the pursuit of a new career and a rewarding future.  We provide quality career education programs to prepare our graduates to pursue in careers in the legal and healthcare field:

  • Correctional Officer
  • Court Reporting
  • Practical Nursing
  • Paralegal

Sumner College programs are specifically designed with your busy schedule in mind.  Day and Evening courses are available and financial aid is available for those who qualify.

Is a Career in the Legal or Healthcare Field Right for You?

To discover if the Legal or Healthcare field is right for you, complete the form on this page and you will receive a FREE Career Information Kit. A friendly and knowledgeable Sumner College Admissions Representative will be contacting you shortly.  If you'd like to speak with a representative immediately please call us at 877-682-5601.

You can schedule a time to come in for a free campus tour, meet with an instructor, observe a class, or meet one-on-one with an Admissions Representative who can answer all your questions. You can even meet with a Financial Aid Representative to discuss the financial aid options that will work best for you.

To arrange a visit, use the online request form on this page or call us at 877-682-5601.

Appointments are available day and evening, Monday - Thursday 8am to 7pm and from 8 am to 4 pm on Fridays.
