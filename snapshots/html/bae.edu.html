Our Mission... Learn English. Live English!™

Boston Academy of English is committed to teaching English as a Second language, Test Preparation, and the Certificate of Teaching English as a Foreign Language to foreign students from around the world and the US through small, high quality, interactive classes in a stimulating environment.

Boston Academy of English wants you to not only learn English – we want you to live it! Experience American culture in the best city for international students in the world – Boston.

Known as America’s “Walking City”, Boston is said to be the city where the best English in the country is spoken.  True or not, the city’s charm is undeniable…from the Charles River to Quincy Market and the waterfront, its neighborhoods, culture, and sense of history will delight you. Opportunities for students of all ages to learn English are abundant here.

Boston Academy of English... We have it all!

Whether you need to improve your English before applying to one of the outstanding colleges and universities in the Boston area - or you need English to prepare you for the better job that you deserve – our Academy is the English school for you!

Accredited by ACCET, our central downtown location enables you to enjoy Boston’s old-world charm within steps from our school…the Boston Common and shopping district are just outside our door! Inside, our warm, caring environment and small classes ensure that you will receive the individual attention you deserve

A friendly school, superior classes, prime location, quality accommodations, and a wide range of services and fun activities - why would you learn English anywhere else? We look forward to seeing you soon at the Boston Academy of English!
