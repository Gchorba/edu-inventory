Stenotype Institute: America's Premier Court Reporting School™

How would you like to get into a career that's prestigious, has flexible hours, is recession proof, and offers an average starting wage of $62K per year? (source: NCRA) Look no further than Stenotype Institute, the premier court reporting school in America!

Why a Court Reporting Career?

In today's economy, many Americans are struggling to find work. This is especially true of college graduates who earned a 4 year bachelor's degree with the hope of beginning a promising new career, only to find themselves joining the ever-growing ranks of America's unemployed. With a court reporting career, you can skip this path and go straight to a career with prestige that pays you what you're worth and is always in demand. And the best part is, you can complete your court reporter school training quickly and be out there working in a field you will truly enjoy!

When you are trained as a stenographer, you will not only be qualified to use your skills inside the courtroom, but also as a general stenographer, where you will be able to work in a variety of fields, including Web Casting & College Lectures and Live & Recorded Broadcast Closed Captioning (note: additional training may be required).

The possibilities are virtually unlimited. And, many of the closed captioning and general stenography jobs today can be done from the comfort of your own home. With all the ways you can put your stenography skills to use, there is little chance you will ever have to worry about finding work -- even in a bad economy.

Learning from the Best

Established in 1940, the Stenotype Institute of Jacksonville, Inc. is one of the oldest and most respected court reporting schools in America. For over 70 years, we have trained thousands of students to become successful court reporters and stenographers. Our philosophy is that success depends on personal integrity, dedication to a strong work ethic, and continual education to keep pace with today's ever-changing technological world. Our highly qualified teachers are experts in their respective fields, who will give you the hands on training you need to prepare you to excel in your new career. At the Stenotype Institute, we have an average 95% job placement rate for the past 10 years, so you can look forward to starting your new high-paying career soon after you graduate.

We have two conveniently located Florida campuses in Jacksonville and Orlando. If you live outside these two cities, we also have online programs that allow you to obtain your certification from the comfort of your own home. Financial aid is available for those who qualify. Contact us today at 888-55-STENO (888-557-8366) and put yourself on an exciting and lucrative career path at the Stenotype Institute, America's premier court reporting school.
