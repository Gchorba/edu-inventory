Program Video of the Week

Explore water ways

Water. We just expect it to be there when we stop by the drinking fountain or go to wash our hands. Thanks to skilled, professional plumbers, it is.

SCTCC's Plumbing Program exposes students to a variety of experiences in a typical plumbing career. Installation of fixtures, pipe threading, hot and cold water supply, drainage systems, fabrication and testing, maintenance and repair of plumbing, and hydronic heating are among these. Students in the program also install plumbing and fixtures in the house project built each year by the construction technology programs. From concept to completion, SCTCC students make sure its move-in ready.
