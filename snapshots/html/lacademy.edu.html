LA Teacher's Play Adaptation to be Performed in Concord
This spring, the Concord Players will perform a stage adaptation, written by Lawrence Academy English teacher David Smith, of Louisa May Alcott's beloved Little Women. The Players produce a version of the Alcott story every ten years, and this is the fourth time that David and the Players have collaborated in this tradition.
