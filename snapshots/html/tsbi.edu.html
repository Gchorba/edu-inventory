          Fortis Institute in Erie Erie, PA

          • Overview
          • Gallery
          • Mission

          For career training in the Erie, Pennsylvania area, check out the programs offered at Fortis Institute! Formerly known as Tri-State Business Institute, Fortis Institute continues TSBI's proud tradition of helping students succeed in life by offering a wide variety of career training programs in the nursing, medical, dental, business, computer, information technology, skilled trades, cosmetology and paralegal career fields

          If you have been looking for a nursing program or emergency medical services program in Erie, Pennsylvania, contact Fortis Institute today! Fortis Institute offers a great opportunity for students in Erie, Pennsylvania, to start accomplishing their goals with a great education! Students in nearby cities in the tri-state area of New York, Ohio and Pennsylvania can also take advantage of the programs offered at Fortis.

          At Fortis Institute, you will learn from instructors who have real-world experience in their field who will partner with you to increase your potential for success, no matter which program you choose! From the moment you contact us, we will focus on making your goal of getting an education and starting a career a reality!

          Fortis Institute, Erie, PA is accredited by the Accrediting Council for Independent Colleges and Schools to award Occupational Associate Degrees and Diplomas (ACICS).

          The program in dental hygiene is accredited by the Commission on Dental Accreditation [and has been granted the accreditation status of “approval without reporting requirements”]. The Commission is a specialized accrediting body recognized by the United States Department of Education. The Commission on Dental Accreditation can be contacted at (312) 440-4653 or at 211 East Chicago Avenue, Chicago, IL 60611.

          It is the mission of Fortis Institute, Erie PA to provide students with the practical skills and the responsible behaviors necessary to secure rewarding careers in their specialized fields of study. To accomplish this mission, the Institute provides students with updated postsecondary technical knowledge and skill level. Our institution's objectives are as follows:

          • To provide job skills based upon current industry standards
          • To provide a dedicated, educationally prepared faculty
          • To assure the highest standards of professional ethics in all student/staff relationships
          • To graduate students capable of assuming a productive place in society and in the business community
          • To provide job placement assistance throughout the professional careers of our graduates.
