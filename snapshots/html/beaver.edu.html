Arcadia University promises students global perspectives, integrated real-world learning and research experiences, and personal attention. Arcadia University offers bachelor's, master's and doctoral degrees full-time, part-time and online, with a respected Forensic Science degree, Peace Studies, study abroad programs and more. Arcadia is a private, comprehensive university in metropolitan Philadelphia.
