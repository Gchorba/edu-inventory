Welcome to United Theological Seminary

At United Theological Seminary we have one goal: to educate dynamic, Spirit-led leaders who will
renew the church for the mission of Jesus Christ in the world. We are committed to teaching the Bible
and the historic Christian faith, instilling a passion for personal and social holiness, and renewing the
Church for its ministry and mission.
