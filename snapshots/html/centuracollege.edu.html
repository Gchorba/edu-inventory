Centura College Career School


Centura College has nine locations in South Carolina and Virginia. We also have an online distance learning division in addition to our Centura Institute school in Florida. Between each of these Centura College schools, we offer associate of applied science degrees, associate of occupational science degrees, a Bachelor of Science completion degree, diplomas, and certificates that may suit your career needs. Please note that not all program lengths are available at all campuses so please contact us for your specific career needs.

Areas of study at Centura College include** business with concentrations in management & accounting administration, paralegal law, criminal justice, practical nursing, massage therapy, dental assistant, HVAC technician, medical assisting in clinical or management studies, medical billing & coding, network technician and computer network management.

Our school is a well-established adult education college with fast track affordable college programs allowing adult students to finish school, develop real world skills, and advance their careers. Centura College develops each program with one goal - to place you in a better career. The school’s curriculum is designed to provide you with the relevant and technological skills to meet the demands of current and emerging industries. You will be prepared for a career in medical, justice, networking, business, government, state or other industries.

With time restraints on career-oriented adults, we know that balancing an education with family, friends and work can be tough. But with Centura College, it’s possible to reach your dreams on your terms – a lot sooner than you think. With adult education classes available during both days and evenings as well as online, achieving your goals is a click away.

Upon college graduation, students are assisted in marketing themselves to employers. Instructions on resume writing, proper attire and personal appearance, interviewing techniques and other areas are taught to help secure a satisfying career position. Personal interviews are arranged between the graduate and potential employer. No job is guaranteed but our Career Services Officer will strive to help graduates achieve their career goals.
