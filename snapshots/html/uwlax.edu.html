Campus Connection, April 30, 2012

Check out this week's campus news highlighting many exciting upcoming events.

Choral Union to perform Mozart Requiem

The UW-L Choral Union will sing Mozart Requiem at 7:30 p.m. Sun., May 6, at the English Lutheran Church in La Crosse. Choral Union’s performance of this major classical work is accompanied by orchestra and soloists.

Public invited to explore new archaeological sites with MVAC

The Mississippi Valley Archaeology Center’s annual volunteer field survey, Sat., May 5 and Sun., May 6, is a chance for the public to discover artifacts and potential archaeological sites for future exploration.
