St. Luke’s College Leads the State with Outstanding Marks

St. Luke's College, announces 2011 results of the National Licensure Examination (NCLEX-RN). The Iowa Board of Nursing released official 2011 NCLEX-RN testing data on January 23, 2012. Among Iowa’s 30 pre-licensure nursing programs, St. Luke’s College ranks at the top with 97.78% of first time test takers passing their licensure examination.(Learn More)
