Welcome to Southern Career College!

It all begins here, where you can find the training and education you need to succeed in your new career.

From the moment you set foot on our campus, you can feel the energy. Look around and you’ll see students who are on the move. Students that are committed to making their career dream a reality.

SCC is a private, two-year junior college that offers both associate degree and diploma programs. The keys to our successful programs include experienced faculty, hands-on instruction, modern facilities and equipment, and a supportive classroom-learning environment.

Our programs are designed to give you the training and skills that today’s employers require and demand.

That’s why at Southern Career College, it all begins here!
