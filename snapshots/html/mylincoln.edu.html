What is MyLincoln.edu

MyLincoln.edu provides students and alumni with a free 10GB lifetime email account, 25GB online storage, online collaboration tools, photo sharing, mobile alerts, instant messaging tools and much more!

This service is powered by Microsoft Live@edu. Live@edu includes access to Office Web Applications built with the same Office interface used by more than 500 million people worldwide. These tools will help you gain additional technical skills and may help you gain a competitive edge at school and in the job market.
