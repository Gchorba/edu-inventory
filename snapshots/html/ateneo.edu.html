The Ateneo welcomes qualified applicants from diverse backgrounds. Strongly rooted in the Jesuit education system that gives importance to a disciplined mind, body, and heart, the school looks for prospective students with strong academic qualifications, a love of learning, a desire to serve others, and a solid potential to contribute to the enrichment of the Ateneo community.
