The AIMS’ educational program is dedicated to providing quality education in the field of Medical Sonography. Our teaching methods, which include clinical and community service for preliminary screening, are designed to help students advance in their field of study. We are instrumental in the education development process allowing students to learn new techniques and procedures. Our goal is to provide an opportunity for personal growth and develop professional competence.
AIMS delivers high-quality education and training programs that will teach, train and assist in placing participants in their chosen field. The educators are registered sonographers who not only teach at the school, but also perform ultrasound exams in acute care hospitals and private physician practices.
The ultrasound equipment used in the classroom is the same models of equipment you will find in hospitals, physician groups and other medical practices. The clinical laboratories and community service are a challenge to each student as they advance in their field of study and provide them an experience that is difficult to find in any other program.
