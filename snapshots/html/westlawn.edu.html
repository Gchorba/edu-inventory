Founded in 1930, Westlawn Institute is the only nationally accredited distance-learning school of small-craft design in the United States. Westlawn Institute is an accredited member DETC, in Washington DC, and the Westlawn Institute Yacht & Boat Design Program is accredited by RINA, in London, England.
