Presidential Update

LVC has extended its search to find a new leader to replace its 17th president, Stephen C. MacDonald who plans to retire later this year.

Learn about this process, read a detailed profile of LVC, or discover more about the distinguished history of our great institution by visiting the Presidential Search Website.

To learn about the tenure of President MacDonald, see the feature in the latest issue of LVC’s The Valley magazine.
Lebanon Valley College™ in Annville, Pa., welcomes 1,630 full-time undergraduates studying more than 30 majors, as well as self-designed majors. Founded in 1866, LVC has graduate programs in physical therapy, business, and music and science education. Annville is 15 minutes east of Hershey and 35 minutes east of Harrisburg; Philadelphia, Washington, D.C., and Baltimore are within two hours.
