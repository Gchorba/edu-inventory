All Moms receive free general admission to the Museum on Mother's Day, Sunday, May 13th! Ask about discounts for Theater and a list of all special activities scheduled in Mom's honor.

The Museum will close at 3:00pm on Thursday, May 10th in order to prepare for a Special Event. There will be a 3:30pm showing of Born To Be Wild and 4:30pm showing of Tornado Alley at the IMAX. We apologize for any inconvenience.

Art and the Animal
See some of the best animal art in the world in this special free exhibition of more than 60 pieces. Opens May 26.
