Learning the Key Foundations of Cooking School

Begin with the basics. At Le Cordon Bleu, you will learn how food preparation is organized, how to talk about food and handle it safely. Throughout your cooking school endeavor, you will continually learn new techniques, fundamentals, and proficiencies of cooking. Experience the world of global cuisine and develop your creativity. Then practice your skills in the industry through your culinary school externship.

Learn the way and:

  • Understand foundational techniques and innovations
  • Train alongside experienced chef instructors
  • Use fine ingredients and professional tools of the trade
  • In commercial kitchens and a real restaurant setting
  • Obtain real-world experience with an externship

Learn the Fundamentals of Baking and Pastry Arts

At Le Cordon Bleu, you will learn the art of baking and pastry. Understand ingredients and the science of baking. Your culinary school education will teach you the essential fundamental techniques and key proficiencies. Explore the world of classical European desserts, pastry, creams, chocolates and decorating. And move on to creating centerpieces and wedding cakes. Finish your culinary school training learning alongside industry professionals in your externship. Learn the way. Experience Le Cordon Bleu.

Learn the way and:

  • Learn fundamentals and proficiencies of the craft
  • Train alongside experienced pastry chef instructors
  • Use fine ingredients and professional tools of the trade
  • In industry kitchens and a real restaurant setting
  • Perfect your skills in your externship
