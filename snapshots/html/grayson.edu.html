New Bacterial Meningitis Vaccination Requirement

Grayson County College in compliance with Texas Education Code, Section 51.9192, Subchapter Z, as amended by the 82nd Texas Legislature, now requires the bacterial meningitis vaccination for all entering students enrolling in classes after January 1, 2012. See the Bacterial Meningitis Vaccination Requirements for more information.
