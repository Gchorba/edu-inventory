Welcome to the Florida College of Integrative Medicine. The staff and students take great pride in the Florida College of Integrative Medicine environment. In addition to leadership in integrative medicine education and an advanced on-site clinic, there is great camaraderie among students-study groups, gatherings and building friendships-and a strong rapport between students and staff. The administrative staff and faculty are always approachable for discussions and questions.

Florida College of Integrative Medicine is a dynamic and progressive school for integrative medicine. As one of oldest acupuncture schools in the U.S, our curriculum places emphasis on Oriental medicine technologies (acupuncture, herbs, etc) and clinical practices, along with Western biomedical sciences to support proper patient treatment and integration of these methodologies within the overall health care delivery system.
