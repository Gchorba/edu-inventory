A little about us:

Seymour Beauty Academy of Seymour Cosmetology Inc. has been successfully training individuals in Cosmetology and Nail Technology for about 10 years. However the business has evolved out of a privately family owned Cosmetology business and school dating back to the 1950s. Some of those former students have gone on to receive national  and  international recognition for their skills.  The school is located in the small community of Seymour, Missouri on the west side of the city square. We are about 35 miles east of Springfield, MO just off highway 60. 

Hours of Operation:
Tuesday through Friday,  8:30 AM to 5:00PM with 2 breaks and 1/2 hour for lunch. 

Goals:
To develop a superior program of study that meets and exceeds the State required standards for schools of cosmetology in Missouri in order to produce skilled and knowledgeable professional cosmetologists who will be prepared with the necessary fundamentals to enter the cosmetology field;

 To create an exceptional environment and atmosphere in which all students can learn;
 
To help each student achieve his or her own professional goals and develop life skills other than cosmetology skills;
 
To adhere to a code of ethics that is empathetic with all individuals, treating them with courtesy and compassion regardless of physical appearance, educational or financial levels;
 
To have the administration and faculty continually seek to improve their own knowledge, the quality of the school and its programs;
 
To produce a reasonable profit, which will ensure growth and improvement in the students, staff, facilities and equipment;
 
To ensure that all administration, faculty, and students work together as team and provide support to each other.
Enrollment Requirements:
Applicants for Cosmetology, Hairdressing, and Nail Technology must provide proof of age and be 17 years of age by the time application is made for State examination. They must have a high school diploma or a GED certificate or equivalent. For the Instructor Training course, the applicants must provide proof of successful completion of a twelfth grade education with a diploma or GED certificate, and a sworn statement that the applicant is in good physical and mental health. The applicant is only eligible for an Instructor Trainee license for the classified occupations for which the applicant is currently licensed as an operator.
