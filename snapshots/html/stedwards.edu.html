St. Edward's At A Glance

Global Learning
DOING GOOD WHILE DOING BUSINESS

What’s the connection between good business and doing good? Our Global Business and Social Justice Institute challenges faculty and students to consider that equation.

Target Your Interests
FLEXIBLE MASTER’S PROGRAM

Master of Liberal Arts gives you the flexibility to design your own program of study to explore your passions — and connect your life interests with your professional ambitions.

Record Philanthropy
DONORS CONTRIBUTE $114 MILLION

Since 1999, the university has raised more than $114 million. This record generosity includes $42 million for the university's endowment and $50 million for new buildings and capital improvements.
