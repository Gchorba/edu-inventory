Attracting some of the finest students and teachers in the country, Albuquerque Academy is an independent, coeducational, college preparatory day school of 1,100 students in grades 6-12. The Academy serves the greater Albuquerque area and commits its resources to a mission comprising accessibility, affordability, excellence, caring, and outreach.
